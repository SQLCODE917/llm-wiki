## Source record

This section covers rules for recovering mental power and magic expansion in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.

## Key supported claims

- Each player must make a resistance roll using monster's magic intensity as the target score (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.60-60).
- The following procedure is used to make a check when data created for a character casts magic on adventurers (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.60-60).
- Each magic has a score called base mental power cost, which is divided by the corresponding rune master skill level (rounded up) to determine the actual mental power consumed (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.60-60).
- If a character's mental power reaches 0, they will lose consciousness and cannot regain consciousness unless they recover their mental power to 1 point or more (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.60-60).
- Magic expansion is done by doubling or tripling the amount of mental power consumed, not the base mental power cost (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.61-61).

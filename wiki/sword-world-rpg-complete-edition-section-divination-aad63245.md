## Statements

- This is the ability to pray and communicate with a god, asking whether or not one's actions are right. Occasionally a god will give you a revelation (given at the game master's discretion). However, a god's words can only be understood abstractly by humans, and you cannot ask detailed questions to the gods. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04321))_
- Revelations should not be used too conveniently for the characters (players). It must be done only when they absolutely need a word from their god to protect their faith. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04322))_

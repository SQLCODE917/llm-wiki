## Source record

This section details various types of undead creatures found in the Sword World RPG, including their characteristics, abilities, and behaviors.

## Key supported claims

- Undead are created from corpses using ancient or dark magic, with varying intelligence and behaviors (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-217).
- Some undead, like zombies and skeletons, are created through magic and obey commands, while others, like ghouls and wights, have more complex abilities (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-217).
- Characters can cast the spell _Turn Undead_ to affect certain undead creatures (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-217).
- Some undead, such as ash, are created from burned corpses and have unique physical characteristics (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-217).
- Undead creatures can possess living beings, with different resistance scores for possession attempts (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-217).

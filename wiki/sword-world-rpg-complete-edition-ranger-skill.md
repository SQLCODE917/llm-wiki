The ranger skill is used in the surprise attack check, where the baseline score is ranger skill level + intelligence bonus. This skill is one of the key factors in determining whether adventurers or monsters can initiate a surprise attack, as detailed in [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-chapter-10-3-surprise-attacks]].

## Sword World RPG - Complete Edition: Chapter 16.7 Throwing Multiple Darts or Daggers

This section introduces an optional rule for throwing multiple darts or daggers at once, with specific restrictions and penalties.

### Key Rules

1.  **Same Weight Requirement**: All objects thrown at the same time must have the _same weight_ (required strength).
2.  **Strength Limitation**: The _total required strength_ must be up to the _character's strength_ (or _half_ that if it's the thief skill).
3.  **Penalty for Number Thrown**: The _number_ thrown at the same time becomes a _penalty_ to attack power. For example, if you throw _three_ at the same time, that’ll incur a -3 penalty to your attack power for each hit check.
4.  **Single Target**: You _cannot_ choose _multiple_ targets. There must _always_ be _one_ target.
5.  **Engagement Restriction**: Projectiles _cannot_ be used while in an _engagement_.

This rule increases the chance that a high-level character will defeat a low-level character or monster in one blow.

## Source record

Summary of animal stat blocks and combat behavior from chapter 13.2.7 (part 2).

## Key supported claims

- Grizzly bears possess unusually high intellect for an animal, meaning players should not let their guard down (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211).
- They are classified among the most terrifying animals due to super strength and agility beyond what their physique suggests (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211).
- Grizzly bear stat block: Level 3, rarity 6, agility 14, speed 21, with attack points fangs 10 (3) and claws 11 (4) (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211).
- They can extend a 5‑meter long tongue to entangle prey and may swallow a human‑sized animal whole (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211).
- Female and male variants share similar stats (Level 3, rarity 5, agility 22) with fangs 10 (3) and claws 11 (4); gender differences are minor (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211).

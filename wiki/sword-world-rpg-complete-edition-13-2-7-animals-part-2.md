## Key supported claims

- Grizzly bears are about 3 meters tall, with some reaching 4 meters. They are classified as one of the most terrifying animals due to their super strength and agility. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211)
- Lions usually live in small family groups, with one male and several females. Males and females differ slightly in strength. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211)
- Giant slugs are about 5 meters long and move slowly, knocking down everything in their path. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211)
- Poison toads are about 50 cm long and can spew venom when startled. Anyone within 3 meters must succeed on a life force resistance roll against target score 9. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211)
- Giant toads are 3 meters long and can entangle prey with their 5-meter-long tongue. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211)

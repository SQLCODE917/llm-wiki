## Statements

- When you cast this spell, a force field surrounds the caster, acting as armor with defense power 30. This armor appears to glow vaguely. There is no required strength, and it does not hinder the caster's movement at all. Even if the caster is already wearing armor, their defense power does not stack (the defense power of the armor you were wearing will be nullified). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04711))_

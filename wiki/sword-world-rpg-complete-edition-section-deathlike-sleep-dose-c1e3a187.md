# [Deathlike Sleep (Dose)]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a liquid poison. Those who fail to resist will stop breathing and their heart will stop, making them appear completely dead. However, they're actually in a state of suspended animation and will return to normal after 12 hours. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06598))_

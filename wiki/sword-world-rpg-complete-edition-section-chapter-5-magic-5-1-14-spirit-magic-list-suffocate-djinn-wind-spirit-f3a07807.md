## Statements

- This spell suffocates the target and ultimately results in death. Those who've had this spell cast on them and fail to resist will be unable to breathe. For this reason, spells cannot be used and all other actions also suffer a -4 penalty. Then, once _life force (points)_ rounds have elapsed, they will suffocate and die. However, if the spell's duration expires before _life force (points)_ rounds have elapsed, the spell becomes meaningless. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02591))_

# Cold Protective Circle

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The effect of this spell is almost the same as Heat Protective Circle , but it protects against damage from ice and cold. Those within the area of effect can subtract 2 points from this kind of damage. Also, frau, an ice spirit, will not try to enter this range. If they are inside when the spell is cast, they will immediately try to escape, and if for some reason that is not possible, they will return to their own spirit world. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03060))_

## Source record

This section details the 9th and 10th level basic holy magic spells available in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, including Recall Spirit, Resurrection, Call God, and Save Soul.

## Key supported claims

- The Recall Spirit spell requires the priest's soul to be called to their god, allowing resurrection of the dead only if the caster knows them well (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.104-104).
- Resurrection spell fails if the caster attempts it again on the same dead person after an initial failure, and cannot be used on those who have become undead or whose souls are destroyed (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.104-104).
- Those who die due to old age cannot be saved by Resurrection, as it's not possible to reverse age (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.104-104).
- Resurrection requires a final score that increases by 1 for each day passed since death, and takes one week for the body to return to normal function (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.104-104).
- The effect of Resurrection is uncertain, with unknown success rates based on various factors including soul condition and time passed (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.104-104).

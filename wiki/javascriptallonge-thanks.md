## Source record

*JavaScript Allongé* was inspired by *The Little Schemer* by Daniel Friedman and Matthias Felleisen, and by Richard Feynman’s *QED: The Strange Theory of Light and Matter*.

## Key supported claims

- JavaScript Allongé was inspired by The Little Schemer by Daniel Friedman and Matthias Felleisen, focusing on functions as first-class values (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.291-292).
- Another inspiration was Richard Feynman’s QED: The Strange Theory of Light and Matter, which explains Quantum Electrodynamics using simple, concise terms (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.291-292).
- The author was inspired by both books, which emphasize clear explanations and foundational concepts (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.291-292).

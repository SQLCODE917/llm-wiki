### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 11.3 Dragon Roar Magic / 《 Summon 》

- Spells of this type are spells that summon and employ the race specified by each spell. When using these spells, the caster must determine the final score of the spell. Then, compare this final score with the mental resistance score of the creature you're trying to summon. The result will determine when it will be summoned (Table 11-1: Summon). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03976))_
- Summoning takes place across space. Therefore, it's possible to instantly summon creatures no matter where they are. However, the closer the target creature is, the easier it will be to summon it. When using the _Summon_ table, the game master can apply any modifiers as desired. If you can basically infer that they'll be in the surrounding 10km, please add +2. If you know for sure they're there, +4. The caster can also increase the final score through spell expansion or rituals. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03977))_
- However, no matter what modifiers are applied, if the 2D roll when calculating the final score is double ones, the summoned creature will immediately appear and attack the caster him or herself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03978))_
- All summons are _non-removable_ magic. Therefore, _Dispel Magic_ has no effect. However, by using Pharis's special holy magic _Banish_ , it's possible to drive the summoned creatures back to where they came from. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03979))_
- Therefore, it's possible to instantly summon creatures no matter where they are. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03977))_
- Therefore, _Dispel Magic_ has no effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03979))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 11.3 Dragon Roar Magic / [ Blood Protection ]

- When using this spell, the caster must pour their own blood on another person. That blood allows you to protect others from _all_ attacks. Specifically, the caster must consume their life force at the same time as their mental power. Losing 1 point of life force can give you 1 point of defense effect. This defense effect reduces _all_ damage from weapons, magic, etc. At this point, the target is no longer able to remove the blood by normal means such as wiping or washing. A spell such as _Remove Curse_ , etc. must be used, or the caster must be killed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04080))_
- While the spell is in effect, the caster _cannot_ regain the life force they've consumed (think of it as their starting life force being lowered). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04081))_
- The caster can expand the effect to provide a greater defense effect. However, in order to do this, you must consume an extra amount of your life force as well as your mental power. For example, a 6th level caster can consume 8 points of mental power and 2 points of life force at the same time to provide 2 points of defense effect. The same applies when expanding the target score. If you wanted to give 2 points of defense effect to three people each, a 6th level caster's mental power consumed would be 24 points and their life force consumed would be 6 points. Life force consumed _cannot_ be reduced by increasing dragon priest skill level. If an 8th level caster were to do the same thing, the mental power consumed would only be 18 points, but the life force consumed would _still_ be 6 points. There is no need to consume extra life force when expanding the final score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04082))_
- Please note that this spell cannot be cast on the caster him or herself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04083))_
- At this point, the target is no longer able to remove the blood by normal means such as wiping or washing. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04080))_
- If an 8th level caster were to do the same thing, the mental power consumed would only be 18 points, but the life force consumed would _still_ be 6 points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04082))_
- For example, a 6th level caster can consume 8 points of mental power and 2 points of life force at the same time to provide 2 points of defense effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04082))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 11.3 Dragon Roar Magic / [ Egg Shelter ]

- Wrap yourself in a dragon egg shell. The caster will fall into a magical sleep inside this egg. During this time, _all_ undesirable conditions such as wounds, exhaustion, poison, illness, and curses vanish, and the caster awakens fully recovered. The period of sleep is at least 10 days, and the more severe the condition, the longer the period. If it's a poison or illness, it's usually one month, and if it's a curse, it's about one year. The shell disappears as soon as you wake up. This shell _cannot_ be broken by any means. However, you can be carried around in the shell, and it can also be removed by _Dispel Magic_ . If it’s forcibly removed, there will be no recovery effect at all, no matter how long you remain in the egg. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04106))_

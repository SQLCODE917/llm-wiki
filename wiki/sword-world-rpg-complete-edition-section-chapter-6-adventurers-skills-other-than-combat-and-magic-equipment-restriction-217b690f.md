## Statements

- This ability is used to hear certain sounds from behind walls, doors, or from a distance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03164))_
- The game master should determine the difficulty according to the situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03164))_
- If you fail, you may not be able to hear anything, or you may not be able to choose a particular sound. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03164))_
- If you are wearing metal armor or heavy armor, the noise it makes will interfere with your hearing. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03165))_
- Also, a helmet will clearly interfere with this ability. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03165))_

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03161))_

> This is the ability to remember what you witness. For example, even if a book is written in a language you don't know, by using this ability you can memorize the shape of each letter exactly as it is, even if you don't know the meaning, and can write it down later. Thieves do this habitually all the time. A success roll should be made when _recalling_ something, not when _trying to memorize_ something.

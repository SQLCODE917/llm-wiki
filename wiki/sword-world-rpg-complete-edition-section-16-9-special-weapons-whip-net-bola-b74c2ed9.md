# 16.9 Special Weapons / Whip, Net, Bola

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-16-9-special-weapons-1b270e2c]] - broader source section

## Statements

- These weapons can perform attacks that **entangle** opponents. If an attack with one of these weapons hits, the evader must make a success roll using _adventurer level + agility bonus_ as the baseline score and _attacker's final score at this time_ as the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07296))_
- If he or she fails, they’ll be _entangled_ and fall _prone_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07297))_
- Once entangled, an escape check is required _every_ round. Make a success roll using _adventurer level + strength bonus_ ( _attack power -7_ for monsters _)_ as the baseline score, and _attacker's final score when entangling (attack power + 2D roll at that time)_ as the target score. If the entangled target has hands or similar organs, the target score will _decrease_ by 1 each round. While entangled, there is _always_ a -4 penalty evading your opponent's attacks. To escape, you must first _release_ any armaments you’re holding. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07300))_

# 16.9 Special Weapons / Whip, Net, Bola

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-16-9-special-weapons-1b270e2c]] - broader source section

## Statements

- If an attack with one of these weapons hits, the evader must make a success roll using _adventurer level + agility bonus_ as the baseline score and _attacker's final score at this time_ as the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07296))_
- If he or she fails, they’ll be _entangled_ and fall _prone_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07297))_
- While entangled, there is _always_ a -4 penalty evading your opponent's attacks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07300))_
- Once entangled, an escape check is required _every_ round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07300))_
- If the entangled target has hands or similar organs, the target score will _decrease_ by 1 each round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07300))_
- To escape, you must first _release_ any armaments you’re holding. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07300))_

## Technical atoms

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07294))_

> Main-gauche and sword breaker have the _same_ attack scores as a _dagger_ . When attacking with _both_ hands, you’ll still suffer a -2 ／ -4 penalty. Also, if you use it for an attack, you’ll _lose_ the evasion bonus.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07296))_

> These weapons can perform attacks that **entangle** opponents.

### Technical atom 2

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07296))_

> These weapons can perform attacks that **entangle** opponents. If an attack with one of these weapons hits, the evader must make a success roll using _adventurer level + agility bonus_ as the baseline score and _attacker's final score at this time_ as the target score.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07299))_

> If the opponent is of a _larger_ race than him or her, the attacker must _release_ the whip or net.

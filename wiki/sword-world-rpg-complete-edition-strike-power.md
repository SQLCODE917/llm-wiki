Strike power is a measure in the Sword World RPG that represents a character's weapon strength. It is used to determine base damage in combat.

When a character successfully hits a monster, the damage dealt is calculated through a strike roll. The strike power is used to look up the appropriate column in the Rating Table to determine base damage.

Strike power is determined by the weapon's required strength and the character's attributes.

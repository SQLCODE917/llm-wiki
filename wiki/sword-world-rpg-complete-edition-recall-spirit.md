# **[ Recall Spirit ]**

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about **[ recall spirit ]**:

## Statements

- Priests who have given their bodies to others cannot be resurrected using _Resurrection_ , because they have lost their soul containers (although they _can_ have another priest cast _Recall Spirit_ on them...). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02895))_
- Also, those who have lost their souls and are destroyed, or those who have become undead, _cannot_ be resurrected. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02894))_
- This is a precious spell of selfsacrifice. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02894))_
- _Only_ those that the caster knows well (or at least is acquainted with) can be resurrected. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02894))_
- However, in return, they can use their body to bring the dead back to life. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02894))_
- If this spell is used, the priest's soul will be called to their god. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02894))_
- The person who was sacrificed loses their soul container and cannot be resurrected. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03894))_
- This spell is almost the same as the divine magic of the same name, except that it uses the body of a sacrifice instead of the caster to bring the dead back to life. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03894))_
- Also, those who have lost their souls and are destroyed, or those who have become undead, cannot be resurrected. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03894))_
- Only those that the caster knows well (or at least is acquainted with) can be resurrected. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03894))_
- This spell requires a final score of 20 or greater while also defeating the sacrifice's resist for it to be fully effective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03895))_
- Even if you defeat the sacrifice's resist, if you do not attain a final score of 20 or greater, the dead who was resurrected will lose their memories. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03895))_

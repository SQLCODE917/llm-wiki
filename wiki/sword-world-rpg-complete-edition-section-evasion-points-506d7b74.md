# 《 **Evasion Points** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This represents the degree to which the monster can evade attacks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04916))_
- In order for a character to hit a monster with an attack, they must succeed on a success roll using these evasion points as the target score and their attack power as the baseline score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04916))_
- If you fail, your character's attack will miss. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04916))_

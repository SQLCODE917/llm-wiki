## **Control Weather**

**Control Weather** is an ancient magic spell that changes the weather within a specified area of effect.

### **Spell Details**

- **Base Mental Power Cost**: 45
- **Distance**: Caster
- **Area**: An area within a 10 kilometer radius of the caster
- **Duration**: Until focus ends
- **Effect**: Change the weather within the area of effect
- **Type**: Focus, Target score, non-removable
- **Expansion**: Final score, area
- **Resist**: None

### **Usage**

This is a spell that can change the weather within its area of effect. Weather changes begin on the round you cast the spell, and the spell is completed in 10 rounds. Even if someone is within the area of effect, they cannot resist this spell. This spell is *non-removable*, but if two *Control Weather* spells are cast within the same area of effect, the one with the higher final score will take effect, and the one with the lower final score will be negated.

Weather you can change to includes sunny, cloudy, rainy, foggy, or snowy. It has nothing to do with the weather, season, etc. before the spell was cast. As the spell ends, the weather will return to its natural state. However, effects caused by this spell will remain and cannot be removed.

### **Weather Types**

- **Sunny**: The sky within the area of effect will be completely clear of clouds. Depending on the angle of the sun, the sun will shine during the day and the ground will be warm. At night, you’ll be able to see the moon and stars.
- **Cloudy**: The sky within the area of effect is covered by clouds, hiding the sun. Since direct sunlight is blocked, the area becomes darker and the temperature on the ground decreases. At night, the moon and stars will be hidden, making it a dark night.
- **Rainy**: Rain clouds cover the sky, the conditions will be the same as cloudy. In addition, rain will begin to fall at about 10 mm per hour, wetting the ground.
- **Foggy**: Visibility within the area of effect is 5 meters or less for fog.
- **Snowy**: Snow clouds cover the sky, the conditions will be the same as cloudy. In addition, snow will begin to fall at about 10 mm per hour. The surrounding temperature will drop to 0°celsius.

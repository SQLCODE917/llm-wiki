## Statements

- This spell is used on objects imbued with magical power to hide the presence of that magical power. For this reason, _Sense Magic_ or _Sense Aura_ will no longer be able to distinguish them. When discerning magic power with _Analyze Enchantment_ , you'll need a final score greater than or equal to the _Seal Enchantment_ that was cast on it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01906))_
- Sometimes there are items from the ancient kingdom period that have this cast on them. Many of them are protected by _Seal Enchantment_ with a final score as high as 30 or greater. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01907))_
- This spell has no effect when using the sage skill to appraise an item (however, if you don't think it has magical power, you might not try to appraise it). This spell cannot be cast on living things. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01908))_

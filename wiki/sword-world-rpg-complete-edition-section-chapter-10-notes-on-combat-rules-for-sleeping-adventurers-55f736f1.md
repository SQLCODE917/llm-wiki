# Chapter 10: Notes on Combat / Rules for Sleeping Adventurers

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- If a sleeping adventurer is approached by a monster, the baseline score is _adventurer level + intelligence bonus_ , and if you succeed on a resistance roll against target score 9, you can wake up before being attacked. If the monster has the ability to sneak up on you, the target score will be even higher. If they approach using _stealthy step_ , etc. their final score will become the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03689))_

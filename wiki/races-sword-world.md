# Races in Sword World RPG

In Sword World RPG, adventurers can be humans or various non-human races. The rulebook explains the characteristics and abilities of different races that are commonly encountered as adventurers in the fictional world of Forcelia.

## Human

Humans are the most common adventurers in Forcelia. They have ability scores ranging from 4-24 (14 on average) for each of the six ability scores: dexterity, agility, intelligence, strength, life force, and mental power.

## Dwarf

Dwarves are members of the fae tribe who have fully immersed into the world inhabited by humans. They are about 120 cm tall, round and plump, with bulbous noses. Men often have long beards, while women do not. They have dark skin, brown to dark brown hair, and similar colored eyes. They can see in the dark and are very dexterous with their hands, excelling in craftsmanship and blacksmithing. Dwarves usually live in human cities and villages, but there are also places where only dwarves live. They have a strong sense of tribalism, worshiping the gods of the earth, with Braki as their main deity, who is also the god of smithing. Their life span is longer than humans, around 200 years.

Dwarf rule characteristics:
1. Among the craftsman general skill, has any one of the following at level 5: carpentry, smithing, woodworking, stonemasonry, gem crafting
2. Unable to learn ancient magic or spirit magic

## Grassrunner

Grassrunners are originally members of the fae tribe who lived in the meadows. They are very curious and many have abandoned the fairy world to become residents of human society. They are about 1 meter tall and have the appearance of a human child. Other than their slightly pointed ears, they are not much different from humans. They have golden to tan hair and similar colored eyes, with predominantly black or reddish skin. They lead a nomadic lifestyle, traveling with their children and rarely settling in one place. They have no concept of tribes or cities, and their personalities are cheerful and simple. They have no custom of believing in gods. Their rule characteristics include:
1. Has 1 level in the thief skill
2. Can communicate with plants and insects
3. Cannot learn any runes

## Elf

Elves are members of the fae tribe who live in the forests. They are known for their beautiful appearance and immortality (no life span). Their most distinctive feature is their long, slender, pointy ears. They are about 160 cm tall and slim, with thin body hair. They have golden to chestnut hair, sometimes silver, and pale blue or green eyes. They are close to humans and can produce offspring called halfelves. They live a closed-off life in the forest, avoiding outsiders and not wanting to be ruled by others. They rarely leave the forests where they live, but occasionally appear in towns. They do not believe in any gods. 

Elves have a life span of about 1000 years (though this is for ordinary elves; high elves avoid contact with humans and have even longer life spans). Their rule characteristics include:
1. Has 1 level in the shaman skill
2. Unable to learn holy magic
3. Has 1 level in the ranger skill
4. Can see in the dark

## Source record

This section covers the second part of character creation in the Sword World RPG, detailing rules for weapons, armor, shields, and how different skills affect equipment choices.

## Key supported claims

- Characters choose weapons whose required strength matches their own strength (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-30).
- Weapons and armor prices vary based on required strength, calculated using formulas like x20+40 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-30).
- Characters must choose one armor whose required strength equals their own strength (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-30).
- Shields require specific strength: small (1), large (13) (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-30).

## Source record

This section explains how to choose weapons, armor, shields, and other equipment based on a character’s strength and skills, including price calculations and restrictions.

## Key supported claims

- Characters select equipment whose required strength matches their own, with skills possibly imposing extra limits. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-30)
- Metal armor other than ring mail is disallowed regardless of strength. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-30)
- If a character lacks a skill related to an action, the baseline score is 0, independent of ability scores. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-30)

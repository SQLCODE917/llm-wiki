# 4.6.1 Hit (Evasion) Checks

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This check is also made with a success roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02195))_
- The baseline score is the character's evasion speed . The target score is still different for each monster, but it's called the monster's attack points . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02196))_
- Since this is a success roll, double sixes and double ones are automatic successes and automatic failures , respectively. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02200))_
- Ducard II has an evasion speed of 5 (fighter level 2, agility bonus +2, has a shield). On the other hand, the goblin has 10 attack points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02202))_
- The player rolls the dice to determine if they evade the goblin's attack. The roll is 4 . 5+4= 9 , which is short of the target score (the goblin's attack points) of 10. Ducard II is hit by the goblin. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02203))_

# Area Aim

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is an attack method that targets a specific body part of an opponent . You'll suffer a penalty to your attack power, but if you hit, you'll attain a special effect . Damage calculation remains exactly the same as usual. If you don't want to kill your opponent, you can declare mercy (if it's a blunt weapon). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08987))_

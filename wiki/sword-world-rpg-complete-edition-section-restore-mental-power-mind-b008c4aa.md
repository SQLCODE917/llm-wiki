# Restore Mental Power (Mind)

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-5-3-8f977924]] - broader source section

## Statements

- Every time you listen to this spell for one hour, you can recover 1 point of mental power. If you interrupt the spellsong midway through, there will be no effect. Those who wish to take part in the effect of this spellsong must remain completely relaxed for the duration of the performance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03445))_
- The bard singing the spellsong cannot recover their own mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03446))_

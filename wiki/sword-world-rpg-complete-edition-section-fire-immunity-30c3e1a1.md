# 《 Fire Immunity 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-13-6-5-d59705fb]] - broader source section: 13.6.5

## Statements

- Fire attacks are completely ineffective. Magic that deals damage with fire ( Fire Bolt and Fireball ) is completely useless, and the strike power bonus of Fire Weapon is also not added. If one of these monsters also has the ability normal weapon immunity , it cannot be wounded by a weapon that had Fire Weapon cast on it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09909))_

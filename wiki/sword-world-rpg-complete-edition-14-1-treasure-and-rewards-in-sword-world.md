## Source record

Treasure and rewards in the Sword World RPG setting, including types of treasure, valuation, and adventurer's shops.

## Key supported claims

- Adventurers in Alecrast search for treasure in ruins of the ancient kingdom, where neither silver nor gold coins exist (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.241-242).
- Magic items from the ancient kingdom are extremely valuable and cannot be produced today (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.241-242).
- The value of treasure from an adventure should match the level of risk, determined by multiplying total adventurer levels by 500 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.241-242).
- Rewards for adventurer jobs are typically 50~200 times the total adventurer level (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.241-242).
- Magical items range from 10 to 10,000 silver coins in value, with some items being of immeasurable value (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242).

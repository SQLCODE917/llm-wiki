# Form=Iron jar

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about form=iron jar:

## Statements

- This jar is a magical jar that can seal things from another world, such as the spirit world, within it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06876))_
- When you chant a password, a daemon will be summoned from the jar. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06928))_
- Please use _Table 14-1: Daemon Jar Summon_ to determine which daemon will appear. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06932))_
- It’s said to be able to seal not only lesser spirits such as salamander and sylph, but _also_ greater spirits such as djinn and efreet. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06876))_
- Once sealed, a spirit remains locked up until the lid is opened. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06877))_
- Anyone who sees this eye (even if it’s an image reflected in a mirror) will fall asleep _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06926))_
- Base Trade Price=1,000,000 gamels (not for sale) This is used by transplanting it into a body in place of a lost eye. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06926))_
- If the summoner is gone, the daemon, according to its intrinsic nature, will begin to act violently. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06932))_
- The summoned daemon will obey the summoner's commands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06932))_

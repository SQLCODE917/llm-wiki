# Form=Iron jar

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about form=iron jar:

## Statements

### Chapter 14: Treasures / Form=Iron jar

- This jar is a magical jar that can seal things from another world, such as the spirit world, within it. It’s said to be able to seal not only lesser spirits such as salamander and sylph, but _also_ greater spirits such as djinn and efreet. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06876))_

- If you point this jar at a spirit and chant a password, any kind of spirit can be entrapped. Once sealed, a spirit remains locked up until the lid is opened. If you open the lid, they immediately return to the spirit world. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06877))_

- Base Trade Price=1,000,000 gamels (not for sale) This is used by transplanting it into a body in place of a lost eye. Anyone who sees this eye (even if it’s an image reflected in a mirror) will fall asleep _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06926))_

- When you chant a password, a daemon will be summoned from the jar. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06928))_

- The summoned daemon will obey the summoner's commands. Please use _Table 14-1: Daemon Jar Summon_ to determine which daemon will appear. If the summoner is gone, the daemon, according to its intrinsic nature, will begin to act violently. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06932))_

# 《 Duration 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This represents how long the magic will remain in effect. This includes instant , 18 rounds , 1 day , permanent , until focus ends or until certain conditions are met . For magic whose duration is instant , the magic itself disappears in an instant, but its effects remain. Wounds caused by a Fireball explosion will remain, and if you receive healing by Cure Wounds , those wounds will be recovered instantly. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02739))_

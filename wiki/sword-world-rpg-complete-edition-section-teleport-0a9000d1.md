## Statements

- This is a spell that instantly moves a target (individual or object) to another location. The movement destination is limited to a location that's visible to the caster or a location that's familiar to the caster. This spell will not work if there's an obstacle blocking the movement destination. Only solids greater than a certain size can be obstacles. What amounts to an obstacle depends on the situation, but objects such as desks, chairs, large rocks, etc. can be obstacles. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02030))_

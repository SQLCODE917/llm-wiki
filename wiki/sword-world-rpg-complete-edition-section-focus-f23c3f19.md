# 《 **Focus** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Magic of the **focus** type is that in which the magic's effect lasts as long as the caster (the one who casts the magic) is _focused_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01471))_
- Magic has a fixed _duration_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01471))_
- you can still attempt to _evade_ it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01477))_
- If magic is cast on you, it's also possible to _resist_ it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01477))_
- Also, when the target is no longer within the magic's area of effect (out of reach, out of sight, etc.), the magic will _lose_ its effect, even if you continue to focus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01478))_

- If the caster suffers damage to their life force or mental power, their focus will be interrupted and the magic's effect will be _lost_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01479))_

# Sword World RPG - Complete Edition

This is the complete rulebook for the Sword World RPG, a fantasy role-playing game set in the world of Forcelia and the continent of Alecrast. It provides comprehensive rules for players and game masters, covering character creation, combat, magic, and optional rules.

## Overview

The Sword World RPG is a fantasy role-playing game set in the world of Forcelia and the continent of Alecrast. The game is designed to provide an immersive experience in a richly detailed fantasy world, with a focus on character-driven storytelling and tactical combat.

## Player Rules

### Character Creation

Character creation in Sword World RPG involves selecting a race, class, and abilities. The rules for character creation are detailed in Chapter 1 of the rulebook. This includes information on ability scores, skills, and other character attributes.

### Combat

Combat in Sword World RPG is a key part of the game, with detailed rules for attacks, defenses, and special abilities. The combat rules are found in Chapter 4 of the rulebook, including sections on attacks from characters against monsters, and movement and actions.

### Magic

Magic is a central element in Sword World RPG, with rules for different types of magic including ancient magic, spirit magic, and holy magic. The magic rules are detailed in Chapter 5 of the rulebook, including sections on how to use magic, magic expansion, and specific magic lists.

### Skills

Skills are important aspects of character development in Sword World RPG. The rules for skills are detailed in Chapter 1 of the rulebook, including sections on adventurer skills, thief skills, sage skills, and other common adventurer abilities.

## Game Master Support Material

### Optional Rules

The rulebook also includes optional rules for various aspects of the game, such as dark magic, dragon roar magic, and rules for poison, illness, and infection. These optional rules are detailed in Chapter 11 and Chapter 12 of the rulebook.

### Monster Catalog

The monster catalog provides detailed information on various creatures that players may encounter in the game. The monster catalog is organized by type, including fae, spirits, titans, mythical beasts, magical beasts, animals, plants, undead, and magical creatures. The monster catalog is detailed in Chapter 13 of the rulebook.

### Treasure and Rewards

The treasure and rewards system in Sword World RPG provides guidelines for how players can gain wealth and rewards for their adventures. The rules for treasure and rewards are detailed in Chapter 14 of the rulebook, including sections on treasure and rewards in Sword World, adventure treasure and reward amounts, and magical item lists.

## Setting and Lore

### World Guide

The _Sword World RPG World Guide_ , published by Fujimi Shobo, is a comprehensive introduction to the continent of Alecrast, the background setting of the game. It is very useful when creating your own scenarios, and for those who are mainly players, it is also effective to have a general outline of the world.

## Source record

The Sword World RPG – Complete Edition is a tabletop role‑playing game system that provides a full set of rules, extensive monster and item catalogs, and detailed setting lore.

## Key supported claims

- Front‑matter and layout details for the complete rulebook, including edition notes and publisher info.
- Player‑facing core rules covering skills, character creation, checks, resistance rolls, movement, attacks, and death checks, which provide the baseline gameplay mechanics.
- Magic system fundamentals: types, usage, expansion, ancient and spirit magic lists, holy and dark magic, and spell‑song mechanics, giving players a rich spellcasting framework.
- GM/Support material: monster catalogs, item tables, treasure rules, combat options, structure destruction, and optional rules such as lost spells, forming a comprehensive facilitator toolkit.

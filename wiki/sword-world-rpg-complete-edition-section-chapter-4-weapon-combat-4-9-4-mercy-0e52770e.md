- You must declare mercy _before_ you make a hit check for your attack. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01068))_
- It _cannot_ be done _after_ a hit or after damage check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01068))_
- However, to do so, their weapon _must_ be a _dull weapon_ (a bludgeoning weapon). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01068))_
- You can also declare mercy when using a _barehanded_ attack. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01068))_
- However, even if an item is classified as one of these, if you _throw_ it, you _cannot_ declare mercy. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01068))_
- It _cannot_ be done _after_ a hit or after damage check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01068))_

## Statements

- This is the ability to move without making a sound. A success roll determines whether the sound of walking can be heard. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04951))_
- At this time, the thief makes a success roll using thief skill + dexterity bonus as the baseline score, and the person trying to hear the sound makes one using adventurer level + intelligence bonus as the baseline score. If the thief's final score is greater, the sound will never be heard. If there is a tie or the listener's final score is greater, the thief will be overheard. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04952))_
- In particular, if they're not paying attention to the sound, impose a -4 penalty on the listener and make the same check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04953))_
- If the person hearing the sound also has the thief skill, they may make a check using thief skill level + intelligence bonus (this is the listen ability from the previous section). At this time, if you can hear the sound, you will be able to discern not only the presence of something sneaking around, but also its general appearance (walking speed, stride length, number of people if there is more than one, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04954))_

Magic expansion in Sword World RPG allows rune masters to increase the effectiveness and certainty of magic by consuming extra mental power. This is done by doubling or tripling the amount of mental power consumed. Instead of doubling or tripling the base mental power cost, the mental power is divided by the skill level (rounded up), or in other words, the mental power consumed when not expanding is doubled, tripled, etc.

## Example:

Suppose a sorcerer with sorcerer skill level 2 doubles the range of his _Energy Bolt_ spell and casts it on two enemies. The base mental power cost of _Energy Bolt_ is _5_. For this reason, the mental power consumed without expansion is 5 ÷ 2, rounded up to _3_. Therefore, for a doubled expansion, the amount of mental power consumed would be 3 x 2 = **6**. You would not calculate (5 x 2) ÷ 2 = 5.

Magic expansion includes the following types:
- Targets Expansion
- Area Expansion
- Volume Expansion
- Duration Expansion
- Distance Expansion
- Damage Expansion
- Final Score Increase
- Effect Expansion

## Combined Expansion

Some magic allows for multiple expansion types. In these cases, you can perform multiple expansions at the same time. However, at this time, the amount of mental power consumed will become extremely large. By multiplying the rates determined for each expansion, you can determine the total multiplied rate of mental power consumed.

### Example:

Suppose a sorcerer casts an _Energy Bolt_ on three enemies (x 3 expansion) with a +1 to his final score (x 2 expansion) and he makes 4 damage checks for each (x 4 expansion). In this case, the multiplied rate of mental power consumed is 3 x 2 x 4 = **24!**

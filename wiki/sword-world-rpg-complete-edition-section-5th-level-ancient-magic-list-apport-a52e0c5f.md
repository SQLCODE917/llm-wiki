## Statements

- This is a spell that allows the caster to teleport their possessions into the palm of their hand. However, even though it's their possession, the caster must have a complete memory of it, and moreover must be able to identify specifically where it's kept. Also, the size of the target is limited to something you can hold in your hand (for example, you can summon a sword, but not armor). If the duration expires or if the spell is canceled, the target immediately returns to its original location. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01799))_

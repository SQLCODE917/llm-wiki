- A success roll may be subject to modifiers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00600))_
- This is to replicate any favorable or unfavorable circumstances when a character is about to perform an action. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00600))_
- In such cases, the game master applies a **bonus** or **penalty** to the success roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00601))_
- Things like, _+2 because they're falling over_ , or _-4 because it's dark_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00601))_

# **[ Cure Disease ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This spell can be used on an individual and has the effect of healing them of any illness they may be suffering from. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02929))_
- This spell can heal multiple diseases at once and restore health. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02929))_

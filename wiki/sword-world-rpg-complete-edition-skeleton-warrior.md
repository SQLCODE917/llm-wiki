## Statements

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.12 Ancient Magic List / [ Skeleton Warrior ]

- A skeleton warrior, also known as a dragontooth warrior, is a kind of golem created from a dragon's tooth, and resembles a human skeleton with a sword and shield. This spell is what creates that formidable warrior. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01827))_

- The caster can give commands to the skeleton warrior in high ancient. Only the caster can give commands. They cannot understand overly complex commands, but they handle simple commands accurately. For example, commands such as _kill anyone who enters this room_ or _kill that man_ are very simple and can be carried out, but a command such as _go to the next village and kidnap the chief's daughter_ cannot be carried out. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01828))_

- The dragon's tooth, which is the source of the skeleton warrior, is a very valuable item as one of the treasures of the ancient kingdom. You'd have to pay a whopping 5000 silver coins to purchase it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01829))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.16 Basic Holy Magic List / [ Cure Wounds ]

- This has no effect on magical creatures that do not have normal life, such as skeleton warriors, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02727))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / [ Steal Life ]

- 143 to the target, and the caster's life force (points) will be restored by the same number of points. If you expand the effect and steal life force (points) from multiple people, you can recover the total amount of life force (points) you stole. However, you _cannot_ increase your life (points) higher than your original score. Also, like _Wounds_ , it has no effect at all if the opponent successfully resists, and it has no effect on opponents that don't have normal life, such as undead, skeleton warriors, etc. This spell also cannot be used if the dark priest him or herself is undead. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03787))_

### 13.2.10 Magical Creatures / [ Puppet Golem ]

- A puppet golem is a small, simple version of a golem that is created by giving temporary life to a tree branch, stone, etc. using an ancient magic spell. Depending on the material, there are four different types of puppet golems that can be created. They are oak, stone servant, bone servant, and skeleton warrior. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05845))_

### 13.2.10 Magical Creatures / Stone Servant

- A skeleton warrior is a golem created from a magical dragon's tooth. Its appearance is that of a fully-armed skeleton, which uses its weapon and shield with mechanical precision. However, its intellect is low and it cannot accept complex or abstract commands. For this reason, skeleton warriors are often used for simple tasks, such as guarding treasure vaults, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05861))_

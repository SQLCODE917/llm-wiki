# [ Skeleton Warrior ]

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about [ skeleton warrior ]:

## Statements

### [ Skeleton Warrior ]

- Resist=None A skeleton warrior, also known as a dragontooth warrior, is a kind of golem created from a dragon's tooth, and resembles a human skeleton with a sword and shield. This spell is what creates that formidable warrior. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01829))_

- The caster can give commands to the skeleton warrior in high ancient. Only the caster can give commands. They cannot understand overly complex commands, but they handle simple commands accurately. For example, commands such as kill anyone who enters this room or kill that man are very simple and can be carried out, but a command such as go to the next village and kidnap the chief's daughter cannot be carried out. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01830))_

- The dragon's tooth, which is the source of the skeleton warrior, is a very valuable item as one of the treasures of the ancient kingdom. You'd have to pay a whopping 5000 silver coins to purchase it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01831))_

### Magic List [ Cure Wounds ]

- This has no effect on magical creatures that do not have normal life, such as skeleton warriors, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02940))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / [ Steal Life ]

- This spell combines the effects of both Wounds and Cure Wounds , allowing the caster to absorb life force (points) from the target to recover when the caster is wounded. First, make a strike power 10 check for damage dealt to the target, and the caster's life force (points) will be restored by the same number of points. If you expand the effect and steal life force (points) from multiple people, you can recover the total amount of life force (points) you stole. However, you cannot increase your life (points) higher than your original score. Also, like Wounds , it has no effect at all if the opponent successfully resists, and it has no effect on opponents that don't have normal life, such as undead, skeleton warriors, etc. This spell also cannot be used if the dark priest him or herself is undead. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04146))_

### [ Puppet Golem ]

- A puppet golem is a small, simple version of a golem that is created by giving temporary life to a tree branch, stone, etc. using an ancient magic spell. Depending on the material, there are four different types of puppet golems that can be created. They are oak, stone servant, bone servant, and skeleton warrior. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07209))_

### Bone Servant

- A bone servant is a puppet golem made from the bones of living creatures. Usually they're humanoids, but other ones exist too. When armed, they're difficult to distinguish from a skeleton warrior. They are superior to stone servants, etc. in that they have eternal life and can carry out fairly complex commands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07221))_

### Skeleton Warrior

- A skeleton warrior is a golem created from a magical dragon's tooth. Its appearance is that of a fully-armed skeleton, which uses its weapon and shield with mechanical precision. However, its intellect is low and it cannot accept complex or abstract commands. For this reason, skeleton warriors are often used for simple tasks, such as guarding treasure vaults, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07227))_

- A skeleton warrior will attempt to carry out any commands they're given, unless the caster who created them gives them new commands in high ancient, or cancels any previously given commands. It's usually not possible to control, or cancel the commands of, a skeleton warrior created by someone else. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07228))_

- Even if you attack a skeleton warrior with a bladed weapon, it will not cause a critical hit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07230))_

### Chapter 14: / [ Dragon Tooth ]

- By casting the 5th level ancient magic Skeleton Warrior on this tooth, you can create a puppet golem, a skeleton warrior (dragontooth warrior). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08708))_

## Source record

Chapter 1.4 of the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition covers character creation, including required strength for weapons and armor, weapon types, armor restrictions, shield options, and equipment purchasing.

## Key supported claims

- Characters must match weapon strength to their own strength for optimal use, raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.22-28
- The most suitable weapon equals character strength, raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.22-28
- Two-handed weapons deal more damage than one-handed ones of same strength, raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.22-28
- Armor must match character strength, raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.22-28

## Statements

- There are no restrictions on armor or weapons to use the priest skill, and priests can cast magic simply by chanting holy prayers. However, this action must be in accordance with the _will_ of the god you believe in. If you try to use magic for a purpose that is far _removed_ from the god's will, it may not work at all. Also, a priest who loses _faith_ immediately loses their priest skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02662))_

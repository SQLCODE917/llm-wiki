# 13.6 Handling Monster Special Abilities / 《 Acid 》

## Statements

- Spits strong acid that deals damage. If it hits, you _cannot_ make a resistance roll. Damage cannot be reduced by armor, only adventurer level and magical defense can help. Whether acid can deal damage to armor (for example, reduce defense by 1 point each time it hits, etc.) is the choice of each game master. Normally, the game will be easier to progress if it has no effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06247))_

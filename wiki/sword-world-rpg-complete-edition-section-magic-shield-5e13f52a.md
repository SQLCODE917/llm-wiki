# 《 **Magic Shield** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a price list for magical shields with enchantments. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07207))_
- For shields with +2 or higher enchantment, small shields tend to be more expensive than large shields due to differences in demand. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07207))_
- For the same reason, high-quality large shields +1/+2 are more expensive than large shields +2/+3. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07207))_
- Shields requiring strength of 13 cannot be used by most adventurers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07207))_

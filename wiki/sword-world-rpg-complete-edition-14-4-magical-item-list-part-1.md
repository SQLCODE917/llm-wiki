## Source record

This section describes the types of treasure and magical items in the Sword World RPG setting, including artwork, gems, precious metals, and magical items. It provides details on how to determine the value of treasure using thief and sage skills, and outlines the magical item list with specific items like weapons and armor, their rarities, enchanter names, forms, required strength, base trade prices, powers, and descriptions. Some item values are unknown.

## Key supported claims

- Treasure types include artwork, gems, precious metals, and magical items, with values determined by thief and sage skills' treasure appraisal ability (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-256).
- Magical items encompass weapons, armor, and other enchanted objects, with values ranging from 10 silver coins to 10,000 silver coins, and some having immeasurable value (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-256).
- Magical items can be identified through spells like Sense Magic and Analyze Enchantment, with base trade prices ranging from 1000 silver coins to immeasurable values, and some items being too powerful for adventurers to obtain (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-256).

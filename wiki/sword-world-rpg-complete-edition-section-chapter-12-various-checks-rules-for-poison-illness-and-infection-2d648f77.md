## Statements

- In Sword World RPG , illness is defined as a state in which the balance of spirit power within the body is disrupted. The concept of bacterial or viral contagion does not exist. There are two types of symptoms that can appear. One is due to poison and the other is due to illness . In addition, a wound may become infected , which is treated as an illness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04736))_
- Poisons force an imbalance in the spirit power within the body. As long as the poison remains in the body, even if the symptoms disappear, the balance of spirit power will be disrupted and the symptoms will appear again. However, if the poison itself is cleared away, the spirit power within the body will naturally return to a balanced state and restore health. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04737))_
- On the other hand, illness is a state where the spirit power within the body remains unbalanced and in a fixed state. Think of it as the spirits inside your body that are under the illusion that a false equilibrium is the correct one. Illness infection occurs when spirits transmit information about the incorrect equilibrium state to spirits in other human bodies. The unknown spirit of life heals illness by showing other spirits in the body how to achieve balance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04738))_
- In Sword World RPG , the phenomenon of a wound becoming infected means that the container was broken and deformed, causing the contents to become unbalanced . The logic goes that the greater the wound, the greater the risk of infection. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04739))_

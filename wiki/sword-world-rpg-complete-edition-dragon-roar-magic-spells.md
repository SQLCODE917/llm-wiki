Dragon roar magic spells are a unique set of magical abilities available to dragon priests in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition. These spells are distinct from other magic types and are tied to the worship of dragons as guardians. They include various categories such as summoning, transformation, and elemental magic.

## Categories and Examples

### Summoning Spells

Summoning spells allow dragon priests to summon reptiles, wyverns, wyrms, and lesser dragons. These spells require the caster to know the creature they're trying to summon and use a specific table to determine when the creature will appear. Summoning takes place across space, and modifiers can be applied based on the caster's knowledge of the creature's location.

### Transformation Spells

Transformation spells enable dragon priests to take on dragon-like features or transform entirely into dragons. These include spells like "Iron Stomach," "Heavy Scale," "Dragon's Wings," and "Partial Dragon." Some transformations grant increased defense, flight capability, or the ability to use other dragon roar magic spells.

### Elemental Magic

Elemental magic spells include "Fire Proof," "Fire Breath," and "Counter Spirit." These spells protect against fire, allow the caster to breathe fire, and nullify attacks from specific spirit powers that control nature.

### Communication Spells

Communication spells allow dragon priests to communicate with reptiles and dragons. These include "Communicate Reptile" and "Communicate Dragon Race," which enable negotiation and understanding with these creatures.

### Utility Spells

Utility spells include "Poison Proof," which nullifies all types of poison, and "Egg Shelter," which allows the caster to sleep inside a dragon egg and fully recover from all undesirable conditions.

## Usage

Dragon roar magic spells can be used by dragon priests who have acquired the dragon priest skill. The spells are not restricted by silence or muteness, and no action is required to cast them. The spells have specific mental power costs, durations, and effects, and some have expansion or ritual requirements.

## Restrictions

Dragon priests cannot join adventurer parties due to their religious beliefs, and the dragon priest skill is incompatible with the priest skill and dark priest skill. The ultimate spell, "Reborn Dragon," requires the user to die and be reborn as a dragon.

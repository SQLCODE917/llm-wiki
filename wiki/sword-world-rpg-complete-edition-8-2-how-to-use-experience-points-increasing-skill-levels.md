## Source record

Chapter 8.2 to 8.4 of Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering experience point usage for skill levels and general skills.

## Key supported claims

- Experience points are given to characters as an indicator of how much experience they have gained as a result of their adventures (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-130).
- A character can advance in any number of skills and by any number of levels at once, with a maximum skill level of 10 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-130).
- The experience points required to increase each skill are shown on Table 8-1: Experience Points by Skill (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-130).
- Experience points cannot be negative, as you can't grow from experiences you haven't had (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-130).
- A character can spend fewer experience points only when acquiring both the sorcerer and sage skills (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.131-132).

# 《 Wehner 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- He is the god of the arts and also the god of bards. He is also believed to write history, and so is also characterized as a god of destiny. Although he does not have great power, he is widely believed in by the masses, and his temples can be seen from time to time. Belief in him is especially popular among artists. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02901))_

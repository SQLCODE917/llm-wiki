## Source record

This section details the rules for surprise attacks in combat, including when they can occur, how to determine who is surprised, and the penalties that apply.

## Key supported claims

- Surprise attacks occur when one party notices the other without being noticed, allowing a free action for one round (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.138-142).
- The game master rolls 2D and adds it to monster level, then adventurers roll ranger skill + intelligence bonus (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.138-142).
- If the score is lower than the monster column, monster has a chance for surprise; if greater than adventurer column, adventurers have a chance (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.138-142).

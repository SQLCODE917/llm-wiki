- This spell creates a 1-meter tall, goblin-esque wood golem from a tree branch. This simplified version of a wood golem came to be called an _Oak_ because the material used was many oak tree branches, so that eventually became the name of the spell. Oaks can only fight, and the caster must specify whom to attack. Or otherwise, they can specify a location, and have the oak attack anyone invading that location.

## Source record

Rules for converting character data to monster data, including advantages, calculations, and examples like werewolves, werebears, and weretigers.

## Key supported claims

- Converting character data to monster data reduces dice rolling during combat and gives more time for game guidance (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.233-233).
- Converting character data to monster data prevents critical hits from causing player frustration (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.233-233).
- Monster level adapts directly from adventurer level, with agility and movement speed using character's agility score (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).

## Statements

- When an opponent is too strong, and fighting normally is of no use, an adventurer can focus wholly on defense . When focusing on defense, an adventurer cannot attack or use any magic during that round. In return, you'll gain a +2 bonus to your evasion speed . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08919))_

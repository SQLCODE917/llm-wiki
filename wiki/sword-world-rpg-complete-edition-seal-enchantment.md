## **Seal Enchantment**

The **Seal Enchantment** is an ancient magic spell that hides the presence of magical power applied to an object, making it undetectable by *Sense Magic* or *Sense Aura*. 

### **Spell Details**

- **Base Mental Power Cost**: 30
- **Distance**: Touch
- **Area**: One object
- **Duration**: Permanent
- **Effect**: Hides magical power applied to an object
- **Type**: Target score, non-removable, lost
- **Countered (Overpowered) By**: *Analyze Enchantment*
- **Expansion**: Final score
- **Resist**: None

### **Usage**

This spell is used on objects imbued with magical power to hide the presence of that magical power. For this reason, *Sense Magic* or *Sense Aura* will no longer be able to distinguish them. When discerning magic power with *Analyze Enchantment*, you'll need a final score greater than or equal to the *Seal Enchantment* that was cast on it.

Sometimes there are items from the ancient kingdom period that have this cast on them. Many of them are protected by *Seal Enchantment* with a final score as high as 30 or greater.

This spell has no effect when using the sage skill to appraise an item (however, if you don't think it has magical power, you might not try to appraise it). This spell cannot be cast on living things.

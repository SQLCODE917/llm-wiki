# 5.1.14 Spirit Magic List

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about 5.1.14 spirit magic list:

## Statements

- A wall of fire cannot be destroyed by physical means or magic that deals damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02586))_
- A wall made of water can't be destroyed by physical means or even magic that deals damage, as it quickly compensates for any damaged areas. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02588))_
- Even if you know that spirit power is at work, you won't be able to tell where that spirit power is coming from unless you get very close. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02276))_
- Also, if there's extremely strong spirit power in your surroundings, you may not be able to sense _weak_ spirit power at work. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02276))_
- Most spirits speak directly to the mind without using words, but when a caster gives commands to a spirit, they must speak these runes out loud. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02278))_
- Area=One lesser spirit Duration=Permanent Effect=Summon and control one type of spirit Type=Target score Counter (Overpowers): _Full Control Spirit_ Countered (Overpowered) By: _Full Control Spirit_ Expansion=Final score, distance Resist=None _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02411))_
- The only spirits you can summon are those that belong to the lesser spirits (monster level 4 or lower). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02412))_
- The biggest advantage of controlling spirits is that you can cast spirit magic that belongs to them, even in places where spirit power would not normally be at work. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02415))_
- The main spirits that can be controlled are gnome (earth spirits), undine (water spirits), salamander (fire spirits), sylph (wind spirits), brownie (building spirits), leprechaun (mind spirits), sprite (mind spirits), etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02416))_
- This magic is a counter spell to the spirit magic _Missile Protection_ , so when the target of the arrow is under the influence of _Missile Protection_ , the wind spirit powers of the same nature will interfere with each other, nullifying both. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02532))_
- Counters (Overpowers): _Control Spirit_ Countered (Overpowered) By: _Control Spirit_ Expansion=Final score, distance Resist=None _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02679))_
- This magical fire will always hit, and the target will suffer strike power 10 damage (strike power 0 and no criticals if they successfully resist). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02323))_
- This spirit can be easily destroyed by applying a small amount of force, and when it does, it emits powerful energy. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02337))_
- At this time, even if an ally casts magic on you in order to help you, you will consider it a _hostile_ act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02351))_

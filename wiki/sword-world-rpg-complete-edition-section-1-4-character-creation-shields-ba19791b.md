## Statements

- In the case of shields, there are only two types: small shields (required strength 1) and large shields (required strength 13). If a character's strength is 13 or greater, a large shield can be chosen. However, in terms of increasing a character's defensive ability, the effects of small and large shields are basically the same in this game. Therefore, it is safe to choose the less expensive small shield (Table 1-10: Shields). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00942))_

## Statements

- Rangers have the ability to predict the weather. Rangers can measure the humidity using their own senses, and they also use small knowledge such as _birds that eat small insects such as flies fly close to the ground = no updraft = thick clouds = rain_ . From these combinations, you can roughly predict the weather. If a ranger succeeds on a success roll against a set target score using _ranger skill level + intelligence bonus_ as the baseline score, the ranger can roughly predict the weather (sunny, cloudy, rain, snow, storm, strong wind...... etc.) The game master must decide whether to use a difficulty or a target score. Normally you would use a target score. This is because failure means that the weather is unpredictable, and this is also very clear to the characters as well. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03249))_

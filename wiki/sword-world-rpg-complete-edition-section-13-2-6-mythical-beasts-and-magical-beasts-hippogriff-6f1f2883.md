- The hippogriff is said to have been born after a griffon (see p. 193) attacked a mare. A flying mythical beast, the front half of its body is an eagle and the back half is a horse. They live in grasslands and feed on small herbivores. Despite their appearance, they're not very ferocious and will not attack humans unless they are very hungry.

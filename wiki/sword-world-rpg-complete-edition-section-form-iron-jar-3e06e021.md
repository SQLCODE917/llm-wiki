# Form=Iron jar

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Anyone who sees this eye (even if it’s an image reflected in a mirror) will fall asleep _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07545))_
- This is used by transplanting it into a body in place of a lost eye. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07545))_
- When you chant a password, a daemon will be summoned from the jar. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07547))_
- The summoned daemon will obey the summoner's commands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07551))_
- If the summoner is gone, the daemon, according to its intrinsic nature, will begin to act violently. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07551))_
- Please use _Table 14-1: Daemon Jar Summon_ to determine which daemon will appear. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07551))_

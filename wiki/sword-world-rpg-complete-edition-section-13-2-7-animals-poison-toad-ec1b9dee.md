- Anyone within 3 meters of a poison toad when it spews its venom must succeed on a life force resistance roll against target score 9. If you fail, the poison will get into your eyes and you will lose your vision. It’ll recover naturally after 3 days, but it can also be cured with the holy magic _Cure Poison_ .

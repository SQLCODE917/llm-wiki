# **[ Holy Weapon ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a spell that applies a negative force against undead to a weapon, rather than to space like _Turn Undead_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02905))_
- It also has no effect on (non-undead) enemies that can only be wounded by magical weapons, such as werewolves, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02906))_
- For _undead_ that can only be wounded by magic weapons, strike power will be _10_ regardless of the strike power of the weapon itself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02906))_
- This additional strike power does not apply to anything other than undead. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02906))_

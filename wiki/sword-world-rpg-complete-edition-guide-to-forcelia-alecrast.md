## Source record

This section describes various nations and regions within the Alecrast continent, including Baikal, Plysis, Oran, Eremire, Saine, Orphan, Ramliearth, Phandria, Lomar, and the western nations. It provides information on their populations, governments, economies, and notable features such as magic usage, conflicts, and cultural aspects.

## Key supported claims

- Baikal is the northernmost country in Alecrast, formed by the union of several tribes, with maritime tribes holding more power than plains tribes due to trade and piracy (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.286-290).
- Plysis is the largest fortified city in Alecrast, a city-state with a population of 40,000, under invasion from Lodorille but has repelled attacks through fortifications and the military advisor Lukial (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.286-290).
- Oran is the largest nation on the southern coast, with a population of 2 million, known for its prosperity, advanced city infrastructure, and the Sorcerers' Guild led by Grand Master Mana Rai (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.286-290).

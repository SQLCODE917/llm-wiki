- The sea worm is a gigantic, disgusting, grub-like monster that’s over 3 meters long. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05455))_
- It is also completely omnivorous, digesting and eating anything the tentacles can catch and fit in its mouth. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05455))_
- When they sense strong light, they'll become ferocious and continue to fight _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05455))_

- Its long, thick body is covered with a thick rubbery membrane and has many segments lined up in a row. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05455))_
- 207 blindly until the light is completely cut off. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05456))_

### 13.6 Handling Monster Special Abilities / 《 Disease 》

- The spirit power of a monster is balanced differently than that of a human, and can always cause illness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06227))_
- However, in most cases the infection power of this _information imbalance_ is weak, so it doesn’t pose much of a problem, but if a character is bitten by a bat or rat, it’s highly contagious, so there’s a high chance he or she will become ill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06227))_
- Illnesses can be treated using the holy magic _Cure Disease_ and _Refresh_ , and the spirit magic _Restore Health_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06228))_
- If your roll is less than the progression intensity, you will be infected with the disease. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06228))_

### 13.6 Handling Monster Special Abilities / 《 Strangling 》

- If an attack with this ability is successful, then in subsequent rounds, the character will remain strangled, and the only thing he or she can do is try to escape. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06231))_

### 13.6 Handling Monster Special Abilities / 《 Entangling 》

- In the case of the giant webspinner, which is a typical monster that performs this attack, it’s almost impossible to cut the thread. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06239))_
- Each time you fail, you’ll normally become more and more entangled, and the target score will increase each time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06239))_
- When a thread is cut, damage will also be applied to the entangled character at the same time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06239))_
- If the difference between the baseline score and the target score reaches 13 or greater, the character will be unable to escape on his or her own, and will eventually suffocate to death. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06239))_
- Whether or not another can rescue them, by attacking or burning the thread, depends on the monster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06239))_

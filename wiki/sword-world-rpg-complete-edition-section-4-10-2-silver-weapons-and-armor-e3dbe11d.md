# **4.10.2 Silver Weapons and Armor**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- **Silver** is a special metal, imbued with magical powers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01132))_
- It is also one of only two types of metal that’s known to exist in the _spirit world_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01132))_
- It is also one of only two types of metal that’s known to exist in the _spirit world_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01132))_
- Even silver weapons and armor have no differences in their strike power, defense power, attack power, or evasion speed at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01133))_
- However, **silver weapons** , due to their magic power, can deal damage to monsters that can _only_ be wounded by magic or magical weapons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01134))_
- Also, shamans are not normally able to wear metal armor (as metal armor prevents them from using spirit magic), but they _can_ use spirit magic while wearing it, if it’s **silver armor** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01135))_
- For example, the price of a silver item with a required strength _2 times lower_ than the original would be 5 x 2 = **10 times** the price of the original. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01136))_
- You can also ask for a _high-quality_ item of silver, but the price will be even _higher_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01136))_
- To buy silver weapons and armor in a store, you must be in a town about the size of the _royal city level in a small country._ Silver weapons and armor will also cost _5 times_ the original price. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01136))_

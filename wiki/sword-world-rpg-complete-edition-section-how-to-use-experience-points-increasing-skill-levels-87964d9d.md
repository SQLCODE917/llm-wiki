# How to Use Experience Points: Increasing Skill Levels

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- At the end of an adventure, characters gain experience points. By spending these experience points, a character can increase their skill level-you pay experience points to buy skill levels. A character can advance in any number of skills and by any number of levels at once. However, in any case, a character's skill level can only reach 10th level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03872))_

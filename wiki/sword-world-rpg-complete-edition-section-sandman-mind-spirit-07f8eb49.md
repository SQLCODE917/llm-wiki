# **(Sandman/Mind Spirit)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This spell has the effect of putting the target into eternal sleep. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02472))_
- They remain asleep forever in the same state they were in when the spell was cast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02473))_
- but in the meantime, the target does not starve or grow. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02473))_
- remain asleep forever unless it's removed by _Dispel Magic_ , another _mind_ spell, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02473))_

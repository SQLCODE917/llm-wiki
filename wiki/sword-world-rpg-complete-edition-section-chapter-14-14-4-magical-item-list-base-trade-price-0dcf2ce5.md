- This is the price at which the item is traded. If it's for sale, adventurers can buy it at the price shown here. If an adventurer sells it, the standard price is half this amount. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10011))_
- If the item is marked as (not for sale), the item is extremely dangerous, is a family heirloom, etc. and for these reasons will never be traded. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10012))_
- You can't even sell dangerous items because there are no buyers. At best, an academy of sages might take it for 1/10th the price. If it's an heirloom and not for sale, you're likely to find a similar case. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10013))_

# 《 **Divination** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is the ability to pray and communicate with a god, asking whether or not one's actions are right. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02706))_
- However, a god's _words_ can only be understood abstractly by humans, and you _cannot_ ask detailed questions to the gods. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02706))_
- Occasionally a god will give you a **revelation** (given at the game master's discretion). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02706))_
- However, a god's _words_ can only be understood abstractly by humans, and you _cannot_ ask detailed questions to the gods. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02706))_
- Revelations should not be used too conveniently for the characters (players). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02707))_

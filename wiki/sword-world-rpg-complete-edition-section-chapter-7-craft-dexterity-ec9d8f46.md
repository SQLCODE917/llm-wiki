## Statements

- To put it simply, this is a technique for creating items. If a craftsman devotes the appropriate amount of time to production (which varies depending on the price and quality of the item they're making), they will be able to create a product that complies with their level. Of course, depending on the item, you may need tools and equipment. The finished product will be picked up by a merchant at half the price listed on the price list. Items made by high level craftsmen are naturally of high quality. Of course, prices will also go up. It's up to the game master to decide what bonuses to give to these high-quality, expensive items. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03788))_
- Success rolls are not required to create items of normal quality, but success rolls are required for weapon The price will increase or decrease by about 3% for every 1 point of difference in the final score. Please round up any fractions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03789))_

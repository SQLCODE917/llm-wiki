The [Lunatic Seed] spell is a special dark magic curse created by The Nameless God of Madness. It plants the seeds of madness deep within the target's mind, making them unable to disobey the caster's commands.

## **Spell Details**

- **Spell Level:** 4
- **Base Mental Power Cost:** 20
- **Distance:** Touch
- **Area:** One
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Effect:** Plant the seeds of madness in the opponent's mind
- **Type:** Mind, curse
- **Expansion:** Final score
- **Resist:** Negated effect

## **Effect**

This is a spell that plants the seeds of madness deep within the target's mind. Those who've had this spell cast on them will be unable to disobey the caster's commands. However, the caster's commands must be something _erratic_ , so to speak. Rational commands will be ignored. They have to be irrational, based on insanity. Those under the spell's influence will be unable to act intelligently, even without the caster's commands. This spell's effect lasts until it is negated (as it is a curse, it cannot be removed by other magic belonging to _mind_ ) or until the caster renounces it.

## **Related Concepts**

This spell is part of the [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-the-nameless-god-of-madness-special-dark-magic-list]] and represents a more insidious form of dark magic that affects obedience and rational thought. It is distinct from [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-insanity]] and [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-berserk]] in its long-term, controlling nature.

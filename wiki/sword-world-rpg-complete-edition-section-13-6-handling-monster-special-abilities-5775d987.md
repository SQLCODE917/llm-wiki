### 13.6 Handling Monster Special Abilities / Special Defense Abilities: Weaknesses

- Different monsters have different weaknesses against certain kinds of attacks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06326))_

### 13.6 Handling Monster Special Abilities / Maintain Life Points 》

- It’s treated as a weakness, but takes the form of an inconvenient habit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06329))_
- In order to maintain its life points, a lamia must suck human blood. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06329))_

### 13.6 Handling Monster Special Abilities / Maintain Mental Points 》

- It’s treated as a weakness, but takes the form of an inconvenient habit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06332))_
- In order to maintain its mental points, a vampire must suck human blood. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06332))_
- Otherwise, it’ll gradually lose its mental points and eventually perish. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06332))_

### 13.6 Handling Monster Special Abilities / Magic 》

- They will suffer damage from _Cure Wounds_ , and if _Healing_ or _Refresh_ are cast on them and they fail to resist, their life points will immediately become 0. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06335))_
- Undead that act with negative life force will suffer damage from healing magic that grants positive life force. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06335))_

### 13.6 Handling Monster Special Abilities / 《 Weak to Fire 》

- This is a weakness seen in plant-type monsters, dry monsters, and monsters that live in cold regions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06337))_
- They’re weak to fire, so checks are made at +10 to strike power when using fire attacks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06337))_
- In addition, for any other _Weak to XX_ , checks against damage magic are made at +10 to strike power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06338))_

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06337))_

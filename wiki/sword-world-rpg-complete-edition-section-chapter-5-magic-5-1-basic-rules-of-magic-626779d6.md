### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1 Basic Rules of Magic / Example:

- There are five types of runes in the world of _Sword World_ . This difference in runes is at the same time a difference in the type of magic. The five runes are **high ancient** , **silent spirit** , **holy prayer** , **daemon scream** , and **dragon roar** . Each has a different source of magical power and a completely different procedure (although _holy prayer_ and _daemon scream_ are essentially the same power, the only difference being the nature of the magic). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01164))_
- Suppose we have an adventurer with priest skill level 1 and shaman skill level 2. This adventurer can use 1st level holy magic and 1st-2nd level spirit magic. He cannot use any ancient magic, nor any holy magic of 2nd level or higher, nor any spirit magic of 3rd level or higher. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01165))_
- One, is that some races may not be able to learn some or all of the runes. These are as explained earlier under 1.2: Races (see p. 13) The other, is that you _cannot_ have more than _one each_ of holy, dark, and dragon roar magic at the same time. These types of magic are obtained through belief in _gods_ , _wicked gods_ , and _dragons_ , respectively. A character cannot hold more than _one_ of these beliefs at the same time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01166))_

### Technical frame 2: Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.12 Ancient Magic List / [ Familiar ]

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01689))_

> If the familiar loses life points or mental points due to an attack from another, etc., the caster will suffer the same pain that the familiar felt, so they will lose the same amount of points in life force and mental power (this damage cannot be reduced by adventurer level). However, the number of points lost cannot go beyond the familiar's maximum life points or mental points. Even if the familiar receives damage that would cause a negative result, the caster will not lose any extra points.

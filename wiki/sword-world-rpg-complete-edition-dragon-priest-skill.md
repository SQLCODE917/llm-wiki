The dragon priest skill is a specialized magical ability related to dragon roar magic, allowing users to communicate with dragons and reptiles, and to perform various dragon-related feats. It is distinct from other rune master skills like the priest skill and is tied to a specific religious and cultural context.

## Acquisition

The dragon priest skill is not acquired through a guild or formal training like other skills. Instead, it is tied to a specific belief system and requires a deep connection with dragons. It cannot be acquired at the same time as the priest skill or dark priest skill.

## Abilities

Dragon priests possess unique abilities related to their faith and training. These include:

- Reptile Check: Identifying dragons, their subspecies, and reptiles using a success roll based on the skill and intelligence bonus.
- Communication with dragons and reptiles.
- Access to a wide range of dragon roar magic spells, including summoning, transformation, and rebirth magic.

## Restrictions

- Dragon priests cannot join adventurer parties due to their religious beliefs.
- The skill is incompatible with the priest skill and dark priest skill.
- The ultimate spell, Reborn Dragon, requires the user to die and be reborn as a dragon.

## Comparison with Other Skills

The dragon priest skill is distinct from other rune master skills such as the sorcerer skill, shaman skill, and priest skill. While it shares some commonalities with the sorcerer skill in terms of magical ability and ancient knowledge, it is more specialized in its focus on dragons and reptiles.

## Experience Points

The experience point requirements for the dragon priest skill are not detailed in this chapter, but it is implied that acquiring and increasing the skill requires significant experience points, similar to other rune master skills.

- Among sages, the reason for this is thought to be that the spell doesn't just require a hand, but a _wing_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02376))_
- _Only_ featherfolk (fae) and gremlins (youma) can use it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02376))_
- This spell is used by borrowing the power of a leprechaun, but humans and elves cannot use it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02376))_
- Those who have this spell cast on them will lose their memory, and be unable to make normal decisions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02377))_
- A gremlin casting this spell on a pegasus carrying the hero Grax, and flinging Grax off of it, is a very well-known tragic episode in Alecrast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02377))_
- You will no longer be able to remember your name, where you were born and raised, or your friends and colleagues, but you will still be able to use language, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02378))_

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Among giant seals, one in every thousand males may be born with a mutation that results in golden skin. Although these mutants are larger than normal males, they're incapable of reproduction, so they never become kings and invariably roam the ice fields. This rare seal is called the golden wanderer, and its skin can sell for tens of times more than the skin of a normal seal.

Rarity in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition is a score that indicates whether the monster is famous or not. The lower this score, the more well-known the monster is. When using the sage skill's monster check ability (see p. 118), this rarity is the target score for a success roll.

Rarity is used to determine the difficulty of identifying a monster through the sage skill's monster check ability. A lower rarity score means the monster is more well-known and easier to identify, while a higher score indicates a more obscure monster that is harder to recognize.

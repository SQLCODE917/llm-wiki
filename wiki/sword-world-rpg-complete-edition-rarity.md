## Statements

### Chapter 6: / [ ⑥ Treasure Appraisal [Intelligence] ] (Not retriable)

- This ability is used to know the value and utility of treasure. The rarity of that treasure becomes the target score for a success roll. For standard ancient kingdom treasure-- many of which are magical items --the target score is around 15. Treat a failed treasure as being unable to determine the treasure's value, not as if you got the value wrong . If the true value is 100 gamels, you will not think that it's worth 10,000 gamels just because you failed the check. However, if the difference from the target score is 6 or greater, there is a possibility of this kind of misunderstanding. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03642))_

- Her doctrine is expressed in the words _be natural_ . This is different from the concept of nature-worship, and her ideal is for humans to be in their natural state. She approves of fighting for selfdefense, but denies any other kind of fighting. She particularly encourages farming and hunting, which are basic activities indispensable for human survival.

Headless horses are horses that pull the chariot with a dullahan on board, and can gallop through the air. They act faithfully to their master's commands and may also attack enemies. They cannot attack while pulling the chariot, but the dullahan can freely _detach_ the chariot from the headless horses at will. When a headless horse's master dullahan is defeated, it loses its ability to act.

## Characteristics

- **Movement**: Can gallop through the air with a speed of 30/30.
- **Abilities**: Normal weapon immunity, mental attack immunity, not affected by poison and illness.
- **Habitat**: Unknown, appears with (or summoned by) dullahan.
- **Languages**: None.
- **Perception**: Magic.

## Interaction with Dullahans

Headless horses are closely associated with dullahans. They serve as the steeds for dullahans, who ride them in the world. When a dullahan is defeated, the headless horses lose their ability to act, indicating a strong dependency on their rider.

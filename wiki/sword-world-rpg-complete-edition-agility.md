# Agility

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about agility:

## Statements

### 【 Agility 】

- Agility represents an adventurer's physical balance and agility. It's related to working with the whole body. In combat, it is important when evading enemy attacks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00142))_

### Loss of Ability Scores and Its Impact

- While adventuring, adventurers often suffer physical and mental injuries and fatigue. In Sword World RPG , this is represented by a loss of life force and mental power (point reduction). If either life force or mental power fall to 0 or less, the adventurer will fall unconscious and be unable to act. The four other ability scores of dexterity, agility, intelligence, and strength are rarely reduced, but if this does happen and these ability scores do fall to 0 or less, the adventurer will still become disabled. At this time, whether they're conscious or not depends on the situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00152))_

### 【 Half-Elf 】

- Half-elves are halfway between human and elf in terms of ability. Therefore, their dexterity, agility, intelligence, and mental power are slightly higher, while their strength and life force are slightly lower. However, they do not deviate very far from the average and are fully capable of handling all skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00277))_

### Action Order

- If, as a result of the action of someone with higher agility, someone with lower agility is unable to perform their intended action , it means that their action is no longer possible . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00644))_

### Withdraw (Flee) From Engagement

- If you do not have a companion to take on your opponent, you will escape with your back completely turned, and you will suffer a -4 penalty when evading the attack. Also, in that case, regardless of your character's agility, they will only escape at the end of the round (agility 0, based on action order). In other words, there is always a chance for the enemy to attack a character before they withdraw. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00700))_

### [ Physical Enchant ]

- There are six variations of this spell, each of which increase or decrease a target's dexterity, agility, or strength by 6. However, even if you reduce them, they can never become 0. If the ability score was originally 6 or less, it'll instead become 1. In most cases, this will result in an increase or decrease in your ability bonus by 1 point. If you use it on a youma, etc. which is not represented by ability scores, all you have to do is increase or decrease its attack points (dexterity), evasion points (agility), or strike points (strength) by 1 point. Also, in the case of agility, action order will also increase (decrease) by 6. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01672))_

### March (Mind)

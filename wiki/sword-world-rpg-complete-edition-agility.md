Agility is one of the six ability scores in Sword World RPG, along with dexterity, intelligence, strength, life force, and mental power. It affects movement speed and evasion speed in combat.

In the context of movement and actions, agility determines how far a character can move during full movement and normal movement. Specifically, the distance a character can move during full movement is **character's agility x 3** meters. For monsters, their movement speed is set separately from agility, so they can move up to **movement speed x 3** meters.

Additionally, agility contributes to evasion speed, which is used to determine how easily a character can evade enemy attacks. A character's evasion speed is calculated as:

**evasion speed** = fighter (thief) skill level + agility bonus

If fighter (or thief) skill level is _0_ , then evasion speed is **0** regardless of the character's agility bonus.

A character holding a _shield_ receives a +1 bonus to evasion speed. The size of the shield (whether it is a small or large shield) is completely irrelevant unless you introduce the optional rule _16.2: Weapon and Armor Modifiers_ (see p. 262). A fighter (or thief) skill level of 0 does _not_ prevent you from receiving this shield bonus. Therefore, the evasion speed of such a character will be **1**.

Agility is crucial for characters who rely on speed and evasion, such as thieves and rangers, who can use their agility to avoid attacks and potentially escape from combat more effectively.

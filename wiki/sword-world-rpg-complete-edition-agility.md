# Agility

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about agility:

## Statements

- If, as a result of the action of someone with _higher_ agility, someone with _lower_ agility is _unable to perform their intended action_ , it means that their action is _no longer possible_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00700))_
- Agility represents an adventurer's physical balance and agility. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00170))_
- Also, in that case, regardless of your character's agility, they will only escape at the _end_ of the round (agility 0, based on action order). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00756))_
- Powers=+6 to agility when worn (agility bonus +1) Adds +6 to the wearer’s agility. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06805))_
- The four other ability scores of dexterity, agility, intelligence, and strength are rarely reduced, but if this does happen and these ability scores _do_ fall to 0 or less, the adventurer will still become disabled. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00168))_
- Therefore, their dexterity, agility, intelligence, and mental power are slightly higher, while their strength and life force are slightly lower. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00315))_
- However, IIDash’s action order = agility is the _same_ as the goblin's, so he would've been slashing at the goblin _at the same time_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00704))_
- Also, in the case of agility, action order will also increase (decrease) by 6. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01690))_
- Regardless of their original agility, everyone's action order will be the same. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03101))_
- An adventurer with adventurer level 4, agility bonus +2, wearing chain mail, is in a cave beneath a volcano. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03399))_
- The caster's agility does _not_ change. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04031))_

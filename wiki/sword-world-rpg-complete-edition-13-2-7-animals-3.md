## Source record

This section describes various dangerous animals found in the Sword World RPG setting.

## Key supported claims

- Giant antlion ambushes prey in sand pits, with -4 penalty to attack and evasion. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.207-211)
- Giant crab has two large pincers, one larger than the other. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.207-211)
- Sea worm is a 3 meter long grub-like monster, spits acidic liquid. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.207-211)
- Rock worm lives underground, 5 meters long, feeds on rocks. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.207-211)
- Sand worm is a carnivorous worm, no growth limit, can reach 20 meters. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.207-211)

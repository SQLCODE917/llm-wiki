- This spell combines the effects of both Wounds and Cure Wounds , allowing the caster to absorb life force (points) from the target to recover when the caster is wounded. First, make a strike power 10 check for damage dealt to the target, and the caster's life force (points) will be restored by the same number of points. If you expand the effect and steal life force (points) from multiple people, you can recover the total amount of life force (points) you stole. However, you cannot increase your life (points) higher than your original score. Also, like Wounds , it has no effect at all if the opponent successfully resists, and it has no effect on opponents that don't have normal life, such as undead, skeleton warriors, etc. This spell also cannot be used if the dark priest him or herself is undead. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04146))_

## Source record

Chapter 11.3 covers dragon roar magic, used by dragon priests who worship dragons as guardians rather than gods. There are no restrictions when using dragon roar magic spells, and users can receive effects by remembering them in their mind.

## Key supported claims

- Dragon priests worship dragons as guardians, not gods (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-153).
- Dragon priests cannot use priest or dark priest skills simultaneously (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-153).
- Dragon priests gain reptile identification and communication abilities (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-153).
- Dragon priests use summon magic to summon reptiles and dragons with specific rules (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-153).
- Dragon priests have 1st-level spells like Iron Stomach and Communicate Reptile (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-153).

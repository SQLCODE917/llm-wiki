## Source record

Dragon roar magic is used by those who worship dragons as guardians. It has no restrictions and can be used even under silence or mute effects. The skill required is the dragon priest skill.

## Key supported claims

- Dragon roar magic has no restrictions when using spells (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-152).
- Users can cast spells by remembering them in their mind (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-152).
- Dragon roar magic users require repeated training (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-152).
- Those who believe in dragons aim to be reborn as dragons (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-152).
- Magic users can even use spells under silence or mute effects (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-152).

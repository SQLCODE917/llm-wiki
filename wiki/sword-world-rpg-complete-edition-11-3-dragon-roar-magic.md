## Source record

Dragon Roar Magic is a rune-based system for dragon worshipers, offering transcendental abilities and summon spells.

## Key supported claims

- Dragon roar magic uses runes worshiped by dragon guardians rather than gods (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-153).
- Through repeated training, users can master the physical, mental, and magical transcendental abilities of dragons (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-153).
- Those who believe in dragons aim to be reborn as dragons (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-153).
- Using Pharis's holy magic Banish may drive summoned creatures back to their origin (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-153).
- Even if Silence or Mute spirit magic is cast, you can still use dragon roar magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.149-153).

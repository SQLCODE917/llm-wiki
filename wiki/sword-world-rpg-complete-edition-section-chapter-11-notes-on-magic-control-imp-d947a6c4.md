## Statements

- This spell is very similar to the ancient magic _Familiar_ . The difference, however, is that the thing you can summon to become your familiar is an imp, not a small animal. One caster _cannot_ summon a second one. See p. 178 for information on imp abilities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03779))_
- An imp that’s become a familiar can be controlled freely from a distance, and the caster can see and hear through the imp's eyes and ears. When casting magic, you can also borrow the imp's mental points to cast spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03780))_
- If the imp loses life points or mental points due to an attack from another, the caster will also suffer the same pain as the imp, and will lose the same amount in life force or mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03781))_

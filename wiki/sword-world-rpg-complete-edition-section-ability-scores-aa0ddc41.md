> Table 1-3: Determining Sub-Ability Scores shows how to determine subability scores for each race. On the table, "D" stands for dice . 1D stands for one die , 2D stands for two dice , and 1/2D stands for half the result of one dice (rounded up) . In addition, a notation such as 1D+6 means to roll one dice and add 6 to the result .

### Technical atom 3

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09193))_

> In the case of humans, assume the ability scores of prime age (from around 15~50 years old) to be 1, and the ability scores of each age are as shown in Table 18-1: Age-Based Ability Score Modification Table. This is just a guideline, and game masters may set individual differences. Some may mature early and quickly develop a fine physique. If you constantly train your body, it's also possible to halt aging to some extent.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09194))_

| Age | Modification |
| --- | --- |
| Dwarves, | Grassrunners, |
| 3 – 5 | All ability scores reduced by 1/6 |
| 6 – 8 | All ability scores reduced by 2/6 |

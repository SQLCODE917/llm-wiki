# Depend

## Statements

### 1.3.1 Skill Basics

- Half-elves vary in their traits depending on whether they were raised by humans or by elves. When raised by elves, they're similar to those of elves. When raised by humans, they're not all that different from humans. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00242))_

### 1.4.2 Determine Ability Scores

- Depending on whether a half-elf was raised by humans or elves, the skills they have from the start and the limitations on the skills they can acquire differ. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00327))_

### Forger of Colors

- However, even today, on rare occasions, mithril silver ore is found, but it is black and has no sparkle whatsoever. Mithril silver is said to acquire different colors and brilliance only when one draws out its power. Depending on the skill of the craftsman, it seems that different colors could be shown in a single item, as in my pen. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02403))_

### 5.1.3 Learning Magic

- Magic is not omnipotent. It is only possible to achieve a defined and limited set of effects by weaving certain words (in runes), called spells . There are several types of spells, each with different effects. Depending on the strength of their effects, spells are divided into levels from 1-10 . 1st level spells are very rudimentary, while 10th level spells are very powerful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02448))_

### [ Suppress Elemental ]

- This spell has the effect of negating any one of the four major spirit powers of earth, water, fire, or wind. However, only one kind of spirit power can be erased at a time. This spell cannot be expanded to block two or more kinds of spirit power from working at the same time. As a result of this, spirit magic that depends on that spirit power will no longer be usable. Also, if a spell effect is present due to the negated spirit power (either gnome, undine, salamander, or sylph) within the spell's area of effect, it will be negated. If that spirit is present, it will also disappear from existence and return to the spirit world. However, this has no effect on greater spirits (behemoth, kraken, efreet, djinn). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03198))_

### Create Undead ]

- Create Undead is a spell that creates undead monsters such as skeletons and zombies. To use this spell, of course, you'll need a corpse (usually a human corpse is used). Depending on the state of decomposition of the corpse, it will determine whether it'll become a skeleton or a zombie. If you use this spell on a mummified or severely decomposed corpse, the corpse will be completely skeletonized and a skeleton will be born. If it's still a corpse before that happens, then a zombie will be born. The caster can give commands to the skeletons and zombies created in this way. The commands must be extremely simple, at best they can be made to protect a specific location. Even walking alongside you as guards is too difficult for them. For details on human skeletons and zombies, see 13.2.9: Undead (see p. 214). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03290))_

### [ Control Weather ]

- Sunny ： The sky within the area of effect will be completely clear of clouds. Depending on the angle of the sun, the sun will shine during the day and the ground will be warm. At night, you'll be able to see the moon and stars. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03573))_

- Additionally, as long as you remain within the area of effect, you will be slammed to the ground by the earthquake every round, suffering strike power 20 damage. However, those who successfully resist will only suffer strike power 10 and no criticals. This earthquake causes all normal houses within the spell's area of effect to collapse (with the exception of strong buildings such as castles and forts, or those with magical protection), and trees to fall down. Depending on the situation, falling rocks and avalanches are quite possible. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04241))_

### 10th Level Spirit Magic List ]

- Within the area of effect, any spirit magic that depends on the neutralized spirit power will no longer be able to be used, and any magic that had an effect will be canceled immediately. This spell's restrictions include the caster him or herself who appealed to the greater spirit. It' s not that the spirit power ceases to exist; the lesser spirits still exist there, but they temporarily become completely neutral and you won't be able to draw out their magical powers. This also applies to spirits sealed with spells such as Control Spirit , etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04273))_

### [ Reincarnation

- This spell is cast immediately after an individual's death. The soul of the target of this spell will be reborn as a baby in another location within one week. Reborn babies have their ability scores preserved (they have the same ability scores after becoming adults). As the baby grows up, at a certain moment it'll remember its former personality. At that stage they will be able to use all their previous memories and skills. When they remember their previous personality depends on the final score from when the spell was cast (see Table 5-8: Reincarnation Memory). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04640))_

### Chapter 6: / Precautions When Using Skills

- The owner of this skill is called a thief . However, not all of them make their living by stealing from others. It is true that the thief skill includes the abilities necessary for theft, such as pickpocketing and lockpicking, but if anything, you can say they're specialized craftsmen who are familiar with the techniques needed behind the scenes of society. For better or worse, society cannot exist without their presence. They may be hired by the king for espionage, etc. and depending on the money they may undertake assassinations. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04907))_

### Chapter 7: / [ ① Craft [Dexterity] ]

- To put it simply, this is a technique for creating items. If a craftsman devotes the appropriate amount of time to production (which varies depending on the price and quality of the item they're making), they will be able to create a product that complies with their level. Of course, depending on the item, you may need tools and equipment. The finished product will be picked up by a merchant at half the price listed on the price list. Items made by high level craftsmen are naturally of high quality. Of course, prices will also go up. It's up to the game master to decide what bonuses to give to these high-quality, expensive items. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05325))_

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / [ Mandrake ]

### 《 Reaction 》

- If it says neutral , it means the monster holds no special feelings of hostility nor allyship towards the adventurers. Depending on the situation, they can be your enemy or your ally. Sometimes they simply avoid interaction and disappear. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06918))_

### [ Brownie ]

- They also have a small amount of telekinesis, and will attack unwanted intruders by hitting them with things inside the mansion. The strike points when this attack hits vary depending on what is used for the attack. A small dish would be 5 points, a knife, etc. would be 7, and a large chest of drawers would be 9. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07136))_

### [ Haunt ]

- A ghost is a moving being whose corpse still holds a lingering grudge. Depending on how long ago they died, they can look like zombies (rotting corpses) or skeletons (moving skeletons). Their strength varies considerably depending on which abilities they had in life. The scores listed here are averages. All ability scores will be the same as previous, except for mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-08673))_

### [ Vampire ]

- Vampires share several common characteristics. First, vampires have pale skin and a red glow in their eyes. These red eyes cause intense fear in those who see them. Anyone who makes eye contact with a vampire must first make a mental power resistance roll. The target score varies depending on the type of vampire, but in any case, if you fail to resist, you will be paralyzed with fear and unable to act. Even if the vampire removes its gaze, this paralysis continues until it disappears. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-08873))_

### [ Puppet Golem ]

- A puppet golem is a small, simple version of a golem that is created by giving temporary life to a tree branch, stone, etc. using an ancient magic spell. Depending on the material, there are four different types of puppet golems that can be created. They are oak, stone servant, bone servant, and skeleton warrior. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-08964))_

### 《 Strangling 》

- While being strangled, a character suffers a -4 penalty to evasion and is normally unable to make sound. Depending on the monster, the penalty may be smaller, or it might be possible to still make sound, but if this is the case it'll be specified in the description . Unless otherwise noted, the evasion penalty is 4 and magic is also considered not possible. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09851))_

### Chapter 14: / Adventure Treasure and Reward Amounts

- Of course, depending on the contents of the scenario and the actions of the player characters, it's fine to introduce unexpected treasure, or to even have an adventure that results in a deficit. Game masters should be aware of how much property and equipment the player characters have. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09963))_

### Chapter 14: / 《 Other 》

- Depending on the item, other entries may be included in the data section. For example, for weapons and armor, an entry called Required Strength will appear. The meaning of these special entries becomes obvious when you consider what the item is like. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10021))_

### Chapter 14: / [Crystal Orb of Clairvoyance]

- This is a magical crystal ball that shows the user every place around the world. The extent to which the effect reaches depends on the magical power stored in the crystal ball. Particularly powerful ones are said to be able to ignore magical barriers that would completely block a normal crystal ball, or show the state of another world. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10906))_

### Success Rolls

- This depends on how much work the adventurers did during the adventure. How assertively the adventurers acted is reflected by the number of dice rolls made by the players. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11151))_

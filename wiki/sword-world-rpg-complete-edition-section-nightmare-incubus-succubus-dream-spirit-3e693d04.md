# **[ Nightmare ] (Incubus, Succubus/Dream Spirit)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The spell begins when the target goes to sleep, while the caster remains awake and focuses on controlling the dream spirit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02719))_
- This spell has no distance restrictions, but the target must be someone the caster knows personally. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02719))_
- At this time, the contents of the nightmare the opponent sees can be chosen as desired by the caster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02719))_
- Also, the ritual must be continued overnight with undivided attention from sunset until dawn. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02719))_
- Thus, the dream spirit controlled in this way enters the opponent's dreams and causes them to have nightmares. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02719))_
- This ritual must be performed every night until the target becomes weak and dies. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02719))_
- If the ritual is interrupted, even for one night, the spell's effect is completely lost. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02719))_
- This is a spell that uses a dream spirit to send nightmares into the target's sleep, cursing your opponent to death. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02719))_
- Thus, the dream spirit controlled in this way enters the opponent's dreams and causes them to have nightmares. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02719))_
- If they fail, their mental power (points) will decrease by 1 point. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02720))_
- The target of the spell must attempt to resist daily. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02720))_
- Also, regardless of whether the resist succeeded or failed, they will no longer be able to recover their mental power (points) by _any_ means (it will _only_ recover once the spell's effect is removed). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02720))_

The projectile range extension optional rule allows for extending the range of throwing and shooting weapons beyond the basic limits.

## Long Distance Modifiers

Hitting a distant target with a weapon is a nigh-impossible task. Throwing weapons have a **-1 modifier to attack power for every meter beyond 20 meters**, and shooting weapons have the same for **every 5 meters beyond 100**.

## Maximum Range

There's no such thing as a bow with infinite reach. Each throwing or shooting weapon has a **maximum range**, and they will _never_ hit a target farther away than this.

| Sling | 100 |

This rule extends the basic range limits of weapons in the game, allowing for more tactical options in combat when facing distant opponents. The modifiers and maximum ranges are designed to reflect the increasing difficulty and limitations of hitting targets at extreme distances.

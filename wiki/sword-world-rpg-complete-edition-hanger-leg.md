The hanger leg is a headless octopus-like creature from the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, classified as a parasitic monster. It is a vicious parasite that lives by taking over the bodies of large animals, and has no visible distinction between male and female. Its body is only 20 cm long, but its supple legs, which also serve as tentacles, are equipped with countless suckers, and can stretch to over 1 meter when extended.

## Characteristics

- **Monster Level**: 1
- **Rarity**: 13
- **Agility**: 18
- **Movement Speed**: 8
- **Number**: One. However, eggs are often found in groups
- **Frequency**: Rare
- **Intellect**: Human
- **Reaction**: Neutral
- **Attack Points**: Needle: 8 (1)
- **Strike Points**: 6
- **Evasion Points**: 11 (4)
- **Defense Points**: 4
- **Life Points/Resistance**: 8/8 (1)
- **Mental Points/Resistance**: 8/8 (1)
- **Special Abilities**: Parasitism (Resist target score=8)
- **Habitat**: Forests
- **Languages**: None
- **Perception**: Five senses

## Behavior

The hanger leg is a parasite that takes over the bodies of large animals. It can easily jump up to 3 meters, and once it attaches itself to a creature's head, it won't come off even if a giant pulls at it. The creature embeds needles extended from the tips of its legs into the opponent's brain, which not only absorbs nourishing blood from the blood vessels but also paralyzes the opponent's brain and controls their nervous system. The victim must make a life force resistance roll against target score 8, and if they fail, their body is taken from them regardless of their will.

## Interaction with Adventurers

When a hanger leg attaches itself to an adventurer, it cannot use that person's skills or adventurer level as is. Although it can perform the corresponding actions, the final score will be the result of rolling the dice as if it did not have any skills, plus 1 point bonus from the parasite's adaptive ability. An unfortunate victim of a hanger leg's possession will lose all senses, unable to see or hear anything, and furthermore, will be unable to think. Naturally, you cannot escape on your own, but once killed, the parasite leaves the host's body. Conversely, if the host dies, it'll attempt to possess the nearest living creature.

## Special Abilities

The hanger leg has a special ability called parasitism, which allows it to take over the body of a host creature. The resist target score for this ability is 8. The hanger leg can freely use the body of the victim it's taken over. However, even if its opponent has human intellect, it cannot speak their languages, nor can it take their knowledge and experience as its own. Of course, using magic is out of the question. Nevertheless, all motor functions and physiological abilities are at the will of the parasite.

## Habitat

The hanger leg is found in forests. It rarely attaches itself to humans, usually preferring to possess animals and livestock.

## Egg Laying

When death is near, the hanger leg will form several balls under the flesh hardened with secretion and lay small eggs. These eggs will hatch and grow inside the balls, and when they grow up, they will form a cocoon and sleep until a host creature approaches. Then, when the long-awaited opportunity arrives, they are quick to pounce on such an unwary creature.

## Combat and Magic

A hanger leg that is lodged in a head cannot be removed by force. If other allies want to help, they must attack and be prepared to hit their own ally. Please refer to _Attacks and Magic Against Grapplers_ on page 266.

## Experience Points

The hanger leg provides experience points to adventurers when defeated, contributing to character growth and skill development.

## Optional Rules

The hanger leg's parasitism ability is a key element in combat and magic interactions. It can be used to control the actions of the host creature, and can be a significant threat to adventurers.

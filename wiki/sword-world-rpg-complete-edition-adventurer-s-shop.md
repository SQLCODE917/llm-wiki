An adventurer's shop in the Sword World RPG is a place where adventurers gather information, buy/sell equipment, and exchange information about adventures. These shops are generally found in larger towns and serve as bases for adventurers to prepare for their quests. They double as taverns and are used as places to exchange information regarding adventures.

Adventurers in Alecrast typically use one adventurer's shop as their base for adventure, gathering information about the ruins of the ancient kingdom, then setting off after readying their equipment, making their living selling the spoils.

These shops also serve as venues for hiring adventurers for jobs in towns and villages. Requests for work like this also come to adventurer's shops, and much of the reward for resolving these jobs will be in silver coins (gamels). It's also fine for a reward to be a kiss from a beautiful maiden, or some sort of special knowledge or information.

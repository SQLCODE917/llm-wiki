## Statements

- A character must be given a name, and their age and gender must be decided. These things may be decided at any stage of character creation. You can decide at the beginning. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00586))_
- In practice, however, this is a good time to decide. This is because the part of the process that uses random numbers for character creation has just been completed. Wouldn't it be a little sad if, after first picking a name, the ability scores didn't fit the image of that name? _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00587))_
- Conversely, once the ability scores are created and the character's initial experience is determined, you can usually get an impression of the character. This should make it easier to decide on the age, gender, and name of the character. In Sword World RPG , these are not related to the numeric aspects of the game. In other words, it is assumed that there are no differences or changes in ability based on gender or age. Of course, no matter what name you choose, it will not affect their abilities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00588))_

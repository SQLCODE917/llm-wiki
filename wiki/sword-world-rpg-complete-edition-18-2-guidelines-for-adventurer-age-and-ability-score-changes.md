## Source record

This section provides guidelines for adventurer age and ability score changes, including age-based modifications for different races. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.277-279)

## Key supported claims

- Adventurers' ability scores change with age, with prime age (15-50) assumed to be 1 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.276-276).
- Non-human races are considered equal to humans until adulthood, after which aging affects them differently (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.276-276).
- Elves have specific age-based ability score modifications, with decreasing scores after age 50 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.276-276).

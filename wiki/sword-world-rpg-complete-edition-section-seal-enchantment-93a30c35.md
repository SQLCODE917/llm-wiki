# **[ Seal Enchantment ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- For this reason, _Sense Magic_ or _Sense Aura_ will no longer be able to distinguish them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01986))_
- This spell is used on objects imbued with magical power to hide the presence of that magical power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01986))_
- When discerning magic power with _Analyze Enchantment_ , you'll need a final score greater than or equal to the _Seal Enchantment_ that was cast on it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01986))_
- Many of them are protected by _Seal Enchantment_ with a final score as high as 30 or greater. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01988))_
- This spell cannot be cast on living things. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01989))_
- This spell has no effect when using the sage skill to appraise an item (however, if you don't think it has magical power, you might not try to appraise it). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01989))_

## Statements

- This spell can be used on an individual and has the effect of healing them of any illness they may be suffering from. If the final score when casting this spell is greater than or equal to _double the healing score_ of each disease, you can heal that disease. For details, see 12.2.2: Illnesses (see p. 164). This spell can heal multiple diseases at once and restore health. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02796))_

Evasion points are calculated as the character's evasion speed + 8. The reason for the +8 modifier is because in combat between characters, if the attacker and the evader have the same final scores, the attack will fail. However, between a character and a monster, the attack will hit. This is the same reason why resistance score is calculated with +8.

This concept is detailed in Chapter 13.5 of the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.

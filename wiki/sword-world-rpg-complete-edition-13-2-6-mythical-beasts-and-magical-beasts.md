## Source record

Section 13.2.6 Mythical Beasts and Magical Beasts from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, describing various creatures including satyrs, ceiling hangers, harpies, hippocampi, medusas, androscorpios, centaurs, cockatrices, scyllas, chimeras, twintail cats, telchines, hippogriffs, and pegasuses. Some creatures may be possible variants or have uncertain traits.

## Key supported claims

- Satyrs are forest-dwelling creatures with human upper body, goat lower body, and horns, manipulating emotions with a flute (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-191).
- Ceiling hangers are magical beasts with arms of different sizes, capable of moving along walls and ceilings, attacking sleeping prey with surprise and strangle (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-191).
- Harpy deelas are neutral, charm humans by singing, while furias are aggressive and have no special abilities (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-191).

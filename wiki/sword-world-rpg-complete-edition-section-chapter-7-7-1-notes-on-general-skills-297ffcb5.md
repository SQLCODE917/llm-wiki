### Chapter 7: / 7.1 Notes on General Skills / The Difference Between General Skills and Adventurer Skills

- The hunter skill is very similar to the ranger skill. The details will be explained later, but the abilities of the two actually overlap quite a bit, and a good ranger can also become a good hunter. Of course, the opposite is also true. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05283))_
- Many of you may be thinking, Then there's no need to separate the two, right? However, you can't do that. This is because the hunter skill and the ranger skill are completely different things. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05284))_
- The reason for this is that the hunter skill is a general skill, while the ranger skill is, as precisely called, an adventurer skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05286))_
- Now, let's explain the difference between general skills and adventurer skills. General skills, so to speak, can be said to be the skills necessary for daily life. Of course, adventurer skills are the necessary skills to live as an adventurer. However, the biggest difference between the two is whether or not you can become a protagonist-- called a hero -who appears in fantasy novels, etc. by perfecting your skills. In Sword World RPG , adventurer level increases as an adventurer's skill level increases, and the higher your adventurer level, the more qualified you are to be called a hero. In other words, adventurer level can even be called hero level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05287, source-range-0b9dec90-05289))_
- Therefore, a good ranger has the potential to be called a hero by the people, but a hunter, no matter how good he is, has a hard time getting people to recognize him as a hero. Of course, being a hunter is also a respectable profession, so you should never look down on it. People with higher levels in general skills should also be given due respect. However, they are not called heroes. They don't even become the protagonist of the story. They are unrelated to these adventures . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05290))_
- If you understand this mentality, then on the other hand, it would be acceptable to recognize adventurer skills for skills that would be called heroes. For example, the famous playwright William Tell, who was just a hunter, was considered a hero for shooting an apple off his child's head. In such cases, it is okay to grant adventurer skills to masters of general skills. However, basically in Sword World RPG , only those who are masters of adventurer skills are worthy of being called heroes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05291))_

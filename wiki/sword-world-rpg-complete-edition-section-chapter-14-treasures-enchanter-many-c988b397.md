## Statements

- A scape doll will break when its life force reaches 0. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06895))_
- If a character ties his or her hair to it and etches his or her name into it, then when he or she suffers physical damage, the scape doll’s life force will be reduced instead of that character’s life force. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06895))_
- The scape doll’s life force will be the same as the life force of the character who etched his or her name into it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06895))_
- If a character ties his or her hair to it and etches his or her name into it, then when he or she suffers physical damage, the scape doll’s life force will be reduced instead of that character’s life force. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06895))_
- However, if the scape doll itself suffers damage, the character’s own life force will decrease, rather than the scape doll’s life force. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06896))_
- Also, if you use multiple scape dolls, all the scape dolls will break at the same time, so it’s completely pointless. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06897))_

# Duration=Instant

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about duration=instant:

## Statements

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.11 Ancient Magic and the Sorcerer Skill / 《 Duration 》

- This represents how long the magic will remain in effect. This includes **instant** , **18 rounds** , **1 day** , **permanent** , **until focus ends** or **until certain** **conditions are met** . For magic whose duration is _instant_ , the magic itself disappears in an instant, but its effects remain. Wounds caused by a _Fireball_ explosion will remain, and if you receive healing by _Cure Wounds_ , those wounds will be recovered instantly. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01463))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.15 Holy Magic and the Priest Skill / Duration=Instant

- This spell has the effect of completely freezing the target instantly. If you successfully resist the spell, there will be no effect, but if you fail, all life activities will cease and you will die immediately. This spell cannot exert its original effect on things that do not have living bodies with blood flowing through them, such as undead, golems, etc. and can only freeze the surface of their bodies and render them unable to act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02632))_

- Those killed by this spell will _not_ come back to life even when thawed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02633))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.16 Basic Holy Magic List / Duration=Instant

- This spell emits a light from your hands that is harmful to undead. As a result, you can deal damage to undead within 10 meters of the caster. Its strike power is 20. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02789))_

- Also, anyone (undead or not) who sees the spell's light and fails to resist loses their vision for one round due to the glare. Those who lose their vision suffer a penalty of -4 to attack power (points) and evasion speed (points). This means that undead will suffer damage _and_ lose vision for one round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02790))_

- This spell cannot take effect within the area of effect of _Darkness_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02791))_

- This spell is cast on a human possessed by the undead monster called the _haunt_ , or on a location where a haunt is bound. If you can attain a final score that's greater than or equal to the haunt's mental point resistance, you can make that haunt vanish from the material world. However, if this magic fails, the haunt will attack and try to kill the caster. It may attempt to _possess_ the caster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02833))_

- This magic's final score can be increased through a ritual, but the haunt must be in a disabled state in order to begin the ritual. Once the ritual has begun, the targeted haunt _cannot_ be active during the ritual. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02834))_

- If a haunt is extinguished by this spell, its soul will be completely annihilated, so even if you use the spells _Resurrection_ and _Save Soul_ , you will no longer be able to bring it back to how it was before it became a haunt. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02835))_

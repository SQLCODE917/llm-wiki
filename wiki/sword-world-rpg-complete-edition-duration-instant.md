# Duration=Instant

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about duration=instant:

## Statements

- This spell has the effect of completely freezing the target instantly. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02632))_
- This means that undead will suffer damage _and_ lose vision for one round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02790))_
- This spell cannot exert its original effect on things that do not have living bodies with blood flowing through them, such as undead, golems, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02632))_
- and can only freeze the surface of their bodies and render them unable to act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02632))_
- If you successfully resist the spell, there will be no effect, but if you fail, all life activities will cease and you will die immediately. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02632))_
- Those killed by this spell will _not_ come back to life even when thawed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02633))_
- As a result, you can deal damage to undead within 10 meters of the caster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02789))_
- This spell emits a light from your hands that is harmful to undead. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02789))_
- Its strike power is 20. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02789))_
- Also, anyone (undead or not) who sees the spell's light and fails to resist loses their vision for one round due to the glare. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02790))_
- Those who lose their vision suffer a penalty of -4 to attack power (points) and evasion speed (points). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02790))_
- This spell cannot take effect within the area of effect of _Darkness_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02791))_
- However, if this magic fails, the haunt will attack and try to kill the caster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02833))_
- It may attempt to _possess_ the caster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02833))_
- This spell is cast on a human possessed by the undead monster called the _haunt_ , or on a location where a haunt is bound. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02833))_
- Once the ritual has begun, the targeted haunt _cannot_ be active during the ritual. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02834))_
- This magic's final score can be increased through a ritual, but the haunt must be in a disabled state in order to begin the ritual. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02834))_
- If a haunt is extinguished by this spell, its soul will be completely annihilated, so even if you use the spells _Resurrection_ and _Save Soul_ , you will no longer be able to bring it back to how it was before it became a haunt. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02835))_

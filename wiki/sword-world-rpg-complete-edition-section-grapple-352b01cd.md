From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This action is like wrestling, in which you attempt to fight by grappling with your opponent. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07177))_
- Then, any attempt to grapple will automatically occur at the _end_ of the round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07178))_
- To attempt a **grapple** , you must first _not_ be holding any large weapons or shields in your hands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07178))_
- Then, any attempt to grapple will automatically occur at the _end_ of the round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07178))_
- If you succeed (and your 265 opponent fails to evade), you’ll enter a grapple. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07179))_

### Technical atom 1

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07179))_

> Anyone who attempts to grapple will suffer a -2 penalty to his or her attack power, then must make a hit check for their attack.

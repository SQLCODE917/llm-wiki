# **(Kraken/Greater Water Spirit)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This spell can be cast on rivers, canals, lakes, oceans, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02707))_
- Casting this spell while on a ship makes it easier to go against the natural flow. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02707))_
- Also, this spell cannot be used to dam the flow or divide the surface of the water to open a path. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02707))_
- Movement speed is limited to 50 meters per round, and can be adjusted by the caster, but the flow cannot be changed in a very complicated way. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02707))_

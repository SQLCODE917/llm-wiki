## Statements

- The sage skill represents a comprehensive understanding and depth of knowledge in all academic fields. Academic fields in Sword World are undifferentiated, and owners of this skill are skilled in all disciplines. Many sorcerers have this skill. There is no requirement that you have to have the sage skill and the sorcerer skill at the same time, but if you do acquire the sage skill and the sorcerer skill at the same time, you will receive a bonus on experience points consumed. For more information, please refer to Chapter 8: Character Growth (see p. 129). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05047))_
- Sages have a wide range of knowledge. This includes knowledge of the various monsters that live in Alecrast. When a sage sees a monster or hears its name, this ability may allow them to discover its true identity. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05057))_
- Each monster has a score called rarity . Using sage skill level + intelligence bonus as the baseline score, make a success roll with this rarity as the target score, and if you succeed, you know the monster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05058))_
- If the check fails, the character has no knowledge of the monster. Even if the player knows, they must treat it as if they had no prior knowledge. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05059))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ② Languages [Intelligence]] / Sage Skill

- The player chooses which language to learn, along with whether they will speak or read it. However, runes cannot be chosen. Even in the same language, being able to read and speak is different, so if you want to become proficient in one language, you need 2 levels of experience. You can also write in any language that you can read and speak. As an optional rule, you can set a target score or difficulty for text or conversations so that it cannot be understood if you fail a success roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05064))_
- There are limitations to learning languages through the sage skill. Characters who cannot read common must fi rst learn how to read common before learning other languages. Then, a character who cannot read low ancient (but has learned common) must learn to read low ancient before learning any other language. If you can read both common and low ancient, you are free to learn subsequent languages. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05065))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ③ Astrology [Intelligence] ] (Not retriable)

- From the movement of celestial bodies, you can know the signs of events that will occur in the near future. Signs should be given as rough information about the incident. It's enough to let you know that a king passes away or that a major war is about to happen (you can think of it as just a headline in a newspaper, etc.). However, this ability only allows you to know about events related to the country or the world, and when no events occur, no signs appear. It is entirely up to the game master to decide what kind of sign there is. You can also set a difficulty and ask for a success roll. Solar and lunar eclipses rarely occur in Forcelia, but when they do, they are always a harbinger of something to come. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05068))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ④ Knowledge [Intelligence]] (Not retriable)

- The scope of this ability is very wide. It's for checks regarding whether you know or don't know about things in all fields, from trivia to politics to science. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05070))_
- There are two methods of making checks. One is one where the game master judges whether or not the player knows based on their sage skill level and tells them, without providing a rarity . The other is to provide a rarity for each piece of knowledge, and ask for a check via success roll, similar to monster check . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05071))_
- Please use whichever method the game master deems appropriate for each situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05072))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ⑤ Research [Intelligence] ] (Not retriable)

- When a sage fails a success roll on a check such as monster check , treasure appraisal (described below), or knowledge and is unable to figure it out, the sage can later find out by researching literature. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05075))_
- Research basically takes about one week for each item (this may be increased or decreased at the game master's discretion). During this time, you cannot do much other than research. Also, if you don't have a place with a wealth of literature (the best place is the academy of sages = sorcerers' guild in each city), research itself is impossible. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05076))_
- If the research is successful, the sage may make another success roll. When something they didn't know (something that failed) becomes a success, the sage has found a description of it in the literature and has access to that knowledge. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05077))_

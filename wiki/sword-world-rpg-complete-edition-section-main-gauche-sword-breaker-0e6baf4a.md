# **Main-gauche/Sword Breaker**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- A **main-gauche** is a dagger held in the left hand (right hand for left-handed adventurers) for parrying. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07925))_
- You'll gain a +1 bonus to evasion speed _only_ if your opponent's weapon is a _dagger_ , _rapier_ , or _short sword_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07925))_
- If you roll an _11_ , and you _evade_ your opponent's attack, you can make your opponent _drop_ his or her weapon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07926))_
- Of course, your opponent's weapons are limited to _daggers_ , _rapiers_ , and _short swords_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07926))_
- It has a similar bonus to evasion speed, and moreover, if used skillfully, can _break_ or _drop_ an opponent's weapon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07926))_
- A **sword breaker** is a type of maingauche with serrated blades lined up along the back. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07926))_
- Of course, your opponent's weapons are limited to _daggers_ , _rapiers_ , and _short swords_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07926))_
- Main-gauche and sword breaker have the _same_ attack scores as a _dagger_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07927))_
- Also, if you use it for an attack, you’ll _lose_ the evasion bonus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07928))_

## Technical atoms

> Context: Pole weapons cannot reach their full power in tight spaces. The length of a pole weapon is **required strength x 15** centimeters. If you don't have a space with this radius, you _cannot_ use attack methods other than _thrust_ . It would not be a good idea to bring them into underground labyrinths, etc.
_(context: Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07923))_

> When you roll 2D for evasion, if you get _double sixes_ , you can _break_ your opponent's weapon.
_(source: Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07926))_

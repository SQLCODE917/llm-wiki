# Throwing Multiple Darts or Daggers

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- All objects thrown at the same time must have the same weight (required strength). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09027))_
- The total required strength must be up to the character's strength (or half that if it's the thief skill). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09028))_
- The number thrown at the same time becomes a penalty to attack power. For example, if you throw three at the same time, that'll incur a -3 penalty to your attack power for each hit check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09030))_
- This optional rule increases the chance that a high-level character will defeat a low-level character or monster in one blow. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09031))_
- You cannot choose multiple targets. There must always be one target. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09032))_
- Never forget the limitation of projectiles: they cannot be used while in an engagement . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09033))_

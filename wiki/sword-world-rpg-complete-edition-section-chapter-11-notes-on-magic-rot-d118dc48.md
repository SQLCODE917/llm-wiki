## Statements

- This spell has the effect of accelerating the rate of decay and causing the target to rot faster. Objects that had this spell cast on them will decay very quickly. Decay, which normally takes two to three days, will be reduced to half an hour. As long as an object is perishable, it _will_ decay, even if under conditions where decay would normally not progress, such as the environment being below freezing, or being soaked in a saturated brine. However, this has no effect on metals or rocks, and it has no effect on living creatures at all. Targets are limited to non-living meat, plants, fibers, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03854))_
- This spell can reduce the time it takes for decay to complete by consuming extra mental power. If _double_ the normal amount of mental power is consumed, the target will decay in _15_ minutes, and if _triple_ , the target will decay in _10_ minutes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03855))_
- Even if the spell is removed with _Perfect Cancellation_ , etc. the target will not return to the state it was in before it decayed (it _will_ return with the _counter_ magic _Preservation_ . See p. 102). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03856))_

## Statements

- Summon and control a lesser dragon. Although greater than a wyvern, a lesser dragon's intellect is also not very high, so it can only be used to fight enemies or to carry you on its back. Up to two adult humans can ride on the back of a lesser dragon. Lesser dragons have super strength, so this restriction is not a matter of weight, but of space for riding (they can carry many more people if you use a basket, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06294, source-range-0b9dec90-06296))_

# **[ Decrease Water Pressure ] (Undine/Water Spirit)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- All water pressure exerted on the body by the spell will be alleviated regardless of its strength, and reduced to a point where normal actions are no longer a problem. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02633))_
- Targets under this spell are saved from the effects of water pressure when diving deep underwater. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02633))_
- In addition, to actually dive, it is essential to use _Water Breathing_ in order to be able to breathe underwater. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02634))_

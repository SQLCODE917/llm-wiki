# **4.5.4 Monster Defense Points**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Monsters can repel and hold off attacks with their thick skins and hard scales. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00960))_
- In monster data, this represented by **defense points** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00960))_
- This indicates the amount of damage a monster can reduce. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00960))_
- Please note that a monster's _defense points_ are treated differently from a character's _defense power_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00960))_

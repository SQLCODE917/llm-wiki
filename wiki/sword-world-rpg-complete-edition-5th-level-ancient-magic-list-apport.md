# 5th Level Ancient Magic List [ Apport ]

## Apport Spell Overview

The Apport spell allows a caster to teleport their possessions into the palm of their hand. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 75))_ 

## Spell Requirements and Limitations

The caster must have a complete memory of the item and be able to identify specifically where it is kept. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 75))_ 

The target must be small enough to fit in the caster's hand, such as a sword, but not armor. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 75))_ 

## Spell Mechanics and Effects

If the spell's duration ends or it is canceled, the item returns to its original location. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 75))_ 

The spell has a base mental power cost of 25, infinite distance, and lasts for 18 rounds. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 75))_

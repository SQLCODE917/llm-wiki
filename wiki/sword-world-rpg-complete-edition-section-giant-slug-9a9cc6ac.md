# [ Giant Slug ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Languages=None underground labyrinths, Perception=Five senses This is a slug with a body length of about 5 meters. It moves very slowly, knocking down everything in its path, and grinding it all up with its jagged belly lined with bumps. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-08066))_
- Those hit by a giant slug attack will be dealt damage and knocked down at the same time. On the next round, the slug will pass over your body, dealing you 14 points of damage. No actions can be taken while the slug is passing over. This damage can be reduced by armor. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-08067))_
- You can put a large amount of salt (at least 500 kg) on a giant slug to dehydrate it and put it into suspended animation, but salt alone will not kill it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-08068))_

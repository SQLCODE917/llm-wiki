## Source record

Chapter 1.4 of the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition covers the process of creating a player character, including race selection, determining ability scores, choosing starting skills and experience points, and purchasing equipment.

## Key supported claims

- Players must choose a race for their character, with five options: human, dwarf, grassrunner, elf, and half-elf, each with distinct characteristics and ability score modifiers (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-21).
- Ability scores are determined by rolling sub-ability scores A-H and combining them, with each race having specific sub-ability score ranges (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-21).
- Each race has specific strengths and weaknesses in ability scores, and different starting skills and experience points based on background (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-21).

# 《 Type 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Represents the type of magic. Please see 5.1.9: Magic Types. Counters (negates): and counters (overpowers): describe magic that can be negated by this magic, while countered (negated) by: and countered (overpowered) by: describe magic that can negate this magic. However, Dispel Magic , Counter Sense , Suppress Elemental , Dispel Order , Rune Shield , Perfect Cancellation , Neutralize Elemental , and Remove Curse omit this. Magic of the mind , physical ability , and weapon enhancement types also omit counters and countered by , since they are sufficiently described. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02743))_

## Source record

This chapter details illness progression, healing checks, and infection power mechanics.

## Key supported claims

- The **Rarity** score is used when making a check to see if you know an illness – higher rarity makes identification harder (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.159-169).
- Illness progression checks must be performed at each **progression speed** interval; you roll dice after the set time passes (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.159-169).
- Only **one** success roll is allowed immediately after an illness’s progression check; additional rolls wait until the next interval (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.159-169).
- Adventurer level may also be hero level, but even if you’re a hero, you cannot win against illness – checks are unaffected by level (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.159-169).
- An **Infection Power** of Very strong (e.g., infection through gaze) signals plague‑level contagion; Moderate or Weak denotes lesser spread (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.159-169).

The thief skill 'stealthy step' allows a character to approach a sleeping monster without waking it up. To get within 5 meters of a sleeping monster, you must successfully perform the thief skill _stealthy step_. If you succeed on a success roll using _thief skill level + dexterity bonus_ as the baseline score, the monster will not wake up. If you fail, the monster will sense your presence and wake up immediately.

This skill is relevant for attacks against sleeping monsters, as detailed in [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-chapter-10-3-surprise-attacks]].

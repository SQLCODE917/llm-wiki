# **Madness (Wicked God)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- It is said that he exists in the star world and causes people to go mad, and it is believed that those who come into contact with the light emitted by this mad god on a full moon's night will be afflicted with madness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02696))_
- He is the god of madness, and because of his madness, he is a deity whose name cannot be identified. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02696))_
- The priests of his cult teach that madness frees its believers from bondage and gives them freedom. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02696))_
- His followers are few and he has no temples either. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02696))_
- It is said that he gives sudden inspiration that is not based in logic, and for this reason he is also considered the god of art and invention, so there are sometimes believers in this god among artists and sages. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02696))_

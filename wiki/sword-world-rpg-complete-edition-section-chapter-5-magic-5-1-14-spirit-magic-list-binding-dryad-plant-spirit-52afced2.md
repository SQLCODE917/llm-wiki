## Statements

- To cast this spell, you need enough grass, tree branches, etc. to seal your target's movement. Targets within the area of effect will become entangled in those plants and be unable to move. Those who fail to resist will be unable to take any actions for 18 rounds and will suffer a -4 modifier to their evasion speed (points). Targets that successfully resist are not affected in any way. Also, even if you're caught by this spell once, you can attempt to escape each round by making a success roll using _adventurer level + strength bonus_ as the baseline score. The target score equals the magic intensity or final score from when the magic was cast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02329))_

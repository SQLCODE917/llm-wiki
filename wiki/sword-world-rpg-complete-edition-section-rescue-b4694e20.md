# [ Rescue ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- When you cast this spell, the hand of the gods will help the caster and their companions escape to a safe location (the caster's home, a temple, a familiar city, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03230))_
- By expanding the effect of this spell, you can simultaneously increase the area of effect as well as the number of people it targets. If you double the mental power consumed, it will target 20 people within a 14 meter radius, and if you triple it, it will target 30 people within a 17 meter radius. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03231))_
- The caster can remove anything from the target of this spell, even within the area of effect. Conversely, you cannot force this spell to take effect on those who do not agree to it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03232))_
- The spells listed here can only be used by those who believe in a specific god among characters with the priest skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03234))_

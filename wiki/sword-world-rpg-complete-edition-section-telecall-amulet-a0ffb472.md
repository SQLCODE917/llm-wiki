# **[ Telecall Amulet ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a magical amulet that allows you to converse with someone in a distant location. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07291))_
- While this useful item has been found relatively frequently in the ruins of ancient kingdom, its communication range is limited to approximately 10 kilometers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07291))_
- In other words, at least two amulets are required to make a call. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07291))_
- The amulet possesses magical power such that when you correctly recite the password of your intended recipient's charm and speak towards the jewel at its tip, your voice will emerge from their jewel. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07291))_

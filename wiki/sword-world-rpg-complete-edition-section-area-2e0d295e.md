### Technical frame 1: 《 Area 》

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01382))_

> There are many other ways to write this, but it should be clear if you read the description.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01381))_

> If it works on a specific space or area , then everything within that area is targeted. For example, a Fireball explosion deals explosion damage to everything within a 3 meter radius. It makes no difference whether it's the caster's enemies, allies, or the caster.

### Technical atom 2

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09529))_

> For example, when you cast the ancient magic Light , whose area is A space with a 10 meter radius , by consuming double the mental power, the resulting area will be a spherical space with a radius of 10 x 1.3 = 13 meters .

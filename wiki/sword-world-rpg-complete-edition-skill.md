### 1.3.1 Skill Basics

- There are several types of skills, which are divided into several groups. For each skill, a character's proficiency is represented by a level from 0~10. 0 levels indicates that someone is completely unrelated to that skill, while 10 levels represents the highest proficiency that a normal human can attain. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00238))_

- Skills are referred to by names such as fighter , thief , merchant , etc. As you can see, they have a strong connotation of professional job functions rather than skills. It is also possible for one character to acquire (have levels in) more than one skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00239))_

### 1.3.2 Skill Types

- Skills are grouped into several types. Below is an explanation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00244))_

- First, skills are divided into two types: adventurer skills and general skills . Adventurer skills, as the name implies, are acquired by player characters, the adventurers, or by equivalent characters. There are eight of these: fighter , thief , priest , sorcerer , shaman , sage , ranger , and bard . There are also two adventurer skills that cannot be acquired by a player character: dark priest and dragon priest . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00245))_

- General skills are skills of an ordinary person as opposed to an adventurer, such as merchant , farmer , etc. Some of these are useful during an adventure, and it is well worth it for an adventurer to acquire general skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00246))_

- Each skill consists of a number of abilities appropriate to the skill name, and having a skill level (1 skill level or higher) means that you can demonstrate those abilities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00247))_

### 1.3.3 Adventurer Level

- In Sword World RPG, a character's capability as an adventurer is determined by their adventurer skill level. The highest level among the adventurer skills that a character has is called that character's adventurer level . For example, a character with priest skill level 1, fighter skill level 4 (these are adventurer skills), sailor skill level 5, and fisher skill level 2 (these are general skills) would be at adventurer level 4 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00253))_

### 1.3.4 Explanation of Adventurer Skills

- Adventurer skills are the most important thing for an adventurer in Sword World RPG. This is because which adventurer skills a character has acquired will determine what that character can do, and will reveal that character's characteristics. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00255))_

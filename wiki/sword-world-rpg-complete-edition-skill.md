### Chapter 1: Character / 1.3 Skills

- In addition to ability scores, _Sword World RPG_ also uses numeric values called **skills** to describe the characteristics of characters. Skills have a greater impact on a character's actions than ability scores. This chapter explains the basics of skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00221))_

### Chapter 1: Character / 1.3 Skills / Skill Basics

- There are several types of skills, which are divided into several groups. For each skill, a character's proficiency is represented by a **level** from 0~10. 0 levels indicates that someone is completely unrelated to that skill, while 10 levels represents the highest proficiency that a normal human can attain. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00224))_

- Skills are referred to by names such as _fighter_ , _thief_ , _merchant_ , etc. As you can see, they have a strong connotation of professional job functions rather than skills. It is also possible for one character to acquire (have levels in) more than one skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00225))_

### Chapter 1: Character / 1.3 Skills / Skill Types

- Skills are grouped into several types. Below is an explanation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00231))_

- First, skills are divided into two types: **adventurer skills** and **general skills** . Adventurer skills, as the name implies, are acquired by player characters, the adventurers, or by equivalent characters. There are eight of these: **fighter** , **thief** , **priest** , **sorcerer** , **shaman** , **sage** , **ranger** , and **bard** . There are also two adventurer skills that _cannot_ be acquired by a player character: **dark priest** and **dragon priest** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00232))_

- General skills are skills of an ordinary person as opposed to an adventurer, such as **merchant** , **farmer** , etc. Some of these are useful during an adventure, and it is well worth it for an adventurer to acquire general skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00233))_

- Each skill consists of a number of abilities appropriate to the skill name, and having a **skill level** (1 skill level or higher) means that you can demonstrate those abilities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00234))_

### Chapter 1: Character / 1.3 Skills / Adventurer Level

- In Sword World RPG, a character's capability as an adventurer is determined by their adventurer skill level. The highest level among the adventurer skills that a character has is called that character's **adventurer level** . For example, a character with priest skill level 1, fighter skill level 4 (these are adventurer skills), sailor skill level 5, and fisher skill level 2 (these are general skills) would be at adventurer level **4** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00241))_

### Chapter 1: Character / 1.3 Skills / 1.3.4 Explanation of Adventurer Skills

- Adventurer skills are the most important thing for an adventurer in _Sword World RPG._ This is because which adventurer skills a character has acquired will determine what that character can do, and will reveal that character's characteristics. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00244))_

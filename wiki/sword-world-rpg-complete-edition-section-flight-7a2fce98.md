From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The speed of flying in the sky is what would be movement speed 50, and during full movement, 50 km per hour is the maximum speed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01929))_
- If the spell's effect is removed while you're flying, you'll naturally fall. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01929))_
- However, even if movement speed changes, the caster's agility itself does not change. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01929))_
- Casting this spell allows the caster to fly freely. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01929))_

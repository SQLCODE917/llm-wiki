## Source record

Summarizes rules for dark magic and dark priests, spell usage limits, and the distinction of dark priests among Phalaris worshippers.

## Key supported claims

- If an identical _type_ of spell or a spell with an identical _name_ appears in dark magic, only that version can be used. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148)
- Magic power is added to damage as usual. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148)
- In a narrow sense, only priests of Phalaris are sometimes called dark priests. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148)
- Some believe that if their numbers rise, the age of darkness may come again. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148)
- Dark magic cannot be used to force intense movements such as combat or casting magic. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148)

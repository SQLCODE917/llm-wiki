# 《 **Types of Poison** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Poisons are sometimes labeled as **magic** , **mind** , or **damage** , depending on how they affect you. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04537))_
- These are basically magical items, so they respond to the ancient magic called _Sense Magic_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04538))_
- Those that say _magic_ are poisons that have magical effects. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04538))_
- Magical poisons and their effects can be removed with _Dispel Magic_ as well as _Purification_ , _Cure Poison_ , etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04538))_
- Poisons that are both _magic_ and _mind_ are equivalent to magic that has a _target score_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04539))_
- The holy magic _Sanity_ is effective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04539))_
- The poison's effect also vanishes when another effect appears due to magic that belongs to _mind_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04539))_
- The _target score_ of a poison is the same as its _toxicity score_ (described later). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04539))_
- Those that say _mind_ are poisons that affect the target's mind. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04539))_
- Each poison has its own unique strike power and bonus damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04541))_
- If you roll 10 or greater, it becomes a critical. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04541))_
- Those that say _damage_ are poisons that damage life force (points), but there are common rules for these types of poisons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04541))_
- If this type of poison enters your body, you will suffer this damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04541))_
- For characters, the score is adventurer level, and for monsters, it’s monster level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04542))_
- After deducting this score, if 1 or more damage remains, your life force (points) will be subtracted by the amount of damage that remains. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04542))_
- After deducting this score, if 1 or more damage remains, your life force (points) will be subtracted by the amount of damage that remains. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04542))_
- Those affected by the poison can attempt a life force resistance roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04543))_

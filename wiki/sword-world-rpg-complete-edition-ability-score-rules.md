# Ability Scores in Sword World RPG

In Sword World RPG, characters have six ability scores that form the basis of their capabilities: dexterity, agility, intelligence, strength, life force, and mental power.

## Determining Sub-Ability Scores

Each character has eight sub-ability scores (A through H) that are determined by rolling dice. The method for determining these scores varies by race and is shown in Table 1-3: Determining Sub-Ability Scores. For example, humans use 2D for all sub-ability scores, while dwarves use a combination of 2D, 2D+6, 1/2D, etc.

## Calculating Ability Scores

A character's six ability scores are each the totals of two of these eight sub-ability scores:

|**dexterity**<br> **agility**<br> **intelligence**<br> **strength**<br> **life force**|=**A**+**B**<br>=**B**+**C**<br>=**C**+**D**<br>=**E**+**F**<br>=**F**+**G**|
|---|---|
|**mental power**|=**G**+**H**|

## Ability Bonuses

Once ability scores are determined, sub-ability scores are never used during the game. Instead, ability bonuses are calculated by dividing the ability score by 6 (rounded down). These bonuses are used in gameplay to modify dice rolls.

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / [ Rot ]

- This spell has the effect of accelerating the rate of decay and causing the target to rot faster. Objects that had this spell cast on them will decay very quickly. Decay, which normally takes two to three days, will be reduced to half an hour. As long as an object is perishable, it _will_ decay, even if under conditions where decay would normally not progress, such as the environment being below freezing, or being soaked in a saturated brine.

## Statements

- This spell has the effect of putting the target into eternal sleep. The target will remain asleep forever unless it's removed by Dispel Magic , another mind spell, etc. but in the meantime, the target does not starve or grow. They remain asleep forever in the same state they were in when the spell was cast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03952))_

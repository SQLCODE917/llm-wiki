- This ability is used to climb walls and cliffs. Detailed rules regarding climbing and falling can be found under ④ Climb (see p. 121) in 6.6: Common Adventurer Abilities . Please refer to that section for details on determining target scores, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04963))_
- Of particular note here is that An owner of the thief skill (as long as they follow equipment restrictions) succeeds when climbing using a rope unless they roll double ones on a success roll . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04964))_

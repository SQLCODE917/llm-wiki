## Statements

- When making a strike roll, if your 2D roll is equal to or greater than the critical target (usually 10 , 9 if using the thief skill), the strike is critical . In other words, you have landed a greater strike than would normally be expected. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00847))_
- Make a note of the score you got on the Rating Table. Then, roll 2D again and look under the same key number on the Rating Table. Add that result to the previous result. This is your base damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00848))_
- If the second 2D roll is also greater than or equal to the critical target, you can make a third roll to add even more damage. This process can continue as long as you keep rolling greater than or equal to the critical target. In theory, this could deal an infinite amount of damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00849))_

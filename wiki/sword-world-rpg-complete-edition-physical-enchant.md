Physical Enchant is a category of spells in *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition* that affects the target's physical abilities. It is subdivided into six spells by their effects: Sharpness, Dullness, Quickness, Slowness, Strength, and Weakness.

## **Effects**

- **Sharpness**: Increases the target's dexterity by 6. Increases attack power (points) and the baseline score of dexterity-related abilities by 1 point.
- **Dullness**: Decreases the target's dexterity by 6. Decreases attack power (points) and the baseline score of dexterity-related abilities by 1 point. Duration is 18 rounds.
- **Quickness**: Increases the target's agility by 6. Affects evasion speed (points) and action order. Also increases the baseline score of related abilities. There are six variations of this spell, each of which increase or decrease a target's dexterity, agility, or strength by 6. However, even if you reduce them, they can never become 0. If the ability score was originally 6 or less, it'll instead become 1.
- **Slowness**: Reduces the target's agility by 6. Affects evasion speed (points) and action order. Also decreases the baseline score of related abilities.
- **Strength**: Increases the target's strength by 6. Affects bonus damage (strike points). Also allows the target to use weapons that they cannot use with their normal strength.
- **Weakness**: Decreases the target's strength by 6. Affects bonus damage (strike points). Also prevents the use of weapons that require more strength than the target currently has.

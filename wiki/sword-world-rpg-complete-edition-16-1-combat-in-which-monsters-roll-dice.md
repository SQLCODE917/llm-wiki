## Source record

In Sword World RPG, the monster side (game master) rarely rolls dice during combat, reducing the burden on the game master. However, some game masters may also want to roll the dice, increasing enjoyment.

## Key supported claims

- The game master rarely rolls dice in combat, reducing burden on the GM (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.261-264).
- Some game masters may roll dice for increased enjoyment (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.261-264).
- In emergencies, the game master can control dice results to weave a story (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.261-264).
- When both sides roll dice, the range of numbers expands, increasing enjoyment (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.261-264).
- The game master rolls 2D and adds it to attack points -7 for monster attacks (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.261-264).

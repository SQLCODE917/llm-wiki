## Source record

Part 2 of the Ancient Magic List summarizes spell limitations for this chapter.

## Key supported claims

- A caster can attempt to resist a spell only on its first casting; later attempts fail (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82).
- Spells cannot be pre‑cast in advance; multiple instances must be recast each time (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82).
- Each casting allows speaking only one language; to speak two or more, the spell must be expanded (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82).
- Using a spell on an object with Seal Enchantment gives no effect unless the caster’s final score exceeds the seal score (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82).
- Reading with the spell is limited to one language per cast; doubling the spell permits two unknown languages (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82).

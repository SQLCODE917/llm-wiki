### Chapter 12: Various Checks / [ Lizardman ] / Rules for Poison, Illness and Infection

- In Sword World RPG , illness is defined as a state in which the balance of spirit power within the body is disrupted. The concept of bacterial or viral contagion does not exist. There are two types of symptoms that can appear. One is due to poison and the other is due to illness . In addition, a wound may become infected , which is treated as an illness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06505))_
- Poisons force an imbalance in the spirit power within the body. As long as the poison remains in the body, even if the symptoms disappear, the balance of spirit power will be disrupted and the symptoms will appear again. However, if the poison itself is cleared away, the spirit power within the body will naturally return to a balanced state and restore health. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06506))_
- On the other hand, illness is a state where the spirit power within the body remains unbalanced and in a fixed state. Think of it as the spirits inside your body that are under the illusion that a false equilibrium is the correct one. Illness infection occurs when spirits transmit information about the incorrect equilibrium state to spirits in other human bodies. The unknown spirit of life heals illness by showing other spirits in the body how to achieve balance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06507))_
- In Sword World RPG , the phenomenon of a wound becoming infected means that the container was broken and deformed, causing the contents to become unbalanced . The logic goes that the greater the wound, the greater the risk of infection. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06508))_

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 12.2.1 Poisons

- Just like in our world, in Alecrast, using poison as a weapon is an immoral act. If a player character uses poison to defeat an enemy, they are sure to be treated as a coward and looked down upon by the public. Therefore, a proud warrior does not want to use poison. On the other hand, assassins, evil rune masters, dark priests, etc. do like to use poison. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06513))_

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 《 Usage 》

- The usage of the poison is written in parentheses after the name. These are divided into injected , inhaled , dose , and touch . Injected poisons are poisons that need to be delivered directly into the bloodstream, usually in liquid form. It's applied to small daggers and needles and used as traps on doors and treasure chests. It can be applied to knives and arrows to increase their killing power, but the poison usually wears off after about three uses and loses its effect, so it must be reapplied frequently. Many creatures, such as giant scorpions, poisonous snakes, etc. use injected poison as a weapon. This type of poison only takes effect if you lose 1 or more points of life force when you are hurt by a poisoned blade. Even if the blade hits, if it doesn't cause a wound, it has no effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06526))_
- Inhaled poisons are in gas or powder form and take effect when inhaled through the respiratory tract. This is also often used as a trap, but another method is to store it in a breakable bottle and use it by slamming it into your opponent. In rare cases, some creatures use poison gas as a weapon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06527))_
- If you're trapped in a room filled with poison gas, you must make a resistance roll every round. If you hold your breath, you will not be affected by the gas, but of course you will not be able to speak or cast spells while holding your breath. An adventurer can only hold their breath for a number of rounds equal to their life force. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06528))_

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 《 Types of Poison 》

- Poisons are sometimes labeled as magic , mind , or damage , depending on how they affect you. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06532))_
- Those that say magic are poisons that have magical effects. These are basically magical items, so they respond to the ancient magic called Sense Magic . Magical poisons and their effects can be removed with Dispel Magic as well as Purification , Cure Poison , etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06533))_
- Those that say mind are poisons that affect the target's mind. The holy magic Sanity is effective. The poison's effect also vanishes when another effect appears due to magic that belongs to mind . Poisons that are both magic and mind are equivalent to magic that has a target score . The target score of a poison is the same as its toxicity score (described later). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06534))_
- Those that say damage are poisons that damage life force (points), but there are common rules for these types of poisons. Each poison has its own unique strike power and bonus damage. If this type of poison enters your body, you will suffer this damage. Make a strike roll as you would for any magic that deals damage. If you roll 10 or greater, it becomes a critical. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06535))_
- Those affected by the poison can reduce this damage. For characters, the score is adventurer level, and for monsters, it's monster level. After deducting this score, if 1 or more damage remains, your life force (points) will be subtracted by the amount of damage that remains. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06536))_
- Those affected by the poison can attempt a life force resistance roll. If you successfully resist, the damage will be reduced (in this case, strike power will be -10 and no criticals will occur) or you will not suffer any damage at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06537))_

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 《 Toxicity Score 》

- Each poison has a toxicity score . When the poison enters the body, make a life force resistance roll (for monsters, a life point resistance roll) using the toxicity score as the target score. If you fail, the effect will appear, but if you succeed, the effect will only appear incomplete or won't appear at all. The toxicity scores listed here are just examples; by diluting the substance, the toxicity score can be made as low as desired, and by concentrating it, it can be made somewhat greater. When you cast the holy magic Cure Poison on someone who has been poisoned, compare the final score when casting the magic to this toxicity score. If the final score is equal to or greater than the toxicity score, the poison can be removed by the magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06541))_
- If you fail, the effect will appear, but if you succeed, the effect will only appear incomplete or won't appear at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06541))_

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 《 Rarity 》

- This represents how famous the poison is. The higher this score, the rarer the poison. An adventurer must make a success roll using sage skill level + intelligence bonus as the baseline score, and if they succeed, they will know about the poison. You can also guess the name of the poison when you see the symptoms of the poisoned person. If your success roll is 4 or more above the rarity , you can also know how to make the poison and its antidote (if it has one). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06543))_
- However, even if you know how to make it, whether or not you can make it is another matter. Some poisons require very rare ingredients. If a player declares that they want to make a poison (or antidote), the game master is responsible for deciding which materials are needed. The time it takes to mix a poison (or antidote) varies from one hour to one week. Also, poisons with magical effects can only be mixed by sorcerers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06544))_

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / [ Eternal Child (Dose) ]

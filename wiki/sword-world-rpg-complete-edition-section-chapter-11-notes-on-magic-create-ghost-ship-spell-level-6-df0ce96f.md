## Statements

- This spell is effective even on ships that have already sunk or are shipwrecked and partially destroyed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03951))_
- However, this does _not_ stop the ship itself from decaying, so over many years it will rot to pieces. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03951))_
- The priests of Mirleaf often load zombie and skeleton sailors onto the ships they create, and use them as ghost ships to wreak havoc on the seas. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03952))_

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03946))_

> A terrifying death spell created by the power of the goddess of death, Kardis. A target who has this spell cast on them and fails a resistance roll will die instantly. It has no effect on things that do not have normal life, such as undead, magical creatures etc.

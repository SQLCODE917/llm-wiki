## Statements

- To get within 5 meters of a sleeping monster, you must successfully perform the thief skill stealthy step . If you succeed on a success roll using thief skill level + dexterity bonus as the baseline score, the monster will not wake up. If you fail, the monster will sense your presence and wake up immediately. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05712))_
- Attacks against sleeping monsters are always a success unless you roll 2D and get double ones on your hit check (even when attacking a sleeping opponent, double ones is always a failure). Furthermore, when making a damage check, you can reduce the weapon's critical target by -1. If you are attacking with the thief skill, your critical target will be -2. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05714))_
- If you attack a sleeping monster but do not deal any damage, or if you deal damage but are unable to reduce its life points to 0, the monster will wake up immediately. It will probably become angry and counterattack the adventurer starting on the next round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05715))_
- If there is combat, etc. going on in the surrounding area or a loud noise is made, the monster will wake up immediately. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05716))_

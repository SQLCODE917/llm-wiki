The Sorcerers' Guild is a central hub connecting Sorcerers' Guilds across different regions in Alecrast. It was the first to be established in Alecrast and is known for its scholarly approach to magic.

The position of Grand Master is held by Mana Rai, renowned as a Great Scholar. It was Mana Rai who established ancient magic as power derived from the magical element "mana," completed the currently accepted theory of magic, and developed common language magic that is activated through the common tongue. In present-day Alecrast, there exists no Sorcerer or Sage who rivals him, and perhaps none will ever appear.

The Sorcerers' Guild is also called the _academy of sages_, and many sorcerers are excellent sages. The guild plays a crucial role in the study and teaching of magic, including ancient magic, common language magic, and other arcane arts.

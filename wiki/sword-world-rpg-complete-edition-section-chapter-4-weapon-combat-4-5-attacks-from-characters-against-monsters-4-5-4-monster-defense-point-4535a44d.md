## Statements

- Monsters can repel and hold off attacks with their thick skins and hard scales. In monster data, this represented by **defense points** . This indicates the amount of damage a monster can reduce. Please note that a monster's _defense points_ are treated differently from a character's _defense power_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00926))_

# What You'll Need to Play Sword World RPG

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- When playing Sword World RPG , you will have to write and rewrite many things during the game. So you'll need pencils, erasers, notepads, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00110))_
- You will also need Character Sheets. A Character Sheet is used to keep track of a character who acts in a fictional world (Alecrast in the case of Sword World RPG ) as the player's alter ego. These are needed per the number of people. Please copy and use the sheets included in this book. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00111))_
- Along with pens and pencils, you must not forget the dice. As mentioned before, Sword World RPG , like many other RPGs, incorporates chance and uses dice to resolve various actions, in order to make the game more fun. The dice used are ordinary six-sided dice. Since two dice are often used at a time, it is advisable to bring two dice per person. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00112))_
- From here on in this book, rolling dice will be referred to as rolling 1D or rolling 2D . When you roll 1D , roll one die and use the result that comes up. When you roll 2D , roll two dice at the same time and use the total of both numbers (2-12). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00113))_
- Due to the nature of the game, there are situations in Sword World RPG where math calculations must be made. If division results in a fraction, the fraction is either rounded up or rounded down. Which one is clearly stated for each situation. However, if it's not mentioned, the fraction should always be rounded up. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00115))_

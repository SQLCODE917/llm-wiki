## Statements

- Indicates the infectiousness of the illness. If it’s **very strong** , it has the infection power of a plague. **Strong** is about the same level as influenza. As in the example, **moderate** means it's just a cold, and **weak** means it's not very infectious. When it's **very weak** , it's not infectious and is unlikely to spread to other people. When an illness is _very strong_ or _strong_ , there is a high possibility that the infection will spread to the healer, etc. who is caring for the patient. If the game master determines that there is a possibility of contracting the illness, have the character make a progression check for the illness (compare a 2D roll with progression intensity). If they fail, 164 the character has contracted the illness at _depth_ 1. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04409))_

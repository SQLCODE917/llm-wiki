## Statements

- This spell has the effect of completely freezing the target instantly. If you successfully resist the spell, there will be no effect, but if you fail, all life activities will cease and you will die immediately. This spell cannot exert its original effect on things that do not have living bodies with blood flowing through them, such as undead, golems, etc. and can only freeze the surface of their bodies and render them unable to act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04252))_
- Those killed by this spell will not come back to life even when thawed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04253))_

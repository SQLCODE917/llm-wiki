# **Sing (Mind)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Anyone who hears this spellsong will start singing the same lyrics along with the bard's song. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03237))_
- Those affected will not be restricted in their actions, but as long as the spellsong continues, they will _also_ be singing and will not be able to cast magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03237))_
- There’s no need to remember the lyrics. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03237))_
- However, it has _no_ effect on creatures that do _not_ sing. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03237))_

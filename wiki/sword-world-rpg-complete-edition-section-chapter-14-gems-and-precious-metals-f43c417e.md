- This refers to wares and handicrafts, etc., made from gems and precious metals. These include crowns, rings, brooches, earrings, necklaces, bracelets, etc., and other accessories that are made with gems or precious metals. This kind of treasure can be judged as treasure or junk by using the thief skill and sage skill's treasure appraisal ability. If you spend enough time, you can determine a specific price. Naturally, merchants who deal with this kind of item can also determine the price. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08090))_

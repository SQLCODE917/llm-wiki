## Statements

- Those who exercise their power as agents of the gods are priests. In the ancient days of myth, the gods who created the world were later divided into light and darkness and waged a great war. A great war that engulfed all the ancient races- including the legendary dragons of old --ended with all the gods losing their bodies. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00249))_
- Gods who have lost their physical bodies cannot engage with the material world , which is the world for those with bodies. Instead, they interact with the world through priests. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00250))_
- Priests, as agents of the gods, can use holy prayer and cast holy magic . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00251))_

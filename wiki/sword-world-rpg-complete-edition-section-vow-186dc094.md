### Technical frame 1: 《 Vow 》

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02923))_

> This is a voluntary vow. When a priest wants to do something, they can make a vow to their god. When a priest's actions contradict or move away from their vows, their god will give them some form of attention or assistance. If a priest of Marfa and his wife make a vow to never divorce at the time of their marriage, but then the relationship between the two turns sour, the two of them may end up having a child together. No vow can be made that violates your faith in your god. For example, a priest of Myrii can make a vow to defeat the traitor XX with my own hands , but a priest of Marfa cannot .

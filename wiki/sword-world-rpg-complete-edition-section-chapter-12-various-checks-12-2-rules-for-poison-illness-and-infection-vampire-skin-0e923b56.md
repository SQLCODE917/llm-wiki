- This is an illness in which the power of dark spirits becomes unnecessarily strong, causing a hypersensitivity to light. At depth 1, painful blisters resembling burns accompany areas exposed to sunlight. At depth 2, vision is weakened and most success rolls suffer a -2 penalty.

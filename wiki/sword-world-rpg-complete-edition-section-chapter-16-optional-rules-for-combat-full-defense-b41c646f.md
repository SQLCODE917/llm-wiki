## Statements

- When an opponent is too strong, and fighting normally is of no use, an adventurer can **focus wholly on defense** . When focusing on defense, an adventurer _cannot_ attack or use any magic during that round. In return, you’ll gain a +2 bonus to your _evasion speed_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07143))_

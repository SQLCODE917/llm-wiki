## Statements

### 3.1.1 Order of Action Announcements

- In an action announcement, you cannot declare something impossible. Casting a spell while running at full speed is prohibited by the rules. If there's an enemy in front of you that's attacking you, attacking another enemy several meters away is not possible, just by common sense. These impossible actions cannot be declared, as a matter of course. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00627))_

### 5.1.3 Learning Magic

- Magic is not omnipotent. It is only possible to achieve a defined and limited set of effects by weaving certain words (in runes), called spells . There are several types of spells, each with different effects. Depending on the strength of their effects, spells are divided into levels from 1-10 . 1st level spells are very rudimentary, while 10th level spells are very powerful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01092))_

### Table 5-1: Area and Size Expansion

- When you cast a spell by consuming that multiplied amount of mental power, multiply the number in area expansion length or volume expansion length by the number (radius, side) that represents the spell's area of effect , and the result will be the spell's area . For spells cast on a flat surface, use the number in the area expansion length column, and for spells cast into space, use the number in the volume expansion length column. For example, when you cast the ancient magic Light , whose area is A space with a 10 meter radius , by consuming double the mental power, the resulting area will be a spherical space with a radius of 10 x 1.3 = 13 meters . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01239))_

### 《 Damage 》

- A spell that deals damage to the target's life force or mental power. Spells of the damage type always have a clearly defined strike power . If you manage to break through the target's resist, use the spell's strike power to determine damage by making a strike roll using the Rating Table, same as if you had hit with a weapon in weapon combat. The key number is, of course, the indicated strike power . If you roll 10 or greater during the strike roll, a critical will occur and deal even more damage, same as in weapon combat. Conversely, If you roll double ones, it does not deal any damage at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01284))_

### 《 Illusion 》

- A spell that deceives the five senses of the target. These spells deceive the opponent's sensory organs and give them false sensations. There are three types of senses through which monsters perceive their surroundings: fi ve senses , pseudo senses, and magic senses. Magic of the illusion type is effective on those that perceive their surroundings with fi ve senses or pseudo senses. Humans and fae perceive their surroundings through fi ve senses , so they can all be targeted by illusions . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01345))_

- The effects of spells of the illusion type can be detected by a resistance roll. However, in order to do this, you must first be suspicious of the illusion. The game master should decide in which cases it seems suspicious. In the case of player characters (adventurers), this may be when the players themselves suspect an illusion. For NPCs and monsters, the game master should decide based on their intelligence. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01347))_

### 《 Lost 》

- The game master gets to determine which spells are considered lost spells. It's acceptable to have all spells on the magic list be lost, or conversely, to have all magic be known and no spells be lost. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01363))_

- If a spell says lost under its magic details, it may be easier to maintain balance when enjoying the game if you consider it a lost spell. Unless the game master has a particular reason, please consider these to be lost spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01364))_

### 《 Distance 》

- This represents the distance the magic can reach. Basically, magic works on things that the caster can see and are within this distance. Any exceptions will always be noted in the explanation that follows. If the distance is touch , the caster must touch the target with their hand or (for ancient magic) with their magical catalyst (see below). To cast these spells in combat, you must be engaged with the target. At this time, there is no need to roll dice to determine whether you've touched your opponent, like with an attack hit check (you can touch them automatically). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01376))_

### 《 Resist 》

- Reduced Effect : If resisted, the spell only takes an incomplete effect. Spells of the damage type will have their strike power reduced by -10 and can no longer critical. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01395))_

### 5.1.11 Ancient Magic and the Sorcerer Skill

- High ancient are the great runes that were developed and studied during the time of the ancient kingdom. The power of these runes releases magic forces and brings about various effects. Many of the spells and runes of this system were lost with the fall of the ancient kingdom, but a small amount of them have been passed down to modern Forcelia, and are now referred to as ancient magic . The skill for using ancient magic is called the sorcerer skill , and those who have this skill are called sorcerers . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01400))_

### 《 Spellbook 》

- For this reason, it is customary for sorcerers to write these spells down in a book called a spellbook . A spellbook is, so to speak, just a handwritten notebook, and the spellbook itself has no magical power of its own. However, because it's a very important thing, it would be a disaster if it were lost or stolen, so some sorcerers may keep several copies in reserve. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01419))_

- When you go on an adventure, or you're in a situation where you'd need to use magic, you'd usually bring your spellbook with you. However, no sorcerer looks at their spellbook every time they try to cast a spell. This is because the words and actions of a spell are usually sharply memorized in your head. A spellbook is used to review them, always confirming and ensuring your memory. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01420))_

### [ Magic Protection ]

- This spell has the effect of giving magic resistance to objects that do not have it. If the targeted object is exposed to another spell that's not detection type, it will resist that spell unless the opponent succeeds on a success roll against a target score equal to the final score from when Magic Protection was cast.

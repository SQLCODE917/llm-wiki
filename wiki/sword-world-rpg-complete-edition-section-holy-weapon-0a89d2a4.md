# [ Holy Weapon ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a spell that applies a negative force against undead to a weapon, rather than to space like Turn Undead . As a result of this, you can add 10 to the strike power of a normal weapon when making a damage check against undead. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04415))_
- This additional strike power does not apply to anything other than undead. It also has no effect on (non-undead) enemies that can only be wounded by magical weapons, such as werewolves, etc. For undead that can only be wounded by magic weapons, strike power will be 10 regardless of the strike power of the weapon itself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04416))_
- For undead that can only be wounded by magic weapons, strike power will be 10 regardless of the strike power of the weapon itself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04416))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / Phalaris Special Dark Magic List

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / The Nameless God of Madness Special Dark Magic List

- This spell has the effect of giving a strong shock to the opponent's mind, with the power to ruin their peace of mind and cause them to lose their sanity. Targets who lose their sanity due to this spell go into bizarre states such as staring vacantly in bewilderment, yelling nonsense, or curling up and sobbing, and become completely unable to take logical actions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03922))_
- Most of the time, they will only be rendered useless, but only when they roll 2D again and the result is double ones, they will go berserk with madness and engage in destructive acts. In this case, they will be in the same state as when _Berserk_ is cast, described below. Please refer to _Berserk_ for handling. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03923))_
- Those who have this spell cast on them and fail to resist will go berserk and feel an urge to destroy everything nearby. Even the caster is not safe from the target. As a result, they will destroy property within sight and blindly attack creatures, regardless of friend or foe. At this time, their attack power and bonus damage will be +2, and their evasion speed will be -4. They also become indifferent to wounds and pain and ignore the risks of injury or life. Also, if no one is around, they will wound themselves. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03929))_
- While under the influence of this spell, you will not fall unconscious even if your life force (points) drops to 0 or less. However, a death check must be made. The first time it becomes 0 or less, and each time you receive a new wound, you must make a death check, and if you fail, you will die. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03930))_
- Targets who lose their sanity due to this spell go into bizarre states such as staring vacantly in bewilderment, yelling nonsense, or curling up and sobbing, and become completely unable to take logical actions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03922))_

- As a result, they will destroy property within sight and blindly attack creatures, regardless of friend or foe. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03929))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / [ Lunatic Seed ] (Spell Level=4)

- This is a spell that plants the seeds of madness deep within the target's mind. Those who've had this spell cast on them will be unable to disobey the caster's commands. However, the caster's commands must be something _erratic_ , so to speak. Rational commands will be ignored. They have to be irrational, based on insanity. Those under the spell's influence will be unable to act intelligently, even without the caster's commands. This spell's effect lasts until it is negated (as it is a curse, it cannot be removed by other magic belonging to _mind_ ) or until the caster renounces it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03935))_
- This is a spell that plants the seeds of madness deep within the target's mind. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03935))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / Distance=Caster

- This magic does not require the spell to be chanted. Instead, you must dance with complex gestures. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03942))_
- Those who see this dance and fail a resistance roll will go insane and begin dancing in response to the caster's dance. Even if you go insane, you won't go berserk, but you will no longer be able to act rationally. This madness lasts for an additional 12 hours even if the caster stops dancing. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03943))_
- This magic can extend the amount of time the target remains insane after the dance ends, if the caster consumes extra mental power to expand the effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03944))_
- **Kardis Special Dark Magic List [ Death ] (Spell Level=9)** Base Mental Power Cost=45 Distance=10 meters Area=One Duration=Instant Effect=Instantly kill the target Type=Expansion=Final score, targets Resist=Negated effect _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03945))_
- A terrifying death spell created by the power of the goddess of death, Kardis. A target who has this spell cast on them and fails a resistance roll will die instantly. It has no effect on things that do not have normal life, such as undead, magical creatures etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03946))_
- This magic can extend the amount of time the target remains insane after the dance ends, if the caster consumes extra mental power to expand the effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03944))_
- It has no effect on things that do not have normal life, such as undead, magical creatures etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03946))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / [ Create Ghost Ship ] (Spell Level=6)

- When this spell is cast on a ship, it will never sink, no matter how much it rots, as long as it only carries as much weight as the original ship could support. However, this does _not_ stop the ship itself from decaying, so over many years it will rot to pieces. This spell is effective even on ships that have already sunk or are shipwrecked and partially destroyed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03951))_
- The priests of Mirleaf often load zombie and skeleton sailors onto the ships they create, and use them as ghost ships to wreak havoc on the seas. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03952))_

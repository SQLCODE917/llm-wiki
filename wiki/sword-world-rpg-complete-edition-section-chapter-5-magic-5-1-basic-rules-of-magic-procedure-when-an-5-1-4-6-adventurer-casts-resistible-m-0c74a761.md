## Statements

- Just like player characters, even those whose data was created for a character may appear as an enemy. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01230))_
- In other words, you compare the _magic power + 2D_ score of the one casting the magic with the _mental power resistance + 2D_ score of the one on whom the magic is being cast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01231))_
- The resolution of _double ones_ and _double sixes_ is also the same as weapon combat. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01232))_
- In other words, if the _resisting side_ rolls double sixes, the resist is an _unconditional success_ , otherwise, if _the side casting the magic_ rolls double sixes, the resist is a _failure_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01232))_

## Technical atoms

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01230))_

> Just like player characters, even those whose data was created for a character may appear as an enemy. If such an enemy casts magic on an adventurer, or if an adventurer casts magic on such an enemy, both sides must roll dice to resolve the situation, just as in weapon combat between adventurers.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01231))_

> If the score of the one casting the magic is greater, the magic will take _full_ effect, but if there is a tie or if the score of the one on whom the magic is being cast is greater, the magic is _resisted_ and will only take an _incomplete_ effect.

### Technical atom 2

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01230))_

> Just like player characters, even those whose data was created for a character may appear as an enemy. If such an enemy casts magic on an adventurer, or if an adventurer casts magic on such an enemy, both sides must roll dice to resolve the situation, just as in weapon combat between adventurers.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01233))_

> When the one casting the magic rolls _double ones_ , the magic takes _no_ effect at all.

### Technical atom 3

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01234))_

> When the resisting side rolls _double ones_ , the resist _fails_ , unless the side casting the magic _also_ rolls double ones.

## Statements

- This is a black liquid poison and is a type of narcotic. Those who fail to resist will lose all will and emotion and become like a doll. If someone pulls their arm, they will stagger, and if someone calls them, they will respond in an emotionless voice, but they cannot do anything of their own will. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04336))_
- Around one additional hour of suggestions can transform the victim into a robot that follows commands. Victims turned into robots faithfully follow the commands of the person who gave them the suggestions, and will casually kill even their family and friends (they _cannot_ use magic). However, if you suffer a very strong physical or mental shock, you may recover from the paralysis even before the duration expires. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04337))_
- **[Dark Blade (Injected/Damage)]** Effect=Become disabled, and suffer strike power 20 damage plus bonus damage 6 every 10 minutes. Resist negates effect Toxicity Score=15 Rarity=10 Effect Start Time= 1 round later Effect Duration=Permanent _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04338))_
- A poison often used by assassins, it is a pitch-black viscous liquid. If you are wounded by a knife coated with this poison, you will suffer damage every 10 minutes thereafter. The victim continues to suffer damage until the poison wears off or they die. During this time, you will be in terrible pain and unable to take any action. If treatment is delayed, you may remain in a coma for several days after the poison wears off. If you succeed on a resistance roll, you will not suffer any damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04339))_
- This is a liquid poison. Those who fail to resist will stop breathing and their heart will stop, making them appear completely dead. However, they’re actually in a state of suspended animation and will return to normal after 12 hours. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04341))_
- A fine powdered poison that requires a resistance roll each time you inhale, resulting in damage if you fail. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04343))_
- However, they’re actually in a state of suspended animation and will return to normal after 12 hours. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04341))_

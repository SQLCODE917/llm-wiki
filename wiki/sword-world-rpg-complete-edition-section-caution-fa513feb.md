## Statements

- It has been noted before that double sixes and double ones are automatic successes or failures and do not imply definitive successes or failures. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00578))_
- It is especially bad to misinterpret an automatic failure as a definitive failure. For example, if a player is trying to find a trap on a door and has rolled double ones, the game master will of course tell the player you didn't find any traps . When the player hears this, if they reply like the following, it's a prime example of this misinterpretation: Oh, I screwed up big time. Which means I can say for sure 'There are definitely no traps on this door'. And yet I'll carelessly open it and get hit. *laughs* _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00579))_

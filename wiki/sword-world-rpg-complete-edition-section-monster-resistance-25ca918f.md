# Monster Resistance

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The baseline score for a monster's mental power resistance roll is resistance points -7 (written in parentheses) . However, a monster's resistance roll fails if there's a tie . The final score must exceed the final score from when the character cast the spell. This is an exception to the rule in order to maintain numerical balance. Please be careful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11660))_

# 《 Not Affected by Poison and Illness 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

## Statements

- Certain mythical beasts, magical beasts, undead powered by negative life, and pseudo-life forms created by magic, are free from poison and illness. These monsters are not affected by poison and illness. Ancient magic that alters the atmosphere ( XX Cloud ) is also ineffective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08040))_

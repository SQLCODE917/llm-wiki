# **(Leprechaun/Mind Spirit)**

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about **(leprechaun/mind spirit)**:

## Statements

- This spell is used by borrowing the power of a leprechaun, but humans and elves cannot use it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02273))_
- At this time, even if an ally casts magic on you in order to help you, you will consider it a _hostile_ act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02249))_
- As a result, you won't be able to take any assertive action, and will be completely unable to attack or use magic on your opponents. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02249))_
- If you have this spell cast on you and you fail to resist, you'll be unable to understand your current situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02249))_
- You'll also lose your ability to make accurate judgments. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02249))_
- Of course, this is not the case if _Confusion_ is removed by that magic's effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02249))_
- You can make similar judgments about those who cast magic on you. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02249))_
- You can also cast _Dispel Magic_ , _Sanity_ , or other magic that belongs to _mind_ on yourself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02250))_
- Also, it doesn't mean you've become stupid, so you won't trust an opponent just because they yell _I'm your friend!_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02250))_
- _Only_ featherfolk (fae) and gremlins (youma) can use it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02273))_
- Among sages, the reason for this is thought to be that the spell doesn't just require a hand, but a _wing_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02273))_
- Those who have this spell cast on them will lose their memory, and be unable to make normal decisions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02274))_
- A gremlin casting this spell on a pegasus carrying the hero Grax, and flinging Grax off of it, is a very well-known tragic episode in Alecrast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02274))_
- You will no longer be able to remember your name, where you were born and raised, or your friends and colleagues, but you will still be able to use language, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02275))_

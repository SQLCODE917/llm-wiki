## Source record

This section details the list of spirit magic spells available in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, categorized by level.

## Key supported claims

- Spirit magic spells can be used to communicate with any spirit, though most spirits speak directly to the mind without using words, and some fae are well versed in silent spirit (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-87).
- These runes used to communicate with spirits must be spoken out loud when giving commands to a spirit, but some fae are well versed in silent spirit (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-87).
- Most spirits speak directly to the mind without using words, but when a caster gives commands to a spirit, they must speak these runes out loud (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-87).
- These spells have a base mental power cost, distance, area, duration, effect, type, expansion, and resist properties (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-87).
- Spirit magic is a form of magic that uses spirit power, which can be sensed as a subtle sensation like smell, and may not be detectable if there's extremely strong spirit power in the surroundings (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-87).

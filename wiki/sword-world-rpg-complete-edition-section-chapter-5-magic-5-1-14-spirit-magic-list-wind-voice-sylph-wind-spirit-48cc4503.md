## Statements

- This spell has the effect of commanding a sylph to deliver air vibrations (sounds) to the caster without weakening them. As a result, you can specify a point in space within 500 meters and hear every conversation and sound exchanged within a radius of 5 meters from that point. Conversely, you can also transmit your voice to the location. Since it can be used to narrow the area of effect, it's also possible to communicate with just a single target. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02188))_
- There must always be a passage of wind between the target and the caster. Sound travels along that path (over the shortest possible distance). Therefore, those in the path can hear the sounds carried by _Wind Voice_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02189))_

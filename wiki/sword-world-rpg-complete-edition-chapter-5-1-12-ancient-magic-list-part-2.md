## **5.1.12** 

## **Ancient Magic List (part 2)**

This chapter details the 4th, 5th, and 6th level ancient magic spells available in the *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition*. Spells are listed with their base mental power cost, distance, area, duration, effect, type, expansion, and resist.

### **Physical Enchant**

Physical Enchant is subdivided into six spells by their effects. They are called Sharpness, Dullness, Quickness, Slowness, Strength, and Weakness, respectively.

#### **[ Sharpness ]**

Increases the target's dexterity by 6. Increases attack power (points) and the baseline score of dexterity-related abilities by 1 point.

#### **[ Dullness ]**

Area=One

Decreases the target's dexterity by 6. Decreases attack power (points) and the baseline score of dexterity-related abilities by 1 point.

Duration=18 rounds

Effect=You can increase the target's dexterity, agility, or strength by 6

Type=Target score, physical ability Expansion=Final score, duration, distance, targets

#### **[ Quickness ]**

Resist=Negated effect

Increases the target's agility by 6. Affects evasion speed (points) and action order.

There are six variations of this spell, each of which increase or decrease a target's dexterity, agility, or strength by 6. However, even if you reduce them, they can never become 0. If the ability score was originally 6 or less, it'll instead become 1. In most cases, this will result

Also increases the baseline score of related abilities.

#### **[ Slowness ]**

Reduces the target's agility by 6. Affects evasion speed (points) and action order.

Also decreases the baseline score of related abilities.

#### **[ Strength ]**

Increases the target's strength by 6. Affects bonus damage (strike points). Also allows you to use weapons that you cannot use with your normal strength.

#### **[ Weakness ]**

Decreases the target's strength by 6. Affects bonus damage (strike points). Also prevents the use of weapons that require more strength than the target currently has.

### **Bone Servant**

#### **[ Bone Servant ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=15

Distance=Touch

Area=One bone

Duration=1 hour

Effect=Create a human-sized bone golem and make it obey your commands

Type=Non-removable

Expansion=Duration, target score

Resist=None

This spell creates a human-shaped bone golem (1 and a half meters tall) from a bone the size of a clenched fist. This simplified version of a bone golem is specifically called a _Bone Servant_ and can be used to engage in combat or simple tasks. A bone servant's power is about 30 if converted to _strength_ , and furthermore, it never gets tired, so it can complete a considerable amount of heavy labor. However, more complex work is beyond a bone servant's hands. Bone servants obey only the commands of their maker, the sorcerer. After the spell's duration expires, the bone servant will turn back into a bone.

See p. 223 for bone servant scores.

Base Mental Power Cost=15

Distance=Caster

Area=Caster

Duration=1 hour

Effect=Changes the caster's appearance

Type=Illusion

Expansion=Final score, duration

Resist=Negated effect

This spell is a type of illusion magic that works on the caster, changing their appearance into something else. However, this spell cannot significantly change the size or shape of your body. So a thin person cannot become an obese person, and a human cannot look like a grassrunner or a dwarf.

#### **[ Sense Aura ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=15

Distance=Caster

Area=Within the caster's view

Duration=18 rounds

Effect=Knows the presence of magical auras

Type=Detection

Expansion=Duration

Resist=None

By casting this spell, a normally invisible magical aura becomes visible to the caster. This lets you know if there are magical items, magical traps, etc. within your field of view. However, it’s not possible to discern exactly what magical powers are at work. You also cannot find things whose form you can't see directly, such as things inside a box.

Distance=30 meters

Area=A space with a 5 meter radius

Duration=1 hour

Type=Illusion

Resist=Negated effect

This is a spell that creates a visual illusion in the space within its area of effect. This illusion does not have to be a static image; it can be something that moves. However, in this case, the movement will repeat on a loop after a maximum of about 5 minutes.

#### **[ Analyze Enchantment ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=15

Distance=Touch

Area=One object

Duration=Instant

Effect=Identifies the type of magic being cast on a target and specifies its effect

Type=Detection

Expansion=Final score

Resist=None

This is a spell that identifies the type of magic being cast on a target and specifies its effect. If it's a regular magical item, you can almost certainly identify the magic. However, some items are safe from the effect of this spell due to the _lost spell_ , _Seal Enchantment_ . When you use this spell on an object that had _Seal Enchantment_ cast on it, its effect cannot be inferred if your final score does not exceed the target score determined by _Seal Enchantment_ . If they fail, the same caster cannot use this spell on the same object until their magic power increases (any attempts will always fail).

#### **[ Geas ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=15

Distance=Touch

Area=One

Duration=Permanent

Effect=Give a target a prohibition command

Type=Curse, target score

Expansion=Final score

Resist=Negated effect

This spell gives the target a " _do not ___ " command and makes them obey it. If the person who had the spell cast on them tries to break this prohibition command, they will suffer severe pain all over their body. The pain is so severe that if you continue to suffer it for more than 10 minutes, you will become mentally abnormal.

The caster can cancel this spell at will, but the spell will not be removed even if the caster dies.

This spell's effect only works on those who have some degree of intelligence. At least, if you're not intelligent enough to use language, this spell cannot be cast on you.

#### **[ Create Undead ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=15

Distance=Touch

Area=One corpse

Duration=Permanent

Effect=Turns a corpse into undead

Type=Non-removable, lost, ritual

Expansion=Target volume

Resist=None

_Create Undead_ is a spell that creates undead monsters such as skeletons and zombies. To use this spell, of course, you'll need a corpse (usually a human corpse is used). Depending on the state of decomposition of the corpse, it will determine whether it'll become a skeleton or a zombie. If you use this spell on a mummified or severely decomposed corpse, the corpse will be completely skeletonized and a skeleton will be born. If it's still a corpse before that happens, then a zombie will be born. The caster can give commands to the skeletons and zombies created in this way. The commands must be extremely simple, at best they can be made to protect a specific location. Even walking alongside you as guards is too difficult for them. For details on human skeletons and zombies, see 13.2.9: Undead (see p. 214).

If you wish, you can turn non-human animals, fae, youma, titans, mythical beasts, magical beasts, etc. into undead, but to cast this on something larger than human size, you must consume additional mental power (points) to expand the effect. The required mental power (points) increases in proportion with the size of the target ( _double_ for an ogre, _seven times_ for a chimera, and over _1000 times_ for a 20-meter long dragon!). On the other hand, turning small animals into undead does not mean that they consume less mental power.

Details for non-human skeletons and zombies should be decided by the game master. A rough guideline is to set life scores to 1/2 for skeletons, and 3/2 for zombies. Other scores may not need to be changed.

#### **[ Create Device ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=15

Distance=Touch

Area=One wooden staff or object

Duration=Permanent

Effect=Can create a magical catalyst

Type=Non-removable

Expansion=Resist=None

This is a spell that creates a mage's staff, which is a magical catalyst necessary for sorcerers to use ancient magic. All you need is a wooden staff of an appropriate length, and by casting this spell on that staff, you'll create a mage's staff. You can also use this spell to create a _magical catalyst_ other than a mage's staff.

#### **[ Create Buau Zombie ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=15

Distance=Touch

Area=One corpse

Duration=Permanent

Effect=Turns a corpse into a buau zombie

Type=Non-removable, lost

Expansion=Resist=None

To cast this spell, you need a corpse within a few days of death, so the corpse can be revived as a buau zombie (see p. 214). The caster can make the buau zombie obey commands. This spell only works on human, fae or youma corpses.

## Statements

- This is the ability to pre-process the prey obtained from hunting so that it can be sold as a product. It’s needed to separate the antlers from the carcasses of animals such as deer, etc. and to strip and tan the hides from prey. You can also use it to take apart and smoke meat. It concerns all other similar acts. If this pre-processing fails, the value of the product will _decrease_ to half of its normal value. For each pre-processing attempt, the game master should set a target score and have the player make a success roll.

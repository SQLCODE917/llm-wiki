Mana Rai is the Grand Master of the Sorcerers' Guild in the Sword World RPG. He is renowned as a Great Scholar and is known for establishing ancient magic as power derived from the magical element "mana." He completed the currently accepted theory of magic and developed common language magic that is activated through the common tongue.

In present-day Alecrast, there exists no Sorcerer or Sage who rivals him, and perhaps none will ever appear. His contributions to the field of magic have made him a pivotal figure in the history of the Sorcerers' Guild and the broader magical community of Alecrast.

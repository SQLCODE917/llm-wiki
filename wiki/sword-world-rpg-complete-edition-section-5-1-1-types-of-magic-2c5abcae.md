# 5.1.1 Types of Magic

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- In Sword World , there is a power called magic that can produce various mysterious effects by chanting words called runes and performing certain procedures at the same time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01078))_
- There are five types of runes in the world of Sword World . This difference in runes is at the same time a difference in the type of magic. The five runes are high ancient , silent spirit , holy prayer , daemon scream , and dragon roar . Each has a different source of magical power and a completely different procedure (although holy prayer and daemon scream are essentially the same power, the only difference being the nature of the magic). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01079))_
- In addition to these runes, there are two others that, although weak, can still manifest magical power. They are called common runes and spellsongs , respectively. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01080))_

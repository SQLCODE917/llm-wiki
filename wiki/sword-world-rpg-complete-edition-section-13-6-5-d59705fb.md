## Statements by subsection

### 《 Mental Attack Immunity 》

- Monsters, etc. that do not have intellect or mind have this ability, so magic with mental effects ( Fear , etc.) is ineffective. If they do not have mental points (expressed as -), magic that deals damage to mental points ( Shade , etc.) is likewise ineffective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09901))_

### 《 Sleepless 》

- They do not require rest or sleep in the usual sense. Therefore, they can never be put to sleep by magic, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09903))_
- Monsters with this ability also automatically recover mental points. Recovery is 1/24th of mental points (rounded up) every hour. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09904))_
- However, vampires cannot automatically recover mental points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09905))_
- Therefore, they can never be put to sleep by magic, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09903))_

### 《 Certain Magic Resistant 》

- Resistant to some magic. This primarily means that damage magic cannot cause criticals. Please refer to each monster's description for further details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09907))_
- This primarily means that damage magic cannot cause criticals. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09907))_

### 《 Fire Immunity 》

- Fire attacks are completely ineffective. Magic that deals damage with fire ( Fire Bolt and Fireball ) is completely useless, and the strike power bonus of Fire Weapon is also not added. If one of these monsters also has the ability normal weapon immunity , it cannot be wounded by a weapon that had Fire Weapon cast on it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09909))_

### 《 Strong to Fire 》

- Resistant to fire attacks, so for magic that deals damage with fire ( Fire Bolt and Fireball ), checks are made at -10 to strike power (the strike power bonus of Fire Weapon is completely ineffective). Even if one of these monsters has the ability normal weapon immunity , it can still be wounded by a weapon that had Fire Weapon cast on it. However, strike power will be 0. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09911))_

### 《 Cold Immunity 》

- Cold-type attacks are completely ineffective. Magic that deals damage with cold ( Ice Storm , etc.) is completely useless, Ice Coffin and Freeze are also ineffective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09914))_

### 《 Strong to Cold 》

- Resistant to cold-type attacks, so for magic that deals damage with cold ( Ice Storm , etc.), checks are made at -10 to strike power. Cold-based magic that does not deal damage ( Ice Coffin and Freeze ) will still take their usual effects. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09916))_
- In addition to these, if it says XX immunity , it' s completely useless, and if it says strong to XX , damage magic checks are made at -10 to strike power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09917))_

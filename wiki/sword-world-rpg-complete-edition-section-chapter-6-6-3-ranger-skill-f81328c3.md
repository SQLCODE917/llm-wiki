- This is the ability to track footprints left on the ground. The game master determines the difficulty by taking into account factors such as the condition of the ground and the amount of time that has passed since the footprints were made. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05011))_

First-aid provided by a ranger will restore your life force to 1 point, allowing you to regain consciousness. It cannot fail, but it will take about 30 minutes of game time, and it cannot be done while in a state where you can't focus, such as in the middle of combat, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05012))_

### Chapter 6: / 6.3 Ranger Skill / [ ③ Camouflage [Intelligence] ] (Equipment restriction)

- This is the ability to hide behind rocks, hide in bushes, or use such things to hide something. Since it's an ability that uses natural terrain, it cannot be used indoors. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05014))_

### Chapter 6: / 6.3 Ranger Skill / [ ④ Listen [Intelligence] ] (Equipment restriction)

- Rangers can listen to even the smallest sounds and can choose the sound they want to hear from a wide range of sounds. This is handed the same way as the thief's ability. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05017))_
- Rangers acquire a good intuition, which can be called a sixth sense. This ability is the ability used to sense when danger is approaching. For example, when you walk through a ravine and see a pebble fall, you may feel like a landslide is about to occur, or you may feel an arrow flying from the darkness. The target score is determined by the game master depending on the situation, but in general it should be set high. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05019))_

### Chapter 6: / 6.3 Ranger Skill / [ ⑥ Stealthy Step [Agility] ] (Equipment restriction)

- Rangers can walk silently and unnoticed. This is handled the same way as the thief skill's ability. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05021))_

### Chapter 6: / 6.3 Ranger Skill / [ ⑦ Survivalism [Life Force] ] (No equipment restriction)

- Rangers have acquired the ability to endure difficult situations brought about by disaster, such as surviving with only a small amount of water or avoiding freezing to death in winter mountains, and either survive until help arrives or escape from a predicament on their own. When an adventurer's survival is in danger, the game master should set a target score and have them make a success roll to overcome the situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05023))_

### Chapter 6: / 6.3 Ranger Skill / [ ⑨ Map Creation [Intelligence]] (No equipment restriction, not retriable)

- Rangers have a good sense of direction and can map out the places they've been. You can also determine direction from the position of the sun or stars. When creating a map, the game master determines a difficulty, and a success roll must be made to determine whether or not the map was drawn correctly. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05028))_

### Chapter 6: / 6.3 Ranger Skill / [ ⑩ Weather Prediction [Intelligence] ] (No equipment restriction, not retriable)

- Rangers have the ability to predict the weather. Rangers can measure the humidity using their own senses, and they also use small knowledge such as birds that eat small insects such as flies fly close to the ground = no updraft = thick clouds = rain . From these combinations, you can roughly predict the weather. If a ranger succeeds on a success roll against a set target score using ranger skill level + intelligence bonus as the baseline score, the ranger can roughly predict the weather (sunny, cloudy, rain, snow, storm, strong wind...... etc.) The game master must decide whether to use a difficulty or a target score. Normally you would use a target score. This is because failure means that the weather is unpredictable, and this is also very clear to the characters as well. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05030))_

### Chapter 6: / 6.3 Ranger Skill / [ ⑫ Projectile Use [Dexterity, Strength] ] (No equipment restriction)

- The ranger skill is treated the same as the fighter skill (attack power, bonus damage modifiers) only when attacking with projectiles. You can use any projectile as long as your strength allows it. The ranger skill is not relevant to the use of or defense from melee weapons. Adventurers attack with the ranger skill and defend with the fighter or thief skill. When using the thief skill for defense, you must be lightly armed (required strength of weapons and armor must be half your own strength or less). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05036))_

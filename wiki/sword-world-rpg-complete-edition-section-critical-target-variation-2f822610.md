# **Critical Target Variation**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- **Critical target variation** indicates that the armor _reduces_ the chance of being dealt a strong blow from some weapons while wearing it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07742))_
- If this field says _yes_ , the _opponent's_ critical target will be +1 when they attack you with a sword, spear, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07742))_
- **Anti-Strangling Protection** The _anti-strangling protection_ indicates whether the armor protects you when being strangled by a garrote or arms around the neck. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07745))_

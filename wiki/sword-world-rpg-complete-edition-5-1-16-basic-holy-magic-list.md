## Source record

Summary of the Basic Holy Magic List section from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering spells available to priests without exception.

## Key supported claims

- The spells listed here are available to characters with the priest (dark priest) skill without exception (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.99-103).
- You can add your own magic power to the result of the Cure Wounds spell (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.99-103).
- The spell Transfer Mental Power cannot transfer more mental power than the target's starting mental power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.99-103).
- The spell Force deals damage to undead only if the undead fails to resist (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.99-103).
- The spell Holy Light can be used on a target that is undead, and if it fails to resist, it will deal damage to the undead (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.101-103).

## Statements

- This is a spell that heals the body by borrowing the power of an unknown spirit of life. When you cast this spell, it can completely heal any wound instantly, but it cannot regenerate a severed arm or leg. Also, although this spell can only be cast _by_ women, you _can_ choose a man as the _target_ of the spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02339))_
- This spell can deal damage to undead. Undead that fail to resist this spell will immediately have their life points become 0 and cease all activity. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02340))_

- The imp looks very similar to the gargoyle (see p. 225) and its model, the zalbard (see p. 228). For this reason, some sages argue that imps are not youma at all, but were originally underworld creatures. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04722))_

### Chapter 13: Monsters! / 13.2 Monster Catalog / 13.2.3 Youma / Habitat=Swamps

- Languages=Impish, regional Perception=Five senses (infravision) Marshmen, also known as water imps, are youma that live in swamps. Their bodies are covered with green skin, and their backs are hard and shellshaped. They have high intelligence comparable to humans, and also have a rudimentary social life. They are exclusionary toward other races and react aggressively to anything that enters their habitat. They seem to have a deep connection with water spirits, and use undine-type spirit magic up to 4th level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04753))_
- They're weak against fire and heat, so when attacking a marshman with firebased magic, add +10 to strike power when making the check. In addition, they are susceptible to dehydration and are nearly unable to leave the swamps where they live. If they're forced to leave and their body becomes dehydrated, they'll lose life points at the rate of 1 point every hour. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04754))_

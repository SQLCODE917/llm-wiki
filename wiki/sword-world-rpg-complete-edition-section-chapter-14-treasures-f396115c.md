### Chapter 14: Treasures / Negotiations With Adventurers

- For a party consisting of three 2nd level characters and two 3rd level characters, the estimated reward is around (2 x 2 x 3 + 3 x 3 x 2) x 50~200 = 1500~6000 gamels total. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06357))_
- Please consider the estimated reward for asking an adventurer to resolve a case to be around 50~200 times the (total of) each adventurer level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06357))_
- Even if there’s variation in level between the party, please make sure the rewards presented are equal per person. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06358))_
- Rune masters tend to be lower level, but that doesn’t mean they’re any less useful than other characters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06358))_
- In the above example, the reward presented would be 300~1200 gamels per person. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06358))_
- A 3rd level character would not be presented 450~1800 gamels nor would a 2nd level character be presented 200~800 gamels. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06358))_
- Such behavior would result in lost trust and likely void the agreement. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06359))_
- Of course, this does not mean that the reward will increase if the adventurer intentionally delays the resolution. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06359))_
- This reward is basically for a case that’s expected to be resolved in one day, or 3~4 days at most. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06359))_
- If it’s shorter, there’ll be less of a reward, and if it’s longer, there’ll be more. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06359))_
- In these contracts, 10~30% is given upfront as an advance payment. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06360))_
- If you fail to resolve the case, you will not be paid the remaining money. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06360))_

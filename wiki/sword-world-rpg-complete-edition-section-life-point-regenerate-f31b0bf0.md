## Statements

- Has the powerful ability to regenerate life points. The indicated number of life points will automatically regenerate each round. Once life points reach 0, handling differs depending on each monster. Most die as is, but some are special, such as vampires. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08036))_

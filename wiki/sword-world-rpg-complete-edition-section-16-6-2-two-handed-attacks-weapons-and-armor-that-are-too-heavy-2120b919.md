## Statements

- A Character can also use weapons and armor whose required strength exceeds his or her own strength. However, at this time, you'll suffer a penalty to your attack power or evasion speed . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11561))_
- For each point of required strength, reduce attack power by -1. If one in one hand is too heavy, you will receive a penalty, but there will be no stacking. For example, if your sword is 3 too heavy in required strength and your shield is 2 too heavy in required strength, your penalty is -3 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11563))_
- For each point of required strength, reduce evasion speed by 1. At the game master's decision, any success roll that uses agility bonus as a baseline score may be subject to an equal penalty _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11565))_
- For example, if your sword is 3 too heavy in required strength and your shield is 2 too heavy in required strength, your penalty is -3 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11563))_

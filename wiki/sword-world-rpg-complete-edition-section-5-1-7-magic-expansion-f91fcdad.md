# 5.1.7 Magic Expansion

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- You can increase the effectiveness and certainty of magic by consuming extra mental power. This is called magic expansion . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01216))_
- Magic expansion is done by doubling or tripling...... the amount of mental power consumed. At this time, you do not double or triple the base mental power cost....... Instead, you divide the mental power by your skill level, rounded up , or in other words, the mental power you would consume when not expanding is doubled, tripled...... etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01217))_

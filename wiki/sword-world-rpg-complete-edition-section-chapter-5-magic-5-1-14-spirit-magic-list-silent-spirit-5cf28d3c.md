## Statements

- These are runes used to communicate with spirits. Those who can use these runes can communicate with any spirit. Most spirits speak directly to the mind without using words, but when a caster gives commands to a spirit, they must speak these runes out loud. Also, some fae are well versed in silent spirit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02178))_

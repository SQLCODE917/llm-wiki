- We have looked at generators as ways of making iterators over static collections, where state is modelled implicitly in control flow. But as we see here, it's also possible to use a generator interactively, passing values in and receiving a value in return, just like an ordinary function. _(javascriptallonge.pdf (source-range-31a4cf47-01946))_

## Source record

This section covers several spirit magic spells, including Protective Light, Plant Shell, Full Control Spirit, Ice Coffin, Ice Storm, Current, Suffocate, Nightmare, Firestorm, Maze Woods, and Earthquake.

## Key supported claims

- Protective Light creates a glowing light that absorbs up to 50 points of damage, protecting the target from weapons or magic, and can only be used by men (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95).
- Plant Shell summons a dome of plants to create a temporary shelter, offering protection from elements and wild beasts (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95).
- Full Control Spirit allows a caster to summon and control lesser spirits completely, overriding their will, requiring a three-hour ritual (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95).
- Ice Coffin traps an opponent in a pillar of ice, causing them to enter a state of suspended animation (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95).
- Ice Storm creates a freezing ice storm that deals cold-type damage to targets within a 10-meter radius (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95).

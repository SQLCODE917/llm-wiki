Summary of the spirit magic list part 3 from Sword World RPG – Complete Edition.

## Key supported claims

- Full Control Spirit mirrors Control Spirit: a three‑hour ritual, one spirit type, can override will—may cause disappearance. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- Caster moves freely and may free any identified object from the spell’s effects. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- Resistance roll may be made only once per round in which the spell is cast. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- If a spirit acts contrary to its nature, it may disappear after the command. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- Protective light absorbs up to 50 damage; excess damage is not transferred and the protection ends. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)

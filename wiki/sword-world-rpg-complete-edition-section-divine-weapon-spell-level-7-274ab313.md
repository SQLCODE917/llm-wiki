# **[ Divine Weapon ] (Spell Level=7)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This _weapon_ can also take effect against monsters that can only be wounded by silver or magic weapons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03112))_
- When you cast this spell, a vaguely glowing weapon-like force field appears in the caster's hand. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03112))_
- Also, it's not possible to give this _weapon_ to someone else. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03112))_
- This spell has no effect if the caster already has something else in their hand. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03112))_
- There is no required strength, and its strike power is treated as 30 (if the caster uses it as a twohanded weapon, its strike power is _35_ ). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03112))_
- with this spell, arrows are automatically generated as well. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03112))_
- This _weapon_ can also take effect against monsters that can only be wounded by silver or magic weapons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03112))_

## Source record

Chapter 16.1-16.3 of Sword World RPG covers combat mechanics where monsters roll dice, weapon and armor modifiers, and projectile bonus damage.

## Key supported claims

- Projectile weapons have limits on firing frequency based on preparation time and weapon type (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.263-263).
- Armor modifiers affect evasion speed, defense power, and critical target changes, with metal armor reducing critical chances (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.263-263).
- Projectile bonus damage is calculated as skill level plus (bow's required strength divided by 6, rounded down) (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.263-263).
- GMs can control dice results in emergencies to aid storytelling, and double ones or sixes do not automatically result in failure/success for characters (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.261-261).
- Weapon modifiers quantify differences in weapons and armor, with attack and strike power modifiers affecting combat outcomes (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.262-262).

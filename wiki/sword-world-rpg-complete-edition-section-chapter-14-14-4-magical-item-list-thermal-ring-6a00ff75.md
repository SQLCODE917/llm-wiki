## Statements

- This ring has the effect of keeping the wearer's body temperature constant. The wearer of the ring can feel comfortable even when exposed to the fierce heat of a desert or the cold weather of a snowy mountain. However, it's not effective against too high or low temperatures or sudden changes in temperature, so it has absolutely no effect against fire or cold produced by magic . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10824))_

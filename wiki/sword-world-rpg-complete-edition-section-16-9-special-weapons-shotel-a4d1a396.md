# 16.9 Special Weapons / Shotel

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-16-9-special-weapons-1b270e2c]] - broader source section

## Statements

- This weapon has a large S-shaped curve at the tip of the blade. Therefore, you can attack **through** an enemy's shield. When attacking a shield-wielding enemy with a shotel, you can _ignore_ the shield's evasion bonus (even if it's a _magic_ shield!). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07328))_
- weapon, _nor_ can you make _two_ attacks, it _can_ be useful when your weapon is knocked away. However, it becomes a _hindrance_ when dealing with ancient magic and spirit magic. It’ll also be a hindrance to the thief skill's _lockpicking_ and _remove trap_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07329))_
- roll. The target score is the final score from _when the attack hit_ . If he or she fails, they’ll fall _unconscious_ and will not wake up for _2D x 10 minutes_ . Normal damage is dealt regardless of success or failure. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07330))_
- The attacked opponent gains a +4 bonus to his or her resistance roll if he or she is wearing _metal armor._ _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07331))_

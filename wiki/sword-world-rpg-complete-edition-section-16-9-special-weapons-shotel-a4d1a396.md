- However, it becomes a _hindrance_ when dealing with ancient magic and spirit magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07329))_
- It’ll also be a hindrance to the thief skill's _lockpicking_ and _remove trap_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07329))_
- The target score is the final score from _when the attack hit_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07330))_

- The attacked opponent gains a +4 bonus to his or her resistance roll if he or she is wearing _metal armor._ _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07331))_

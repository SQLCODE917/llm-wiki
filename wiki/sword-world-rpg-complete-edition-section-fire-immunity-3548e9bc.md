# 《 **Fire Immunity** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Fire attacks are completely ineffective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06883))_
- Magic that deals damage with fire ( _Fire Bolt_ and _Fireball_ ) is completely useless, and the strike power bonus of _Fire Weapon_ is also not added. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06883))_

## Technical atoms

> If one of these monsters also has the ability _normal weapon immunity_ , it cannot be wounded by a weapon that had _Fire Weapon_ cast on it.
_(source: Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06883))_

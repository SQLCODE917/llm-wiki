# **[ Staff of Sorcery ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- When the staff controls its user, it will attempt to cunningly and ruthlessly slaughter people, targeting magic users first. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07342))_
- If they fail, they become controlled by the staff. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07342))_
- However, for each spell cast, they must make a _mental power resistance_ roll with a target score of 16. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07342))_
- If the staff is released for more than 1 day, the _target score_ returns to its original value. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07342))_
- If consciousness is taken away through magic or if the staff is wrested away, those controlled by the staff will return to normal. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07343))_

# **[ Falling Control ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- You also cannot fall faster than _twice_ your normal falling speed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01641))_
- A target with this spell cast on them can fall at any speed they desire, ignoring their normal falling speed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01641))_
- The falling speed can be infinitely close to 0, but it cannot be _completely_ 0, nor can it infinitely _increase_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01641))_
- Also, if the target on whom the spell is cast has lost consciousness, is unconscious, or is an animate object and has no mind, the caster can instead control the target's falling speed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01642))_

## Source record

This section details various types of undead creatures found in the Sword World RPG, including their characteristics, abilities, and behaviors. Some details are unknown.

## Key supported claims

- Dullahan appears as headless knight with sword and head, rides chariot pulled by headless horse (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.218-221).
- Dullahan can only be wounded by silver or magical weapons, summons chariot and headless horses (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.218-221).
- Undead knight is ghost of knight in rusty armor, wields large sword, immune to ordinary weapons (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.218-221).
- Wraith is ethereal being separated from physical body by ancient magic, unaffected by physical attacks (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.218-221).
- Mummy is monster transformed by ancient ritual, weak to fire attacks (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.218-221).

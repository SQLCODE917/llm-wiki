- For a success roll, the baseline score is usually calculated as _relevant skill level + ability bonus_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00661))_
- For actions that _anyone_ would be able to perform if they became proficient in _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00662))_
- adventuring, the baseline score for a success roll is _adventurer level + ability bonus_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00663))_

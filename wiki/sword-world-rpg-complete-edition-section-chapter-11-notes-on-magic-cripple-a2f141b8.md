## Statements

- This spell has the effect of taking away freedom of a part of the target's body, such as a limb, etc. Those who’ve had this spell cast on them will suffer various penalties depending on the body part. If you lose freedom of your arms, you won't be able to hold anything in those arms, and if you lose freedom of your legs, you will have difficulty moving, so you will suffer a -2 penalty on attack and evasion in combat, respectively. If freedom of your head is taken away, you will be unable to see, hear, speak, or smell. As a result, it’ll be difficult to willingly take action. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03867))_

## Statements

- This weapon has a large S-shaped curve at the tip of the blade. Therefore, you can attack through an enemy's shield. When attacking a shield-wielding enemy with a shotel, you can ignore the shield's evasion bonus (even if it's a magic shield!). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11634))_

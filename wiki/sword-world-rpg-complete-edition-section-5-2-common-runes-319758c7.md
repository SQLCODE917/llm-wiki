- Common runes, like other magic, consume mental power when used. The amount of mental power consumed is determined by each common rune. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04804))_
- Common runes can be used by anyone who knows the password. Also, even if you can't use your hands or are wearing metal armor, you can use them as long as you can say the word. However, no matter how high your rune master skill is, you cannot reduce the amount of mental power consumed. When using common runes, if magic power is required for a game procedure, treat it as 0 regardless of the user. Common runes can also be expanded by consuming extra mental power. However, increasing the final score is the one thing you cannot do. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04805))_

# 16.6.2 Two-Handed Attacks

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- If a character has weapons in both hands, he or she has three options for attacking. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09019))_
- Attack with both hands at the same time = -2 penalty to attack with dominant hand, -4 penalty to attack with opposite hand. Also, the required strength for one of the weapons is limited to half your own strength (rounded up) . If you're fighting with the thief skill, the required strength for both weapons is only half the character's strength. There's no need to reduce one to a quarter. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09022))_
- When a character is ambidextrous, there is no opposite hand. If you attack with only one hand, there is no penalty, and if you attack with both hands, the penalty is only -2 for each. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09023))_

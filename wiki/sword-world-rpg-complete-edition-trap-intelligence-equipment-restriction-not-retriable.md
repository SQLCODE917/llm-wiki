# **[** ⑯ **Find/Sense Trap [Intelligence]] (No equipment restriction, not retriable)**

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about **[** ⑯ **find/sense trap [intelligence]] (no equipment restriction, not retriable)**:

## Statements

- Traps that have been successfully _sensed_ can always be found by attempting to _find_ them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03206))_
- Indoor mechanical traps cannot be _found/sensed_ with the ranger skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03263))_
- This is the ability to sense and find traps set indoors, such as doors, hallways, boxes, drawers, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03204))_
- The **find** ability is used by the thief to actively identify a location and determine if a trap is set there. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03205))_
- Never say _no_ , even when there are no traps. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03205))_
- This is determined by making a success roll just before the trap is about to go off. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03206))_
- **Sense** is the ability to sense the presence of a trap based on past experience and knowledge without actively taking any action. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03206))_
- This is basically the same ability as that of the thief skill, but it _only_ applies to traps that use outdoor nature. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03263))_
- This check (success roll) is based on _difficulty_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03205))_
- For _sense_ , make the difficulty the same as _find_ , but impose a -4 modifier on the player side. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03206))_
- This includes those that are made by tying grass into a ring, or are hung from a tree using string, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03263))_

## Technical atoms

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03205))_

> The **find** ability is used by the thief to actively identify a location and determine if a trap is set there. This check (success roll) is based on _difficulty_ . In the case of a success, please accurately tell them of the presence of the trap and the difficulty to disarm it, and in the case of a failure, or if a trap is not present in the first place, make sure to tell them that it cannot be found. Never say _no_ , even when there are no traps.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03206))_

> If successful, the presence of a trap will be sensed at a perilous point, and the game master must inform the player of the type of trap.

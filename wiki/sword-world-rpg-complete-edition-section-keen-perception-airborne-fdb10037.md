# 《 **Keen Perception/Airborne** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- They have keen perception and react very well to approaching weapons, etc., or can move quickly through the air. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06863))_
- Therefore, they're difficult to hit with a weapon, so when you attack with a weapon, you’ll suffer a -2 penalty to attack power (points). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06863))_

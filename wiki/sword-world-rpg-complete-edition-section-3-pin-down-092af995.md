# 3. Pin Down

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Use your strength and weight to **pin down** your opponent. To resolve this, the one attempting to pin down must make a success roll using _fighter/thief skill level + strength bonus_ as the baseline score, while the one resisting must make one using _adventurer level + strength bonus_ . If the pinner’s is greater, the pin is successful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07191))_
- Once pinning, the pinner will automatically continue in that state and can perform normal attacks (of course, weapons are restricted). However, the one being pinned can attempt to break free. At this time, please make the same check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07192))_

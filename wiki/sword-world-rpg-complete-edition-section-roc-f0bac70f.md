From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The most feared beast second only to the dragon, the roc is a gigantic bird resembling an eagle, with a wingspan of 20 meters. Once every few years, they may be swept close to the continent by the wind, and are the subject of gossip among sailors. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06039))_
- A roc's talons alone are over 1 meter long, and it can lift and carry humansized animals with one foot, or a cow or horse with both feet. Those hit by a roc's talon attacks will be grabbed by the talons, lifted into the air, and carried to its nest. Besides roc infants, its nest is said to be full of treasure. Like the griffon, it may have a habit of collecting shiny objects. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06040))_
- Rock eggs can measure up to 2 meters in diameter, making even newborn infants larger than humans. Here is the data of an infant for your reference. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06041))_

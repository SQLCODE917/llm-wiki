# Prelude: Values and Expressions over Coffee

## Beginning with the Basics

The introductory material in this section is considered basic, following the common approach of starting at the beginning of a story. _(raw/javascriptallonge.pdf (p. 18))_ 

## A Coffee Shop Analogy

The section uses a coffee shop visit as a metaphor to explain how JavaScript works. _(raw/javascriptallonge.pdf (p. 18))_ 

In this analogy, the coffee shop makes a variety of drinks, ranging from espresso to complex flavored syrups and milks. _(raw/javascriptallonge.pdf (p. 18))_ 

## Expressions and Values

JavaScript's environment operates like a coffee shop where expressions are given and values are returned. _(raw/javascriptallonge.pdf (p. 18))_ 

The process mirrors ordering a drink and receiving the prepared beverage in return. _(raw/javascriptallonge.pdf (p. 18))_

# 13.2.9 Undead / Chariot of Dullahan

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Rarity=14 Agility=16 Movement Speed=30/30 (air) Number=One Frequency=Rare Intellect=None Reaction=Adversarial Attack Points=Ram:14 (7) Strike Points=15 Evasion Points=13 (6) Defense Points=8 Life Points/Resistance=30/15 (8) Mental Points/Resistance=-/12 (5) Special Abilities=Normal weapon immunity _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05727))_
- Not affected by poison and illness Habitat=Unknown, appears with (or summoned by) dullahan Languages=None Perception=None _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05729))_
- A chariot ridden by a dullahan, which has the ability to fly. The chariot can also attack while moving up to a maximum of 90 meters in one round. Also, it can _hit-and-run_ different opposing characters again and again. A dullahan riding and controlling a chariot cannot attack on its own at the same time (because it’s pulling the reins with one hand and holding its head in the other). Headless horses also cannot attack while pulling a chariot. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05730))_
- A chariot can only be dealt damage by silver or magical weapons. Furthermore, additional critical damage cannot be dealt by _swords_ , _spears_ , _clubs_ , _bows_ , _crossbows_ , or _barehanded (with magic cast)_ . Criticals can occur as usual with _axes_ , _maces_ , _flails_ , _slings_ , _rocks_ , and _warhammers_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05731))_
- In the case of magic, _cold-type_ and _poison gas-type_ magic is completely ineffective, but other damage magic are effective. _Turn Undead_ has no effect. Also, healing magic cannot deal damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05732))_
- To attack a dullahan aboard a chariot, you must suffer a -2 penalty to attack power. You can attack a headless horse that is pulling a chariot normally. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05733))_
- When its master dullahan is defeated, or when any one of the headless horses pulling it are defeated, the chariot loses the ability to act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05734))_
- A dullahan riding and controlling a chariot cannot attack on its own at the same time (because it’s pulling the reins with one hand and holding its head in the other). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05730))_
- A chariot can only be dealt damage by silver or magical weapons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05731))_

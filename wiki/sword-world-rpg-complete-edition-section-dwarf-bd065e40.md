## Statements

- Dwarves were once members of the fae tribe who lived in the fairy world, but now they have fully immersed into the world inhabited by humans (which we call the material world) and no longer have the ability to travel to and from the fairy world. Their lives are no different from those of humans. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00201))_
- They are about 120 cm tall, round and plump, and have bulbous noses. Men often have long beards on their chins, while women do not. Their hair ranges from brown to dark brown and their eyes are a similar color. Their skin color is dark, almost brown. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00202))_
- They can see things in the dark as well as they can in daylight. They are also very dexterous with their hands, and excel in such techniques as craftsmanship and blacksmithing. Many of their works are stunning and cannot be imitated by humans. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00203))_
- Dwarves usually live in human cities and villages using their own skills, but there are several cities and villages where only dwarves live. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00204))_
- They have a very strong sense of tribalism, respecting the rules of their tribe and seldom violating them. They mainly worship the gods of the earth, with Braki as their main deity, who is also the god of smithing. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00205))_
- The life span of a dwarf is longer than that of a human, thought to be around 200 years. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00206))_

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The baseline score for a monster's mental power resistance roll is _resistance points -7 (written in parentheses)_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07990))_
- However, a monster's resistance roll _fails_ if there’s a _tie_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07990))_

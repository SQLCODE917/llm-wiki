## Statements

- You can move something in your right hand to your left hand, or hold something you had in one hand with both hands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01362))_
- Some weapons can be held in either one or both hands. If you have such a weapon in one hand and a shield in the other, if you drop the shield, the weapon is now considered to be in only one hand, so you can then hold it with both hands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01363))_

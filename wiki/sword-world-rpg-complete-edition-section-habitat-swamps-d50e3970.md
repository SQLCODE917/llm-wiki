# Habitat=Swamps

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Their bodies are covered with green skin, and their backs are hard and shellshaped. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04753))_
- They seem to have a deep connection with water spirits, and use undine-type spirit magic up to 4th level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04753))_
- They have high intelligence comparable to humans, and also have a rudimentary social life. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04753))_
- Languages=Impish, regional Perception=Five senses (infravision) Marshmen, also known as water imps, are youma that live in swamps. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04753))_
- They are exclusionary toward other races and react aggressively to anything that enters their habitat. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04753))_
- In addition, they are susceptible to dehydration and are nearly unable to leave the swamps where they live. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04754))_
- If they're forced to leave and their body becomes dehydrated, they'll lose life points at the rate of 1 point every hour. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04754))_

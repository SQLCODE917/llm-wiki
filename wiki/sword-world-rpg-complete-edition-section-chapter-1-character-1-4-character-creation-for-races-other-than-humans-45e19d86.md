- Conversely, once the ability scores are created and the character's initial experience is determined, you can usually get an impression of the character. This should make it easier to decide on the age, gender, and name of the character. In _Sword World RPG_ , these are not related to the numeric aspects of the game. In other words, it is assumed that there are no differences or changes in ability based on gender or age. Of course, no matter what name you choose, it will not affect their abilities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00351))_

Therefore, we'll have 1 level in the fighter skill as our starting skill, and 2500 starting experience points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00352))_

## Key supported claims

- A simulacra duplicates the appearance and abilities of an enemy human creature and makes them its own. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.222-227)
- A homunculus is a pseudo-life form created by ancient magic, and is close to human in intelligence and speech. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.222-227)
- A blob is a failed artificial life turned feral, and is a black, slimy, amorphous creature with no intellect. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.222-227)
- A stalker is a ghostly being created through ancient magic rituals, using gas and many human souls, and is meant to assassinate others. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.222-227)

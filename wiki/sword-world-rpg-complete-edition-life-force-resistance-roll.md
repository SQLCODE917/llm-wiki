A **life force resistance roll** is a type of resistance roll that uses _adventurer level + life force bonus_ as the baseline score. These rolls are made to avoid or reduce the effects of physical dangers such as poison.

### Baseline Score Calculation

The baseline score equals _adventurer level + life force bonus_.

### Example

Ducard II has an adventurer level of 2 and a +3 life force bonus. The baseline score of his life force resistance roll is _5_.

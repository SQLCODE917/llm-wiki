## Statements

- Life force and mental power will be taken from the caster's current scores ( _not_ their maximum scores), but other details such as attack power and defense power will be the same as a lesser dragon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04095))_
- Even if you suffer damage from magic while transformed into a dragon, the damage reduction will be 10 points, which is the lesser dragon's monster level, _not_ the caster's adventurer level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04095))_

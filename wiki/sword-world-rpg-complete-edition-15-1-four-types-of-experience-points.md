## Source record

This section describes the four types of experience points in Sword World RPG: accomplishing missions, fighting monsters, success rolls, and special bonuses.

## Key supported claims

- There are four ways to gain experience points in Sword World RPG: accomplishing missions, fighting monsters, success rolls, and special bonuses (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.258-258).
- Characters receive 1000 experience points for achieving mission goals, and 500 if they cannot achieve the goal (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.258-258).
- Experience points from fighting monsters is calculated as monster level x 10 ÷ number of adventurers (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.258-258).
- Special bonuses should be awarded sparingly, about 1/10th of the experience points earned from accomplishing a mission (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.258-258).

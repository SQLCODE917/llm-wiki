Giant ants are large and dangerous insect monsters found in caves and ruins. They are larger and more dangerous than ordinary ants and are known for their strength, aggression, and various forms including worker, soldier, and queen.

## Characteristics

Giant ants are omnivorous and just over 2 meters long. Although their bodies are gigantic, their nature is no different from that of ordinary ants. They have almost no intellect, making them fearless and a troublesome opponent to deal with. They are extremely voracious creatures, and will attack anything that moves, thinking it's food. Their shiny black carapace is as strong as plate armor, and their attacks with their sharp fangs are not to be underestimated.

## Combat

Giant ants are extremely voracious creatures and will attack anything that moves. They are fearless due to their lack of intellect and are considered a troublesome opponent. The giant ant soldier's fangs secrete a highly toxic acid. Anyone hit by a giant ant soldier will have their wound burned by the acid and, in addition to normal damage, must make a life force resistance roll. This damage can only be reduced by adventurer level. If the fangs cannot penetrate armor, damage from the acid will not be dealt.

The giant ant queen is the queen of all giant ants and is always protected by giant ant soldiers. She also has her own means of attack. Giant ant queens can spit out mucus from their mouths that's used to alter the food they feed to their young. Those hit by this mucus will be deprived of their bodily freedom. If you’re hit by the mucus, you won’t suffer damage, but you will become more and more entangled. While entangled in the mucus, attack and evasion suffer a -4 penalty, and ancient magic and spirit magic cannot be used.

## Habitat

Giant ants are found in caves and ruins, where they live in large colonies. The giant ant queen is the leader of the colony and has the ability to command other giant ants, devise some strategy, and make them fight. Once a giant ant queen is defeated, the giant ants under its command will begin to attack everything around them indiscriminately.

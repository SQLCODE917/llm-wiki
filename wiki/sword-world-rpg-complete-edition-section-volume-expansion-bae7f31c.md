# 《 **Volume Expansion** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Rounding should be handled the same way you would for area expansion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01368))_
- Some spells that affect a space or object can have their volume expanded. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01368))_
- the mental power consumed will also be _doubled_ or _tripled_ ....... _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01368))_
- If the volume is expanded by _two_ or _three_ times...... _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01368))_
- Since it’s the volume that gets expanded, the radius of a sphere, a side of a cube, etc., can only be increased by the _cube root_ of the expansion rate. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01369))_
- Since it’s the volume that gets expanded, the radius of a sphere, a side of a cube, etc., can only be increased by the _cube root_ of the expansion rate. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01369))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, **target volume** is written in the _expansion_ section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01370))_
- Note: On the table, _expansion rate_ is the multiplier for mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01373))_
- For example, when you cast the ancient magic _Light_ , whose area is _A space with a 10 meter radius_ , by consuming _double_ the mental power, the resulting area will be a spherical space with a radius of _10 x 1.3 =_ _**13 meters**_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01374))_
- When you cast a spell by consuming that multiplied amount of mental power, multiply the number in _area expansion length_ or _volume expansion length_ by the number (radius, side) that represents the spell’s _area of effect_ , and the result will be the spell’s _area_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01374))_
- For spells cast on a flat surface, use the number in the _area expansion length_ column, and for spells cast into space, use the number in the _volume expansion length_ column. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01374))_
- For example, when you cast the ancient magic _Light_ , whose area is _A space with a 10 meter radius_ , by consuming _double_ the mental power, the resulting area will be a spherical space with a radius of _10 x 1.3 =_ _**13 meters**_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01374))_

## Statements

- This spell is used when a high priest of Pharis discovers a great evil that absolutely cannot be overlooked. Gather as many Pharis believers as you can to a temple, and cast this in front of them. As a result, anyone who agrees to the spell, including the person who cast it, will experience the following effects. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02951))_
- 1. Temporarily increase fighter skill level by 2 (+2 bonus to attack power, evasion speed, and damage. Those with fighter skill 0 will be temporarily given 2 levels) 2. Absolute obedience to the caster's commands (even if it is a suicidal act) It has no impact at all on those who do not agree to the spell, but _no_ devout follower of Pharis would not agree to the high priest's spell. The caster can also exclude anyone from the spell, even if another person willingly agrees. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02952))_

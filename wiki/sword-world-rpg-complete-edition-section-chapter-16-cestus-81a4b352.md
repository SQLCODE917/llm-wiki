## Statements

- These are combat gloves . This weapon allows you to use your hands freely while it's equipped. After putting on a cestus, you can also hold a weapon in your hand. Although you cannot add the strike power of a cestus to another weapon, nor can you make two attacks, it can be useful when your weapon is knocked away. However, it becomes a hindrance when dealing with ancient magic and spirit magic. It'll also be a hindrance to the thief skill's lockpicking and remove trap . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11636))_

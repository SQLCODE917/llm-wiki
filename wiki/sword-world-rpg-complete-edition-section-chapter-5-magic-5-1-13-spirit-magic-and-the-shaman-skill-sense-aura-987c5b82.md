## Statements

- This ability is used to know what kind of spirit power is at work in your surroundings. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02172))_
- When a player declares that they will use this ability, the game master must tell the player what spirit power is at work in that location. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02172))_
- Since there are spirits of life and spirits of undeath (which control negative life force), the sense aura ability can be used to distinguish between living things and undead. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02173))_

## Statements

- The goblin lord, as the name suggests, is the king of the goblins. However, not all tribes have this type of lord-species, if anything they're rare. They are a greater species of goblin, so to speak, who retain a limited amount of power from the time when goblins lived in the fairy world. Although they lack magical abilities, they have bodies that are about the same size or even larger than humans and hobgoblins. It is said that there are quite a few of this goblin lord species in the Youma Forest to the east of Alecrast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-05299))_

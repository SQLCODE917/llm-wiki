## Source record

Section 5.1.9 of the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition details the various types of magic, including damage, target score, counters (negates), counters (overpowers), mind, physical ability, weapon enhancement, non-removable, curse, detection, and focus magic.

## Key supported claims

- These characteristics are presented here as **magic types**. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.63-64)
- A spell that deals damage to the target's life force or mental power. Spells of the damage type always have a clearly defined _strike power_. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.63-64)
- Some magic can nullify the effects of other magic. For example, the ancient magic _Dispel Magic_ nullifies the effects of most magic. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.63-64)

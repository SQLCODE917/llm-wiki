# 3.4.2 Actions You Can Take

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Full movement means that a character runs without looking aside. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00685))_
- Naturally, you can advance farther, but the restrictions on your actions will also be greater. At this time, attacks from the enemy are also much more likely to hit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00686))_
- The distance you can go with full movement is character's agility x 3 meters. For monsters, their movement speed is set separately from agility, so they can move up to movement speed x 3 meters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00687))_

## Statements

- This spell is used on an individual to impose penalties that are not directly life-threatening. It' s up to the game master to decide what kind of penalty to impose. However, the higher the caster's level, or if some kind of price is paid at the same time as the spell, the more terrifying the effect will be. For example, if the caster sacrifices their own life, it is acceptable to apply a fairly powerful curse. An example of a curse is making a beautiful face ugly or changing the color of one's skin. Game masters should limit the effects of curses so that they do not immediately kill the individual. Curses can be removed by Remove Curse or Perfect Cancellation , but there are also always items or actions that are the key to lifting the curse. The curse will be lifted when you obtain the item or complete the action. This key is usually unknown to the target of the curse. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04151))_

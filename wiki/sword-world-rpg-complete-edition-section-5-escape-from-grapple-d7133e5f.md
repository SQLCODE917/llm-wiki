# **5. Escape from Grapple**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- An opponent who is trying to maintain the grapple may counter this by making one using _adventurer level + agility bonus_ _**or** adventure level + strength bonus_ as the baseline score, whichever is greater. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07827))_
- If the one trying to escape’s final score is greater, or if there’s a tie, the grapple will be broken. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07827))_
- Both sides will fall _prone_ , with a short distance between them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07827))_
- To escape from a grapple, make a success roll check using _adventurer level + agility bonus_ as the baseline score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07827))_
- you dropped when entering the grapple will probably be lying around nearby. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07828))_
- If the strangler or pinner attempts to escape, he or she will automatically succeed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07829))_
- Those being strangled or pinned down _cannot_ attempt to escape from a grapple unless they _first_ escape from _those_ conditions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07829))_
- Those being strangled or pinned down _cannot_ attempt to escape from a grapple unless they _first_ escape from _those_ conditions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07829))_
- Of course, a monster that does not have hands cannot _strangle_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07830))_
- For adventurers who rely on weapons, this may pose a threat. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07830))_
- It’s fine for the game master to have certain monsters attempt to grapple. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07830))_

A **mental power resistance roll** is a type of resistance roll that uses _adventurer level + mental power bonus_ as the baseline score. These rolls are made to avoid or reduce the effects of magic or curses. All checks against magic, even if it causes physical damage, are made with mental power resistance rolls. Checks against special attacks by monsters that are magical in nature are also made with mental power resistance rolls.

### Baseline Score Calculation

The baseline score equals _adventurer level + mental power bonus_.

### Example

Ducard II has an adventurer level of 2 and a +2 mental power bonus. The baseline score of his mental power resistance roll is _4_.

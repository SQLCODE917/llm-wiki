In *Sword World RPG*, resurrection refers to the process of bringing a dead character back to life. The Resurrection spell is a powerful magic that can restore life to a deceased character, but it has certain limitations. 

If a character is resurrected using the Resurrection spell, they can be brought back to life, but if their soul is destroyed after the spell's duration has expired, they cannot be brought back to life again. This is especially relevant in cases where the character has been reincarnated (see [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-reincarnation]]), as their soul can be destroyed upon the expiration of the reincarnation spell's duration. 

The Resurrection spell can also be used in conjunction with the Save Soul spell, which can save the souls of those who have become undead, allowing them to be resurrected if they agree to the spell. 

It is important to note that the Resurrection spell cannot be used to bring back someone whose soul has been destroyed, even if they have been reincarnated.

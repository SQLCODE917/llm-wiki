## Statements

- Monsters with this ability can use magic at the indicated level and magic intensity (magic power). Those with this ability often have an intellect equal to or greater than that of a human, and although they have their own preferences for which magic they use, they do not use it foolishly. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07929))_

## Source record

Chapter 4.3 Projectile Restrictions through 4.4 Scores Used in Weapon Combat from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering projectile use, range, movement, and weapon combat scores. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.41-42)

## Key supported claims

- Weapons and armor require strength to use, with limits based on character's strength (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.40-40).
- Characters may use weapons or armor only if their strength meets or exceeds the required strength (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.40-40).
- There are restrictions on handling projectiles like bows and javelins (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.40-40).
- Projectiles cannot be used in melee combat (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.40-40).
- Weapons used for throwing or shooting have maximum ranges: 100m for shooting, 20m for throwing (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.40-40).

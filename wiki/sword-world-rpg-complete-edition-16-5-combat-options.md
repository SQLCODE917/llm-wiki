## Source record

This section covers optional rules for combat in the Sword World RPG, including projectile range, full defense, strong blow, charge, snipe, mow down, grapple, ram, and area aim.

## Key supported claims

- Projectile range can be extended beyond basic limits with optional rules, with -1 modifier per meter for throwing weapons beyond 20 meters and -1 per 5 meters for shooting weapons beyond 100 meters (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-268).
- Each throwing or shooting weapon has a maximum range, and they will never hit a target farther away than this (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-268).
- Strength is required for using crossbows, and characters must meet the required strength to use them (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-268).
- You cannot declare mercy when using the strong blow action (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-268).

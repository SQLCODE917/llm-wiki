## Source record

Optional combat rules for Sword World RPG, including full defense, strong blow, charge, snipe, mow down, grapple, ram, and area aim.

## Key supported claims

- Full defense grants +2 bonus to evasion speed (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-268).
- Strong blow allows bonus damage +2 or critical target -1 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-268).
- Charge requires minimum distance and adds +10 to strike power when hitting (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-268).

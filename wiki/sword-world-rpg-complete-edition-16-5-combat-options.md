## Source record

Optional combat rules for the Sword World RPG, including full defense, strong blow, charge, snipe, mow down, grapple, ram, and area aim.

## Key supported claims

- In the basic rules, the range limit is 20 meters for throwing weapons and 100 meters for shooting weapons. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-267)
- Throwing weapons have a -1 modifier to attack power for every meter beyond 20 meters. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-267)
- Shooting weapons have a -1 modifier for every 5 meters beyond 100. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-267)
- Each weapon has a maximum range, and they will never hit a target farther away than this. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-267)
- Strong blow allows choosing bonus damage +2 or critical target -1. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-267)

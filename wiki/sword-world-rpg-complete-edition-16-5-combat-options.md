## Source record

The Combat Options section outlines optional combat rules for dual‑wielding, projectile range limits, and weapon maximum ranges.

## Key supported claims

- A character wielding weapons in both hands has three possible attack styles (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-268).
- Throwing weapons have a 20‑meter range limit; shooting weapons, 100 m (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-268).
- Each throwing or shooting weapon has a maximum range per Table 16‑4 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.264-268).

- This spell is a spell that transfers the caster's mental power (points) to another person as it is. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02748))_
- Even those who have lost their consciousness with 0 mental power can be recovered with this spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02748))_
- For example, if the caster spends 10 points of mental power with this spell, 9 points will be added to the target's mental power, excluding the base mental power cost of 1 point. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02748))_

- The amount of mental power being transferred _cannot_ be divided by priest skill level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02749))_

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02748))_

> This spell is a spell that transfers the caster's mental power (points) to another person as it is. For example, if the caster spends 10 points of mental power with this spell, 9 points will be added to the target's mental power, excluding the base mental power cost of 1 point.

An air stalker is a type of stalker created from transparent gas. Therefore, even when it materializes, it is completely invisible. For this reason, attacks on an air stalker and evasion against an air stalker's attacks are always -4. Of course, it's not certain what form an air stalker will materialize in. Air stalkers inhabit ruins and have no language. They are not affected by poison and illness, and are sleepless. Their special ability includes normal attack immunity, and they are completely transparent. 

Air stalkers are classified as magical beasts with a monster level of 6, rarity 15, agility 15, movement speed 15, and a number of one, rarely multiple. They have a weapon attack point of 13 (6), strike points of 12, evasion points of 14 (7), defense points of 8, and life points/resistance of 16/14 (7). They also have mental points/resistance of 20/15 (8). Their habitat is ruins, and they perceive with pseudo and magic while in ghostly form.

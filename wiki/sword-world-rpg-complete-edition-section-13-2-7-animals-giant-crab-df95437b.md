- This is a huge crab with a shell that’s about 2 meters wide. They usually live in the water, but sometimes search for food on land. Its weapons are its two large pincers, but the right pincer is considerably larger than the left one, and can grasp anything the size of a human torso. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05447))_
- Anyone hit by the right pincer of a giant crab will be caught by the pincer. This is resolved as strangling (see p. 236). While a giant crab is holding one person with its right pincer, it can attack another target with its left. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05448))_

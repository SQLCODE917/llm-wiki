# **4.10.4 Mithril Silver Weapons and Armor**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- **Mithril silver** , also known as **true silver** , is produced in very few places in the world. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01154))_
- However, the art of forging mithril silver was lost with the fall of the _magical kingdom_ , so it has not been handed down to the present day. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01154))_
- If a character ever sees them, mithril silver weapons and armor are definitely a _legacy_ from the era of the magical kingdom. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01155))_
- No one has yet discovered anything with a power of +4 or higher. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01156))_
- This power affects attack power, evasion speed, bonus damage, and damage reduction. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01156))_
- Mithril silver weapons and armor are always _magical_ items and, like magical weapons and armor, have a power of +1 or +2. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01156))_
- For this reason, strike power and defense power are calculated by adding _+5_ to required strength. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01157))_
- Mithril silver is also a very strong metal. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01157))_
- Therefore, you can find a broadsword clad in mithril silver that has _required strength 16_ and _strike power 21_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01158))_
- What makes them different from high-quality weapons and armor is that they do not _lower required strength_ but rather _increase strike power and defense power_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01158))_
- Therefore, you can find a broadsword clad in mithril silver that has _required strength 16_ and _strike power 21_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01158))_
- Mithril silver _and_ high-quality-- such as a broadsword made of mithril silver, originally with required strength _16_ but made with _5 less_ , and _also_ with _+5_ strike power because it’s mithril silver, resulting in required strength _11_ and strike power _21_ --is almost _always_ rare. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01159))_

- Mithril silver weapons and armor are _never_ , if ever, placed in shops or traded for money. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01164))_
- It should be noted that, although this does not affect the numeric aspects of the game, mithril silver can be made to emit a variety of colors depending on how it’s processed, and these colors will _never_ fade. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01165))_

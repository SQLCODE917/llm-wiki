## Source record

This section covers spirit magic spells including Fear, Restore Health, Control Plant, Shoot Arrow, Charm, Valkyrie Javelin, Missile Protection, Control Sound, Spirit Wall, Decrease Water Pressure, and Preserve Sound, as well as 6th and 7th level spells like Windstorm and Crack, and Valkyrie Blessing.

## Key supported claims

- Fear spell instills fear in opponent, effects based on Table 5-5: Fears (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- Restore Health heals poison and illness, usable only by women (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- Control Plant allows control of plants, with scores for attack and evasion (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- Shoot Arrow ensures hit with strike power 20, counters Missile Protection (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- Charm uses Dryad's mind power to charm target, effects depend on mutual intentions (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)

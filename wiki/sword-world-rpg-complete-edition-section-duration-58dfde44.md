# 《 **Duration** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This represents how long the magic will remain in effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01528))_
- This includes **instant** , **18 rounds** , **1 day** , **permanent** , **until focus ends** or **until certain** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01528))_
- For magic whose duration is _instant_ , the magic itself disappears in an instant, but its effects remain. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01529))_
- Wounds caused by a _Fireball_ explosion will remain, and if you receive healing by _Cure Wounds_ , those wounds will be recovered instantly. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01529))_

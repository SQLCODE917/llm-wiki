## Statements

- Many games set some kind of weight on weapons, armor and equipment, limiting the amount of goods a character can carry. However, Sword World RPG does not do this. Instead, please use your common sense to make decisions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01020))_
- A character cannot have an infinite number of things. It is impossible to hold 1000 torches or 1 kilometer of rope. This would be a matter of course even if the rules do not stipulate it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01021))_
- Ducard II has 580 gamels left. How much equipment can we buy with this? _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01023))_
- First, we'll need a rucksack (50G). Without it, it'll be difficult to carry luggage. We should also have a belt pouch (15G) for small items and money. Since we're going on an adventure, a water bag (20G) is a must. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01024))_
- If we're planning to sleep outdoors, we'll need a mantle (40G) and a blanket (50G). A tent is too expensive and we don't need something large enough for five people. If he has friends, though, it would be nice if we could pool our money together to buy it. Tableware (12G) and cooking utensils (50G) will also be needed for camping. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01025))_
- For a lighting tool, we'll first need a tinderbox (20G). Without that, a fire cannot be started. We'll also need torches (6 for 5 G). A lantern would be brighter, but money is tight and we can't afford that luxury. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01026))_
- Now let's buy some parchment (10 sheets for 10 G) and quill pen ink (5 G). Ducard II's ranger skill includes the map creation ability. It may be useful at some point. The thieves' tools are for a thief and the musical instruments are for a bard, so they're not necessary for Ducard II. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01028))_
- So far, 277 gamels have been spent on purchases, with 303 gamels remaining. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01029))_
- We won't buy preserved food yet. There's still no adventure in store for Ducard II. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01030))_
- Many games set some kind of weight on weapons, armor and equipment, limiting the amount of goods a character can carry. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01020))_

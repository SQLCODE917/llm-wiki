# 5.1.11 Ancient Magic and the Sorcerer Skill

## Ancient Magic Requirements

Ancient magic requires a mage's staff as a magical catalyst to function. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 66))_ 

Those who use ancient magic must wear cloth or soft leather armor due to the complex gestures required. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 66))_

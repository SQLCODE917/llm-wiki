# 《 Area Expansion 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- If the area is doubled or tripled ...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... You can also expand an area by a fraction (x1.5, x8/3, etc.), in which case the fraction is rounded up when determining the multiplier for mental power consumed. So for expansions such as x 1.5 and x 8/3 , the cost would be double and triple , respectively. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02581))_

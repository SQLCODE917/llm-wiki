## Source record

This section covers the rules for action announcements and canceling in combat, including the order of announcements, specificity of actions, and how actions can be canceled or delayed.

## Key supported claims

- Order of announcements determined by intelligence, with highest intelligence group announcing later (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.35-35).
- Three or more groups, same rule applies, highest intelligence group announces last (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.35-35).
- Players must be specific in action announcements, not just general (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.35-35).
- Impossible actions not allowed, like casting while running (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.35-35).
- NPCs announce via game master, players act from character's point of view (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.36-36).

## Fire Weapon Magic

Fire Weapon is a spell in the Sword World RPG that enhances weapons with fire properties. When cast, it increases the strike power of the weapon by +10 (or +20 for paper materials) and allows weapons that normally cannot cause critical hits to now be able to cause critical hits. Additionally, if the weapon is exposed to fire, it will ignite immediately, and its defense points will automatically decrease by 1 point every round. When its defense points reach 0, its destruction points begin to decrease, and on the next round when destruction points reach 1, the paper object will burn out.

This spell is particularly effective against materials like paper, which have low defense points and can be easily destroyed by fire. It also enhances the effectiveness of fire-type magic when used in conjunction with weapons.

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 《 Common 》

- Common is the language most widely used in the world of Sword World . This language was first used during the early new kingdom period, replacing low ancient which had been the normal language of the ancient kingdom. Language was a major barrier for peddlers without borders, traveling bards, mercenaries and adventurers. Therefore, these people and those who often had contact with them (merchants, innkeepers, kings, nobles, etc.) began to use common as a means of communicating with each other. ### Chapter 12: Various Checks / Rules for Poison, Illness and Infection

- In Sword World RPG , illness is defined as a state in which the balance of spirit power within the body is disrupted. The concept of bacterial or viral contagion does not exist. There are two types of symptoms that can appear. One is due to poison and the other is due to illness . In addition, a wound may become infected , which is treated as an illness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04736))_
- Poisons force an imbalance in the spirit power within the body. As long as the poison remains in the body, even if the symptoms disappear, the balance of spirit power will be disrupted and the symptoms will appear again. However, if the poison itself is cleared away, the spirit power within the body will naturally return to a balanced state and restore health. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04737))_
- On the other hand, illness is a state where the spirit power within the body remains unbalanced and in a fixed state. Think of it as the spirits inside your body that are under the illusion that a false equilibrium is the correct one. The unknown spirit of life heals illness by showing other spirits in the body how to achieve balance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04738))_

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 《 Toxicity Score 》

- Each poison has a toxicity score . When the poison enters the body, make a life force resistance roll (for monsters, a life point resistance roll) using the toxicity score as the target score. If you fail, the effect will appear, but if you succeed, the effect will only appear incomplete or won't appear at all. The toxicity scores listed here are just examples; by diluting the substance, the toxicity score can be made as low as desired, and by concentrating it, it can be made somewhat greater. When you cast the holy magic Cure Poison on someone who has been poisoned, compare the final score when casting the magic to this toxicity score. If the final score is equal to or greater than the toxicity score, the poison can be removed by the magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04776))_
- If you fail, the effect will appear, but if you succeed, the effect will only appear incomplete or won't appear at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04776))_

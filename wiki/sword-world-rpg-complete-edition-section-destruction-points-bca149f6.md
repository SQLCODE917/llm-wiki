In the case of paper, the score is 1~10, so the minimum destruction points are 1 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09217))_
- Defense points and destruction points are used in the basic system for destroying structures. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09218))_
- When a character attempts to destroy something with a weapon, magic, etc. damage is calculated in the same way as when an attack hits a monster. Then, you subtract the defense points from the determined damage. If the result is greater than or equal to the destruction points , the structure is destroyed by that blow. If the result is greater than 1 but less than the destruction points, the defense points are reduced by 1 point. However, the structure itself is not destroyed. If the result is 0 or less (when damage is less than or equal to defense points), that means there was no effect at all. After the defense points fall to 0, the destruction points will decrease by 1 point. However, this will only be reduced to the minimum destruction points depending on the material.

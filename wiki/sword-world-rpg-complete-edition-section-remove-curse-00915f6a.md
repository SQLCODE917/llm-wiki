# [ Remove Curse ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This spell negates all magic except for non-removable . It's especially often used for spells with curse effects, such as Curse and Geas . However, you can only attempt to remove one spell's effect once . If the attempt fails, the caster cannot try again until their magic power increases. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04483))_

## Source record

This section covers the rules for ancient magic and the sorcerer skill in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.

## Key supported claims

- Ancient magic draws power directly from matter and has powerful effects (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.66-67).
- Sorcerers use a mage's staff as a magical catalyst to cast ancient magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.66-67).
- Ancient magic requires cloth or soft leather armor for casting (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.66-67).
- Sorcerers can speak, read, and write in both high and low ancient (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.66-67).
- Spellbooks are used by sorcerers to preserve and review learned spells (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.66-67).

### `technical-atom-87ace50ab782fb97` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.66-67)

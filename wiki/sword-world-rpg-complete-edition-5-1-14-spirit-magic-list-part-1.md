## Source record

Summary of Spirit Magic List part 1, including first‑level spirit spells, rules on sensing spirit power, silent spirit rune usage, and interaction with other spirits.

## Key supported claims

- Spirit power cannot be pinpointed unless you are very close. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- Spirits speak directly to the mind; when commanding, runes must be spoken aloud. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- Within the area of effect, resist attempts let only the caster’s own voices be heard. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- A will‑o‑wisp touching a shade or Darkness vanishes without emitting energy. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)
- Strong spirit power may mask weaker power, preventing detection of faint spirit influence. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95)

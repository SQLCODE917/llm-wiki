## Source record

This section details the first part of the Spirit Magic List, covering spells from 1st to 4th level. It outlines the mechanics of spirit magic, including how to communicate with spirits, the types of spirits involved, and the effects of various spells. Some aspects of spirit magic are unknown.

## Key supported claims

- Spirit magic involves communication with spirits, often using runes (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95).
- Some fae are well versed in silent spirit, which allows communication with spirits (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95).
- Most spirits speak directly to the mind, but some require spoken runes for commands (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95).
- Spirit magic has various effects, such as detection, damage, and mind manipulation (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95).
- Some spells require specific conditions to be cast, such as the presence of fire or natural ground (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.84-95).

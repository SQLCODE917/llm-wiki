The [Evil Impulse] spell is a special dark magic spell created by the wicked god Phalaris. It amplifies the evil spirit that lies dormant in a person's heart, causing targets to impulsively act in evil ways based on their current desires and circumstances.

## **Spell Details**

- **Spell Level:** 4
- **Base Mental Power Cost:** 20
- **Distance:** 30 meters
- **Area:** One
- **Duration:** 18 rounds
- **Effect:** Cause the target to impulsively commit evil acts
- **Type:** Mind
- **Expansion:** Final score, duration, distance, targets
- **Resist:** Negated effect

## **Effect**

This spell has the effect of amplifying the evil spirit that lies dormant in a person's heart. Targets that fail to resist will impulsively act in evil ways. What the target does depends on the situation at the moment and their desires. For example, if you think deep down "I want money", you'll be tempted to steal it immediately.

This spell has no effect on beings without intelligence or beasts who always act on impulse.

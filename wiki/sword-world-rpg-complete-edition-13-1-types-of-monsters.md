## Source record

Alecrast’s 12 monster types are summarized, including key rules on movement, escape, and undead traits. May include further detail in future updates.

## Key supported claims

- Alecrast hosts 12 monster types: fae, youma, spirits, titans, mythical beasts, magical beasts, animals, plants, undead, magical creatures, daemons, humans, and others (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.170-172).
- Undead move via forces opposite normal animals (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.170-172).
- If a monster’s movement speed exceeds a character’s agility, escape may be impossible unless a special action is taken (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.170-172).
- That speed rule determines whether a character can escape from a monster (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.170-172).
- Daemons are underworld monsters inhabiting a world distinct from ours (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.170-172).

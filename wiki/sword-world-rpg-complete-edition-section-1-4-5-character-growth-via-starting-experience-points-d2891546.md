# 1.4.5 Character Growth via Starting Experience Points

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-1-4-1e239ef6]] - broader source section: 1.4

## Statements

- A character has already acquired certain skills (starting skills) according to Table 1-5: Backgrounds or Table 1-6: Starting Experience by Race. In addition to this, starting experience points can also be used to acquire new skills or increase a character's skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00613))_
- However, the skills that can be acquired or increased are limited to those of adventurer skills, and general skills cannot be acquired or increased. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00614))_
- Characters can spend their starting experience points to gain skill levels. For example, if you want to acquire 1 level in ranger, you can do so by subtracting 500 from your starting experience points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00616))_
- This should be understood as purchasing skills with experience points . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00617))_
- It is also possible to increase the level of skills that have already been acquired. You may even acquire 2 or more levels at a time, so long as there are enough experience points to spend. Note, however, that experience points are spent at each level attained. For example, if you do not have the ranger skill and you want to acquire 2 levels in ranger at once, you must spend 500 + 1000 for a total of 1500 experience points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00618))_
- Only adventurer skills can be acquired or increased using experience points. General skills cannot be acquired or increased with experience points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00620))_
- In addition, starting experience points do not have to be used up. Leaving them in place will give you an advantage in the growth of your character after the game. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00621))_
- Ducard II has 2500 experience points. With these, for example, we could acquire 1 level in sorcerer (25002000= 500 remaining) or increase fighter to 2 levels (2500-1500= 1000 remaining). In addition, we can even acquire 2 levels in thief at once (2500-1000-1500= 0 remaining). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00623))_
- Here, we'll increase fighter to 2nd level and also acquire 1 level in ranger. This leaves us 500 points, which we'll save for future use. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00624))_
- When you have the sorcerer skill, there's a few things to be careful of. This skill is closely related to the sage skill and requires fewer experience points when acquiring both. If a character has neither the sorcerer nor sage skill, they can acquire 1 level in both the sorcerer and sage skills by spending 2000 experience points. You may acquire sorcerer only, but it's better to avoid this, as it will put you at a disadvantage during the game. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00654))_
- If a character has 1 level in the sage skill (so if they're a human or half-elf, and if merchant/scholar or noble was rolled on Table 1-5: Backgrounds), they can acquire 1 level in the sorcerer skill for 1500 points. Of course, having the sage skill does not mean that you're required to have the sorcerer skill. The sage skill is useful enough on its own. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00655))_
- It is important to note that it is against the rules for a character who has neither the sorcerer nor sage skills to acquire only the sorcerer skill for 1500 points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00656))_
- Table 1-7: Experience Points by Skill (at Character Creation) is almost identical to a segment of Table 8-1: Experience Points by Skill, which is used for character growth as explained in chapter 8. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00657))_
- As this shows, the use of starting experience points is exactly the same as the use of experience points during character growth, so it might be a good idea to read the explanation in chapter 8 first. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00658))_

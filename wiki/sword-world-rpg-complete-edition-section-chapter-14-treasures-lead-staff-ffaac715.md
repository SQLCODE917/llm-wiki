## Statements

- The gem on the tip can be removed, and if the staff falls down while it’s removed, the staff will fall in the direction of the gem, regardless of the distance between the staff and the gem. When the gem is on, it’ll always fall to the north. This staff also functions as a magical catalyst. The price of the gem alone is 5000 gamels, and the staff with the gem removed is worth 3200 gamels. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06773))_

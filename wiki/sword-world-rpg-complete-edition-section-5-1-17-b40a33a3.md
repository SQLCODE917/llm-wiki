### [ Divine Armor ] (Spell Level=7)

- When you cast this spell, a force field surrounds the caster, acting as armor with defense power 30. This armor appears to glow vaguely. There is no required strength, and it does not hinder the caster's movement at all. Even if the caster is already wearing armor, their defense power does not stack (the defense power of the armor you were wearing will be nullified). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04711))_

### [ Divine Weapon ] (Spell Level=7)

- Resist=None When you cast this spell, a vaguely glowing weapon-like force field appears in the caster's hand. The shape depends on the caster's preference. There is no required strength, and its strike power is treated as 30 (if the caster uses it as a twohanded weapon, its strike power is 35 ). This spell has no effect if the caster already has something else in their hand. Also, it's not possible to give this weapon to someone else. This weapon can also take effect against monsters that can only be wounded by silver or magic weapons. When you create a bow, etc. with this spell, arrows are automatically generated as well. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04721))_
- This weapon can also take effect against monsters that can only be wounded by silver or magic weapons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04721))_

### Marfa Special Holy Magic List

- Marfa, as the earth mother and the goddess of affection, preaches strong warnings against unnecessary conflict. Therefore, priests of Marfa can use this spell to take away the feelings of hatred that have arisen in each other's hearts and prevent them from fighting. Those who fail to resist will lose their hostility and attempt to stop the fight. At this point, if the opponent attempts to take hostile action again, this becomes a natural battle for Marfa, so you can counterattack. This spell has no effect on those without a mind, or when it is natural for them to attack another due to hunger, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04778))_

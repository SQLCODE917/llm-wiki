# **(Kraken/Greater Water Spirit)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a spell that creates a large whirlpool on the surface of the water, dragging everything floating within its range and sinking it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02640))_
- The spell's effect can work on anything that can fit inside the whirlpool. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02640))_
- To cast this spell, a sea or lake with a depth of at least 100 meters is essential. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02640))_
- The spell's effect can work on anything that can fit inside the whirlpool. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02640))_
- This is a spell that creates a large whirlpool on the surface of the water, dragging everything floating within its range and sinking it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02640))_
- If successful, the ship will be able to safely survive the whirlpool, but if you fail, it will be swallowed up by the whirlpool and sink into the water. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02641))_
- The target score is the final score from when the spell was cast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02641))_
- The baseline score is _sailor skill level/adventurer level + dexterity bonus_ for a small ship, and _sailor skill level + intelligence bonus_ for a large ship. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02641))_
- In that case, make a success roll using the _swim_ ability to determine whether you drown. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02642))_
- If the ship is swallowed up by the whirlpool, everyone on board will be thrown into the whirlpool. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02642))_

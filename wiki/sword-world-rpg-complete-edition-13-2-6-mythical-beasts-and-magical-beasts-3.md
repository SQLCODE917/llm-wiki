## Source record

This section describes various mythical and magical beasts, including Wyrm, Buckbaird, Hydra, Humbaba, Roc, Lesser Dragon, and Elder Dragon.

## Key supported claims

- Wyrm is a large snake-like creature with bat-like wings, human-like intellect, and can speak lizardman but not use magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.196-198).
- Buckbaird is a giant eyeball that floats in the air, with several subspecies, found only in underground labyrinths, and uses five types of rays in combat (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.196-198).
- Hydra is a serpent with nine heads, lives in lakes and rivers, and can regenerate severed heads (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.196-198).

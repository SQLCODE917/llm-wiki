# [ Save Soul ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- There are several cases in which a normal life form becomes undead. Those that have had their mental power (points) taken away by other undead (wights, lesser vampires, etc.), those created by magic (skeleton, zombies, etc.), those created by their own will (haunts, undead kings, etc.), and there are also things such as ghouls whose causes are still unknown. In either case, if you become undead, your soul will become abnormal and you will not be able to be resurrected even if Resurrection is used normally. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04623))_
- This spell saves the souls of those who have become undead. If the undead agrees, the spell is almost guaranteed to succeed (with the exception of double ones), but otherwise you must attain a final score greater than or equal to the undead's mental point resistance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04624))_
- If the spell is successful, the undead will cease all activity at that point. If you then cast Resurrection , it is also possible to restore their life. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04625))_
- One priest can only attempt one Save Soul against a given undead. If you fail, you cannot try again until your magic power increases. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04626))_

# 《 Distance Expansion 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Magic basically only works up to a certain distance. However, there are spells that can extend their reach. If reach distance is doubled or tripled ...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... You can also increase it by a fraction, such as x1.5, in which case the fraction is rounded up when determining the multiplier for mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02603))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, distance is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02604))_

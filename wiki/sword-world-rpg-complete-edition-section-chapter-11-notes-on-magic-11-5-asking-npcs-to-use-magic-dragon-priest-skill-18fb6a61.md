## Statements

- Dragon priests are based on a unique belief in dragons. This is only possible if you're part of a group of people who believe in dragons (mostly considered savages). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04140))_
- The dragon priest skill is increased by spending experience points. By increasing their level, they can have deeper connections to dragons, and they can gain access to new powers (spells) immediately. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04141))_

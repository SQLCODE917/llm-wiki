# 《 **Shaman Skill** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- To acquire the shaman skill, you must be taught how to communicate with spirits by a shaman with a shaman skill of 1st level or higher. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04361))_
- The art of communicating with spirits is not based on theory, but must be learned through experience, by watching and imitating other shamans. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04361))_
- Although there are no specific restrictions in the rules, training for one year or more is usually required. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04361))_
- As your shaman skill increases, your magic power increases and at the same time you also immediately gain access to higher level spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04362))_
- This is because your relationship with the spirits becomes stronger. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04362))_
- The shaman skill is increased by spending experience points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04362))_
- This is because your relationship with the spirits becomes stronger. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04362))_

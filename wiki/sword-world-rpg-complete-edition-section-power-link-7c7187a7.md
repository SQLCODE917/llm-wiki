## Statements

- Base Mental Power Cost=24 Distance=Caster Area=A space within a 10 meter radius from the caster Duration=Until focus ends Effect=Create an accumulation field for mental power Type=Focus, target score Expansion=Final score, area _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04559))_
- This spell has the effect of accumulating the mental power (points) of those within the area of effect (can be chosen by the caster at will) and creating a sort of accumulation field. Those within range must agree to the spell, and if so, decide how many points of mental power (points) they want to pour into the accumulation field. The caster him or herself can also pour their mental power (points) into the pool. Those who agree to the spell will have their mental power (points) reduced by the amount they sent to the accumulation field (at this time, if their mental power/points are reduced to 0, they will lose consciousness), but they can freely draw out the mental points stored in the accumulation field and use them to cast magic, etc. However, when your mental power (points) suffer damage from certain monster attacks or spells such as Mental Attack , etc. you cannot substitute them with mental points from the collection field. It's only effective if you try to use your mental power (points) voluntarily. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04561))_
- If the priest's focus is interrupted or the spell is canceled by Dispel Magic , etc. the mental power (points) accumulated at that moment will be released and disappear. Those who agreed to the spell cannot take back the mental power (points) poured into the collection field. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04562))_
- Also, those who do not agree cannot be forced into this spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04563))_

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- They also have body hair of the same color as the hair on their heads, but it's not all over their bodies. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05288))_
- They have rich heads of hair, the color of which ranges from jet black to deep crimson, depending on the individual. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05288))_
- A fire giant is a giant about 7 meters tall, with reddish-brown skin. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05288))_
- Fire giants are ferocious creatures that will attack anything that enters their territory without mercy. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05289))_
- Since they often live near human villages, they are greatly feared by humans. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05289))_
- Also, they will not suffer any damage at all from fire. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05290))_
- Fire giants have a deep connection with the spirit power of fire, and can use spirit magic sourced from the power of fire spirits (salamander, etc.) up to 7th level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05290))_

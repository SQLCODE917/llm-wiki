## Statements

- If you want to make use of your sorcerer skill, your armor must be cloth or soft leather . Either of these should be the one with the greatest required strength within the character's strength range. Also, you should not buy a shield. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00999))_
- For weapons, be sure to purchase a mage's staff . This is essential when using ancient magic . If the character's strength is 10 or less, it is appropriate to buy one with the same required strength as the character's strength; if the character's strength is 10 or greater, it is appropriate to buy one with a required strength of 10. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01000))_

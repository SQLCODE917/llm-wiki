## Statements

- If you ready a shield, it'll be easier to avoid your opponent's attacks. With this rule, if you ready a large shield, your evasion speed will be +2. However, if you do use a large shield, it's inevitable that you'll have to neglect your attacks. Therefore, when using a large shield, you will suffer a -1 penalty to your attack power . However, large shields with a magical power of +1 or greater do not have this penalty. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11367))_

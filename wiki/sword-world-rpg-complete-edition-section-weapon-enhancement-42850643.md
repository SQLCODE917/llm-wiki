# 《 **Weapon Enhancement** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Two or more of this kind of magic cannot affect the same weapon at the same time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01399))_
- Weapons that have been given power by ancient magic, silver weapons, or mithril silver weapons are assumed to already have _non-removable_ (see below) **weapon enhancement** magic cast on them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01400))_
- Therefore, that power _cannot_ be nullified, so even if you cast new _weapon enhancement_ magic, it’ll be _useless_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01400))_

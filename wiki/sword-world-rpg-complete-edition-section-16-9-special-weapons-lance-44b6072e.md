# 16.9 Special Weapons / Lance

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

## Statements

- A **lance** is a long weapon used by cavalry. It's held under your arm with one hand. It _must_ be used on horseback and can _only_ be used in a _charge (lance charge)_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07317))_
- When performing a lance charge, the movement distance must be within _lance length (required strength x 15 cm) x 2_ _**and** 90 meters (horse's full movement distance)_ . During this time, any attacks made against the horse or mounted adventurer _must_ be evaded with a -4 penalty. Also, even if the horse is a war horse, you _cannot_ make the horse itself attack. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07318))_
- If you _both_ have lances, or if your opponent is using a _spear_ or a _pike_ , follow the rules for _charging_ each other. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07321))_
- In the case of lance charge, it is also possible to _run straight through_ without entering an engagement. However, keep in mind that there _is_ a possibility that you’ll face a counterattack from your opponent each time you use a lance charge. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07322))_

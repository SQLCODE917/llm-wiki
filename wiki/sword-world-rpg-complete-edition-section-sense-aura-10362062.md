# 《 **Sense Aura** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This ability is used to know what kind of spirit power is at work in your surroundings. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02271))_
- When a player declares that they will use this ability, the game master must tell the player what spirit power is at work in that location. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02271))_
- Since there are spirits of life and spirits of undeath (which control negative life force), the sense aura ability can be used to distinguish between living things and undead. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02272))_

## Technical atoms

> You cannot use spells that belong to a specific spirit in places where that spirit power is not at work.
_(source: Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02271))_

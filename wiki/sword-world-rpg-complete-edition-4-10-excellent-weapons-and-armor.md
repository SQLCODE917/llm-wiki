Chapter 4.10 Excellent Weapons and Armor from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, pages 51-53.

## Key supported claims

- High-quality weapons and armor are easier to handle and made with lower required strength than usual (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.51-53).
- Characters can declare mercy with dull weapons, but not with thrown weapons or after a hit check (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.51-53).
- To declare mercy, a weapon must be a dull weapon, such as maces, clubs, or staves (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.51-53).

- To escape, make a success roll using _adventurer level + strength bonus_ as the baseline score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06232))_
- This is treated as normal damage, so it’s possible to reduce it due to armor. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06232))_
- The attack points of the attack is the target score (or the attack points of the _hit_ , if using the optional rule to give monsters variance in attack points). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06232))_
- While being strangled, a character suffers a -4 penalty to evasion and is normally unable to make sound. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06233))_
- Unless otherwise noted, the evasion penalty is - 4 and magic is also considered not possible. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06233))_
- Depending on the monster, the penalty may be smaller, or it might be possible to still make sound, but if this is the case it'll be specified in the _description_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06233))_

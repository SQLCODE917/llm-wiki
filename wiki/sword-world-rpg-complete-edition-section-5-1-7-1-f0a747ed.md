### Expansion Types

- Magic expansion includes the following types. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01222))_

### 《 Targets Expansion 》

- If it's two or three targets...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01225))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, targets is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01227))_

### 《 Area Expansion 》

- If the area is doubled or tripled ...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... You can also expand an area by a fraction (x1.5, x8/3, etc.), in which case the fraction is rounded up when determining the multiplier for mental power consumed. So for expansions such as x 1.5 and x 8/3 , the cost would be double and triple , respectively. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01230))_

### 《 Volume Expansion 》

- Some spells that affect a space or object can have their volume expanded. If the volume is expanded by two or three times...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... Rounding should be handled the same way you would for area expansion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01234))_
- Since it' s the volume that gets expanded, the radius of a sphere, a side of a cube, etc., can only be increased by the cube root of the expansion rate. Please see Table 5-1: Area and Size Expansion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01235))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, target volume is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01236))_
- Since it' s the volume that gets expanded, the radius of a sphere, a side of a cube, etc., can only be increased by the cube root of the expansion rate. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01235))_

### Table 5-1: Area and Size Expansion

- Note: On the table, expansion rate is the multiplier for mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01238))_
- When you cast a spell by consuming that multiplied amount of mental power, multiply the number in area expansion length or volume expansion length by the number (radius, side) that represents the spell's area of effect , and the result will be the spell's area . For spells cast on a flat surface, use the number in the area expansion length column, and for spells cast into space, use the number in the volume expansion length column. For example, when you cast the ancient magic Light , whose area is A space with a 10 meter radius , by consuming double the mental power, the resulting area will be a spherical space with a radius of 10 x 1.3 = 13 meters . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01239))_

### 《 Duration Expansion 》

- For spells whose effects last a certain amount of time, you can expand that duration. If the duration is doubled or tripled ...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... Y ou can also increase it by a fraction, such as x1.5, in which case the fraction is rounded up when determining the multiplier for mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01241))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, duration is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01242))_

### 《 Distance Expansion 》

- Magic basically only works up to a certain distance. However, there are spells that can extend their reach. If reach distance is doubled or tripled ...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... You can also increase it by a fraction, such as x1.5, in which case the fraction is rounded up when determining the multiplier for mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01244))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, distance is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01245))_

### 《 Damage Expansion 》

- For spells that deal damage, expansion can increase the certainty of that damage. If you double or triple the amount of mental power you consume...... you can make a damage check (strike roll) twice or three times . The damage you will deal is equal to the greatest of all the strike rolls you made in this way ( not the total). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01247))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, damage certainty is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01248))_

### 《 Final Score Increase 》

- There's some magic whose effects are negated or reduced if they're resisted by the target, but will also let you increase the magic's final score (resist target score) in order to prevent this. This increase in final score may also take effect if a nullifying spell is cast later. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01250))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, fi nal score is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01252))_
- This expansion requires a deep proficiency in magic. Therefore, it cannot be done infinitely. Final score can only be increased up to a maximum of caster's rune master skill level ÷ 2 (rounded up) . That's up to +1 (double) for 1st~2nd level, +2 (triple) for 3rd~4th level, and +5 (sextuple) for 9th~10th level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01253))_
- Therefore, it cannot be done infinitely. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01253))_
- Final score can only be increased up to a maximum of caster's rune master skill level ÷ 2 (rounded up) . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01253))_

### 《 Effect Expansion 》

- Some expansions don't fall into any of the above types. For spells that can perform these special expansions, effect is written in the expansion section of the magic details, and an explanation is given under each spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01255))_

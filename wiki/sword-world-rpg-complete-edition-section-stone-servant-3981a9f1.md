# **[ Stone Servant ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This simplified version of a stone golem is specifically called a _Stone Servant_ and can be used to engage in combat or simple tasks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01667))_
- After the spell's duration expires, the stone servant will turn back into a stone. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01667))_
- This spell creates a human-shaped stone golem (1 and a half meters tall) from a stone the size of a clenched fist. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01667))_
- However, more complex work is beyond a stone servant's hands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01667))_
- After the spell's duration expires, the stone servant will turn back into a stone. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01667))_

## Statements

- This sheath is made to fit a broadsword and can only hold a broadsword. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08748))_
- If it is a broadsword, the sheath will automatically expand or contract to adjust for any slight differences in length. If you keep a sword in this sheath for more than one hour, then draw the sword from the sheath, it'll have +5 to strike power and will be able to harm monsters that cannot be wounded by normal weapons for 18 rounds, just like if Enchant Weapon magic were cast on it. The sheath's Enchant effect can be removed with a Dispel Magic spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08749))_

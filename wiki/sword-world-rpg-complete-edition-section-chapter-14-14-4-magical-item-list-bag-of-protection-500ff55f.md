## Statements

- A bag created during the ancient kingdom period that protects objects put inside from the effects of any detectiontype magic. However, the objects being protected must be completely inside the bag, if they stick out of the bag even a little, the effect will not be attained. In addition, not only are the things inside not affected by detection-type magic, but so is the bag itself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11055))_

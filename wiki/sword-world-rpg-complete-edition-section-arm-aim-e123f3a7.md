# **Arm Aim**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Since you can only aim at _one_ arm at a time, you _cannot_ make your opponent drop a _two-handed_ weapon using this method (if you make _two_ attacks with _two_ weapons, each aimed at a _different_ arm, and hit, they’ll drop _both_ if they fail _both_ resistance rolls, as described below). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07852))_
- If he or she fails, they’ll drop what they were holding. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07852))_

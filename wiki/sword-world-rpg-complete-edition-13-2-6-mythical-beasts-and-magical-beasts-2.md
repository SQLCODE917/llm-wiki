## Source record

This section describes various mythical and magical beasts found in the Sword World RPG setting, including the Head Displacer, Lamia, Lizardman (both slave and noble types), Griffon, Manticore, Minotaur, Unicorn, Lynx, Sphinx, Wyvern, Basilisk, Small Basilisk, and Sea Dragon. Some creatures are noted for their magical capabilities, while others are defined by their combat behaviors or unique traits. Unknown aspects of their origins and full behaviors are also mentioned.

## Key supported claims

- Head displacers can devour humans without being noticed, possibly created for castle attacks (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.192-195).
- Lamias cannot survive without young human blood, often disguising as girls to deceive (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.192-195).
- Lizardmen are sorted into noble and slave types; nobles are superior and can use magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.192-195).
- Unicorns have high intellect and communicate in silent spirit, trusting only virgin girls (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.192-195).
- Noble lizardmen are far superior to slaves, with some having nearly 10th level dragon roar magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.192-195).

## Statements

- This is a spell that identifies the type of magic being cast on a target and specifies its effect. If it's a regular magical item, you can almost certainly identify the magic. However, some items are safe from the effect of this spell due to the lost spell , Seal Enchantment . When you use this spell on an object that had Seal Enchantment cast on it, its effect cannot be inferred if your final score does not exceed the target score determined by Seal Enchantment . If they fail, the same caster cannot use this spell on the same object until their magic power increases (any attempts will always fail). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03273))_

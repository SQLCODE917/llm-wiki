- This spell nullifies the effects of the types of spells that give commands, such as Quest , Geas , etc. It also removes puppet and golem-type magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03414))_
- This spell can also cancel commands given to a golem. Once a golem has had its commands canceled, it becomes neutral and can be given new commands using the 9th level spell Command Golem .

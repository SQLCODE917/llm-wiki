# 《 **Undead** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Undead are monsters that exist and move around through forces that are the exact opposite of those of us humans and normal animals. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04833))_
- There are many mysteries surrounding the origin of these undead, but it's completely different from how normal animals give birth. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04834))_
- This negative life force is sometimes called the _undeath force_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04834))_
- If you meet them, there is a high probability that you'll fight. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04835))_
- Undead are not affected by poison or illness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04836))_
- They do not require rest in the normal sense and can never be magically put to sleep. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04836))_
- Many spells with mental effects are also meaningless. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04836))_

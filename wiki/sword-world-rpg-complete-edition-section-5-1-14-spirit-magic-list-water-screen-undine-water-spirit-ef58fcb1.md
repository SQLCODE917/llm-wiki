## Statements

- This spell forms a thin film of water on the surface of the target's body, reducing fire damage by 1 point. It doesn't matter what kind of fire. Whether it's magical fire or natural fire, it takes effect. The same applies for attacks with weapons that have Fire Weapon cast on them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03774))_

## Source record

This section lists various magical items from the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, including details on their rarity, form, enchanter, base trade price, and powers. Items include the Guard Glove, Candlestick of Infinite (Un)Luck, Crystal Orb of Clairvoyance, and several others. Some items have unknown enchanters.

## Key supported claims

- Guard Glove generates force field like shield, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-260)
- Candlestick of Infinite (Un)Luck grants luck, but causes misfortune if candle's fire goes out, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-260)
- Crystal Orb of Clairvoyance shows places around world, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-260)

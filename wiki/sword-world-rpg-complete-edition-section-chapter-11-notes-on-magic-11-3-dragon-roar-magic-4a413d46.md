- Dragon roar magic are runes used by those who worship dragons as guardians rather than gods. In modernday Alecrast, there is no custom of worshiping dragons in civilized countries, so no one uses this type of magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06070))_
- There are no restrictions when using dragon roar magic spells. If your consciousness is clear, you can receive the effects of a spell just by remembering it in your mind . Even if Silence or Mute spirit magic is cast on you, you can still use dragon roar magic! _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06071))_
- The skill for using this magic is the dragon priest skill , and its owners are called dragon priests . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06072))_

Due to the nature of their faith, dragon priests do not form a cult or gather believers. ### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 11.3 Dragon Roar Magic / 《 Summon 》

- Spells of this type are spells that summon and employ the race specified by each spell. When using these spells, the caster must determine the final score of the spell. Then, compare this final score with the mental resistance score of the creature you're trying to summon. The result will determine when it will be summoned (Table 11-1: Summon). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06089))_
- Summoning takes place across space. Therefore, it's possible to instantly summon creatures no matter where they are. However, the closer the target creature is, the easier it will be to summon it. When using the Summon table, the game master can apply any modifiers as desired. If you can basically infer that they'll be in the surrounding 10km, please add +2. If you know for sure they're there, +4. The caster can also increase the final score through spell expansion or rituals. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06090))_
- However, no matter what modifiers are applied, if the 2D roll when calculating the final score is double ones, the summoned creature will immediately appear and attack the caster him or herself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06091))_
- All summons are non-removable magic. Therefore, Dispel Magic has no effect. However, by using Pharis's special holy magic Banish , it's possible to drive the summoned creatures back to where they came from. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06092))_
- Therefore, it's possible to instantly summon creatures no matter where they are. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06090))_
- Therefore, Dispel Magic has no effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06092))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 11.3 Dragon Roar Magic / [ Communicate Reptile ]

- You will be able to communicate with reptiles such as snakes and lizards, as well as lizardmen. This spell cannot give any binding commands to the opponent. If the opponent's intelligence is high enough, you can negotiate, but if the opponent's intelligence is not sufficient, you can only know about the opponent's emotions and degree of hunger. However, the risk of being attacked by the opponent is reduced. Your opponent will no longer attack you unless they're enraged or extremely hungry. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06111))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 11.3 Dragon Roar Magic / [ Swordclaw ]

- Sharp claws grow on both hands of the caster. These claws have strike power 15 and critical target 10, and are furthermore treated the same as a weapon imbued with magical power. The attack power is the caster's dragon priest skill level + dexterity bonus , and if it hits, in addition to the damage from the claws, it can also deal bonus damage equal to dragon priest skill level + strength bonus . Claw attacks allow you to attack with both hands at the same time without penalty. However, while these claws are grown out, you cannot grasp or hold anything. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06152))_

- Expansion=Final score, duration, effect Resist=Effect negate A spell that raises a magical roar emitted by a dragon. ### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 11.3 Dragon Roar Magic / [ Blood Protection ]

- When using this spell, the caster must pour their own blood on another person. That blood allows you to protect others from all attacks. Specifically, the caster must consume their life force at the same time as their mental power. Losing 1 point of life force can give you 1 point of defense effect. This defense effect reduces all damage from weapons, magic, etc. At this point, the target is no longer able to remove the blood by normal means such as wiping or washing. A spell such as Remove Curse , etc. must be used, or the caster must be killed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06257))_
- While the spell is in effect, the caster cannot regain the life force they've consumed (think of it as their starting life force being lowered). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06258))_
- The caster can expand the effect to provide a greater defense effect. However, in order to do this, you must consume an extra amount of your life force as well as your mental power. For example, a 6th level caster can consume 8 points of mental power and 2 points of life force at the same time to provide 2 points of defense effect. The same applies when expanding the target score. If you wanted to give 2 points of defense effect to three people each, a 6th level caster's mental power consumed would be 24 points and their life force consumed would be 6 points. Life force consumed cannot be reduced by increasing dragon priest skill level. If an 8th level caster were to do the same thing, the mental power consumed would only be 18 points, but the life force consumed would still be 6 points. There is no need to consume extra life force when expanding the final score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06259))_
- Please note that this spell cannot be cast on the caster him or herself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06260))_
- At this point, the target is no longer able to remove the blood by normal means such as wiping or washing. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06257))_

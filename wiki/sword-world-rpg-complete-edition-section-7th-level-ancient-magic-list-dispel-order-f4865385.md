## Statements

- This spell nullifies the effects of the types of spells that give commands, such as Quest , Geas , etc. It also removes puppet and golem-type magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02007))_
- This spell can also cancel commands given to a golem. Once a golem has had its commands canceled, it becomes neutral and can be given new commands using the 9th level spell Command Golem . However, the final score of commands given during the ancient kingdom period is very high, so you'll need a final score of at least 30 or greater to cancel a golem's commands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02008))_
- Once this spell fails, you cannot attempt it again until your magic power increases. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02009))_

The **Floyd's cycle-finding algorithm** (also known as the **tortoise and hare algorithm**) is used to detect cycles in a sequence or linked list. It employs two pointers moving at different speeds to identify loops. This algorithm is discussed in *JavaScript Allongé* (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.241-256).

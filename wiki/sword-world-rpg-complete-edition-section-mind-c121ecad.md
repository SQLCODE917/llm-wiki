# 《 Mind 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is magic that affects the target's mental state. This kind of magic can only take effect on one target at a time. If magic of the mind type is cast on a target, and other magic also of the mind type is cast on that target, the new magic will take effect, and the prior magic will be nullified . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01317))_
- In other words, all mind magic counters (overpowers) other mind magic. Note that many are resistible magic, and some have target scores . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01318))_
- This kind of magic can only take effect on one target at a time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01317))_

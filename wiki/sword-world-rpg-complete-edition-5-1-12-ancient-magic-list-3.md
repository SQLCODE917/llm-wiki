## Source record

This page summarizes the Ancient Magic List section (pages 76-79) from *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition*. It details a variety of spells, including Read Languages, Vision, Flight, Blizzard, Acid Cloud, Analyze Enchantment, Geas, Create Undead, Create Device, Create Buau Zombie, See-Through, Seal Enchantment, Steal Mind, Slow, Haste, Rune Rope, Dispel Order, Telekinesis, Teleport, Full Potential, Blade Net, Bend Bar, Polymorph, and Magic Reflection.

## Key supported claims

- Read Languages spell allows reading unknown languages, one per use (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.76-79)
- Vision spell increases caster's vision several times (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.76-79)
- Flight spell allows caster to fly with movement speed 50 km/h (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.76-79)
- Analyze Enchantment identifies magic power on objects, but blocked by Seal Enchantment (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.76-79)
- Create Device creates mage's staff or magical catalyst (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.76-79)

# **4.9**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- If, as a result of damage (accumulated damage), a character's (monster's) life force (points) becomes _0 or negative_ , that character (monster) will fall **unconscious** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01038))_
- Also, that character (monster) is now in danger of _dying_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01038))_
- It is no longer possible for them to act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01038))_

## Statements

- This is almost the same ability as the thief and sage skills' treasure appraisal . However, you can only make a check for items that the merchant deals with. Therefore, a merchant who sells art can tell you the value of the art, and a merchant who sells weapons and armor can tell you the value of the weapons and armor. The result is a specific price converted to silver coins. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03773))_

# **Final Damage**

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about **final damage**:

## Statements

### Chapter 4: Weapon Combat / 4.5 Attacks From Characters Against Monsters / Example:

- If the base damage dealt by Ducard II is _4, 18_ , or _14_ , respectively, the final damage he could deal to the goblin would be: _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00934))_

### Chapter 4: Weapon Combat / 4.6 Attacks From Monsters Against Characters / Example:

- If the result of Ducard II's defense roll is _0_ (roll 3) or _6_ (roll 12), respectively, the final damage he will suffer is calculated to be: _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00984))_

- If final damage is _0 or negative_ , that means you took _no_ damage at all. The monster's attack was completely blocked by your armor. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00988))_

### Chapter 4: Weapon Combat / 4.7 Attacks From Characters Against Characters / Example:

- Ducard II now determines how much damage his armor prevents. His defense roll result is _7_ , and his armor's defense power is 7, so the damage that’ll be reduced by his armor is _3_ . After adding his damage reduction of _2_ , 3+2= **5 points** is the final amount of damage Ducard II is able to reduce. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01021))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / Example:

- Suppose a sorcerer casts an _Energy Bolt_ on three enemies ( _x 3_ expansion) with a +1 to his final score ( _x 2_ expansion) and he makes 4 damage checks for each ( _x 4_ expansion). In this case, the multiplied rate of mental power consumed is 3 x 2 x 4 = **24!** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01336))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 《 Magic Power Decrease 》

- Magic power can be optionally reduced. This will reduce the final score and, if it's damage magic, _also_ reduce bonus damage (it’s not possible to _only_ reduce the final score or _only_ reduce bonus damage). You must declare how many points you’ll reduce _before_ you roll the dice. If used on multiple targets, you must reduce it by the same amount for _all_ of them. Also, if the side casting the magic rolls double sixes, the resisting side will _also_ need double sixes, no matter _how much_ magic power was declared to be decreased (if it's a monster, it _cannot_ resist). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01349))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / Damage Poison

- Deals damage to the opponent's life force (points). First, make a strike roll with a strike power of 20. Magic power should be added to damage as usual. Also, your opponent _cannot_ reduce this damage using adventurer level. This poison's damage is not applied immediately, but in the form of 1 point at the end of each round, starting on the round in which the spell is used. The poison then lasts until the final determined damage is dealt. In this case, if the poison is removed by a spell such as _Cure Poison_ , etc. midway, the damage will stop accumulating at that point. However, the damage suffered up to that point will _not_ be recovered. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03771))_

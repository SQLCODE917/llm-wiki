## Statements

- This spell has the effect of accumulating the mental power (points) of those within the area of effect (can be chosen by the caster at will) and creating a sort of accumulation field. Those within range must agree to the spell, and if so, decide how many points of mental power (points) they want to pour into the accumulation field. The caster him or herself can also pour their mental power (points) into the pool. Those who agree to the spell will have their mental power (points) _reduced_ by the amount they sent to the accumulation field (at this time, if their mental power/points are reduced to 0, they will lose consciousness), but they can freely _draw out_ the mental points stored in the accumulation field and use them to cast magic, etc. However, when your mental power (points) suffer damage from certain monster attacks or spells such as _Mental Attack_ , etc. you _cannot_ substitute them with mental points from the collection field. It's only effective if you try to use your mental power (points) voluntarily. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02873))_
- If the priest's focus is interrupted or the spell is canceled by _Dispel Magic_ , etc. the mental power (points) accumulated at that moment will be released and disappear. Those who agreed to the spell _cannot_ take back the mental power (points) poured into the collection field. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02874))_
- Also, those who do not agree _cannot_ be forced into this spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02875))_

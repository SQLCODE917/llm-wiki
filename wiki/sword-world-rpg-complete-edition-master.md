### What is a Role-Playing Game (RPG)?

- Before playing a game, the game master must prepare what's called a scenario . You may either create your own, or you may use an existing one. A number of scenario collections have already been produced for Sword World RPG , and will continue to be released in the future. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00010))_

- The game proceeds according to a prepared scenario. The game master is the one who facilitates the scenario, while the players play the role -- of the characters --in the scenario. If you think of the game master as a director and the players as actors, it's similar to making a movie or a play. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00011))_

- However, there is something that's very different from film or theater. Which is: the players do not know the scenario. They only know the characters they're taking on. The players must make their characters act based on their abilities and characteristics. The game master's scenario does not provide any dialogue, etc., for the characters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00012))_

- Neither the game master nor the players know whether the characters will be able to kick the thugs out of the tavern and become heroes of justice , or get beaten by the thugs and instead become heroes of comedy . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00034))_

Defense points in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition are calculated by applying defense power to the _Key Number = Expected_ Equivalents table (No Critical), then adding the adventurer level to the result. This calculation is used for both characters and monsters.

This concept is detailed in Chapter 13.5 of the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.

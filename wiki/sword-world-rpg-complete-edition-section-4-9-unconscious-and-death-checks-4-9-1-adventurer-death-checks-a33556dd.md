# 4.9 Unconscious and Death Checks / 4.9.1 Adventurer Death Checks

## Statements

- Any adventurer who falls to 0 or negative life force is now at risk of dying . You must roll the dice to determine if your character survives. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02321))_
- This check is made using a life force resistance roll . The baseline score is life force resistance (adventurer level + life force bonus) and the target score is 7 . If the character's life force is negative , the negative amount will be used as a penalty . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02322))_
- Ducard II, with a life force of -4 , must make a death check . His life force resistance is 5 (adventurer level 2, life force bonus +3). Since Ducard II has a life force of -4 , that will also be the penalty. His result after rolling 2D is 7 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02326))_
- 5 (life force resistance) + 7 (roll) - 4 (penalty) = 8 , which is greater or equal than 7. Ducard II survives. If the result of this death check had been 6 or lower, Ducard II would have met an untimely end . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02327))_
- His result after rolling 2D is 7 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02326))_

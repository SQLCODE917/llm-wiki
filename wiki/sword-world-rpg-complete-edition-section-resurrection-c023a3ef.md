# [ Resurrection

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Resurrection is a spell that brings back lost life and resurrects the dead. In order to bring someone back to life, you need something that is strongly connected to the dead person who is the target of the spell. If you have a corpse, that's fine, but if you don't, you'll need items that were loved by the deceased. Also, if the soul is not in normal condition, the spell has no effect. In other words, this spell is useless if the soul is destroyed, and those who have become undead must first save their soul with the Save Soul spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03177))_
- The effect of this spell is not guaranteed. The more time has passed _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03178))_
- since you died, the less likely you are to come back to life. If you die within one day, you can be resurrected with a final score of 20 or greater, but for each day that passes, the required final value score will increase by 1, such as 21, 22, 23...... etc. Once this spell fails, the caster cannot attempt Resurrection on the same dead person (it will always fail) until their magic power increases. Also, since it's not possible to reverse your age, those who die due to old age cannot be saved. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03180))_
- You can add +6 to the final score of the spell if you have a corpse. However, it must not be in a state where it would die immediately even if it came back to life, such as a decapitated corpse, etc. The same goes for rotting corpses. These corpses will only be treated as something connected to the dead person . If they died due to poison, etc. or if their life force was dealt damage by weapons or magic, you must cast Cure Poison or Cure Wounds beforehand. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03181))_
- Immediately after being resurrected, they have almost no ability to act. It takes one week for the body to return to its normal state and to be able to function without hindrance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03182))_

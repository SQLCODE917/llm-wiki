## Statements

- There are several types of skills, which are divided into several groups. For each skill, a character's proficiency is represented by a **level** from 0~10. 0 levels indicates that someone is completely unrelated to that skill, while 10 levels represents the highest proficiency that a normal human can attain. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00224))_
- Skills are referred to by names such as _fighter_ , _thief_ , _merchant_ , etc. As you can see, they have a strong connotation of professional job functions rather than skills. It is also possible for one character to acquire (have levels in) more than one skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00225))_
- Those who have these skills are often referred to by their skill names. So instead of _someone who has the fighter skill_ , they are simply called _fighters_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00226))_
- Half-elves have a longer life span than humans due to their elven characteristics, with a life span of around 200 years. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00227))_
- Half-elves vary in their traits depending on whether they were raised by humans or by elves. When raised by elves, they're similar to those of elves. When raised by humans, they're not all that different from humans. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00228))_

# Scenario Collections

The _Sword World RPG Scenario Collections_ are published works that provide pre-made scenarios for Sword World RPG game masters. These collections save game masters the trouble of creating their own scenarios and also serve as useful models when creating custom scenarios.

## Key Points

- **Source**: Published by Fujimi Dragon Books
- **Purpose**: To save game masters the trouble of creating their own scenarios
- **Content**: Pre-made scenarios for gameplay
- **Limitations**:
  - Some collections follow the old version of rules
  - Since there are almost no revisions, they can be used nearly as-is
  - Details of monsters and magical items listed in individual scenarios may differ from the rules in this book
  - When using these scenarios, players should follow the details as described in the scenario, with the exception of item prices
  - Item prices in the scenarios should be followed, as the information in this book has been modified for more general use
  - Game balance within the scenario may not be fully supported due to price differences

## Statements

- The ancient kingdom of Kastuul, which fell more than 500 years ago, is sometimes referred to as the magical kingdom . As its nickname suggests, Kastuul is said to have accomplished various miracles through the power of magic. It is this magical power that is known as high ancient and is the magic that the sorcerers of today have inherited. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00240))_
- With the fall of Kastuul, much of high ancient was lost, but some has survived to the present day. The skill that can handle it is the sorcerer skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00242))_
- Many sorcerers are also sages. The rules do not require one character to acquire both of these skills, but if they are acquired at the same time, it gives them a significant advantage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00243))_

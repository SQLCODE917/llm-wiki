- Satyrs are a forest-dwelling race with the upper body of a hairy human, pointed ears, short horns sprouting from their heads, and the lower body of a goat. Their staple food is nuts and berries, they have gentle personalities, they're fond of alcohol, music, and women, and they love festivities. They don't like fighting and will run away if danger approaches. Female satyrs are called she-satyrs. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07396))_
- They always carry a flute and can manipulate human emotions with their melodies. Humans who hear the satyr's flute may suddenly become frightened and run away, get excited and start dancing, or become lewd and start taking off their clothes. To resist this, you must succeed on a mental power resistance roll against target score 10. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07397))_

- Healers can help a wounded person recover their life force naturally. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03650))_

- The patient can recover life force equal to the result plus the healer's intelligence bonus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03651))_

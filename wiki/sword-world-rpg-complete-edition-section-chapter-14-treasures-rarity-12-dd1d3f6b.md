## Statements

- The effect of this item activates when the wearer directly looks at their target. If the target fails a _mental power resistance roll_ with a _target score of 16_ , they become affected by the effect. The effect cannot manifest on more than one target at a time. The effect continues as long as the item is worn. Once affected, the target will not change their beliefs no matter how they are persuaded or what evidence they are shown. However, if the wearer of the necklace themselves stops believing in the truth, the necklace's effect will disappear. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06666))_
- Targets are limited to humans, fae, youma, titans, and animals. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06667))_

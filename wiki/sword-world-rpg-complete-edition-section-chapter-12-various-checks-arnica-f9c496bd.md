## Statements

- This is a perennial plant that's often found in high mountains. Each plant has 2 to 3 flowers. The flowers can alleviate fever, and when used on a patient suffering from a fever, they'll receive a +2 bonus on progression/healing checks. One flower is enough for one day for an adult. The flowers can only be stored for 2 to 3 days. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-05025))_

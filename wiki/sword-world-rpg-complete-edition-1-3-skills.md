## Source record

Chapter 1.3 Skills from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering skill basics, types, and adventurer skills.

## Key supported claims

- Skills in Sword World RPG are numeric values that describe character characteristics and have greater impact on actions than ability scores (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.14-16).
- Skills are grouped into adventurer skills and general skills, with adventurer skills being acquired by player characters and general skills being useful during adventures (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.14-16).
- Adventurer skills include fighter, thief, priest, sorcerer, shaman, sage, ranger, bard, dark priest, and dragon priest, with the latter two not available to player characters (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.14-16).
- The highest level among adventurer skills determines a character's adventurer level, which is a key measure of adventurer capability (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.14-16).

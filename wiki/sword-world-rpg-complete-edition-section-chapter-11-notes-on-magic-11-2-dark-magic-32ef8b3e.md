- As mentioned earlier, these runes, like holy magic, use the gods as its source of power. However, it is derived from the power of the gods of evil (wicked gods) such as Phalaris, who are believed to cause harm to us humans. For this reason, the rune masters of these runes are both hated and feared by the general public. A rune master who uses dark magic is called a dark priest . In a narrow sense, only the priests of Phalaris are sometimes called dark priests. ### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 11.2 Dark Magic / [ Mental Attack ]

- This is a very special spell. Before using this spell, you must declare in advance how many points of mental power (points) you will use. You can reduce the target's mental power (points) by twice the mental power (points) consumed. The caster can use any number of points up to the amount of mental power they have. As a result, if the target's mental power (points) reaches 0, the opponent will lose consciousness. However, mental power (points) will not fall below 0. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05777, source-range-0b9dec90-05778))_
- Note that this spell does not allow the target to reduce damage using adventurer level. Also, the caster cannot add their magic power to the damage dealt, and cannot divide the mental power consumed by the spell by their level (for both a caster with a 1st level dark priest skill and a caster with a 3rd level dark priest skill, the mental power consumed is the same). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05779))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 11.2 Dark Magic / [ Steal Life ]

- This spell combines the effects of both Wounds and Cure Wounds , allowing the caster to absorb life force (points) from the target to recover when the caster is wounded. First, make a strike power 10 check for damage dealt to the target, and the caster's life force (points) will be restored by the same number of points. If you expand the effect and steal life force (points) from multiple people, you can recover the total amount of life force (points) you stole. However, you cannot increase your life (points) higher than your original score. Also, like Wounds , it has no effect at all if the opponent successfully resists, and it has no effect on opponents that don't have normal life, such as undead, skeleton warriors, etc. This spell also cannot be used if the dark priest him or herself is undead. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05852))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 11.2 Dark Magic / [ Rot ]

- This spell has the effect of accelerating the rate of decay and causing the target to rot faster. Objects that had this spell cast on them will decay very quickly. Decay, which normally takes two to three days, will be reduced to half an hour. As long as an object is perishable, it will decay, even if under conditions where decay would normally not progress, such as the environment being below freezing, or being soaked in a saturated brine. However, this has no effect on metals or rocks, and it has no effect on living creatures at all. Targets are limited to non-living meat, plants, fibers, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05927))_
- This spell can reduce the time it takes for decay to complete by consuming extra mental power. If double the normal amount of mental power is consumed, the target will decay in 15 minutes, and if triple , the target will decay in 10 minutes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05928))_
- Even if the spell is removed with Perfect Cancellation , etc. the target will not return to the state it was in before it decayed (it will return with the counter magic Preservation . See p. 102). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05929))_

- This spell has the effect of taking away freedom of a part of the target's body, such as a limb, etc. Those who've had this spell cast on them will suffer various penalties depending on the body part. If freedom of your head is taken away, you will be unable to see, hear, speak, or smell. As a result, it'll be difficult to willingly take action. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05941))_

## Statements

- A monster level’s score is basically set so that if it's equal to a character's adventurer level, the character will be stronger. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06178))_
- A monster's monster level is a rough guide to its strength when it appears in a scenario. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06178))_
- If they're 1-2 levels higher, you’ll be evenly matched, and if they’re 3 levels higher, they’ll be a formidable enemy. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06178))_
- When introducing them into a scenario, assume that enemies of the same or 1 level lower than a character's adventurer level will appear frequently, while an enemy of 2-3 levels greater will appear at the climax. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06179))_

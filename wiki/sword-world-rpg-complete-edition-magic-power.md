Magic power in Sword World RPG is a score given to adventurers who use magic. It determines the strength of spells they can cast and is calculated as:

**magic power** = magic skill level + intelligence bonus

If a character has multiple magic skills, they must calculate the magic power for each separately.

## Example:

An adventurer with priest skill level 1, shaman skill level 2, and 13 intelligence (+2 bonus) would have a magic power of _3_ in holy magic and a magic power of _4_ in spirit magic.

Magic power determines the effectiveness of spells cast by rune masters, with higher power allowing for more powerful spells. A rune master can use all magic up to their skill level, but cannot use magic of a higher level than their current skill.

Magic power is also used in resistance checks when casting spells on others. When an adventurer casts resistible magic on a monster, the caster must make a success roll using their magic power as the baseline score and the monster's mental point resistance as the target score.

Similarly, when a monster casts magic on an adventurer, the adventurer must make a mental power resistance roll using their mental power resistance as the baseline score and the monster's magic intensity as the target score.

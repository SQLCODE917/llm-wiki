## Statements

- Rangers have a good sense of direction and can map out the places they've been. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03246))_
- When creating a map, the game master determines a difficulty, and a success roll must be made to determine whether or not the map was drawn correctly. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03246))_

## Technical atoms

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03244))_

> This is the ability to search for hidden things. The game master should determine the difficulty depending on what’s hidden. Since it's an ability that relies on feeling out of place in nature, it can _only_ be used outdoors.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03246))_

> You can also determine direction from the position of the sun or stars.

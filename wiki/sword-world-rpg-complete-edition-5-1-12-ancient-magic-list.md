## Source record

This section details the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd level ancient magic spells available in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition. It includes spells such as Unlock, Energy Bolt, Enchant Weapon, and many others, each with specific costs, effects, and limitations.

## Key supported claims

- Unlock allows you to deal with locks, doors, and lids (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-71).
- Kindle ignites flammable materials (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-71).
- Enchant Weapon adds +5 to weapon's strike power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-71).
- Protection reduces damage dealt to life force by 1 point (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-71).
- Items gain permanent magical power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-71).

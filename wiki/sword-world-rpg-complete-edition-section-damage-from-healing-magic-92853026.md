## Statements

- Undead that act with negative life force will suffer damage from healing magic that grants positive life force. They will suffer damage from Cure Wounds , and if Healing or Refresh are cast on them and they fail to resist, their life points will immediately become 0. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08058))_

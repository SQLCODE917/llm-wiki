## Source record

This section covers the use of common runes, which were invented by Mana Rai, the great sage of Oran. These runes involve putting the power of ancient magic into items, mainly rings, and activating them using a password in common.

## Key supported claims

- Common runes were invented by Mana Rai, the great sage of Oran, and involve putting ancient magic into items like rings (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.108-108).
- Common runes consume mental power, and the amount is determined by each rune; anyone knowing the password can use them (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.108-108).
- Theoretically, any ancient magic can be made into common runes, but the sorcerers' guild limits what gets put on the market to protect the reputation of magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.108-108).
- Common runes can be expanded by consuming extra mental power, but increasing the final score is not possible (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.108-108).
- Common runes cannot reduce the amount of mental power consumed, no matter how high your rune master skill is (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.108-108).

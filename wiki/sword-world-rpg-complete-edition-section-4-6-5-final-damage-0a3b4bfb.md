- The final damage your character suffers from a monster equals the monster's strike points minus the result of your defense roll plus your character's damage reduction . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02227))_
- If the result of Ducard II's defense roll is 0 (roll 3) or 6 (roll 12), respectively, the final damage he will suffer is calculated to be: _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02230))_
- If the defense roll came up double ones , no such calculation would be made, and the goblin's 7 strike points would be the damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02232))_
- If final damage is 0 or negative , that means you took no damage at all. The monster's attack was completely blocked by your armor. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02233))_

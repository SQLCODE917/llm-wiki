## Statements

- Some spells that affect a space or object can have their volume expanded. If the volume is expanded by two or three times...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... Rounding should be handled the same way you would for area expansion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01234))_
- Since it' s the volume that gets expanded, the radius of a sphere, a side of a cube, etc., can only be increased by the cube root of the expansion rate. Please see Table 5-1: Area and Size Expansion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01235))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, target volume is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01236))_

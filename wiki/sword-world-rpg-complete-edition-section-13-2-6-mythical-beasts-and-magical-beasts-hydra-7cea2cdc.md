## Statements

- The hydra is a terrifying serpent with nine heads and a total length of 10 meters. They mainly live in lakes and rivers, but can also act on land without penalty. In combat, a hydra attacks with its nine heads. At this time, it can attack a single target 9 times, or aim at different targets. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07764))_
- If you attack a hydra with a bladed weapon and deal 6 or more points of damage (20 or more if defense points are taken into account) in one blow, you will have cut off one head, and the hydra's number of attacks will be reduced by 1. A severed head will grow back after 5 rounds, restoring the number of attacks, and also recovering 6 life points. However, if a head is dealt fire or electric damage immediately after it falls off, that head will not regenerate. Once its life points reach 0, its heads will no longer regenerate. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07765))_

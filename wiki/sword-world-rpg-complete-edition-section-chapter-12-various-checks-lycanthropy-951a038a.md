## Statements

- Progression Speed/Progression Healing Score=Special/7/- (16) Fatal Depth=None _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04992))_
- This illness, which turns humans into lycanthropes, is thought to be different from normal illnesses caused by abnormalities in spirit power. Some sages claim that this is a type of curse (although these sages' claim is not accepted, as it cannot be removed with a Remove Curse spell). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04994))_
- Anyone wounded by a lycanthrope must roll 2D. If the roll is a 6 or less, you'll be infected with lycanthropy (the game master must secretly roll the dice if the adventurer does not know that lycanthropy is an infectious disease). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04995))_
- The progression of lycanthropy differs from the rules of normal illnesses. Those infected with lycanthropy will have swollen wounds and a high fever. This fever continues endlessly. Every night from that day onwards, compare 2D + 3 with the number of days since the new moon (if it's 16 days or more, then 30 - the number of days ). Once the score is equal to or less than the number of days, the character becomes a full lycanthrope and transforms into a beast form. This cannot heal naturally. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04996))_
- Before becoming a full lycanthrope, it can be cured with a spell such as the holy magic Cure Disease , etc. (the target score is 16), but once you become a beast, you cannot be cured again. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04997))_

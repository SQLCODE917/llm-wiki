# 《 Damage Expansion 》

## Related pages

## Statements

- For spells that deal damage, expansion can increase the certainty of that damage. If you double or triple the amount of mental power you consume...... you can make a damage check (strike roll) twice or three times . The damage you will deal is equal to the greatest of all the strike rolls you made in this way ( not the total). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01247))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, damage certainty is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01248))_

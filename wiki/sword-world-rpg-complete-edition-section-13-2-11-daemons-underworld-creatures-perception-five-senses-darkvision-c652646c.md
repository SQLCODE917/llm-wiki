## Statements

- A grunel is a humanoid lesser daemon with steel-blue skin. It's characterized by its long tail, which it can manipulate freely like a whip to attack or entangle enemies. If it uses its tail to entangle, treat this as a strangling attack (see p. 236). The target will be unable to move, and the grunel can attack with the weapon in its right hand. They have the ability to use ancient magic. They seem to have been especially proficient at enchanting, and often carry a magical sword. If a grunel has a magic sword, add a bonus of 1 point to both its attack points and strike points. They can also use dark magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06021))_

A member of the fairy family that lives in the forest, resembling a grassrunner but smarter, with the ability to shapeshift into forest animals.

## Characteristics

The Pooka is a member of the fairy family that lives in the forest, and looks similar to a grassrunner, but is a little smarter. They're not in the habit of wearing clothes. However, they don't always wander around naked; they have the ability to transform into forest animals, and most of the time they live as animals.

## Habitat and Behavior

Pookas live in forests and are friendly to characters they meet in the forest. They will contact them in an attempt to befriend them. However, they like mischief, and in order to surprise you, they will sometimes call out to you while disguised as an animal, hiding their appearance, etc. However, once you get in touch with them, they will help you in many ways. They will guide you through the forest and tell you where dangerous places are. In addition, they will show lifelong loyalty to a character who saves them from danger, and will act together with them.

## Abilities

Pookas can use spirit magic. Their shaman skill level is 3. They can transform into forest animals such as foxes, fawns, rabbits, and squirrels. This transformation cannot be detected even by using _Sense Magic_ or _Sense Aura_.

## Key Concepts

- **Shapeshifting**: Pookas can transform into forest animals, such as foxes, fawns, rabbits, and squirrels, without detection. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Spirit Magic**: Pookas can use spirit magic with a shaman skill level of 3. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Forest Life**: Pookas live in forests and are friendly to characters they meet in the forest. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)

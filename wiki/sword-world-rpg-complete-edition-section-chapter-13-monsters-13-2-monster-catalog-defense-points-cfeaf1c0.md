## Technical atoms

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04640, source-range-870a16bc-04644))_

> This represents the degree to which the monster can evade attacks. In order for a character to hit a monster with an attack, they must succeed on a success roll using these evasion points as the target score and their attack power as the baseline score. If you fail, your character's attack will miss. Monster level, like adventurer level, prevents all damage, but a monster's defense points _already_ include damage reduction due to monster level. Therefore, _only_ defense points should be applied

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04642))_

> Just as characters can reduce damage through armor, monsters also have thick skin, shells, scales, etc.

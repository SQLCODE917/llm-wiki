# **Monster Magic Power**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Monster magic power is predetermined for each monster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07987))_
- The magic procedure is resolved the same way regardless of whether a _monster_ or an _adventurer_ is casting the spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07988))_
- Using that as the target score, everyone on the side on which it was cast must attempt a mental power resistance roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07988))_
- If they succeed, the magic will _not_ be cast or its effect will be _reduced_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07988))_
- In other words, you roll 2D and add it to your _magic power_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07988))_
- If they fail, the magic will be cast with its full effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07988))_

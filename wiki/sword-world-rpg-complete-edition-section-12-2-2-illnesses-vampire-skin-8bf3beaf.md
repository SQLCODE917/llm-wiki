## Statements

- This is an illness in which the power of dark spirits becomes unnecessarily strong, causing a hypersensitivity to light. At depth 1, painful blisters resembling burns accompany areas exposed to sunlight. At depth 2, vision is weakened and most success rolls suffer a -2 penalty. At depth 3 you lose your vision, and at depth 4 you fall into a coma. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06748))_

# Chapter 1: Character

This chapter introduces the fundamentals of character creation and development in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition. It outlines the core concepts and procedures for building a character within the game's framework.

## Key Concepts

- **Character Creation**: The process of building a character, including choosing a race, class, and other defining characteristics.
- **Attributes**: Core traits that define a character's capabilities and limitations.
- **Skills**: Abilities that characters can develop and improve over time.
- **Equipment**: Items and gear that characters can wield and utilize in their adventures.

## Purpose

The chapter serves as a foundational guide for players to understand how to create and develop their characters, ensuring they are well-equipped for the challenges of the Sword World.

## Procedure

1. Choose a race and class for your character.
2. Determine your character's attributes and skills.
3. Select appropriate equipment and gear.
4. Review and finalize your character's profile.

## Examples

The chapter provides examples of character builds to help players understand the mechanics of character creation and development.

## Exceptions

While the chapter provides general guidelines, specific rules and exceptions may apply based on the game's context and the Dungeon Master's discretion.

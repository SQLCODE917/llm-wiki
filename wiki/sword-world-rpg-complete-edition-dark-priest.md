A **dark priest** is a rune master who uses dark magic. They are practitioners of the wicked gods' magic, such as Phalaris, and are both hated and feared by the general public due to historical associations with slavery and the ancient kingdom.

## Role and Perception

In Alecrast, dark priests are respected in a sense by the general public because they have special powers called magic. However, public sentiment towards magic is far from good. This is because, in the days of the ancient kingdom, citizenship was not recognized for anyone _except_ rune masters, and all other people were used as slaves. Therefore, there is a great sense of crisis regarding the power of rune masters (especially sorcerers). Some believe that once their numbers become sufficient and they regain the power they had during the ancient kingdom, the age of darkness may come again.

Although dark priests are not openly persecuted due to the necessity of their presence, they are also viewed with fear and disgust by the general public. Countries that are negative towards magic sometimes have policies that exile rune masters from the country. Of course, these people also rely on magical power when they themselves need it.

## Magic Usage

Dark priests can use most of the basic holy magic, in addition to wicked magic that the gods of light do not allow (do not give power to). Furthermore, just as there is special holy magic that is unique to each god among holy magic, there is also special dark magic.

Dark priests can use a variety of spells, including:

- **Divination**: Ability to pray and communicate with a god.
- **Vow**: Ability to make a voluntary vow and have a god protect you. Wicked gods often impose harsh punishments on actions that violate their vows.
- **Basic dark magic spells** of various levels (1st through 10th), including:
  - **Wounds**: Cuts up the opponent's body, dealing damage.
  - **Mental Attack**: Reduces the target's mental power.
  - **Deafness**: Takes away the target's hearing.
  - **Blindness**: Takes away the target's vision.
  - **Poison**: Creates a poison within the target's body.
  - **Control Imp**: Summons and controls an imp familiar.
  - **Steal Life**: Absorbs life force from a target.
  - **Curse**: Imposes penalties that are not directly life-threatening.
  - **Create Zombie**: Resurrects the deceased as a zombie slave.
  - **Mesmerize**: Hypnotizes the target and makes them move at will.
  - **Rot**: Accelerates the decay of objects.
  - **Cripple**: Takes away freedom of a part of the target's body.
  - **Summon Servant**: Summons animals that represent a god.
  - **Plague**: Infects the target with a highly contagious plague.
  - **Recall Spirit**: Brings a soul back to life using a sacrifice.
  - **Call God**: Summons a dark god into the body of a sacrifice.

Dark priests are also capable of using special dark magic spells, which are unique to the wicked gods they worship.

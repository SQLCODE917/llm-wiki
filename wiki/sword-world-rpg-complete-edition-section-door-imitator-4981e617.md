# Door Imitator

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is an imitator in the shape of a door. As soon as you touch the knob, it'll attack. Anyone hit by a door imitator's attack on the first round will have one arm grabbed and will suffer a -4 penalty to evasion on subsequent rounds. Y ou can only use your other arm in combat, and your movement is restricted, so you cannot use ancient magic. The method of shaking your arm free is the same as strangling (see p. 236), but you will not suffer any damage from being grabbed, and you'll still be able to make sound. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07278))_

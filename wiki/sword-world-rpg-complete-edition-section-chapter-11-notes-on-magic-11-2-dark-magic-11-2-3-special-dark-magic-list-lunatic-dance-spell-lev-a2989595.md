## Statements

- This magic does not require the spell to be chanted. Instead, you must dance with complex gestures. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06039))_
- Those who see this dance and fail a resistance roll will go insane and begin dancing in response to the caster's dance. Even if you go insane, you won't go berserk, but you will no longer be able to act rationally. This madness lasts for an additional 12 hours even if the caster stops dancing. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06040))_
- This magic can extend the amount of time the target remains insane after the dance ends, if the caster consumes extra mental power to expand the effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06041))_

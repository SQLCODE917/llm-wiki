In _Sword World RPG_, poison is a substance that disrupts the balance of spirit power within the body. It is distinct from illness, which is a state where the spirit power remains unbalanced and in a fixed state. Poisons force an imbalance in the spirit power within the body, and as long as the poison remains in the body, even if the symptoms disappear, the balance of spirit power will be disrupted and the symptoms will appear again. However, if the poison itself is cleared away, the spirit power within the body will naturally return to a balanced state and restore health.

## **Types of Poison**

Poisons are sometimes labeled as **magic**, **mind**, or **damage**, depending on how they affect you. Those that say _magic_ are poisons that have magical effects. Those that say _mind_ are poisons that affect the target's mind. Those that say _damage_ are poisons that damage life force (points).

## **Usage**

The **usage** of the poison is written in parentheses after the name. These are divided into **injected**, **inhaled**, **dose**, and **touch**. _Injected_ poisons are poisons that need to be delivered directly into the bloodstream, usually in liquid form. _Inhaled_ poisons are in gas or powder form and take effect when inhaled through the respiratory tract. _Dose_ poison is used by mixing it into food or drink and comes in powder or liquid form. _Touch_ poison comes in liquid or powder form and can penetrate through the skin.

## **Toxicity Score**

Each poison has a **toxicity score**. When the poison enters the body, make a life force resistance roll (for monsters, a life _point_ resistance roll) using the toxicity score as the target score. If you fail, the effect will appear, but if you succeed, the effect will only appear incomplete or won't appear at all.

## **Treatment**

Poisons that have entered the body can be cleared using specific antidotes (if any), the holy magic _Cure Poison_ or _Refresh_, or the spirit magic _Restore Health_.

To remove a poison with magic, the caster's final score (magic power + 2D roll) must be greater than or equal to the poison's **toxicity score**. Once a detox fails, the same caster cannot attempt detox magic until their magic power increases (and even if they try, they will always fail).

The spirit magic _Purification_ can make liquid poison harmless if the final score when casting the magic is greater than or equal to than the toxicity score. However, it's _not_ possible to drive off a poison that's _already_ entered the body.

## **Resistance**

Poisons are not effective against everything. It has no effect on monsters that have no life such as undead, golems, etc. Some mythical and magical beasts have strong life force that makes them immune to poison. Some poison effects may or may not affect specific monsters.

Monsters that use poison as weapons are immune to their _own_ poisons (but are still affected by _other_ poisons).

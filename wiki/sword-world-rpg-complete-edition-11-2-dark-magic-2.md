## Source record

Chapter 11.2 Dark Magic from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition covers rules for dark magic spells, including Recall Spirit, Call God, and various special dark magic spells.

## Key supported claims

- Recall Spirit spell requires a final score of 20 or greater to be fully effective (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.147-148).
- Call God spell requires a final score of 30 or greater while defeating the sacrifice's resist for full effect (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.147-148).
- Special dark magic spells are unique to each wicked god and can be used by priests of that god (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.147-148).

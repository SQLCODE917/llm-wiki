# **Who Knows Lost Spell**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- There are some sorcerers who, as a result of years of hard work or through sheer luck, have acquired lost spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07996))_
- You can learn lost spells by learning from these sorcerers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07996))_
- Lost spells cannot be used simply by increasing your sorcerer skill level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07998))_
- You must know of a spell’s existence and learn how to use it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07998))_
- The learning methods are as follows. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07999))_
- However, if the game master wishes, spells _not_ on the list may be designated as _lost spells_ , and some or even _all_ of the spells on the list may be designated as _general spells_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-08000))_
- As a guideline, _Table 17-1: Lost Spells_ is included, and the individual detail section of those spells also says _lost_ under their type. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-08000))_
- Which spells are considered _lost_ is entirely up to the game master's choice. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-08000))_

## Technical atoms

> However, even if you learn a lost spell, you cannot _use_ it unless your sorcerer skill is of the _spell's level or greater._ If your skill level is insufficient, you must wait until your skill level increases before you can use it.
_(source: Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07997))_

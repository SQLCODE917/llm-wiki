# Duration=Permanent

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about duration=permanent:

## Statements

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.12 Ancient Magic List / Duration=Permanent

- This spell has the effect of giving magic resistance to objects that do not have it. If the targeted object is exposed to another spell that's not _detection_ type, it will resist that spell unless the opponent succeeds on a success roll against a target score equal to the final score from when _Magic Protection_ was cast. If a successful resist, the opponent’s spell will either completely lose its effect, or will take only an incomplete effect. If the resist fails, the spell will take its full effect, and furthermore the _Magic Protection_ spell itself will be removed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01589))_

- Even spells with a _resist_ of _none_ can be resisted by _Magic Protection_ . In this case, the spell will take no effect at all. For example, if you cast _Kindle_ on a bundle of straw that has _Magic Protection_ cast on it, if _Kindle_ cannot break through the protection of _Magic Protection_ (the final score is insufficient), it won't be able to ignite. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01590))_

- This spell is powerless against spells that belong to _detection_ . Therefore, objects with this spell cast on them _will_ respond to _Sense Magic_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01591))_

- Also, if you cast it on something that naturally has resistance (humans, fae, youma, etc.), it will have no effect at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01592))_

- This spell creates a puppet golem called a bone servant from the bones of a living creature. The bones used for this spell can be human or animal, but they must be of a certain size and consistency, such as ribs or femurs, as very small bones (such as teeth) or fragments are of no use. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01723))_

- A bone servant appears in the form of a skeleton. The form it takes depends on the bones used in the spell. If you use human bones, it'll take the form of a human skeleton, such as a skeleton (see p. 215) or a skeleton warrior (see p. 223), or if you use animal bones, it'll take the form of an animal skeleton. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01724))_

- See p. 223 for bone servant details when using human bones. If bones of other animals are used, the game master is responsible for creating the details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01725))_

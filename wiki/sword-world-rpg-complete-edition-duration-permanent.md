## Statements

- If the targeted object is exposed to another spell that's not _detection_ type, it will resist that spell unless the opponent succeeds on a success roll against a target score equal to the final score from when _Magic Protection_ was cast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01589))_
- This spell has the effect of giving magic resistance to objects that do not have it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01589))_
- If the resist fails, the spell will take its full effect, and furthermore the _Magic Protection_ spell itself will be removed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01589))_
- Even spells with a _resist_ of _none_ can be resisted by _Magic Protection_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01590))_
- For example, if you cast _Kindle_ on a bundle of straw that has _Magic Protection_ cast on it, if _Kindle_ cannot break through the protection of _Magic Protection_ (the final score is insufficient), it won't be able to ignite. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01590))_
- In this case, the spell will take no effect at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01590))_
- This spell is powerless against spells that belong to _detection_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01591))_
- Also, if you cast it on something that naturally has resistance (humans, fae, youma, etc.), it will have no effect at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01592))_
- This spell creates a puppet golem called a bone servant from the bones of a living creature. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01723))_
- The bones used for this spell can be human or animal, but they must be of a certain size and consistency, such as ribs or femurs, as very small bones (such as teeth) or fragments are of no use. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01723))_

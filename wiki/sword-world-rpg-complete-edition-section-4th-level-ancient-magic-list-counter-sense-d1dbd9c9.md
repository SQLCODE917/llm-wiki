## Statements

- Effect=You can negate the power of detection spells and items, and know the type (effect) of spell or item, the one who cast it, their whereabouts, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01721))_
- If you cast this spell, you can know if you or something you're wearing is being detected by a magical item or spell. At the same time, it will also reveal the type (effect) of spell or item used on you, as well as the clear identity of the person who used it (not just knowing their name, but what they look like). After that the effect of the Counter Sense spell vanishes (although you can recast it, of course). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01725))_
- This spell targets spells of the detection type, and items such as crystal orb of clairvoyance that have the same effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01726))_
- Also, the effects of detection items and spells, no matter how long their duration, will vanish after 1 round due to Counter Sense activating. In other words, for 1 round, the opponent's detection spell or item will work normally, and information of what happened during that time will be told to the opponent. They'll also discover that you have used the spell Counter Sense . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01727))_

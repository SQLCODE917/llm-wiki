## Statements

- This doll, which looks like a woman with one arm raised, was made to prevent the loss of important things, so it's endowed with the magical power to _remember_ specific objects or people, and always points in the direction they're located (are). In a state where it does not have anything to _remember_ , or if the thing it _remembers_ has been lost, the doll cannot point anywhere and will just spin around. To make it _remember_ something, you must touch the doll to the object and chant a password. What the doll _remembers_ is displayed on its pedestal, so you can know this if you read low ancient. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06947))_

## Source record

Monster Level and Adventurer Level rules for the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, including level comparisons and scenario scaling.

## Key supported claims

- Monster level is a rough guide to strength when appearing in a scenario (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).
- If monster level equals adventurer level, the character will be stronger (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).
- Monster level scores are set so that 1-2 levels higher means evenly matched, 3 levels higher means formidable enemy (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).
- Enemies of same or 1 level lower appear frequently, 2-3 levels greater at climax (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).
- A crisis in a small village occurs at level 2-4, while a city-wide crisis is at level 9-10 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).

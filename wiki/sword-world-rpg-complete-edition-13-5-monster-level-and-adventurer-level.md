## Source record

Chapter 13.5 of Sword World RPG - Complete Edition covers monster levels, adventurer levels, and how to represent special monsters with character data.

## Key supported claims

- Monster level is a rough guide to strength, matching adventurer level for balance (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).
- Use the Equivalents table (No Critical) for defense points, then add adventurer level (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).
- Evasion points are evasion speed + 8, and resistance scores are each resistance + 8 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).
- A monster's level corresponds to a crisis scale, from village-level to Alecrast-wide (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).
- Special monsters like dark elves can be represented with ability scores and skill levels (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.234-234).

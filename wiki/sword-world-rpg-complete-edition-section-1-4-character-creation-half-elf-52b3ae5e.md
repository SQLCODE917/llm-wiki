## Statements

- Half-elves are halfway between human and elf in terms of ability. Therefore, their dexterity, agility, intelligence, and mental power are slightly higher, while their strength and life force are slightly lower. However, they do not deviate very far from the average and are fully capable of handling all skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00318))_
- If they were raised by elves, there is a restriction that they cannot acquire the priest skill. On the other hand, if they were raised by humans, there is no limit on acquiring skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00319))_
- First, a race must be chosen. Here, we've chosen a human, with average ability scores and no restrictions on skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00322))_

## Statements

- This weapon has the potential to cause fae, youma, or animals of human size or smaller to lose consciousness when you attack them unnoticed from behind. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11638))_
- When struck by such an attack, the target must make a life force resistance roll. The target score is the final score from when the attack hit . If he or she fails, they'll fall unconscious and will not wake up for 2D x 10 minutes . Normal damage is dealt regardless of success or failure. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11639))_
- The attacked opponent gains a +4 bonus to his or her resistance roll if he or she is wearing metal armor. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11640))_

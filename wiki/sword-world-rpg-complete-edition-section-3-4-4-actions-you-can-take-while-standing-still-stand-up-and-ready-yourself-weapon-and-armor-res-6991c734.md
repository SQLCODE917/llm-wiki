## Statements

- A character's arms are largely divided into weapons, shields, and armor. Required strength should not be added up, but rather considered separately for each of these. In other words, a character with 14 strength can wield a weapon with a required strength of 14, wield a shield with a required strength of 14, and wear armor with a required strength of 14. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01410))_

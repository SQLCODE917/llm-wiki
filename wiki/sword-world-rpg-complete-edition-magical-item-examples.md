In the Sword World RPG, a magical item is an object imbued with magical properties that can provide various benefits to the wearer or user. These items can range from simple accessories to powerful artifacts, each with unique characteristics and effects.

## Properties of Magical Items

Magical items in the Sword World RPG are characterized by several key properties:

- **Rarity**: The rarity of an item is indicated by a number, which reflects how uncommon or powerful the item is.
- **Enchanter**: The enchanter is the individual or entity responsible for creating the magical item.
- **Form**: The physical form of the item, such as a robe, cloak, or amulet.
- **Required Strength**: The minimum strength required to wield the item effectively.
- **Base Trade Price**: The base price of the item in gamels, which can vary based on the item's properties and rarity.

### Cloaks

#### Air Cloak
- **Rarity**: 12
- **Enchanter**: Many
- **Form**: Transparent blue cloak
- **Required Strength**: 1 (Defense power 1)
- **Base Trade Price**: 16,000 gamels
- **Powers**: +2 to evasion speed only against projectiles

#### Grey Suit
- **Rarity**: 15
- **Enchanter**: Somska, "The Forger of Colors"
- **Form**: Mithril silver chain mail armor that glows dull grey
- **Required Strength**: 6 (Defense power 16)
- **Base Trade Price**: 160,000 gamels
- **Powers**: +1 to evasion speed and damage reduction
- This mithril chain mail is high-quality.

#### Cold Cloak
- **Rarity**: 18
- **Enchanter**: Many
- **Form**: Cloth armor
- **Required Strength**: 3 (Defense power 3)
- **Base Trade Price**: 250,000 gamels
- **Powers**: +3 to evasion speed and damage reduction. Also gives +3 to resistance to fire-type attacks.

### Accessories

#### Earrings of Interpretation
- **Rarity**: 15
- **Enchanter**: Unknown
- **Form**: Earrings with blue gems set in a teardrop-shaped gold base
- **Base Trade Price**: 96,000 gamels
- **Powers**: Can understand languages you don’t know
- By wearing these earrings, you can understand the contents of conversations in languages you do not know. However, you can only understand conversations you hear through the earrings, you cannot speak, read or write that language.

#### Earrings of Pooka
- **Rarity**: 13
- **Enchanter**: Unknown
- **Form**: Small silver earrings with intricately colored gems
- **Base Trade Price**: 170,000 gamels
- **Powers**: Can shapeshift into a small animal.
- When wearing these earrings and focusing your thoughts, you can transform into small animals such as mice or birds. After shapeshifting, you can return to your original form by either touching the earrings while concentrating, or by using _Dispel Magic_ (target score 15). Additionally, after shapeshifting, you retain the same intelligence you had before the transformation.

#### Container Bracelet
- **Rarity**: 15
- **Form**: A bracelet with a large gem
- **Enchanter**: Many
- **Base Trade Price**: 120,000 gamels
- **Powers**: Sucks in an object
- When you bring an object close to the jewel attached to the bracelet and speak the password in low ancient, the object will be absorbed into the jewel. While the object is absorbed, if you speak the password again, you can retrieve the object.
- This bracelet cannot absorb more than one object at a time, and it cannot absorb anything that exceeds 50 centimeters in length in any dimension.

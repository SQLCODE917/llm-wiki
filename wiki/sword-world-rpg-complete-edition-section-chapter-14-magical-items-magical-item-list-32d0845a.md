- Here is a list of magical items commonly seen in Alecrast. Of course, these are just examples, and the game master is free to create all sorts of other magical items. The base trade price is the price if an adventurer is lucky enough to be able to buy it (though such opportunities are rare). When adventurers sell them to an adventurer's shop or an academy of sages , they're usually accepted at half this price. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09988))_
- In Lomar , the Kingdom of Travelers , there is a huge market called the black market , where magical items are said to be traded. Magical weapons and armor are also said to be similarly sold in Reide , the City of Mercenaries , also located in Lomar. It' s fine if the game master chooses to only allow adventurers to pay for these items when the setting is in these cities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09989))_
- However, those with commands in high ancient can only be handled by those who have the sorcerer skill. - These magical treasures can be understood through spells such as Sense Magic , etc. and their specific power can be appraised using Analyze Enchantment . Even the cheapest ones cost as much as 1000 silver coins, and if they're expensive you can't even put a price on them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09991))_
- Also, even if there's no magical power, books and scrolls may contain high ancient spellbooks or valuable information about the culture of the ancient kingdom, so don't ever treat them lightly. If you're a sage who can read ancient, you can discover the specific value and cost of these items using the treasure appraisal ability. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09992))_

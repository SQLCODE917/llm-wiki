## Source record

Section 9.2 on choosing between target score and difficulty checks, and 9.4 on automatic success restrictions in Sword World RPG.

## Key supported claims

- There are two types of success rolls: target score and difficulty checks. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.135-135)
- The choice depends on whether the character can tell if the action succeeded or failed. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.135-135)
- Actions with clear outcomes use target scores. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.135-135)
- Actions without clear outcomes use difficulty checks. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.135-135)
- Automatic success occurs on double sixes, a 1 in 36 chance. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.136-136)

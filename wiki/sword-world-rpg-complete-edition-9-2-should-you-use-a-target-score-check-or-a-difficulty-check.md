## Source record

Chapter 9.2 discusses when to use target scores versus difficulty checks in Sword World RPG, focusing on the clarity of success or failure for actions.

## Key supported claims

- Target scores are used for actions with clear success or failure (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.135-138).
- Difficulty checks are for actions where success or failure cannot be clearly determined (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.135-138).
- Game masters should decide based on whether characters can tell if action succeeded or failed (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.135-138).
- Difficulty should be clear to players to avoid suspicion (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.135-138).
- Modifiers affect final score of success rolls (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.135-138).

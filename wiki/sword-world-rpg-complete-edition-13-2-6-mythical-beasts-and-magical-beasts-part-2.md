## Source record

Section 13.2.6 describes mythical and magical beasts, including lamia, lizardmen, griffon, manticore, minotaur, unicorn, lynx, sphinx, wyvern, basilisk, sea dragon, wyrm, buckbaird, hydra, and humbaba (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198).

## Key supported claims

- Lamia need bloodsucking to survive, often disguise as human girls (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198).
- Lizardmen have noble and slave types, with nobles using dragon roar magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198).
- Unicorns have healing abilities, can communicate in silent spirit, unknown lifespan (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198).

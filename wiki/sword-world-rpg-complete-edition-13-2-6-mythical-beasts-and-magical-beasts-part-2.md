## Source record

This chapter details various mythical and magical beasts, including griffons, lamia, lizardmen, sphinxes, and others.

## Key supported claims

- The griffon is a magical beast with a lion body, eagle head and wings, and can fly (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198).
- The sphinx is a mythical beast created by ancient sorcerers to protect true knowledge and prevent lost magic spell books (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198).
- Lizardmen are related to dragons and were classified as mythical or magical beasts to avoid premature categorization; exact numbers may be unknown (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198).
- Ramliearth has deployed a forest guard regiment to protect these mythical beasts (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198).
- Buckbairds are bizarre magical beasts created by ancient sorcerers; their nature may be unknown (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198).

In the Sword World RPG, a shield is a defensive item that can be used in combat to reduce damage or increase evasion speed. Shields are categorized into small and large types, each with different properties and requirements.

## Types of Shields

### Small Shield
- **Required Strength**: 1
- **Properties**: Provides a +1 bonus to evasion speed.
- **Usage**: Can be used in combination with a weapon in the other hand.

### Large Shield
- **Required Strength**: 1
- **Properties**: Provides a +1 bonus to evasion speed.
- **Usage**: Can be used in combination with a weapon in the other hand.

## Enhanced Shields

Shields can also be enchanted to provide additional bonuses:

### High-quality Large Shield
- **Required Strength**: 8
- **Properties**: Provides a +1 bonus to evasion speed and damage reduction.
- **Price**: 63,000 gamels (for +1) or 180,000 gamels (for +2) or 270,000 gamels (for +3).

## Usage in Combat

Shields are used in combat to enhance a character's defensive capabilities. When a character wields a shield, they gain a bonus to evasion speed, which helps in avoiding attacks. Additionally, some shields provide damage reduction, which lessens the impact of incoming attacks.

Shields can be used in combination with weapons, but they cannot be used simultaneously with two-handed weapons. When a character is wielding a shield, they can choose to drop the shield or put it away to use a two-handed weapon, or vice versa.

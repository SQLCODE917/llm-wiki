# **(Undine/Water Spirit)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Area=One, or a boat-sized object Duration=1 hour Effect=Eliminates the buoyancy of water, causing floating people and objects to sink Type=Counters (Negates): _Water Walking_ Countered (Negated) By: _Water Walking_ Countered (Overpowered) By: _Create Ghost Ship_ _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02463))_
- If you cast this spell on something under the influence of _Water Walking_ , their effects will negate each other, removing the effects of both spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02465))_
- Those who've had this spell cast on them lose their liquid buoyancy and sink completely to the bottom. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02465))_
- This spell's effect only works on objects up to the size of a small boat. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02465))_

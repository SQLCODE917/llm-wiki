## Statements

- If the attack hits and the opponent is _not_ entangled, the whip will _return_ to the attacker's hand. The attacker may _choose_ , even if it _hits_ , to return the whip to his or her hand _instead_ of letting it entangle the opponent. At this time, roll 2D, and it'll _only_ entangle the opponent if you roll _double ones_ . **Net** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07302))_
- Even if this hits, it _cannot_ deal damage. It’s used in melee, but you can also use it by throwing it. However, its range is limited to 5 meters. **Bola** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07303))_
- However, its range is limited to 5 meters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07303))_

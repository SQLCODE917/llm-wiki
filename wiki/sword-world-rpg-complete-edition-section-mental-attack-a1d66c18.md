# **[ Mental Attack ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The caster can use any number of points up to the amount of mental power they have. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03744))_
- This is a very special spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03744))_
- You can reduce the target's mental power (points) by _twice_ the mental power (points) consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03744))_
- However, mental power (points) will _not_ fall below 0. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03744))_
- As a result, if the target's mental power (points) 142 reaches 0, the opponent will lose consciousness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03744))_
- Also, the caster _cannot_ add their magic power to the damage dealt, and _cannot_ divide the mental power consumed by the spell by their level (for both a caster with a 1st level dark priest skill and a caster with a 3rd level dark priest skill, the mental power consumed is the same). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03745))_
- Note that this spell does _not_ allow the target to reduce damage using adventurer level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03745))_

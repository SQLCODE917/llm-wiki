# **Attack Power Modifier**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Weapons with higher numbers are more likely to hit an opponent, while weapons with smaller numbers are less likely to hit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07709))_
- **Attack power modifier** indicates the modifier added to an adventurer's attack power when using that weapon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07709))_

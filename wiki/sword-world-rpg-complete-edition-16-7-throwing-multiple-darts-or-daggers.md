## Source record

This section details the rules for throwing multiple darts or daggers at once in Sword World RPG, including restrictions on weight, strength requirements, and attack penalties.

## Key supported claims

- All objects thrown at the same time must have the same weight (required strength). (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)
- The total required strength must be up to the character's strength (or half that if it's the thief skill). (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)
- The number thrown at the same time becomes a penalty to attack power; for example, throwing three at once incurs a -3 penalty to attack power for each hit check. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)
- You cannot choose multiple targets; there must always be one target. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)

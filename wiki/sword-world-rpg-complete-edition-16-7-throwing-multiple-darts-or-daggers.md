## Source record

This section details the rules for throwing multiple darts or daggers at once in Sword World RPG, including restrictions on weight, strength requirements, and attack penalties.

## Key supported claims

- All objects thrown at the same time must have the same weight (required strength). (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)
- The total required strength must be up to the character's strength (or half that if it's the thief skill). (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)
- The number thrown at the same time becomes a penalty to attack power; for example, throwing three at once incurs a -3 penalty to attack power for each hit check. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)
- You cannot choose multiple targets; there must always be one target. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)

### `technical-atom-1ff8a4a497150b83` formula

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)

**time** = _-2_ penalty to attack with _dominant_ hand, _-4_ penalty to attack with _opposite_ hand.

### `technical-atom-59223c68d81ce8d9` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)

In the basic rules, characters cannot hold separate weapons in each hand.

### `technical-atom-cac4a750bc96b2c5` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)

Also, the required strength for _one_ of the weapons is limited to _half your own strength (rounded up)_ .

### `technical-atom-d218b1dc5fc194e8` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)

If you’re fighting with the thief skill, the required strength for _both_ weapons is only _half_ the character's strength.

### `technical-atom-83ca853abb8222fb` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)

If you attack with only _one_ hand, there is _no_ penalty, and if you attack with _both_ hands, the penalty is only _-2_ for each.

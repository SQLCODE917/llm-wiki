## Source record

Rule on throwing multiple darts or daggers, covering weight, strength limits, attack penalties, single‑target restriction, and optional nature.

## Key supported claims

- All thrown objects must have the same weight (required strength). (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)
- Total required strength may not exceed character’s strength (or half when using thief skill). (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)
- Number of objects thrown at once incurs a penalty to attack power; e.g., three darts = -3 per hit check. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)
- One target must be chosen; multiple targets are not allowed. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)
- It is possible to throw multiple darts or daggers at once, though the rule is optional. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.268-273)

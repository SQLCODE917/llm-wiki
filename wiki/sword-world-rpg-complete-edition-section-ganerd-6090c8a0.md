# 《 Ganerd 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Although he is said to be the god of thieves, he was originally the god of craftsmanship, and so has believers among craftsmen. He is also considered the god of gifts, as he brings unexpected wealth. Temples dedicated to Ganerd can sometimes be seen in large towns, but his power is by no means great. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02903))_

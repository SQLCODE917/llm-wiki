- A simulacra is a kind of golem built during the ancient kingdom period. A simulacra duplicates the appearance and abilities of an enemy human creature and makes them its own. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05822))_
- They were often used to watch over people who did not use magic, such as slaves and savages, and to guard the places where they worked. A simulacra duplicates the belongings, weapons, armor, etc. of people it sees and attacks them. However, magical weapons and armor cannot be duplicated. Although they look the same, they don't even have magical power. If it duplicates someone who is holding a broadsword +1, it will attack with a broadsword with the same strike power, but will _not_ receive any bonuses to attack power or bonus damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05823))_
- A simulacra's replication is only superficial, and is limited to weapon combat capability. It cannot duplicate someone's personality or even their memories. As far as is currently known, simulacra seem to be unable to speak or use any magic at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05824))_
- The only way to attack a simulacra is to attack directly with a weapon. Although it’s a formidable enemy for a 10th level fighter, it’s not a formidable opponent for a 1st level sorcerer (without the fighter, thief, or ranger skill). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05825))_

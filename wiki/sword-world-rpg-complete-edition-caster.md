# Caster

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about caster:

## Statements

### 《 Damage 》

- In either case, the caster's magic power can be added as bonus damage to the result of the strike roll. Also, the target's adventurer level (or the monster's monster level) is subtracted from the damage. However, there's an exception if you roll double ones during your strike roll. In this case, no matter how much magic power your character (monster) has, they cannot deal damage to the target at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01286))_

### 《 Focus 》

- If the caster suffers damage to their life force or mental power, their focus will be interrupted and the magic's effect will be lost . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01342))_

### 《 Ritual 》

- With these spells, you can add +1 to your final score for each day you perform the ritual. If additional casters who can use the same spell also participate in the ritual, you will gain +1 to your final score for each one. Even humans who cannot use spells can participate in the ritual. In this case, you can gain +1 to your final score for every 10 people (rounded down). However, the ritual procedures are very complicated, so those who have not been taught the procedure in advance will not be able to participate in the ritual. If it's ancient magic, the caster must have been taught at a school such as an academy of sages , etc. and if it's holy (dark) magic, they must be a believer. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01355))_

### 《 Distance 》

- This represents the distance the magic can reach. Basically, magic works on things that the caster can see and are within this distance. Any exceptions will always be noted in the explanation that follows. If the distance is touch , the caster must touch the target with their hand or (for ancient magic) with their magical catalyst (see below). To cast these spells in combat, you must be engaged with the target. At this time, there is no need to roll dice to determine whether you've touched your opponent, like with an attack hit check (you can touch them automatically). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01376))_

### [ Familiar ]

- This spell must always be performed as a ritual. The caster requires 3 full days of preparation, followed by 12 hours of spell chanting. Please refer to Table 5-2: Familiars to see what kind of animal will appear as a familiar in response to a summon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01657))_

- The caster can also borrow mental points from their familiar to cast spells. In this case, the mental power that would've been consumed by the caster will instead be reduced from the familiar's mental points. Of course, you cannot make a substitution that would cause mental points to become negative, and a familiar whose mental points reach 0 will lose consciousness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01659))_

- When a familiar is involved in magic, etc. the caster can use their adventurer level as the baseline score for the resistance roll. When a familiar takes damage, the caster can also use their adventurer level to reduce the damage. If a caster and their familiar are separated by more than 1 kilometer, contact between them will be temporarily interrupted. Until they're both within 1 kilometer of each other again, they cannot share senses, nor can the caster borrow mental points from their familiar. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01662))_

### [ Conceal Self ]

- Distance=Caster Area=Caster Duration=Until focus ends Effect=Makes the caster invisible to others Type=Focus, illusion Expansion=Final score Resist=Negated effect This spell has the effect of completely concealing the caster's presence from others. In other words, not only will they become invisible, you will no longer be able to hear sounds made by the caster, and of course you won't be able to smell them. However, you can feel their presence if you touch them. This spell cannot be cast on others. The duration lasts as long as your focus continues. If you take any action that breaks your focus, the effect of this spell vanishes immediately. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01731))_

### [ Shape Change

- Only the caster's body will change shape, the clothes and accessories they're wearing do not change. They will fall off or break in the process of changing. Also, after changing form, the caster's physical abilities will become the same as the _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01742))_

### [ Paralyze ]

- To maintain this spell, the caster must keep focus. Someone who's had this spell cast on them may only attempt to resist the first time the spell is cast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01772))_

### [ Location ]

- No matter how far away the object is from the caster, the spell will still take effect. Also, the closer you get, the more you can feel its presence, so you can get a rough estimate of the distance. The caster must know the actual object being targeted by the spell well enough to specify it. Therefore, unspecified objects such as a sword or a goblin cannot be detected (it's not possible if it's my favorite sword ). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01794))_

### 5th Level Ancient Magic List [ Apport ]

- This is a spell that allows the caster to teleport their possessions into the palm of their hand. However, even though it's their possession, the caster must have a complete memory of it, and moreover must be able to identify specifically where it's kept. Also, the size of the target is limited to something you can hold in your hand (for example, you can summon a sword, but not armor). If the duration expires or if the spell is canceled, the target immediately returns to its original location. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01799))_

### [ Skeleton Warrior ]

- The caster can give commands to the skeleton warrior in high ancient. Only the caster can give commands. They cannot understand overly complex commands, but they handle simple commands accurately. For example, commands such as kill anyone who enters this room or kill that man are very simple and can be carried out, but a command such as go to the next village and kidnap the chief's daughter cannot be carried out. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01830))_

### [ Sense Lie ]

- By casting this spell, the caster can discover parts that are untrue, exaggerated, and dramatized in conversations they overhear. During the duration, all words that the caster hears are subject to a true-or-false check. However, while you can discover lies, you cannot know truths. You can only know that if it's a lie, it's a lie, and if it's an exaggeration, it's an exaggeration. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01842))_

### [ Translate ]

- Expansion=Duration, effect (see description) Resist=None By casting this spell, the caster can read unknown languages. ### [ Analyze Enchantment ]

- This is a spell that identifies the type of magic being cast on a target and specifies its effect. If it's a regular magical item, you can almost certainly identify the magic. However, some items are safe from the effect of this spell due to the lost spell , Seal Enchantment . When you use this spell on an object that had Seal Enchantment cast on it, its effect cannot be inferred if your final score does not exceed the target score determined by Seal Enchantment . If they fail, the same caster cannot use this spell on the same object until their magic power increases (any attempts will always fail). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01894))_

### Create Undead ]

- Create Undead is a spell that creates undead monsters such as skeletons and zombies. To use this spell, of course, you'll need a corpse (usually a human corpse is used). Depending on the state of decomposition of the corpse, it will determine whether it'll become a skeleton or a zombie. If you use this spell on a mummified or severely decomposed corpse, the corpse will be completely skeletonized and a skeleton will be born. If it's still a corpse before that happens, then a zombie will be born. The caster can give commands to the skeletons and zombies created in this way. The commands must be extremely simple, at best they can be made to protect a specific location. Even walking alongside you as guards is too difficult for them. For details on human skeletons and zombies, see 13.2.9: Undead (see p. 214). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01911))_

### [ Force Field ]

- This spell is invisible around the caster's body, moreover it creates a magical barrier that changes shape with the caster's movement. This barrier works to reduce damage from attacks such as weapons, etc. The damage it can reduce is 30 points. The caster can also add their own damage reduction to this. As a result, most physical attacks should be nullified, but you will be wounded if your defense roll is double ones , or if you receive more than 30 points of damage. Even if you fall from a very high place or a huge piece of rubble falls on you, you will still be wounded if the damage you receive exceeds 30 points. This spell also prevents attacks from magical weapons. However, it cannot prevent damage from pure magic, such as Energy Bolt or Fireball , nor damage from fire, acid, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02123))_

### [ Wraith Form ]

- A caster who casts this spell will be able to separate their spiritual body from their physical body, allowing only their ethereal body to move around freely. This ethereal body is called a wraith. Wraiths are a kind of undead, but they can resume their life activities once they return to their own bodies. If you do not (or cannot) return to your original body after the spell's duration has expired, you will lose complete contact with your body, and must remain adrift for eternity as a wraith. The same applies if your body is destroyed (your life force or life points become 0 or less) while you are a wraith. If the wraith itself is destroyed, the caster dies. In this case, the body becomes nothing more than an empty husk without a soul, so it cannot be brought back to life by any means. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02144))_

### ⬤ Mind Spirits

- This spirit power is at work wherever there are living things with functioning minds. Of course if the caster him or herself is like that, there's no problem. Some of these types of spirits include those that control the nature of the mind, and those that control fluctuation of emotions. It's said that if one emotion has too much of an outburst, it can cause phenomena such as alienation or complete loss of functions of other spirits, which can lead to mental disorders. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02276))_

### 《 Silent Spirit 》

- These are runes used to communicate with spirits. Those who can use these runes can communicate with any spirit. Most spirits speak directly to the mind without using words, but when a caster gives commands to a spirit, they must speak these runes out loud. Also, some fae are well versed in silent spirit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02291))_

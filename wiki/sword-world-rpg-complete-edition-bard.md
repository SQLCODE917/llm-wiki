The bard skill in Sword World RPG allows characters to use magical songs called **spellsongs**. Bards are not considered rune masters, as their magic is only a superficial imitation compared to the runes used by rune masters. However, even those without magical background can use spellsongs, as they only require the superficial form.

## Spellsongs

Spellsongs are songs with magical power created during the ancient kingdom period. Those with the bard skill can use magic by singing these spellsongs, although the magic is weak. Using spellsongs does not consume a bard's mental power, and the effect cannot be expanded.

A spellsong remains in effect as long as the bard sings it. If the song is interrupted, the effect disappears. While singing a spellsong, the bard cannot take any other actions and cannot move more than 3 meters in one round. Unlike focus, spellsongs are not interrupted by damage to life force or mental power (unless unconscious). Resistance rolls are generally made only once at the beginning of the song (depending on the spellsong, they may be on the fourth round). Once successfully resisted, no further rolls are needed.

To use a spellsong, a bard must play an instrument unique to them and sing the lyrics in high ancient. If using a flute, the lyrics are not required. Spellsongs affect everyone who hears them, both friends and enemies, but have no effect on those unable to hear.

Bards can learn one spellsong per bard level, choosing which spellsong to learn. Every time the bard levels up, they must learn a new spellsong.

## Key Concepts

- **Spellsong**: A magical song with effects on listeners.
- **Mental Power Consumption**: Spellsongs do not consume the bard's mental power.
- **Instrument Requirement**: Each bard must have a unique instrument for spellsongs.
- **Lyrics**: Must be sung in high ancient, though meaning is not required.
- **Flute Exception**: Using a flute allows spellsongs without singing lyrics.
- **Effect Duration**: Lasts as long as the bard sings, ending if interrupted.
- **Action Restrictions**: Bard cannot take other actions or move more than 3 meters while singing.
- **Resistance**: Generally rolled once at the start, with some spells having specific rounds.

### Technical atom 6

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01739))_

> By casting this spell, the caster can levitate up to 10 meters above the ground. During this time, you're completely free to adjust the height. You can use other spells while floating, and perform attacks and evasion as usual. However, you cannot take actions that seem impossible depending on the situation (for example, it would be impossible for a caster 10 meters above the ground to engage in melee combat with a monster on the ground). If someone casts _Dispel Magic_ , etc. on you and the spell is removed, you will fall. If you do fall, apply the rules for falling damage (see p. 122).

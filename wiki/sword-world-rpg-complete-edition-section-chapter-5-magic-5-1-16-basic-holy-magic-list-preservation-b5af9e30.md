## Statements

- This is a spell that protects objects that would normally rot from decay. Objects that have this spell cast on them will no longer decay naturally. It can be used to preserve food, and it is also effective in protecting corpses that are planned to be brought back to life in the future with _Resurrection_ . It is also a counter spell to the dark magic _Rot_ , and if cast within the duration of _Rot_ , it can restore things that have decayed back to their original state. However, even if you cast it on something that has completely decayed after the duration of _Rot_ has expired, or something that has naturally decayed, it will _not_ return to its original state. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02839))_
- If the effect of this spell is removed, such as with _Perfect Cancellation_ , etc. the decay will begin to progress at normal rate from that moment on. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02840))_

# Replay Collections

The _Sword World RPG Replay Collections_ are published works that reproduce game sessions of Sword World RPG. These collections help players understand how to actually play the game by showing the progress of the game through conversations between the game master and the players.

## Key Points

- **Source**: Published by Fujimi Dragon Books
- **Purpose**: To help players understand how the game is meant to progress
- **Content**: Actual gameplay reproduced via conversation between game master and players
- **Limitations**: 
  - The first nine volumes (① ~ ⑨) are based on the old version of the rules, not the current rulebook
  - Some descriptions may be contradictory due to rule revisions
  - When playing, players should follow the current rulebook's descriptions
  - These replays are based on actual play but have been edited for readability, with some rule errors corrected
  - They should be considered references, not complete manuals

## Duda RPG Replay Collection

The _Duda RPG Replay Collection_ series is a variant that emphasizes humor, fantasy, and mystery aspects. While it's 99% Sword World RPG, it includes modified rules not found in the standard rulebook, such as **inept detective checks**, to demonstrate its uniqueness. For more information, refer to the Introduction and Commentary sections of _Duda RPG Replay Collection_ ① _: The Lost Detective Appears!_.

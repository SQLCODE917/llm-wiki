## Statements

- This is a spell that identifies the type of magic being cast on a target and specifies its effect. If it's a regular magical item, you can almost certainly identify the magic. However, some items are safe from the effect of this spell due to the _lost spell_ , _Seal Enchantment_ . When you use this spell on an object that had _Seal Enchantment_ cast on it, its effect cannot be inferred if your final score does not exceed the target score determined by _Seal Enchantment_ . If they fail, the same caster cannot use this spell on the same object until their magic power increases (any attempts will always fail). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01869))_

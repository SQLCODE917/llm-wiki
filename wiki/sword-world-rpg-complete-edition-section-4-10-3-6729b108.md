### Magical Weapons and Armor

- In Forcelia, there are magical weapons and armor that were once created during the age of the magical kingdom (Kastuul). These magical weapons and armor are seldom found, and are usually rarely traded. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01017))_
- Characters may obtain these magical weapons and armor through their adventures. The power of magical weapons and armor is represented by a +1 or +2 score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01018))_
- In Forcelia, the limit for this power is +3 . There are only a scant few weapons and armor in the world with +3 power, and any with +4 or more have yet to be found. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01019))_

### 【 Magical Weapons 】

- Magical weapons have exceptional attack power and bonus damage . If you use a +1 magical weapon, you receive a +1 bonus to attack power and bonus damage. If +2, those bonuses are +2, and if +3, they're +3. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01021))_

### 【 Magical Shields 】

- Magical shields increase evasion speed. A +1 magic shield grants +1 evasion speed, +2 if +2. If +3, it is +3. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01024))_

### 【 Magical Armor 】

- Magical armor increases evasion speed and damage reduction . If you wear +1 magical armor, you gain +1 to evasion speed and damage reduction. +2 means +2, and +3 means +3. The damage reduction bonus from magical armor applies only to weapon attacks. It does not reduce damage caused by magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01026))_
- Magical weapons and armor are not usually traded, but if you are very lucky you may be able to buy them if they're +1. A guide to prices can be found in Chapter 14: Treasure (see p. 240). Also, they offer basically no bonus to strike power or defense power, same as required strength. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01028))_
- High-quality ones (made with lower required strength) seem to exist only rarely . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01029))_
- It does not reduce damage caused by magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01026))_

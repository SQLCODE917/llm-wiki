## Statements

- Sailors have the ability to swim. Follow the rules for _swim_ under _common adventurer abilities_ -- replace _adventurer level_ with _sailor skill level_ --and make a check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03526))_
- In order to perform delicate maneuvers such as _surviving a storm_ , _avoiding a reef_ , and _ramming an enemy ship_ , the person at the helm must succeed on a success roll using their _sailor skill level + intelligence bonus_ as the baseline score. At this time, the captain can also give instructions to the helmsman. In this case, make a success roll using the captain's _sailor skill level + intelligence bonus_ as the baseline score. Even if a large ship fails, it will not sink immediately, but it will likely suffer some damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03527))_

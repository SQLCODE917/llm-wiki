## Statements by subsection

### Drop a Weapon, Etc. On the Ground

- Weapons and shields can be dropped on the ground at any time , regardless of whether they're one-handed, twohanded, or both-handed. If you do drop a weapon, etc. consider it as if you never had it in the first place , then choose your action. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00682))_

The treasure appraisal skill is a skill used in the Sword World RPG to determine the value and nature of treasures. It is used by both thieves and sages to identify the worth of items.

## Skill Usage

The treasure appraisal ability can be used to determine the value of various types of treasures, including artwork, gems and precious metals, and magical items. The skill is particularly useful when dealing with items that have magical properties, as it can reveal the true identity of the treasure.

## Rarity and Appraisal

The **rarity** of a magical item is the target score for a success roll when using the sage skill's _treasure appraisal_ ability. If the success roll results in a final score greater than or equal to this rarity, you will discover the true identity of the treasure. Some treasures may have more than one listed rarity, which can result in an incomplete appraisal.

## Appraisal Process

When appraising treasures, the skill allows adventurers to determine if an item is a treasure or junk, and to assess its specific value. For items with magical properties, the skill can reveal the item's magical powers through spells such as _Sense Magic_ and _Analyze Enchantment_.

## Special Considerations

- The skill can be used to identify items that are not immediately obvious as treasures.
- For items with no magical power, books and scrolls may contain valuable information about the culture of the ancient kingdom.
- The final value of the treasure is determined entirely by the player character, although it might not necessarily match the NPC's valuation.

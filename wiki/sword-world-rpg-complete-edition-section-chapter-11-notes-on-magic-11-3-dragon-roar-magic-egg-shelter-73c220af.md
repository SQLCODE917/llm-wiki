## Statements

- Wrap yourself in a dragon egg shell. The caster will fall into a magical sleep inside this egg. During this time, _all_ undesirable conditions such as wounds, exhaustion, poison, illness, and curses vanish, and the caster awakens fully recovered. The period of sleep is at least 10 days, and the more severe the condition, the longer the period. If it's a poison or illness, it's usually one month, and if it's a curse, it's about one year. The shell disappears as soon as you wake up. This shell _cannot_ be broken by any means. However, you can be carried around in the shell, and it can also be removed by _Dispel Magic_ . If it’s forcibly removed, there will be no recovery effect at all, no matter how long you remain in the egg. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04106))_

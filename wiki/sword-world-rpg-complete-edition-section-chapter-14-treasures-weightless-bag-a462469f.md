## Statements

- This is a backpack made of a mysterious material that repels water. The weight of objects inside can be ignored. However, you can _only_ put in as many objects as you can outwardly _see_ , so if you put in something of an _abnormal_ weight (the game master should make a suitable decision), the magical power will disintegrate and it’ll tear. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06824))_

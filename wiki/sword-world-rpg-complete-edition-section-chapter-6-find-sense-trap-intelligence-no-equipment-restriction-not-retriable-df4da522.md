## Statements

- This is basically the same ability as that of the thief skill, but it only applies to traps that use outdoor nature. This includes those that are made by tying grass into a ring, or are hung from a tree using string, etc. Indoor mechanical traps cannot be found/sensed with the ranger skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03598))_

## Statements

- When this spell is cast on a ship, it will never sink, no matter how much it rots, as long as it only carries as much weight as the original ship could support. However, this does not stop the ship itself from decaying, so over many years it will rot to pieces. This spell is effective even on ships that have already sunk or are shipwrecked and partially destroyed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04359))_
- The priests of Mirleaf often load zombie and skeleton sailors onto the ships they create, and use them as ghost ships to wreak havoc on the seas. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04360))_

## Statements

- The lucerne hammer has a similar shape to a halberd, but the tip of the spear is longer than a halberd, and the striking part is a hammer rather than an ax. On the other side, there’s a hookshaped protrusion. This weapon can be used to _thrust_ , _cut_ and _beat_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07284))_
- It allows you to sweep your opponent by hooking their foot. For this attack, the attack power modifier is ±0, and the strike power is 0 (the _modifier_ is _not_ 0. The strike power _itself_ is 0-- this attack method is not suitable for dealing damage), and there are _no_ criticals. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07285))_
- The glaive is a weapon with a knifelike blade attached to the end of a long handle. This weapon can be used in two ways: _thrust_ and _cut_ . **Bill** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07287))_
- Pole weapons cannot reach their full power in tight spaces. The length of a pole weapon is **required strength x 15** centimeters. If you don't have a space with this radius, you _cannot_ use attack methods other than _thrust_ . It would not be a good idea to bring them into underground labyrinths, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07290))_

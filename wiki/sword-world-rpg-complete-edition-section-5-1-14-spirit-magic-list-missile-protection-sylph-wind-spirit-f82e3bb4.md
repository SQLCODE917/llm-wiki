## Statements

- This spell specifies a point in space and affects the space within a 5 meter radius from it. Its effect is to protect those within range from projectile attacks. However, projectiles with a strike power that exceeds 30 are beyond the limits of sylph's abilities, and therefore cannot be the target of the effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04045))_

## Source record

Chapter 4.7-4.9 of Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering attacks between characters, hit checks, damage checks, and unconscious/death checks. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-50)

## Key supported claims

- Hit checks require 2D rolls for attacker and defender, with attacker's score (attack power + 2D) exceeding defender's (evasion speed + 2D) to hit, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.48-49).
- Double ones cause automatic attack failure; double sixes cause automatic hit, unless defender also rolls double sixes, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.48-49).
- Damage checks are identical for characters and monsters, using strike and defense rolls with bonuses and reductions, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.49).
- When life force reaches 0 or below, character falls unconscious and must make death checks with target score 7, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50).
- Attack checks reference Table 4-4 for outcomes of various dice rolls, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.48-49).

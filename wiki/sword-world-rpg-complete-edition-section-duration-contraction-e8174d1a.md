# 《 **Duration Contraction** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- You may _only_ set it to wear off at the _end_ of a round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01347))_
- Magic with a duration can be optionally shortened. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01347))_
- However, you _cannot_ set a time that causes the effect to wear off in the middle of a round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01347))_
- Magic that’s already been cast cannot be canceled at will. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01347))_
- You must also decide how long you want the magic to last _before_ you cast it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01347))_

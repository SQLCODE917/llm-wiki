## Source record

Magical items list from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, section 14.4, pages 242-256. Some enchantments and effects are unknown.

## Key supported claims

- Shields have +1, +2, and +3 enhancements, some with strength requirements (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-256).
- Robes, cloaks, and armor provide evasion and damage reduction, with some having additional resistances (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-256).
- Some items have hidden drawbacks, such as Magic Acceptor neutralizing magic resistance, or Shield of the Brave causing enemies to focus attacks (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-256).

# 《 **Physical Ability** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is magic that affects the target’s physical abilities (strength, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01395))_
- Magic of this type can _only_ take effect on _humans_ , _fae_ , and _youma_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01395))_
- Also, with magic of the **physical ability** type, two or more cannot affect the same target at the same time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01396))_

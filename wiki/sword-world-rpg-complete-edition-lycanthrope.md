## Lycanthrope

A Lycanthrope is a monster with the ability to change from human to beast form. When in human form, they may acquire and use various skills, including magic, just like an adventurer. When in beast form, they cannot act intelligently, but rather follow their instincts and go on killing sprees. Magic and skills cannot be used. The game master can decide on a lycanthrope's abilities while in human form (however, it should basically only have acquired skills up to its monster level), or use the fixed scores for a normal man as given at page 230 (please set the life points high). (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.232)

### **Transformation**

Shapeshifting is affected by the waxing and waning of the moon. Compare the number of days since the new moon with _2D + 3_ at nighttime or _2D + 9_ in the daytime. When the number of days is 16 or more, compare it with _30 - the number of days_ (the waxing and waning of the moon in Forcelia is one cycle every 30 days). When the score is equal to or less than this, the lycanthrope will always enter beast form (the lycanthrope will always enter beast form on nights when the moon is full = 15 days). (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.232)

Even when it's not, a lycanthrope can transform into a beast at their own will. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.232)

### **Abilities**

Lycanthropes have the ability to summon and control other beasts of their kind in their surroundings (wolves for werewolves, bears for werebears, etc.). Their habitat depends on the type of beast they shapeshift into. There may be some hiding their true identity and living among humans in towns and villages, but when their time comes, they're likely to take odd actions to hide their transformations. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.232)

Lycanthropy is a type of infectious illness, and those who are wounded by a lycanthrope in beast form are at risk of becoming a lycanthrope of the same species. See p. 166 for details on this illness. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.232)

### **Types**

There are several types of lycanthropes mentioned in the rulebook:

- **Werewolf**: A lycanthrope with the power to shapeshift into a wolf. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.232)
- **Werebear**: A lycanthrope with the power to shapeshift into a bear. Like a bear, it attacks with a bear hug (strangling). (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.232)
- **Weretiger**: A lycanthrope with the power to shapeshift into a tiger. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.232)

### **Related Concepts**

Lycanthropy is a key concept in the Sword World RPG, representing a transformation that changes both the physical form and behavior of the affected individual. The [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-shapeshifting|shapeshifting]] ability of lycanthropes makes them particularly dangerous, as they can appear as normal humans during the day and transform into beasts at night or when provoked.

The concept of lycanthropy also touches on themes of infection and disease, as it can be passed from one individual to another through wounds inflicted by a lycanthrope in beast form. This adds a layer of risk and horror to encounters with these creatures.

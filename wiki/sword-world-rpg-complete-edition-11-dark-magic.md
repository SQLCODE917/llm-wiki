# 11.2 Dark Magic

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about 11.2 dark magic:

## Statements

- Dark magic basically has the same origin as holy magic, and dark priests can use most of the basic holy magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03923))_
- In addition, dark priests can use wicked magic that the gods of light do not allow (do not give power to). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03923))_
- However, public sentiment towards magic is far from good. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03912))_
- Of course, these people also rely on magical power when they themselves need it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03937))_
- Magic power should be added to damage as usual. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03977))_
- This magic does not require the spell to be chanted. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04166))_
- This magic can extend the amount of time the target remains insane after the dance ends, if the caster consumes extra mental power to expand the effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04168))_
- Rune masters are respected in a sense by the general public because they have special powers called magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03912))_
- Some believe that once their numbers become sufficient and they regain the power they had during the ancient kingdom, the age of darkness may come again. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03913))_
- A rune master who uses **dark magic** is called a **dark priest** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03917))_
- As mentioned earlier, these runes, like holy magic, use the gods as its source of power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03917))_
- In a narrow sense, only the priests of Phalaris are sometimes called dark priests. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03917))_
- In addition to these, priests of the wicked gods can _also_ use most of the basic holy magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03925))_
- However, if an identical _type_ of spell or a spell with an identical _name_ is listed under dark magic, they can only use _that_ version of it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03925))_

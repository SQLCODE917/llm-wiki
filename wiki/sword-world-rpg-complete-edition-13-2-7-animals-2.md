## Source record

This section describes various animals and monsters found in the Sword World RPG setting, including vampire bats, giant bats, bears, lions, giant slugs, toads, ants, apes, and giant antlions.

## Key supported claims

- A vampire bat is a giant bloodsucking bat with a wingspan of about 2 meters, preferring to suck the blood of living creatures rather than eat their meat. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.203-206)
- A giant bat is a massive bat, with a wingspan of 3 meters across, completely nocturnal, flying around forests and wastelands to hunt at night. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.203-206)
- Grizzlies are generally solitary, except when a mother bear is with her cubs, and are omnivores said to eat anything they can fit into their mouths. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.203-206)

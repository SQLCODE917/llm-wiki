## Source record

Summary of ranger and sage skills from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering outdoor abilities, trap setting, first aid, and sage knowledge skills. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.118-118)

## Key supported claims

- Ranger skill allows setting traps up to difficulty equal to thief skill level (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.116-117).
- First-aid provided by ranger restores life force to 1 point, takes 30 minutes, cannot fail (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.116-117).
- Ranger skill is limited by equipment, armor must be ring mail or non-metal, required strength up to half own strength (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.116-117).

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.116-118)

The armor you wear must have a required strength of up to _half_ your own strength, and must be ring mail or non-metal

### `technical-atom-25d33d3e405745c8` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.116-118)

For **set** , you can set traps up to a difficulty equal to your thief skill level without failing (you cannot set traps of a higher level).

### `technical-atom-9009e1694c6f9342` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.116-118)

It cannot fail, but it will take about 30 minutes of game time, and it cannot be done while in a state where you can't focus, such as in the middle of combat, etc.

### `technical-atom-5e3c6ce9f2cc9ad1` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.116-118)

The ranger skill is also limited by equipment, just like the thief skill, if you want to make full use of its abilities.

### `technical-atom-ed51c886a0ebc8ba` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.116-118)

However, while the thief skill can be used both outdoors and indoors, the ranger skill is mostly useful only outdoors.

### `technical-atom-e6a7b3d2126c33db` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.116-118)

Since it's an ability that uses natural terrain, it cannot be used indoors.

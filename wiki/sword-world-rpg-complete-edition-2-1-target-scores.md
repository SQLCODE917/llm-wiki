# 2.1 > Target Scores

## Understanding Target Scores

A character's baseline score represents their ability, but the success of an action depends on both the character's ability and the difficulty of the action itself. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 30))_ 

## How Target Scores Are Used

Players roll two dice, add their baseline score to the roll, and compare the final score to the target score to determine if the action succeeds or fails. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 30))_

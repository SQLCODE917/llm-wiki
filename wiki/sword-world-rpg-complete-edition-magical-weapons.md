Magical weapons in the Sword World RPG are weapons imbued with magical properties that provide advantages to adventurers in combat. These weapons are categorized by their enchantment levels and can be found in various forms.

## Types of Magical Weapons

Magical weapons are primarily categorized by their enchantment levels:

- **Magic Sword (+1)**: Provides +1 to attack power and bonus damage.
- **Magic Sword (+2)**: Provides +2 to attack power and bonus damage.
- **Magic Sword (+3)**: Provides +3 to attack power and bonus damage.

Other magical weapons include:

- **Assassin's Flute**: An assassination weapon made by the assassin's guild, with no magical power.
- **Undead Ruler**: A staff that grants the power to make undead monsters obey.
- **Impulse**: An heirloom sword passed down through the family of Güzelbahn.
- **Van Blade**: A greatsword with a shining white blade that glows to inform the user when danger approaches.
- **Cat Claw**: A small knife with a sharp curved blade that gives a -2 bonus to critical target.
- **Crusher**: A heavy mace that provides a +12 bonus to the user's strength.
- **Green Light**: A broadsword with a green blade.
- **Sea Slayer**: A trident that allows the user to breathe underwater and move freely like a fish.
- **Shy Blade**: A shortsword that turns invisible with a password.
- **Stone Statue**: A bastard sword that petrifies opponents it deals damage to.
- **Slice Slider**: A scimitar that provides +2 to attack power and bonus damage.
- **Twisted Sling**: A worn-out sling that can fire an Energy Bolt with a password.
- **Fame Face**: A broadsword with high ancient runes engraved in bright red on the back of the blade.
- **Blade of Bravery**: A rapier passed down as proof of the head of the Sokran family.
- **Flame Smasher**: A bastard sword that always has Fire Weapon cast on it.
- **Blaster**: A greatsword that can use unlimited shockwaves.
- **Brain Blade**: A broadsword that has knowledge about enemies and speaks to the user's mind.
- **Mind Eater**: A broadsword that deals damage to mental points of whoever's body was dealt damage.
- **Misty Sword**: A shamshir that always has dew trickling down the blade and can produce fresh water.
- **Red Eye**: A dagger with a ruby set in a silver handle.
- **Roan Ax**: A battle-ax with +1 bonus damage.

## Power Levels and Enchantments

Magical weapons are enchanted with varying levels of power, indicated by the +1, +2, or +3 enchantment. These enchantments provide bonuses to attack power and damage. Some weapons also have additional properties that affect combat effectiveness or provide unique abilities.

## Required Strength

Many magical weapons have a required strength value that must be met or exceeded for the weapon to be used effectively. This value is listed in the item's description and is important for determining usability and effectiveness in combat.

## Base Trade Price

The base trade price is the price at which the item is traded. If it's for sale, adventurers can buy it at the price shown here. If an adventurer sells it, the standard price is half this amount.

## Special Considerations

- Items with commands in high ancient can only be handled by those who have the sorcerer skill.
- The final value of the treasure is determined entirely by the player character, although it might not necessarily match the NPC's valuation.
- Adventurers should not be able to get their hands on items that are too powerful.

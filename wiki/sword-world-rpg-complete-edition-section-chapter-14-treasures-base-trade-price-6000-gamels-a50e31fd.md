## Statements

- This sheath is made to fit a broadsword and can _only_ hold a broadsword. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06976))_
- If it _is_ a broadsword, the sheath will automatically expand or contract to adjust for any slight differences in length. If you keep a sword in this sheath for more than one hour, then draw the sword from the sheath, it’ll have +5 to strike power and will be able to harm monsters that cannot be wounded by normal weapons for 18 rounds, just like if _Enchant Weapon_ magic were cast on it. The sheath’s _Enchant_ effect can be removed with a _Dispel Magic_ spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06977))_

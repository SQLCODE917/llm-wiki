## Statements

- This spell entangles the target in a shining silver magical net, binding their actions. At this time, the target can move if they want to against the binding of the net. If a target fails to resist against this spell and attempts to act, they will suffer a -4 penalty on all success rolls because the net coiled around their body will hinder them. In addition, each thread that forms the magical net is razor sharp, causing the target to suffer strike power 20 damage each round in which they attempt to act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03458))_
- Movement and combat, as well as casting spells, all count as having taken an action (using holy magic also counts as having taken an action). If you do not take action, you will not suffer this damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03460))_
- Please note that when making a check, you may only make a resistance roll against this spell once per round in which the spell is cast. At this time, the result lasts for the duration of the spell. You can determine the damage by rolling the dice each round your character takes an action. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03461))_
- This spell works on targets slightly larger than a human (such as an ogre or a manticore), but has no effect on targets of a larger size than that (such as a dragon or a roc). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03462))_

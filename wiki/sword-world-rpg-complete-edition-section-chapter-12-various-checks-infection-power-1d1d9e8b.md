## Statements

- Indicates the infectiousness of the illness. If it' s very strong , it has the infection power of a plague. Strong is about the same level as influenza. As in the example, moderate means it's just a cold, and weak means it's not very infectious. When it's very weak , it's not infectious and is unlikely to spread to other people. When an illness is very strong or strong , there is a high possibility that the infection will spread to the healer, etc. who is caring for the patient. If the game master determines that there is a possibility of contracting the illness, have the character make a progression check for the illness (compare a 2D roll with progression intensity). If they fail, the character has contracted the illness at depth 1. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04925))_

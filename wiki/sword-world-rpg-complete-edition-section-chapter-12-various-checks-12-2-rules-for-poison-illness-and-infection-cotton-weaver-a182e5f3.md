- This is a strange disease that occurs due to a significant decrease in the power of undine within the body. The cause is a very small wound on the fingertip, etc. When infected with this illness, the wound will not close at all, but moreover, something that looks like cotton will start to bulge out of the wound. At _depth_ 1, the character's actions are not affected at all. If you pluck a little bit of the cotton, it will instantly disappear, but at 2, the character will fall into a coma and the cotton will come out no matter how much you pluck. Although the progression speed is not that fast, it is an incurable disease that is difficult to treat once contracted. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04423))_

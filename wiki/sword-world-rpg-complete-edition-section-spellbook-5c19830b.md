# 《 Spellbook 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- A sorcerer must somehow preserve the ancient magic spells they've learned. Otherwise, even those with the greatest minds cannot possibly memorize all the words, actions, etc. needed to cast spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02773))_
- For this reason, it is customary for sorcerers to write these spells down in a book called a spellbook . A spellbook is, so to speak, just a handwritten notebook, and the spellbook itself has no magical power of its own. However, because it's a very important thing, it would be a disaster if it were lost or stolen, so some sorcerers may keep several copies in reserve. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02774))_
- When you go on an adventure, or you're in a situation where you'd need to use magic, you'd usually bring your spellbook with you. However, no sorcerer looks at their spellbook every time they try to cast a spell. This is because the words and actions of a spell are usually sharply memorized in your head. A spellbook is used to review them, always confirming and ensuring your memory. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02775))_
- There are no special restrictions on spellbooks according to the rules. Nevertheless, keep in mind that sorcerers in Sword World always keep their spellbooks with them, and remember to take very good care of them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02776))_

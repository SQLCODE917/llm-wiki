# What is a Role-Playing Game (RPG)?

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Role-Playing means to act out a role . A role-playing game (RPG), as the name suggests, progresses by having each participant in the game play out an assigned role . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00034))_
- Participants in an RPG can be roughly divided into two groups: One is the game master ( GM ), while the other are the players . RPGs are played by several people, but there is only one game master while the rest are the players. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00035))_
- Before playing a game, the game master must prepare what's called a scenario . You may either create your own, or you may use an existing one. A number of scenario collections have already been produced for Sword World RPG , and will continue to be released in the future. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00036))_
- The game proceeds according to a prepared scenario. The game master is the one who facilitates the scenario, while the players play the role -- of the characters --in the scenario. If you think of the game master as a director and the players as actors, it's similar to making a movie or a play. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00037))_
- However, there is something that's very different from film or theater. Which is: the players do not know the scenario. They only know the characters they're taking on. The players must make their characters act based on their abilities and characteristics. The game master's scenario does not provide any dialogue, etc., for the characters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00038))_
- Let's give a real example. GM is the game master, and A , B , and C are different players. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00039))_
- GM ： You guys were traveling and you arrived at a small village. You're now resting at a tavern now that the sun is setting. Then, about five or six unsavorylooking people enter. They start to have fun by making nasty remarks at the poster girl at the bar, much to her annoyance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00040))_
- A ： That doesn't sound good... okay, I'll give them a little warning. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00041))_
- A ： What do you mean? That would soil the name of a hero of justice! _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00043))_
- GM ： They have very dirty leather armor and shortswords. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00048))_
- C ： Even I have the backing of the Thieves Guild (laughs). These guys don't seem like a big bunch, so I say we be "heroes of justice". Hey you guys, leave her alone! _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00050))_
- GM ： But you're all sitting at the same table, so I don't think they'll believe you. (laughs) One of the thugs, who looks particularly rough, snarls 'What, you have the gall to complain about us - the Brando family?!' . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00053))_
- Thus, the game proceeds. In this case, the scenario prepared by the game master does not say there will be a fight with thugs (the Brando family) . It only says thugs will misbehave in the tavern , then it's up to the players to decide what they'll do about it. If the players were all like player B , there would've been no conflict. The conflict only arose because the players wanted it. So, what will be the outcome of this fight? _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00056))_
- Here is where another major feature of role-playing games comes into play. That is… _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00057))_
- Not knowing what the results will be. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00058))_
- Neither the game master nor the players know whether the characters will be able to kick the thugs out of the tavern and become heroes of justice , or get beaten by the thugs and instead become heroes of comedy . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00060))_
- Even in real life, if you start a fight, you won't know who will win without actually fighting. The only thing you should know is Who is considered stronger? . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00061))_
- In other words, based on a number that represents a character's strength, a result is determined by rolling the dice. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00063))_
- Player ： (rolls) Hey, I got a good roll, my attack is a hit! _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00065))_
- Player ： (rolls) Agh, my roll was bad. My attack failed! _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00067))_
- In this way, due to two types of randomness, player's choice and random numbers (dice) , the actual game can display a wide degree of fluctuation, sometimes the "story" will evolve beyond the expectations of the game master. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00068))_

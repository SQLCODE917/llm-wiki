## Statements

- If a character drinks this potion, it'll permanently increase an ability score by 1 point. Just like when increasing with experience points, there is no maximum. Which ability score is increased is determined by each potion (all six types exist). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11090))_
- Even if the game master approves the trade of magical items, this item should be considered in short supply, nearly impossible to find, and not for trade. Otherwise, adventurers with money could buy up this item, which could potentially have a serious impact on game balance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11091))_

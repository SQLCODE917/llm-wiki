Spirit magic in Sword World RPG - Complete Edition is a form of magic that draws power from various spirits. It is categorized into different levels, each with its own set of spells and abilities.

## Types of Spirits

The spirits that can be invoked for magic include:

- **Wind Spirits** (Sylphs, Djinn)
- **Water Spirits** (Undines)
- **Fire Spirits** (Salamanders)
- **Earth Spirits** (Gnomes, Ent, Behemoth)
- **Plant Spirits** (Dryads, Ents)
- **Mind Spirits** (Valkyries, Leprechauns, Shades)
- **Light Spirits** (Will-O-Wisps)
- **Dark Spirits** (Shades)
- **Building Spirits** (Brownies)
- **Greater Spirits** (Greater Plant Spirits, Greater Wind Spirits, Greater Earth Spirits)

## Spell Levels

Spirit magic spells are categorized by level, with each level having specific spells and effects.

### 1st Level Spirit Magic

Spells at this level include:
- **Wind Voice** (Sylph/Wind Spirit) - Hears sounds occurring at the target location.
- **Snare** (Gnome/Earth Spirit) - Knocks the target prone.
- **Destruction** (Leprechaun/Mind Spirit) - Disturb the opponent's focus.
- **Purification** (Undine/Water Spirit) - Removes impurities from water.
- **Firebolt** (Salamander/Fire Spirit) - Deals fire damage to the target.
- **Brownie** (Brownie/Building Spirit) - Does chores around the house.

### 2nd Level Spirit Magic

Spells at this level include:
- **Will-O-Wisp** (Will-O-Wisp/Light Spirit) - Illuminates the space within the area of effect.
- **Water Screen** (Undine/Water Spirit) - Reduces fire damage by 1 point.
- **Confusion** (Leprechaun/Mind Spirit) - Confuses the target.
- **Shade** (Shade/Dark Spirit) - Negates natural light within the area of effect.
- **Fanaticism** (Valkyrie/Mind Spirit) - Raises the target's spirit.
- **Forget** (Leprechaun/Mind Spirit) - Target loses their memory.
- **Hold** (Gnome/Earth Spirit) - Stops an opponent's movement.

### 3rd Level Spirit Magic

Spells at this level include:
- **Invisibility** (Sprite/Mind Spirit) - Makes the caster invisible.
- **Water Breathing** (Undine/Water Spirit) - Allows breathing underwater.
- **Control Spirit** (Various lesser spirits) - Summons and controls one type of spirit.
- **Healing** (The Unknown Spirit of Life) - Completely heal the target's wounds.
- **Mute** (Sylph/Wind Spirit) - Negate sounds made by the target.

### 4th Level Spirit Magic

Spells at this level include:
- **Water Walking** (Undine/Water Spirit) - Become able to walk on water.
- **Sink** (Undine/Water Spirit) - Eliminates the buoyancy of water, causing floating people and objects to sink.
- **Sleep** (Sandman/Mind Spirit) - Put an opponent into eternal sleep.
- **Tunnel** (Gnome/Earth Spirit) - Drill a hole in the ground, a stone wall, etc.

### 5th Level Spirit Magic

Spells at this level include:
- **Fear** (Shade/Dark Spirit) - Affects the target's mental state and can cause fear.
- **Restore Health** (The Unknown Spirit of Life) - Restores health to the target.
- **Control Plant** (Dryad/Plant Spirit) - Controls the growth and movement of plants.
- **Shoot Arrow** (Sylph/Wind Spirit) - Fires an arrow of wind.
- **Charm** (Dryad/Plant Spirit) - Charms the target.
- **Valkyrie Javelin** (Valkyrie/Mind Spirit) - Creates a strike power 30 energy javelin.
- **Missile Protection** (Sylph/Wind Spirit) - Protects against projectile attacks.
- **Control Sound** (Various lesser spirits) - Controls the sound made by the target.
- **Spirit Wall** (Various lesser spirits) - Creates a wall of spirits that blocks movement and attacks.
- **Decrease Water Pressure** (Undine/Water Spirit) - Relieves water pressure when diving.
- **Preserve Sound** (Sylph/Wind Spirit) - Records and transmits sound to a specific location.

### 6th Level Spirit Magic

Spells at this level include:
- **Windstorm** (Djinn/Greater Wind Spirit) - Creates a tornado, knocking targets prone and dealing damage.
- **Crack** (Behemoth/Greater Earth Spirit) - Traps the target between a crack in the ground, dealing damage.
- **Valkyrie Blessing** (Valkyrie/Mind Spirit) - Gives pure energy protection to the target's armor, absorbing incoming damage.

## Key Spells

### Fear

The **Fear** spell, associated with the Shade/Dark Spirit, affects the target's mental state and can cause fear. It negates natural light within a 5 meter radius, closing it in complete darkness. The darkness created by a shade has no effect in a space where the ancient magic *Light* is at work. In addition, if the light emitted by a will-o-wisp and the darkness produced by a shade overlap, the powers of both will be negated.

### Restore Health

The **Restore Health** spell, associated with the Unknown Spirit of Life, restores health to the target. It can completely heal any wound instantly, but cannot regenerate a severed arm or leg.

### Control Plant

The **Control Plant** spell, associated with the Dryad/Plant Spirit, controls the growth and movement of plants.

### Shoot Arrow

The **Shoot Arrow** spell, associated with the Sylph/Wind Spirit, fires an arrow of wind.

### Charm

The **Charm** spell, associated with the Dryad/Plant Spirit, charms the target.

### Valkyrie Javelin

The **Valkyrie Javelin** spell, associated with the Valkyrie/Mind Spirit, creates a strike power 30 energy javelin.

### Missile Protection

The **Missile Protection** spell, associated with the Sylph/Wind Spirit, protects against projectile attacks.

### Control Sound

The **Control Sound** spell, associated with various lesser spirits, controls the sound made by the target.

### Spirit Wall

The **Spirit Wall** spell, associated with various lesser spirits, creates a wall of spirits that blocks movement and attacks.

### Decrease Water Pressure

The **Decrease Water Pressure** spell, associated with the Undine/Water Spirit, relieves water pressure when diving.

### Preserve Sound

The **Preserve Sound** spell, associated with the Sylph/Wind Spirit, records and transmits sound to a specific location.

## Usage

The use of spirit magic in Sword World RPG - Complete Edition requires the use of the **Shaman Skill**. This skill allows the use of spirit magic and is detailed in the *Shaman Skill* section of the game manual.

# 《 Duration Contraction 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Magic with a duration can be optionally shortened. However, you cannot set a time that causes the effect to wear off in the middle of a round. You may only set it to wear off at the end of a round. You must also decide how long you want the magic to last before you cast it. Magic that's already been cast cannot be canceled at will. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01272))_

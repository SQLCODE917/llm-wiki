## Statements

- Deals damage to the opponent's life force (points). First, make a strike roll with a strike power of 20. Magic power should be added to damage as usual. Also, your opponent _cannot_ reduce this damage using adventurer level. This poison's damage is not applied immediately, but in the form of 1 point at the end of each round, starting on the round in which the spell is used. The poison then lasts until the final determined damage is dealt. In this case, if the poison is removed by a spell such as _Cure Poison_ , etc. midway, the damage will stop accumulating at that point. However, the damage suffered up to that point will _not_ be recovered. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03771))_

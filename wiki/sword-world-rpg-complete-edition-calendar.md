### 18.3 Rules for Destroying Structures / Calendar and Time

- The "present" in Alecrast is considered to be the year 520 of the New Kingdom Calendar. The New Kingdom Calendar marks its first year from the fall of the ancient Kingdom of Kastuul, which occurred more than 500 years ago. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11893))_

- A year is divided into 12 months, and each month has 30 days. This period of 30 days also corresponds to one complete lunar cycle. To express a specific date, the common format is Year X of the New Kingdom Calendar, Month Y, and Day Z. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11894))_

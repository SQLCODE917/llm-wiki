# 【 Bard 】

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Those who play musical instruments and sing songs for a living are called bards. Thus, those with the bard skill have the ability to play an instrument and sing songs. Many legends and folklore are often woven into songs passed down from ancient times, and natural bards are familiar with these legends and folklore. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00274))_
- Bards also have the ability to sing magical songs called spellsongs . These songs have magical effects on all who hear them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00276))_

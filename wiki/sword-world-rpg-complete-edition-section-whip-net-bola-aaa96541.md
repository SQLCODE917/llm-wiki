# **Whip, Net, Bola**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- If an attack with one of these weapons hits, the evader must make a success roll using _adventurer level + agility bonus_ as the baseline score and _attacker's final score at this time_ as the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07930))_
- If he or she fails, they’ll be _entangled_ and fall _prone_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07931))_
- Once entangled, an escape check is required _every_ round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07935))_
- To escape, you must first _release_ any armaments you’re holding. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07935))_
- While entangled, there is _always_ a -4 penalty evading your opponent's attacks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07935))_
- If the entangled target has hands or similar organs, the target score will _decrease_ by 1 each round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07935))_

- Once you've chosen a race, you can determine your ability scores. First, you must roll the dice to determine subability scores A~H . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00324))_
- A character's six ability scores are each the totals of two of these eight subability scores. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00326))_
- Depending on whether a half-elf was raised by humans or elves, the skills they have from the start and the limitations on the skills they can acquire differ. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00327))_
- On the table, "D" stands for dice . 1D stands for one die , 2D stands for two dice , and 1/2D stands for half the result of one dice (rounded up) . In addition, a notation such as 1D+6 means to roll one dice and add 6 to the result . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00413))_
- Once ability scores are determined, sub-ability scores are never used during the game. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00498))_
- For reference, the average ability scores of each race are shown on Table 14: Average Ability Scores by Race. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00499))_
- Now, let's determine ability scores. Since our race is human, all we have to do is roll 2D, that's two dice, from A-H, all the way down. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00501))_
- Our rolls are 9, 7, 5, 8, 6, 8, 10, and 4. These can be considered average, with neither extremely high nor low rolls. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00502))_
- The average for a human is 14 in all ability scores, so other than a slightly higher life force, this character seems to be pretty average. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00517))_
- Once the ability scores have been determined, also find the ability bonuses and write them down on the Character Sheet. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00518))_
- An ability bonus equals the ability score divided by 6 (rounded down), as shown under 1.1: Ability Scores. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00519))_
- For this character being created, the bonuses are dexterity +2, agility +2, intelligence +2, strength +2, life force +3, and mental power +2. Since the average human ability score is 14, +2 is a very average ability bonus. This character can be considered fairly mediocre except for the excellent life force bonus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00521))_

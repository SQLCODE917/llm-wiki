In *Sword World RPG*, reincarnation is a process that allows a soul to be reborn into a new body after death. The Reincarnation spell is a powerful magic that facilitates this process, enabling a soul to be reborn as a baby in another location within one week. 

The spell allows the reborn baby to retain their ability scores, and as they grow up, they will remember their former personality at a certain moment, regaining all their previous memories and skills. The time it takes for the reborn soul to regain their memories depends on the final score from when the spell was cast, as detailed in the Reincarnation Memory table (see [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-chapter-5-1-16-basic-holy-magic-list-part-2]]). 

Additionally, the Reincarnation spell can also be used to help the party escape to a safe location (the caster's home, a temple, a familiar city, etc.) when cast immediately after an individual's death. 

The spell can be expanded to target more people or increase the area of effect, but it cannot force the spell to take effect on those who do not agree to it. 

It is important to note that even if someone is resurrected using the Reincarnation spell, they cannot be brought back to life again if their soul is destroyed after the spell's duration has expired (see [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-chapter-5-1-16-basic-holy-magic-list-part-2]]).

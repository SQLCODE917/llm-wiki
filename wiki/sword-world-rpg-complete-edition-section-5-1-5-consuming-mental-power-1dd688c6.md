# 5.1.5 Consuming Mental Power

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Adventurers cannot use magic endlessly. When using magic, an adventurer must temporarily reduce their mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02554))_
- Each magic has a score called base mental power cost . This base mental power cost, divided by the corresponding rune master skill level _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02555))_
- A character with shaman skill level 2 uses the Fire Bolt spell. The base mental power cost for Fire Bolt is 5 . The character must consume 5 ÷ 2 = 2.5, rounding up to 3 points of mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02558))_
- A character can reduce their mental power to 0, but they cannot make it negative. For example, a character with 5 points of mental power remaining can use magic that costs 5 points of mental power, but cannot use magic that costs 6 or more points of mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02559))_
- If a character's mental power reaches 0 , they will lose consciousness . The character cannot regain consciousness unless they recover their mental power to 1 point or more in some way. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02560))_
- For example, a character with 5 points of mental power remaining can use magic that costs 5 points of mental power, but cannot use magic that costs 6 or more points of mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02559))_
- The character cannot regain consciousness unless they recover their mental power to 1 point or more in some way. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02560))_

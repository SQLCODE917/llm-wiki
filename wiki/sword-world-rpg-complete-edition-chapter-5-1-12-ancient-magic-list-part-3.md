## **5.1.12** 

## **Ancient Magic List** 

This chapter details the 4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, 8th, and 9th level ancient magic spells available in the *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition*. Spells are listed with their base mental power cost, distance, area, duration, effect, type, expansion, and resist. 

## **4th Level Ancient Magic List** 

## **[ Seal Enchantment ]** 

Base Mental Power Cost=30 

Distance=Touch Area=One object Duration=Permanent 

Effect=Hides magical power applied to an object 

Type=Target score, non-removable, lost Countered (Overpowered) By: _Analyze Enchantment_ 

Expansion=Final score Resist=None 

This spell is used on objects imbued with magical power to hide the presence of that magical power. For this reason, _Sense Magic_ or _Sense Aura_ will no longer be able to distinguish them. When discerning magic power with _Analyze Enchantment_ , you'll need a final score greater than or equal to the _Seal Enchantment_ that was cast on it. 

Sometimes there are items from the ancient kingdom period that have this 
cast on them. Many of them are protected by _Seal Enchantment_ with a final score as high as 30 or greater. 

This spell has no effect when using the sage skill to appraise an item (however, if you don't think it has magical power, you might not try to appraise it). This spell cannot be cast on living things. 

## **[ Steal Mind ]** 

Base Mental Power Cost=24 

Distance=10 meters 

Area=One 

Duration=Instant 

Effect=Steals strike power 20 mental power from the target and gives it to the caster Type=Lost, damage (Mind-type, see description) 

Expansion=Final score, distance, targets, damage certainty Resist=Negated effect 

This spell allows you to steal mental power from a target. The target suffers a strike power 20 damage to mental power, and the caster gains that mental power. The spell cannot be used on targets that do not have mental power, such as objects or creatures without a mind. 

## **[ Slow ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=12 
Distance=10 meters 
Area=One 
Duration=18 rounds 
Effect=Reduces the target's speed by half 
Type=Lost 
Expansion=Duration, distance, targets 
Resist=Negated effect 

This spell reduces the target's speed by half, making it difficult for them to move or act quickly. The spell can be used on living creatures or objects. The target's movement speed, attack speed, and reaction time are all reduced. 

## **[ Haste ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=12 
Distance=10 meters 
Area=One 
Duration=18 rounds 
Effect=Increases the target's speed by double 
Type=Lost 
Expansion=Duration, distance, targets 
Resist=Negated effect 

This spell doubles the target's speed, making it easier for them to move or act quickly. The spell can be used on living creatures or objects. The target's movement speed, attack speed, and reaction time are all increased. 

## **5th Level Ancient Magic List** 

## **[ Sense Aura ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=15 
Distance=Caster 
Area=Within the caster's view 
Duration=18 rounds 
Effect=Knows the presence and type of magical power in the area 
Type=Detection 
Expansion=Duration 
Resist=None 

This spell allows the caster to detect the presence and type of magical power in their area of effect. Unlike _Sense Magic_, which only detects that magic is present, this spell reveals the type of magic. However, it does not reveal the strength or specific effects of the magic. 

## **[ Analyze Enchantment ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=15 
Distance=Touch 
Area=One object 
Duration=Instant 
Effect=Reveals the magical properties of an object 
Type=Detection 
Expansion=Final score, distance, targets 
Resist=None 

This spell reveals the magical properties of an object, including the type of magic, its strength, and its effects. It can be used on any object that has magical properties. 

## **[ Dispel Enchantment ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=15 
Distance=Touch 
Area=One object 
Duration=Instant 
Effect=Removes all magical enchantments from an object 
Type=Ritual 
Expansion=Final score, distance, targets 
Resist=None 

This spell removes all magical enchantments from an object, including those that are non-removable. It can be used on any object that has magical properties. 

## **[ Detect Magic ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=15 
Distance=Caster 
Area=Within the caster's view 
Duration=18 rounds 
Effect=Detects the presence of magical power in the area 
Type=Detection 
Expansion=Duration 
Resist=None 

This spell detects the presence of magical power in the area, but does not reveal the type or strength of the magic. It is similar to _Sense Magic_, but more general.

## **7th Level Ancient Magic List** 

## **[ Command Golem ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=45 
Distance=Touch 
Area=1 golem 
Duration=Permanent 
Effect=You can control and give commands to golems that have not received commands yet 
Type=Target score, non-removable Expansion=Resist=None 

This spell is _not_ a spell that creates golems. It's only a spell that gives _commands_ to a golem. Moreover, the target golem must be in a state where _no_ commands have been given. This spell is ineffective on a golem that's _already_ following a command. Golems in a state without commands are extremely rarely found, but may be found in the ruins of the ancient kingdom. 

## **[ Control Weather ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=45 Distance=Caster 
Area=An area within a 10 kilometer radius of the caster 
Duration=Until focus ends 
Effect=Change the weather within the area of effect 
Type=Focus, Target score, non-removable Expansion=Final score, area Resist=None 

This is a spell that can change the weather within its area of effect. Weather changes begin on the round you cast the spell, and the spell is completed in 10 rounds. Even if someone is within the area of effect, they cannot resist this spell. This spell is _non-removable_ , but if two _Control Weather_ spells are cast within the same area of effect, the one with the higher final score will take effect, and the one with the lower final score will be negated. 

Weather you can change to includes sunny, cloudy, rainy, foggy, or snowy. It has nothing to do with the weather, season, etc. before the spell was cast. As the spell ends, the weather will return to its natural state. However, effects caused 
by this spell will remain and cannot be removed. 

Below is a brief description of the extent of the weather. 

_Sunny_ ： The sky within the area of effect will be completely clear of clouds. Depending on the angle of the sun, the sun will shine during the day and the ground will be warm. At night, you’ll be able to see the moon and stars. 

_Cloudy_ ： The sky within the area of effect is covered by clouds, hiding the sun. Since direct sunlight is blocked, the area becomes darker and the temperature on the ground decreases. At night, the moon and stars will be hidden, making it a dark night. 

_Rainy_ ： Rain clouds cover the sky, the conditions will be the same as cloudy. In addition, rain will begin to fall at about 10 mm per hour, wetting the ground. 

_Foggy_ ： Visibility within the area of effect is 5 meters or less for fog. 

_Snowy_ ： Snow clouds cover the sky, the conditions will be the same as cloudy. In addition, snow will begin to fall at about 10 mm per hour. The surrounding temperature will drop to 0°celsius. 

## **[ Disintegrate ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=45 
Distance=Touch 
Area=One individual or object up to 1 cubic meter in volume 
Duration=Instant 
Effect=Break the target down into the smallest unit of matter, annihilating them Type=- 
Expansion=Final score, target volume Resist=Negated effect 

Targets who have this spell cast on them and fail to resist will, after a moment of brightness, be broken down into the smallest unit of matter they were composed of, literally disappearing. It's impossible to regenerate a target that has become like this. If it was a living thing, it can never be revived again, and cannot be revived by _Resurrection_ . 

Usually it's limited to 1 cubic meter, but by expanding the volume, it's possible to cast it on a larger target. 

## **[ Petrification ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=36 
Distance=10 meters 
Area=One 
Duration=Permanent 
Effect=Petrify your opponent Type=Target score Expansion=Final score, distance, targets Resist=Negated effect 

This spell turns the target into stone, making them unable to move or act. The spell can be used on living creatures or objects. The target is completely petrified and cannot be revived by any means. 

## **8th Level Ancient Magic List** 

## **[ Wraith ]** 
Base Mental Power Cost=40 
Distance=Touch 
Area=One 
Duration=18 rounds 
Effect=Separates the caster's spiritual body from their physical body, allowing only their ethereal body to move around freely 
Type=Focus, non-removable 
Expansion=Duration, distance, targets 
Resist=None 

This spell allows the caster to separate their spiritual body from their physical body, allowing only their ethereal body to move around freely. This ethereal body is called a wraith. Wraiths are a kind of undead, but they can resume their life activities once they return to their own bodies. If you do not (or cannot) return to your original body after the spell's duration has expired, you will lose complete contact with your body, and must remain adrift for eternity as a wraith. The same applies if your body is destroyed (your life force or life points become 0 or less) while you are a wraith. If the wraith itself is destroyed, the caster dies. In this case, the body becomes nothing more than an empty husk without a soul, so it cannot be brought back to life by any means. 

See p. 219 for wraith details.

## Statements

- The ancient kingdom of Kastuul, which fell more than 500 years ago, is sometimes referred to as the _magical kingdom_ . As its nickname suggests, Kastuul is said to have accomplished various miracles through the power of **==> picture [147 x 283] intentionally omitted <==** magic. It is this magical power that is known as **high ancient** and is the magic that the sorcerers of today have inherited. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00270))_
- With the fall of Kastuul, much of high ancient was lost, but some has survived to the present day. The skill that can handle it is the sorcerer skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00271))_
- Many sorcerers are also sages. The rules do not require one character to acquire both of these skills, but if they are acquired at the same time, it gives them a significant advantage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00272))_

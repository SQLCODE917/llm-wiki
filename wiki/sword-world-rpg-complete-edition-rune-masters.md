In _Sword World_, all those who use magic utilizing runes are called **rune masters**. Rune masters are divided into five types according to the type of magic (runes) they use. Namely,

Sorcerer uses **ancient magic**
Shaman uses **spirit magic**
Priest uses **holy magic**
Dark priest uses **dark magic**
and dragon priest uses **dragon roar magic**.

Each corresponds to the skill for which it is named. Those with the _sorcerer skill_ can use _ancient magic_ and are called _sorcerers_.

Magic is not omnipotent. It is only possible to achieve a defined and limited set of effects by weaving certain words (in runes), called **spells**. There are several types of spells, each with different effects. Depending on the strength of their effects, spells are divided into _levels_ from _1-10_. 1st level spells are very rudimentary, while 10th level spells are very powerful.

However, working with higher level (i.e., more advanced) spells requires a high rune master skill. A rune master can use _all_ magic up to _their_ skill level, but _cannot_ use magic of a _higher_ level than their current skill.

For those who have acquired multiple rune master skills, consider the level of spells you can use for each type of magic.

**magic power** = magic skill level + intelligence bonus

If you've acquired _multiple_ magic skills, consider the magic power of each _separately_.

## Example:

An adventurer with priest skill level 1, shaman skill level 2, and 13 intelligence (+2 bonus) would have a magic power of _3_ in holy magic and a magic power of _4_ in spirit magic.

## Filling out the Character Sheet

On the Character Sheet, there is a _magic_ field. Characters with rune master skills must write their _runes_, _skill level_, and _magic power_ in this field.

For the character in the above example, we'd write _holy prayer_ and _silent_.

## Key Concepts

- **Rune Mastery**: The ability to chant runes and perform procedures to cast magic.
- **Skill Levels**: The different levels of proficiency in using runes, which determine the power and complexity of magic that can be cast.
- **Magic Cost**: The price for magic services, which is calculated based on the skill level of the rune master, the base mental power cost for the magic, and the expansion rate.
- **NPC Presence**: The likelihood of finding high-level rune masters in different locations based on their skill level.

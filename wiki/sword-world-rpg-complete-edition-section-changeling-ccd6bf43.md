# 《 Changeling 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Half-elves are not a single generation of mixed blood, but are free to have offspring. The child will be a full elf, a half-elf, or a human. There is no such thing as inheriting only 1/4 of the characteristics of one or the other. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00209))_
- Even among humans, elves and halfelves can be born from humans who have had elven blood in their past due to intermittent inheritance. These children are called changelings and are looked down upon in human society. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00210))_

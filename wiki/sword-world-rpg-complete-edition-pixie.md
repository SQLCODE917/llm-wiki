A small, friendly fae creature with insect-like wings, about 30 cm tall, known for playing pranks on humans.

## Characteristics

Pixies look similar to elves, but are only about 30 cm tall. They have wings on their backs reminiscent of insects. Most of them have the appearance of women, but there are also individuals that have the appearance of men. Also, for some reason, they seem to be wearing something that looks like clothes.

## Habitat and Behavior

Pixies are relatively friendly towards humans, but do not actively interact with humans and rarely appear in public. They seem to have a very close relationship with the small spirits called sprites, and have as a basic ability the power of the _Invisibility_ spell that the sprites provide to shamans, which makes them all the more difficult to witness.

## Abilities

Pixies can use up to 2nd level spirit magic. They possess the ability to use _Invisibility_, which they can maintain without consuming mental points, but requires focus. If they lose their concentration (such as when attacking or using other magic), they will appear instantly. They sometimes play pranks on humans, whispering in their ear while remaining hidden and thrusting a weapon (a needle-thin dagger) into their butt.

## Combat

Combat is unlikely to occur, but when it does, pixies can use up to 2nd level spirit magic.

- Sense is the ability to sense the presence of a trap based on past experience and knowledge without actively taking any action. This is determined by making a success roll just before the trap is about to go off. For sense , make the difficulty the same as fi nd , but impose a -4 modifier on the player side. If successful, the presence of a trap will be sensed at a perilous point, and the game master must inform the player of the type of trap. Traps that have been successfully sensed can always be found by attempting to fi nd them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03537))_

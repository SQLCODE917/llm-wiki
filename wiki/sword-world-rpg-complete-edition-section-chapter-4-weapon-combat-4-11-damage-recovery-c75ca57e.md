## Statements

- Damage dealt to a character's life force can be recovered in a variety of ways. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01135))_

## Statements by subsection

### Chapter 4: Weapon Combat / 4.11 Damage Recovery / Magical Recovery

- Life force that a character has lost due to damage is relatively easy to recover through the use of magic or items with magical powers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01138))_
- The degree of this recovery can be found in the descriptions of each spell or magic item. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01139))_

### Chapter 4: Weapon Combat / 4.11 Damage Recovery / First-Aid

- A character with 0 or negative life force will recover up to 1 (positive) point if given treatment by someone with the ranger skill, healer skill, or similar. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01142))_
- This treatment takes _30 minutes_ . It can also instantly recover life force to 1 point, no matter how great the negative, but it _cannot_ recover life force to 2 or more points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01143))_

### Chapter 4: Weapon Combat / 4.11 Damage Recovery / Natural Recovery

- If a character is conscious and at _rest_ (full rest in a safe place), that character recovers 1 point of life force per day. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01146))_
- If a character is _not_ at rest (e.g., traveling, fighting monsters in a labyrinth, not getting much sleep at night due to keeping watch), that character cannot recover any life force at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01148))_

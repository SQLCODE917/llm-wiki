# Sorcerer

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about sorcerer:

## Statements

### 【 Rune Master 】

- The five adventurer skills of sorcerer, shaman, priest, dark priest, and dragon priest are sometimes collectively referred to as rune master skills . This is because these five classes use runes and have the ability to handle magic. Rune masters are viewed with fear by the general public because of their magical abilities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00278))_

### 【 Sorcerer 】

- Many sorcerers are also sages. The rules do not require one character to acquire both of these skills, but if they are acquired at the same time, it gives them a significant advantage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00284))_

### 【 Dwarf 】

- Dwarves excel in strength, life force, and mental power. On the other hand, they tend to be less agile and less intelligent. In terms of skills, sorcerer and shaman cannot be chosen. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00312))_

### Baseline Scores

- Fighter is only useful in combat. The sorcerer, shaman, and priest rune master skills are also of little use for anything other than casting magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01169))_

### 5.1.2 Rune Masters and Rune Master Skills

- Sorcerer uses ancient magic Shaman uses spirit magic Priest uses holy magic Dark priest uses dark magic _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02440))_

- Each corresponds to the skill for which it is named. Those with the sorcerer skill can use ancient magic and are called sorcerers . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02442))_

In this case, the multiplied rate of mental power consumed is 3 x 2 x 4 = 24! _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02618))_

### 《 High Ancient 》

- Sorcerers can speak, read, and write in high ancient. It seems that these runes were used in official texts and conversation in the ancient kingdom, but now much of it has been lost, except for the few that remain in spellbooks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02766))_

### 《 Low Ancient 》

- This was an everyday language of the ancient kingdom, called low ancient , and was the language used by citizens and slaves. Sorcerers can speak, read and write in this language. Low ancient was used in informal texts in the ancient kingdom. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02768))_

### 《 Spellbook 》

- A sorcerer must somehow preserve the ancient magic spells they've learned. Otherwise, even those with the greatest minds cannot possibly memorize all the words, actions, etc. needed to cast spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02773))_

### 《 Magical Catalyst 》

- Sorcerers require a special medium to cast spells. This is a magical catalyst , and it is through this that a sorcerer exercises the power of a spell. Usually, a specially-made mage's staff is used as a magical catalyst, made from a wooden staff with a Create Device spell cast on it. This is why you must have a mage's staff as a restriction when using ancient magic. However, some sorcerers substitute other things besides staves. For example, Laverna, the witch of Orphan, uses an amethyst ring she wears on her right middle finger as her magical catalyst, and Cyanos, leader of the magic knights of Ramliearth, carries a small twig wand in his left hand as a magical catalyst. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02778))_

### [ Familiar ]

- This spell has the effect of allowing a sorcerer to summon and control an animal to become their familiar. The animals that can become familiars are somewhat limited. For example, frogs or crows. Cats and owls are also famous familiars, and these animals will appear in response to the caster's summons and serve the caster. A sorcerer can have only one familiar. If a sorcerer who already has a familiar tries to summon a second one, it'll show no effect whatsoever. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03011))_

### 8th Level Ancient Magic List

- In order for anyone other than the target to discover the illusion, they must have knowledge of this spell. Sorcerers can always discover it, but those who do not must attempt and succeed on a success roll with the sage skill's knowledge . Its target score is 16. However, even if you cannot discover it, it's possible to determine that the target is being attacked by an invisible monster, so although you cannot attack this monster directly , you can still support the target indirectly (casting an Enchant Weapon spell on the target's weapon, healing their wounds with a Healing spell, etc.). Damage dealt by an illusionary beast is not an illusion, but is actually treated as a reduction in life force. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03505))_

### 10th Level Ancient Magic List [ Dimension Gate ]

- A normal Dimension Gate lasts for only 3 minutes, but the sorcerers of the ancient kingdom knew the technique for fixing gates semi-permanently, and so used them as a way of moving between distant places or within labyrinths. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03624))_

### Chapter 6:

- The sage skill represents a comprehensive understanding and depth of knowledge in all academic fields. Academic fields in Sword World are undifferentiated, and owners of this skill are skilled in all disciplines. Many sorcerers have this skill. There is no requirement that you have to have the sage skill and the sorcerer skill at the same time, but if you do acquire the sage skill and the sorcerer skill at the same time, you will receive a bonus on experience points consumed. For more information, please refer to Chapter 8: Character Growth (see p. 129). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05047))_

### Sage Skill

- There is a close connection between the sorcerer skill and the sage skill. The sorcerer skill is the mastery of ancient through study and the improvement of magical ability, while the sage skill is knowledge gained primarily through the study of ancient books. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05500))_

- These two skills have something in common in that their basics are the study of ancient as well as ancient books. In fact, many sorcerers are excellent sages, and the sorcerers' guild is also called the academy of sages . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05501))_

### Chapter 14: / [ Delay Staff ]

- This staff can be pre-imbued with magic that activates either when a specific password is spoken or after a predetermined time has elapsed. The spells that can be stored in this staff are limited to ancient magic with a consumed mental power (not the base mental power cost) of 3 or less. In other words, as a sorcerer reaches higher levels, they become capable of storing more powerful spells. To store a spell in this staff, you must expend the same amount of mental power as casting the spell normally would require. Furthermore, the mental power used to store a spell in this staff cannot be recovered by any means until the stored magic is used. Anyone who knows the password can activate the magic stored in the staff. If set to activate after a certain time instead of with a password, the spell will be centered on the staff. In this case, if the activating magic is an area-effect damage magic like Fireball , the staff will be destroyed upon activation. While this staff can be used as a normal magical catalyst, it provides no bonus when used as a weapon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10778))_

Magic power varies from 4~10. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11048))_

### Thieves Guild/Sorcerers' Guild (Academy)

- Though not official guilds, the Thieves Guild and Sorcerers' Guild (Academy) are essential organizations. Both are global organizations that regularly exchange information between headquarters and branches. Thieves, Sorcerers, and Sages belong to these groups. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11929))_

- The Sorcerers' Guild, on the other hand, is less restrictive and operates more as a cooperative for mutual development. It's closest to an academic society. While the advice is readily available, it might not be particularly reliable in times of crisis. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11931))_

### Oran (Language = Eastern/Mārdan)

- What brings the greatest prestige to Oran is its Sorcerers' Guild. Oran's Sorcerers' Guild was the first to be established in Alecrast and serves as the central hub connecting Sorcerers' Guilds across different regions. The position of Grand Master is held by Mana Rai, renowned as a Great Scholar. It was Mana Rai who established ancient magic as power derived from the magical element "mana," completed the currently accepted theory of magic, and developed common language magic that is activated through the common tongue. It is said that in present-day Alecrast, there exists no Sorcerer or Sage who rivals him, and perhaps none will ever appear. In northern Oran, there are ruins known as the "Fallen City." These ruins were created when Rex, one of the largest aerial cities of the ancient kingdom, fell from the sky. Although various adventurers continue to explore these ruins to this day, treasures from the ancient kingdom era still lie deep within. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11960))_

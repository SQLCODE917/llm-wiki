## Key supported claims

- Gizmos are poisonous gas creatures, armor cannot protect against gas damage (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.226-227)
- Gizmos cannot be attacked by weapons, only fire-type magic is effective (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.226-227)
- Even if struck by poison, the effect does not completely turn them into a monster (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.226-227)
- Golems are generally slow-moving creatures created in ancient times (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.226-227)
- Golems have resistance to mental attacks and poison (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.226-227)

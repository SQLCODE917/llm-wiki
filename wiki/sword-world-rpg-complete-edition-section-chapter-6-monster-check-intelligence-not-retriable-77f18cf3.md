## Statements

- Sages have a wide range of knowledge. This includes knowledge of the various monsters that live in Alecrast. When a sage sees a monster or hears its name, this ability may allow them to discover its true identity. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03613))_
- Each monster has a score called rarity . Using sage skill level + intelligence bonus as the baseline score, make a success roll with this rarity as the target score, and if you succeed, you know the monster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03614))_
- If the check fails, the character has no knowledge of the monster. Even if the player knows, they must treat it as if they had no prior knowledge. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03615))_

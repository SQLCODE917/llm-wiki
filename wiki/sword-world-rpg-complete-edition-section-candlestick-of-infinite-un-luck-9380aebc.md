## Statements

- If you light a candle in this candlestick (any normal candle on the market is fine), whoever lights it will be blessed with great luck. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07472))_
- Even if that person relights the candle, this misfortune _cannot_ be gotten rid of. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07472))_

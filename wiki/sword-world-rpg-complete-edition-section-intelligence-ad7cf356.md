# 【 Intelligence 】

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Intelligence represents the speed of an adventurer's mind and the amount of knowledge they hold, or in other words, their so-called smarts. It also represents the ability to perceive things, such as finding hidden objects and sensing abnormalities spur of the moment. Above all, it is important when using magic, a major attraction in fantasy. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00170))_

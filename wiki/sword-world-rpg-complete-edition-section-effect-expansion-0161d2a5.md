# 《 Effect Expansion 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Some expansions don't fall into any of the above types. For spells that can perform these special expansions, effect is written in the expansion section of the magic details, and an explanation is given under each spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02614))_
- Some magic allows for multiple expansion types. In these cases, you can perform multiple expansions at the same time. However, at this time, the amount of mental power consumed will become extremely large. By multiplying the rates determined for each expansion, you can determine the total multiplied rate of mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02616))_
- Suppose a sorcerer casts an Energy Bolt on three enemies ( x 3 expansion) with a +1 to his final score ( x 2 expansion) and he makes 4 damage checks for each ( x 4 expansion). In this case, the multiplied rate of mental power consumed is 3 x 2 x 4 = 24! _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02618))_

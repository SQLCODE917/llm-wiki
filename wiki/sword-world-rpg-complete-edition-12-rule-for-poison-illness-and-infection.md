- This score is used when making a check to see if you know the illness. ### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 12.2 Rules for Poison, Illness and Infection / Intensity/ Healing Score 》

- After a certain period of time has elapsed after becoming ill, the illness either progresses further or gets better. The degree of progression of an illness is indicated by a score called **depth** . Immediately after contracting an illness, the _depth_ is 1. After the time indicated by **progression speed** has elapsed since you contracted the illness, roll two dice and look at the result. If the roll is less than the **progression intensity** , the disease gets worse and the _depth_ increases by 1. If the roll is greater than or equal to the _progression intensity_ but less than the **healing score** , the illness neither worsens nor improves, and the _depth_ remains the same. If the result is greater than or equal to the _healing score_ , the illness will be cured and the _depth_ will be reduced by 1. When the _depth_ becomes 0, you are completely recovered. The patient's adventurer level and life force bonus have _no_ influence on this check. Adventurer level may also be hero level, but _even if you're a hero, you cannot win against illness_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04401))_

- Even if a shaman cannot use the spell _Restore Health_ , they can still work with one type of spirit to alleviate symptoms. For example, by working with salamander, fever, which is the main symptom of a cold, can be alleviated. When a shaman performs such a treatment, they gain a +1 bonus on progression/healing checks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04402))_

- In addition to these, depending on the illness, there are herbs and special medicines that can provide bonuses to progression/healing checks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04403))_

- When the holy magic _Cure Disease_ or _Refresh_ , or the spirit magic _Restore Health_ are cast, the caster must make a success roll using their magic power as the baseline score. The target score is double the _healing score_ (added in parentheses). If you succeed, the _depth_ of the illness is reduced by 1 and you can make an additional success roll. You can continue to make this check until you fail or until the illness is completely cured. If this does _not_ completely cure the illness, it’ll be _useless_ to cast these spells on the same patient until the caster's magic power increases. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04404))_

- If a healer who knows the true nature of an illness cares for the patient for more than one hour per day, they can make a similar check using that healer's _healer skill level + intelligence bonus_ as the baseline score. However, this must be at each progression speed of the illness (i.e. immediately after the illness's progression check), and you can only make one success roll. However, even if they fail, the same healer can continue to care for the patient. If after some time has passed, an illness progression check occurs, the healer can also make a healing check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04405))_

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 12.2 Rules for Poison, Illness and Infection / 《 Fatal Depth 》

- If, as an illness progresses, its _depth_ reaches the **fatal depth** , the character will die from the illness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04407))_

### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 12.2 Rules for Poison, Illness and Infection / 《 Infection Power 》

- Indicates the infectiousness of the illness. If it’s **very strong** , it has the infection power of a plague. **Strong** is about the same level as influenza. As in the example, **moderate** means it's just a cold, and **weak** means it's not very infectious. When it's **very weak** , it's not infectious and is unlikely to spread to other people. When an illness is _very strong_ or _strong_ , there is a high possibility that the infection will spread to the healer, etc. who is caring for the patient. If the game master determines that there is a possibility of contracting the illness, have the character make a progression check for the illness (compare a 2D roll with progression intensity). If they fail, 164 the character has contracted the illness at _depth_ 1. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04409))_

- This is a strange disease that occurs due to a significant decrease in the power of undine within the body. The cause is a very small wound on the fingertip, etc. When infected with this illness, the wound will not close at all, but moreover, something that looks like cotton will start to bulge out of the wound. At _depth_ 1, the character's actions are not affected at all. If you pluck a little bit of the cotton, it will instantly disappear, but at 2, the character will fall into a coma and the cotton will come out no matter how much you pluck. Although the progression speed is not that fast, it is an incurable disease that is difficult to treat once contracted. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04423))_

- This illness, which turns humans into lycanthropes, is thought to be different from normal illnesses caused by abnormalities in spirit power. - Anyone wounded by a lycanthrope must roll 2D. If the roll is a 6 or less, you'll be infected with lycanthropy (the game master must secretly roll the dice if the adventurer does not know that lycanthropy is an infectious disease). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04465))_

- The progression of lycanthropy differs from the rules of normal illnesses. Those infected with lycanthropy will have swollen wounds and a high fever. This fever continues endlessly. Every night from that day onwards, compare 2D + 3 with _the number of days since the new moon_ (if it's 16 days or more, then _30 - the number of days_ ). Once the score is equal to or less than the number of days, the character becomes a full lycanthrope and transforms into a beast form. This _cannot_ heal naturally. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04466))_

- Before becoming a full lycanthrope, it can be cured with a spell such as the holy magic _Cure Disease_ , etc. (the target score is 16), but once you become a beast, you _cannot_ be cured again. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04467))_

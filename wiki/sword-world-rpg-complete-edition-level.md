# Level

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about level:

## Statements

### 1.3.1 Skill Basics

- There are several types of skills, which are divided into several groups. For each skill, a character's proficiency is represented by a level from 0~10. 0 levels indicates that someone is completely unrelated to that skill, while 10 levels represents the highest proficiency that a normal human can attain. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00238))_

### 1.3.3 Adventurer Level

- In Sword World RPG, a character's capability as an adventurer is determined by their adventurer skill level. The highest level among the adventurer skills that a character has is called that character's adventurer level . For example, a character with priest skill level 1, fighter skill level 4 (these are adventurer skills), sailor skill level 5, and fisher skill level 2 (these are general skills) would be at adventurer level 4 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00253))_

### 1.4.5 Character Growth via Starting Experience Points

- Characters can spend their starting experience points to gain skill levels. For example, if you want to acquire 1 level in ranger, you can do so by subtracting 500 from your starting experience points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00616))_

### 【 Attack Power 】

- If fighter (or thief, ranger) skill level is 0 , then attack power is 0 regardless of the character's dexterity bonus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01441))_

### 【 Bonus Damage 】

- If fighter (or thief, ranger) skill level is 0 , bonus damage is 0 regardless of the character's strength bonus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01451))_

### 【 Evasion Speed 】

- A fighter (or thief) skill level of 0 does not prevent you from receiving this shield bonus. Therefore, the evasion speed of such a character will be 1 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01463))_

### 【 Damage Reduction 】

- Those who are called heroes do not die from just a few injuries. In Sword World RPG , a character with a high adventurer level, that is, a character who has a high level in any adventurer skill, is considered a hero . ### 5.1.3 Learning Magic

- Magic is not omnipotent. It is only possible to achieve a defined and limited set of effects by weaving certain words (in runes), called spells . There are several types of spells, each with different effects. Depending on the strength of their effects, spells are divided into levels from 1-10 . 1st level spells are very rudimentary, while 10th level spells are very powerful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02448))_

- However, working with higher level (i.e., more advanced) spells requires a high rune master skill. A rune master can use all magic up to their skill level, but cannot use magic of a higher level than their current skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02449))_

### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / 5th Level Basic Dark Magic List

- This spell is used on an individual to impose penalties that are not directly life-threatening. It' s up to the game master to decide what kind of penalty to impose. However, the higher the caster's level, or if some kind of price is paid at the same time as the spell, the more terrifying the effect will be. ### Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / [ Blood Protection ]

- The caster can expand the effect to provide a greater defense effect. However, in order to do this, you must consume an extra amount of your life force as well as your mental power. For example, a 6th level caster can consume 8 points of mental power and 2 points of life force at the same time to provide 2 points of defense effect. The same applies when expanding the target score. If you wanted to give 2 points of defense effect to three people each, a 6th level caster's mental power consumed would be 24 points and their life force consumed would be 6 points. ### Chapter 12: Various Checks / 《 Rarity 》

- After a certain period of time has elapsed after becoming ill, the illness either progresses further or gets better. The degree of progression of an illness is indicated by a score called depth . Immediately after contracting an illness, the depth is 1. After the time indicated by progression speed has elapsed since you contracted the illness, roll two dice and look at the result. If the roll is less than the progression intensity , the disease gets worse and the depth increases by 1. If the roll is greater than or equal to the progression intensity but less than the healing score , the illness neither worsens nor improves, and the depth remains the same. If the result is greater than or equal to the healing score , the illness will be cured and the depth will be reduced by 1. When the depth becomes 0, you are completely recovered. The patient's adventurer level and life force bonus have no influence on this check. Adventurer level may also be hero level, but even if you're a hero, you cannot win against illness . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06682))_

# Level

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about level:

## Statements

- 0 levels indicates that someone is completely unrelated to that skill, while 10 levels represents the highest proficiency that a normal human can attain. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00230))_
- For example, a character with priest skill level 1, fighter skill level 4 (these are adventurer skills), sailor skill level 5, and fisher skill level 2 (these are general skills) would be at adventurer level **4** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00247))_
- The highest level among the adventurer skills that a character has is called that character's **adventurer level** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00247))_
- Adventurer level is equal to the highest adventurer skill level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00539))_
- In _Sword World RPG_ , a character with a high adventurer level, that is, a character who has a high level in any adventurer skill, is considered a _hero_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00894))_
- 1st level spells are very rudimentary, while 10th level spells are very powerful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01231))_
- In other words, adventurer level can even be called hero level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03591))_
- However, in any case, a character's skill level can only reach 10th level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03730))_
- A character with a 3rd level sorcerer skill or a 3rd level sage skill will not receive any experience point reductions regardless of whether they reach 4th level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03757))_
- 5th level signifies being sufficient at a general skill, so consider reaching levels beyond that to take a large amount of effort. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03774))_
- Their priest skill level is a reference for how close the sacrifice is to a god, but there are some humans who are close to a god regardless of their skill level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04123))_

- Adventurer level may also be hero level, but _even if you're a hero, you cannot win against illness_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04655))_

# 4.11.2 First-Aid

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- A character with 0 or negative life force will recover up to 1 (positive) point if given treatment by someone with the ranger skill, healer skill, or similar. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01063))_
- This treatment takes 30 minutes . It can also instantly recover life force to 1 point, no matter how great the negative, but it cannot recover life force to 2 or more points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01064))_

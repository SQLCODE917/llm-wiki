Lost spells are ancient magic spells that are not taught in the sorcerers' guild. They are known only to a limited number of sorcerers or were lost with the fall of the ancient kingdom.

## **Acquisition Methods**

Lost spells can be acquired through two main methods:

1. **Learning from Sorcerers**: Some sorcerers have acquired lost spells through years of hard work or sheer luck. Learning these spells requires finding such sorcerers.

2. **Obtaining Spellbooks or Scrolls**: Spellbooks and scrolls containing lost spells can be found as treasure in the ruins of the ancient kingdom, and by studying them, one can learn lost spells.

## **Usage Restrictions**

Even after learning a lost spell, a character cannot use it unless their sorcerer skill is of the spell's level or greater. If the skill level is insufficient, they must wait until their skill level increases before they can use it. Lost spells cannot be used simply by increasing the sorcerer skill level; one must know of a spell's existence and learn how to use it.

The determination of which spells are considered "lost" is up to the game master's choice. While Table 17-1 provides a guideline, the game master may designate spells not on the list as lost spells or convert spells on the list to general spells.

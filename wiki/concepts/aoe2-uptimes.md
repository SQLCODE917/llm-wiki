# AoE2 Uptimes

Uptimes refer to the timing and efficiency of upgrading from one age to the next in Age of Empires II.

## Source-backed details

| Claim | Evidence |
| --- | --- |
| Getting up to the next age requires specific timings that can cause significant damage to the enemy economy. | [aoe2-basics:claim_aoe2-basics_c004_787f0e1d] |
| Castle age timings require balancing economy while producing and using your army to achieve optimal performance. | [aoe2-basics:claim_aoe2-basics_c004_f697af32] |
| Imperial age follows similar resource stacking principles as castle age but offers more varied strategic options. | [aoe2-basics:claim_aoe2-basics_c004_3ee18738] |

# AoE2 Water Maps

Water maps in Age of Empires II are maps such as Islands and Team Islands where water control, transports, fishing economy, landings, and build execution shape the game.

## Source-backed details

| Claim | Evidence | Locator | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Water maps include maps like Islands and Team Islands, and players may struggle with them because they have not practiced them or do not understand how they are played. | "Many people struggle with playing water maps, most just because they have never tried them and others because they just can't understand the way these maps are played." | `normalized:L614` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Older water-map play was more galley-focused, with the player who had more galleys often winning. | "all you had to do was to make galleys and the guy with more galleys won." | `normalized:L616` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Basic water-map play uses a build to gain water control and block transport attempts. | "you use the build and gain control of water and block any attempts to transport." | `normalized:L618` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Older-style water games could become one-town-center games focused on advancing through ages while spamming and microing galleys. | "water maps were just 1TC fiestas where you just try to progress trough the ages while spamming galleys whole game and microing them against opponents galleys." | `normalized:L618` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| With transports in dark age, fires, and demos, players can end up playing both water and land at the same time. | "now that you can do transports in dark age and there are fires and demos etc. in the game, it has become a bit more complex than that. It has become more of a default that you end up playing both water and land at the same time." | `normalized:L620` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Defending against a landing starts with scouting, then may involve walling, spending resources on defense, and keeping water control to prevent more transports. | "Simplest way is to scout and know that the opponent is landing first and foremost. Then you can try to wall them off and if you gain control of water you can easily prevent them from transporting over again." | `normalized:L622` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| A landing player aims to disrupt the opponent's economy quickly, but the landing invests many resources into buildings and may lose either land or water. | "If you are the one landing the opponent, your goal is to disrupt his eco as soon as possible. The problem with landing is that you're investing A LOT of resources on buildings. You're nearly guaranteed to lose either land or water." | `normalized:L624` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Water-map strategies need practiced builds because water maps are highly execution-based and snowball hard. | "you need to have a build for it. Water maps are highly execution based, even more so than your normal arabia games." | `normalized:L626` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |

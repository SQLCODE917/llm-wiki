# AoE2 Decision Making

Decision making in Age of Empires II is the use of available and missing information to choose actions in a game state where plans, unit use, attention, and timing can change the outcome.

## Source-backed details

| Claim | Evidence | Locator | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Decision making evaluates available information and also reasons from missing information. | "Decision making refers to evaluating the information available to you and deriving from the lack of information as much." | `normalized:L557` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Examples include man-at-arms going left or right, archers going forward, an enemy scout sniping them, and X Y Z or A B C depending on the game state. | "Do I go left or right with my man at arms, do I send the archers forward instantly to meet up with the man at arms, or is the enemy scout going to snipe them on the way? Am I going to do X Y Z or A B C depending on the game state." | `normalized:L557` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| A valid game plan can become disadvantageous through decision making and unit use. | "Your gameplan can be valid, but your decision making and use of the units you gain through gameplan or how the things are going can turn in an instant disadvantageous for you." | `normalized:L559` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Waiting for an unnecessary upgrade can be a decision-making mistake. | "Giving a minute extra time to an opponent while waiting for an upgrade that you don't actually need can be a crucial decision making mistake." | `normalized:L559` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Decision making is difficult to reduce to fixed rules because answers depend on how the question is defined. | "There are multiple sides to these questions and the answer is always depends until you've defined the answer to the question well enough." | `normalized:L561` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Part of decision making is choosing which part of the map needs attention at a given time. | "Part of decision making is also splitting your attention, you have to know what part of the map you've to watch at any given time." | `normalized:L565` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Attention is treated as a resource that requires choices about where to spend it. | "Attention is a resource too and choosing where to spend it requires right decisions." | `normalized:L567` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |

# AoE2 Team Play Strategies

Team play strategies in Age of Empires II refer to coordinated tactics and approaches used by players working together in multiplayer matches.

## Source-backed details

| Claim | Evidence | Locator | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Team games require coordination with allies to prevent players from quitting. | "The game is not lost unless one of your allies quits and this can happen because of opponents or because of your and your teams actions." | `normalized:L683` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Team games require using only gold units to maintain resource efficiency. | "First of all, you should use only gold units in team games, unless you have a solid reason to do halb + siege push." | `normalized:L677` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Starting trading early in team games prevents resource depletion. | "You should always start trading before gold runs out, but you also need to be careful to not start trading too soon because it is a huge resource sinkhole to start the trade." | `normalized:L677` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Trash units are disadvantageous in team games. | "Why are trash units bad? Well the most powerful team game unit composition that nearly every civ combination has access to is Knights + Xbows." | `normalized:L681` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |

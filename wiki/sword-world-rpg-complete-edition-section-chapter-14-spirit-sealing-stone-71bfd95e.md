## Statements

- If spirit magic is used around the wearer (used towards the wearer, or the wearer him or herself attempts to use spirit magic), this stone will seal the spirit, and the spirit magic is nullified as a result. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10964))_
- However, if you try to block magic that uses a spirit with an average monster level of 5 or greater, the magic itself will be blocked but the spirit will not be sealed and the stone will break . Also, greater spirits cannot be sealed, so spirit magic used will take effect as-is, and at the same time the stone will break . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10965))_
- Only one lesser spirit can be sealed at a time. If a spirit has already been sealed, the stone is of no use. A shaman can release a sealed spirit. Also, if it's an undine , salamander , sylph , or gnome , it' s possible to expel them (to the spirit world) using the ancient magic spell Suppress Elemental . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10966))_

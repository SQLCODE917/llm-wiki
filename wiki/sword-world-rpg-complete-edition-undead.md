In the Sword World RPG, undead are monsters that exist and move around through forces that are the exact opposite of those of humans and normal animals. They despise creatures with positive life force and also target them both as prey and for the expansion of their species.

## Nature

If the source of our life is called _positive_ life force, then the undead's source can be called _negative_ life force. This negative life force is sometimes called the _undeath force_. There are many mysteries surrounding the origin of these undead, but it's completely different from how normal animals give birth. They may be created by magical power, reborn, or infected and transformed like an illness.

## Characteristics

Undead are not affected by poison or illness. Many spells with mental effects are also meaningless. They do not require rest in the normal sense and can never be magically put to sleep. They despise creatures with positive life force and also target them both as prey and for the expansion of their species. If you meet them, there is a high probability that you'll fight.

## Key Entities

- **Positive Life Force**: The life force of humans and normal animals.
- **Negative Life Force**: The life force of undead, also known as the undeath force.
- **Undeath Force**: Another name for the negative life force of undead.
- **Sorcerers**: Characters who may create undead through magical power.
- **Shamans**: Characters who may encounter undead in their travels.

## Key Concepts

- **Life Force**: The fundamental life energy that distinguishes living beings from undead.
- **Undeath Force**: The opposite of positive life force, used by undead to exist and move.
- **Rebirth**: A possible origin of undead, where they are reborn rather than created.
- **Infection**: A possible origin of undead, where they are infected and transformed like an illness.
- **Magical Creation**: A possible origin of undead, where they are created by magical power.
- **Despise**: Undead despise creatures with positive life force and target them as prey.
- **Expansion**: Undead seek to expand their species by targeting creatures with positive life force.

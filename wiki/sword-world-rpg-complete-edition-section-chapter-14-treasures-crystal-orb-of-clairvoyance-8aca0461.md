- This is a magical crystal ball that shows the user every place around the world. The extent to which the effect reaches depends on the magical power stored in the crystal ball. - The commonly found _Crystal Orb of Clairvoyance_ can project any place you’ve been to _before_ , but can only use this power _once per day_ . The duration is 15 minutes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06866))_

## Statements

- Naturally, a character must have a name. It's not convenient to enjoy the fictional world of Forcelia if they're always called by the player's name or just _hey.... you_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00372))_
- Therefore, a character should be given a name that is appropriate for Forcelia. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00373))_
- When deciding on a name for a character, it is usually best to stick with a basic western-style name. After all, the names of most people in _Alecrast_ , the setting of _Sword World RPG_ , are like that. Purely Japanese or Chinese names are not suitable. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00374))_
- In _Alecrast_ , first name, then last name are usually written in that order. In rare cases, a middle name may also be added between the first and last names. Alternatively, it's acceptable to simply decide on a first name and leave the last unspecified, or to use a nickname or alias, as is often used by thieves. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00375))_

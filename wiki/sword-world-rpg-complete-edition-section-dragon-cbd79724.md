# **[ Dragon ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- There are three main types of dragons in Forcelia: ancient dragons (drakes), elder dragons, and lesser dragons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05187))_
- All of these dragons are similar in appearance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05188))_
- Their entire body is covered with hard scales, and the color of these scales varies depending on the individual. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05188))_
- Their legs are thick and sturdy, for bipedal walking on the ground. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05189))_
- They have a snake-like head and tail, with horns and spikes growing along their spine. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05189))_
- These wings are comparatively small for their body size. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05189))_
- They have bat-like wings on their backs. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05189))_
- It is said that there is no way for a human to ever take on a drake. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05190))_
- The 30 meter long drake, feared as a _god slayer_ monster, and also a master of a variety of magic, is nowhere to be seen in Forcelia today. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05190))_
- Even the elder and lesser species, which are weaker than drakes, are far too powerful enemies for humans to face, and those who defeat these dragons are hailed as _dragon slayer_ heroes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05190))_

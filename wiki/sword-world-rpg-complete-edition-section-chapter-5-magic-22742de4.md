## Statements by subsection

### Chapter 5: Magic / Magic Contraction

- Magic can also be **contracted** as opposed to expanded. However, this does _not_ reduce the amount of mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01339))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 《 Area Contraction 》

- You can make the area of effect narrower than originally intended. The shape (usually a circle) _cannot_ be changed. You also cannot cast it in such a way that it hits the enemy directly in the eyes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01343))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 《 Volume Contraction 》

- Similar to area contraction, you can also reduce the volume of the targeted object or space. Again, the shape cannot be changed, and you cannot directly aim it at the enemy's eyes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01345))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 《 Duration Contraction 》

- Magic with a duration can be optionally shortened. However, you _cannot_ set a time that causes the effect to wear off in the middle of a round. You may _only_ set it to wear off at the _end_ of a round. You must also decide how long you want the magic to last _before_ you cast it. Magic that’s already been cast cannot be canceled at will. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01347))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 《 Magic Power Decrease 》

- Magic power can be optionally reduced. This will reduce the final score and, if it's damage magic, _also_ reduce bonus damage (it’s not possible to _only_ reduce the final score or _only_ reduce bonus damage). You must declare how many points you’ll reduce _before_ you roll the dice. If used on multiple targets, you must reduce it by the same amount for _all_ of them. Also, if the side casting the magic rolls double sixes, the resisting side will _also_ need double sixes, no matter _how much_ magic power was declared to be decreased (if it's a monster, it _cannot_ resist). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01349))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 《 Effect Contraction 》

- All magic that can expand their effects can also contract their effects. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01351))_

### Technical frame 4: Chapter 5: Magic / Combined Expansion 5.1.8.2 and Contraction

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01353))_

> Although it's not often done, it's possible to perform magic expansion _and_ contraction at the same time. For example, you could _extend_ the area of effect while _reducing_ magic power. The mental power required at this time is decided by _expansion_ . It _cannot_ be reduced by contraction. For example, if you _double_ the area _and_ reduce magic power by _-4_ , the mental power consumed will still be _double_ .

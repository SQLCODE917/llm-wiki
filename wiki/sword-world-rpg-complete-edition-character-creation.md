# 1.4 Character Creation

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about 1.4 character creation:

## Statements

- A Character Sheet is used to write down various information about a character during the course of the game. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00305))_
- Table 1-7: Experience Points by Skill (at Character Creation) is almost identical to a segment of Table 8-1: Experience Points by Skill, which is used for character growth as explained in chapter 8. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00415))_
- The **Character Sheet** that accompanies this book is designed to record and list various character details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00505))_
- First, the player character's race must be chosen. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00308))_
- A character's six ability scores are each the totals of two of these eight subability scores. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00328))_
- For this character being created, the bonuses are dexterity +2, agility +2, intelligence +2, strength +2, life force +3, and mental power +2. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00346))_
- This character can be considered fairly mediocre except for the excellent life force bonus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00346))_
- If a character has acquired the merchant skill, they must _always_ come from the merchant background. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00355))_
- The character ended up coming from the _mercenary_ background. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00364))_
- Since the character we're creating is a human, we'll use the _backgrounds_ table. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00364))_
- The character has 1200 gamels. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00365))_
- A character must be given a name, and their age and gender must be decided. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00370))_
- When deciding on the age of a character, it should be between 15 years old and around 30 years old at most for a human. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00371))_
- Naturally, a character must have a name. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00385))_

Therefore, their dexterity, agility, intelligence, and mental power are slightly higher, while their strength and life force are slightly lower.

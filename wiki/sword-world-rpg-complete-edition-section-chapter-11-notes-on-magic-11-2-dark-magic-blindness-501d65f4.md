- This spell has the effect of taking away the target's vision. If the target successfully resists, it'll have no effect. If a human or monster in this state (only those that rely on vision) attempts to attack, they suffer a -4 modifier to attack and evasion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05803))_

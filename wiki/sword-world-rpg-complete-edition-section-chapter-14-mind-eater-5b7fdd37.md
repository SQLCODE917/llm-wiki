## Statements

- Anyone whose life force has been dealt damage by this sword will suffer 2 points of damage to their mental points unless they succeed on a mental power resistance roll against target score 11. This damage cannot be reduced by adventurer level, similar to that of Mental Attack . Also, even if the sword does hit, this effect does not apply if damage is not dealt to life force. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08252))_

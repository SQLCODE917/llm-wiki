## Statements

- A score equal to a monster's evasion points - 7 is written next to it in parenthesis. This is used here. The game master should roll 2D and add it to the number in parenthesis. This is the target score for a character to hit the monster with an attack. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11176))_
- As in the previous section, this is the process for a success roll with monster's evasion points -7 as the difficulty . Therefore, a tie would result in character's attack success = monster's evasion failure . Please remember that the game master should be considered fi rst to roll the dice. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11177))_
- Once the attack hits, it's the same. A monster's defense points are fixed, and the difference is based on the character's strike roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11178))_

# [ Dryad ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Dryads are plant spirits. They usually reside in trees, but a shaman can summon them anywhere there are plants. Their figure is that of a completely naked elf woman, with long green hair wrapped around her entire body. Their skin is also green. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07250))_
- A mad dryad uses the spirit magic " Charm " to cause division among allies, and tries to block their opponent's movements with " Binding ". Using these spells does not consume any of the dryad's mental points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07251))_
- To harm a dryad, it must be a magical or silver weapon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07253))_

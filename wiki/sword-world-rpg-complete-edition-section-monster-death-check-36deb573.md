# **Monster Death Check**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Subtract the amount of negative life points, if any, from life point resistance, and if the result is _7 or greater_ , the monster is _alive_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01100))_
- A score called the monster’s **life point resistance** is used instead. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01100))_
- If the result is _6 or lower_ , the monster is _dead_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01100))_

## Technical atoms

> Monsters with 0 or negative life points must also make a check to see if they live or die.
_(source: Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01100))_

## Source record

This section details a list of ancient magic spells, organized by level.

## Key supported claims

- The _Magic Reflection_ spell reflects magic cast on the caster back to the opponent, but only works on magic with an area of effect of one. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.80-82)
- The _Illusionary Beast_ spell creates an illusionary beast that attacks the target, which can only be seen by the target. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.80-82)
- The _Stun Cloud_ spell paralyzes creatures within its area of effect, causing them to lose consciousness for one hour. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.80-82)
- The _Force Field_ spell creates an invisible barrier protecting the caster from physical attacks, reducing damage by 30 points. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.80-82)
- The _Lightning Bind_ spell entangles the target in an electric net, dealing 20 damage each round and reducing their movement. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.80-82)

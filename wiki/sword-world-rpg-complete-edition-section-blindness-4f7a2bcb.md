From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This spell has the effect of taking away the target's vision. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03963))_
- If the target successfully resists, it’ll have no effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03963))_
- If a human or monster in this state (only those that rely on vision) attempts to attack, they suffer a -4 modifier to attack and evasion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03963))_

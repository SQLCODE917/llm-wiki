## Statements

- Once race is chosen, sub-ability scores can be determined. Roll the dice according to Table 1-3: Determining Sub-Ability Scores and write down the sub-ability scores from A-H in order. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00444))_

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00284, source-range-de1806a5-00288))_

> Table 1-3: Determining Sub-Ability Scores shows how to determine subability scores for each race. On the table, "D" stands for dice . 1D stands for one die , 2D stands for two dice , and 1/2D stands for half the result of one dice (rounded up) . In addition, a notation such as 1D+6 means to roll one dice and add 6 to the result .

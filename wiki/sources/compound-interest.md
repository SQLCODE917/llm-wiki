# Compound Interest

## Summary

This source explains compound interest: interest is added to the principal, and future interest is calculated on the new total.

## Key claims

- Compound interest grows money by repeatedly applying interest to the updated balance.
- The formula is `A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)`.
- Compound interest is used in savings accounts, loans, investing, and finance.

# 1. Replay Collections

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The Sword World RPG Replay Collections , published by Fujimi Dragon Books, are a good way to learn how to actually play the game. The progress of the game is reproduced via conversation between the game master and the players. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00120))_
- Once you read these, you will quickly understand how the game is meant to progress. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00121))_
- There are three points to be aware of, however. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00122))_
- First, Sword World RPG Replay Collections ① ~ ⑨ are not based on this rulebook, but rather on the previously released old version of the rules (see below). Since some of the rules in this book you're reading right now have been revised, there are rare cases where the descriptions are contradictory. When you play, please follow what's written in these rules. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00123))_
- Second, although the Duda RPG Replay Collection series uses the rules of Sword World RPG , it is a variant that emphasizes the aspects of humor, fantasy and mystery. As a game, it's nearly the same, but in this series, modified rules not found in Sword World RPG , such as inept detective checks , have been introduced to demonstrate its uniqueness. So please note that even though it's 99% Sword World RPG, it's still not 100% (for more information on this, please read the Introduction and Commentary sections of Duda RPG Replay Collection ① : The Lost Detective Appears! ). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00124))_
- Finally, these replays are based on actual play and reproduction of the situations, but have been cut or edited for redundancy and other aspects in order to prioritize readability. Some rule errors that occurred during the game have been corrected and dealt with, especially in areas where they do not have a major impact. In actual play, things do not go as smoothly as this. A replay should not be considered a complete manual, but merely a reference. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00125))_

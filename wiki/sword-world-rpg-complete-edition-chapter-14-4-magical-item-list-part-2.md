- Form: Mithril silver chain mail armor that glows dull grey
- Required Strength: 6 (Defense power 16)

#### Cold Cloak
- Rarity: 18
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Cloth armor
- Required Strength: 3 (Defense power 3)
- Base Trade Price: 250,000 gamels
- Powers: +3 to evasion speed and damage reduction. Also gives +3 to resistance to fire-type attacks.

#### Magic Acceptor
- Rarity: 12/17
- Enchanter: Unknown
- Form: Soft leather armor
- Required Strength: 7 (Defense power 7)
- Base Trade Price: 16,000 gamels
- Powers: +1 to evasion speed and damage reduction. Unable to resist magic.
- When performing a _treasure appraisal_ with the _sage skill_, if the _final score_ of the success roll is between 12 and 17, it will only be identified as a +1 soft leather. Only when the _final score_ reaches 17 will you notice that this armor has a hidden drawback. This armor completely neutralizes the wearer's resistance to magic. As a result, while wearing this armor, all attempts to resist any magic automatically fail.

#### Shield of the Brave
- Rarity: 13/18
- Enchanter: Cyrus, "The Uncertain"
- Form: Large shield with an emerald set in the center
- Required Strength: 13
- Base Trade Price: 3300 gamels
- Powers: +1 to Evasion, +2 to resistance to Breath attacks, concentrates attacks on the owner
- When performing a _treasure appraisal_ with the sage skill, if the success roll's _final score_ is 13 or higher but less than 18, you can only determine that it is a +1 large shield that also provides +2 resistance against breath attacks. Only when the final score reaches 18 do you realize this shield has a catch. The emerald gleaming in the center of the shield attracts the attention of opponents, causing them to focus their attacks on the user. Enemies that use breath attacks will especially target the shield's wielder. Those who carry this shield, which trades higher evasion speed for drawing enemy attacks, are destined to become shields for their companions. If the emerald is removed, all magical power is lost. The emerald itself is worth 1000 gamels.

### Accessories

#### Earrings of Interpretation
- Rarity: 15
- Enchanter: Unknown
- Form: Earrings with blue gems set in a teardrop-shaped gold base
- Base Trade Price: 96,000 gamels
- Powers: Can understand languages you don’t know
- By wearing these earrings, you can understand the contents of conversations in languages you do not know. However, you can only understand conversations you hear through the earrings, you cannot speak, read or write that language.

#### Earrings of Pooka
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Unknown
- Form: Small silver earrings with intricately colored gems
- Base Trade Price: 170,000 gamels
- Powers: Can shapeshift into a small animal.
- When wearing these earrings and focusing your thoughts, you can transform into small animals such as mice or birds. After shapeshifting, you can return to your original form by either touching the earrings while concentrating, or by using _Dispel Magic_ (target score 15). Additionally, after shapeshifting, you retain the same intelligence you had before the transformation.

#### Container Bracelet
- Rarity: 15
- Form: A bracelet with a large gem
- Enchanter: Many
- Base Trade Price: 120,000 gamels
- Powers: Sucks in an object
- When you bring an object close to the jewel attached to the bracelet and speak the password in low ancient, the object will be absorbed into the jewel. While the object is absorbed, if you speak the password again, you can retrieve the object.
- This bracelet cannot absorb more than one object at a time, and it cannot absorb anything that exceeds 50 centimeters in length in any dimension.

#### Sleepless Pendant
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Cyrus, "The Uncertain"
- Form: Small silver pendant
- Base Trade Price: 80,000 gamels
- Powers: Wearer never falls asleep.
- This was created to allow working for long periods without sleep. Those who wear this pendant will never fall asleep. Neither the _Sleep Cloud_ of _ancient magic_ nor the _Sleep_ of _spirit magic_ will have any effect. However, it cannot reduce the fatigue and other effects that come with staying awake for long periods. If worn for too long, one might die from sleep deprivation.

#### Necklace of Faith
- Rarity: 12
- Enchanter: Gerebeis, "The Sharptongued"
- Form: Necklace adorned with bluish diamonds
- Base Trade Price: 170,000 gamels
- Powers: Makes one target believe what the wearer believes.
- The effect of this item activates when the wearer directly looks at their target. If the target fails a _mental power resistance roll_ with a _target score of 16_ , they become affected by the effect. The effect cannot manifest on more than one target at a time. The effect continues as long as the item is worn. Once affected, the target will not change their beliefs no matter how they are persuaded or what evidence they are shown. However, if the wearer of the necklace themselves stops believing in the truth, the necklace's effect will disappear.
- Targets are limited to humans, fae, youma, titans, and animals.

### Amulets

#### Amulet of Countersense
- Rarity: 14
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Amulet
- Base Trade Price: 190,000 gamels
- Powers: Know who is detecting
- If the owner of this amulet has a _Detection_ spell cast on him or her, the amulet will glitter and project the opponent who cast the spell.

#### Amulet Versus Undead
- Rarity: 15
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Amulet
- Base Trade Price: 6,000 gamels
- Powers: +1 to effect checks against undead
- If the person wearing this amulet uses the holy magic _Turn Undead_ or the spellsong Requiem, they can add +1 to a 2D roll when making an _effect check_ against undead (if the roll is 12, treat it as 12).

#### Telecall Amulet
- Rarity: 10
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Amulet with a jeweled ball at the tip
- Base Trade Price: 48,000 gamels
- Powers: The wearers can talk to each other over a far distance.
- This is a magical amulet that allows you to converse with someone in a distant location. The amulet possesses magical power such that when you correctly recite the password of your intended recipient's charm and speak towards the jewel at its tip, your voice will emerge from their jewel. In other words, at least two amulets are required to make a call. While this useful item has been found relatively frequently in the ruins of ancient kingdom, its communication range is limited to approximately 10 kilometers.

### Crystals

#### Magic Crystal
- Rarity: 13
- Form: A stone similar to topaz that emits a bluish-white light from inside
- Enchanter: Many
- Base Trade Price: Stored mental points x mental points x 100 gamels
- Powers: Concentrates magic power.
- When referring to a magic crystal, it means a stone that concentrates magic power. While it looks similar to topaz at first glance, upon closer inspection it emits a bluish-white light from within. Normally, this stone stores 2D+10 mental points, and a rune master can use magic by consuming these mental points instead of their own mental power. However, only one stone can be used at a time, and it cannot be used simultaneously with the caster's own mental power. It can also be used as a substitute for mental power when using common runes.
- When the stored mental points are depleted, the magic crystal shatters. The method of creating magic crystals has been lost, and it is impossible to replenish mental points in magic crystals.
- It is believed that these treasures were used as currency during the Ancient Kingdom period, and they are frequently discovered in the ruins of the Ancient Kingdom. The game master may decide that these are widely traded at the academy of sages. In this case, adventurers can purchase these stones if they have enough money.
- However, in most cases, the crystals being sold have few remaining mental points (meaning they have been partially used by others).

#### Earth Crystal
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Spherical polished amber crystal
- Base Trade Price: 2,600 gamels
- Powers: Makes 10 square meters of ground underfoot (sand or mud) more stable
- An earth crystal will break after one use.

#### Ice Crystal
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Spherical polished white crystal
- Base Trade Price: 5,000 gamels
- Powers: Has the same effect as Blizzard when thrown
- When thrown it deals strike power 20 damage within its area of effect, equivalent to _Blizzard_. Its magic power is 5. Crystal breaks after one use.

#### Water Crystal
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Spherical polished blue crystal
- Base Trade Price: Remaining absorption points x 480 gamels
- Powers: Absorbs 10 points of fire damage
- Absorbs 10 points of fire and heat damage. For attacks like _Fireball_, it absorbs all damage (except what exceeds 10 points). When hit by a weapon enchanted with _Fire Weapon_, it only absorbs 2 points of the damage. After absorbing 10 points total, it breaks.

#### Air Crystal
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Spherical polished purple crystal
- Base Trade Price: Remaining number of uses x 800 gamels
- Powers: Can blow wind up to a speed of 10m in any direction
- By chanting a password, it’ll blow winds up to a speed of 10m within a 100m radius. It’ll break after 10 uses.
- Powers: Casts a Darkness spell
- When you chant the password, a darkness equivalent to _Darkness_ will appear. It’ll break after 10 uses.

#### Fire Crystal
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Spherical polished rose-colored crystal
- Base Trade Price: 3,200 gamels
- Powers: Has the same effect as Fireball when thrown
- When thrown it deals strike power 20 damage within its area of effect, equivalent to _Fireball_. Its _magic power_ is 4 and breaks after one use.

#### Light Crystal
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Spherical polished rainbow-colored crystal
- Base Trade Price: Remaining number of uses x 120 gamels
- Powers: Casts a Light spell
- When you chant the password, a light effect equivalent to _Light_ will appear. It’ll break after 10 uses.

#### Lightning Crystal
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Spherical polished yellow crystal
- Base Trade Price: 1800 gamels
- Powers: Has the same effect as Lightning when thrown
- When thrown, a 20 meter bolt of lightning, equivalent to _Lightning_, runs in that direction, dealing strike power 20 damage to those in its path. Its magic power is 3 and breaks after one use.

### Staves and Wands

#### Ice Blaze Wand
- Rarity: 11
- Enchanter: Ladios, "The Sage of the Seabed"
- Form: Silver wand with a white crystal set in the tip
- Base Trade Price: Number of uses x 2,400 gamels
- Powers: Throws blades of ice to attack the target.
- When you wave this wand while chanting a password, countless small blades of ice will fly in a straight line. These blades of ice deal damage to all targets in their path. _Area of effect_ is 20m, _strike power_ is 20, _magic power_ is 6. This wand can only be used 1D+1 times.

#### Staff of Sorcery
- Rarity: 15
- Enchanter: Wolfen "The Powerless"
- Form: Pitch-black mage’s staff
- Required Strength: 5 (Strike power 10)
- Base Trade Price: 490,000 gamels (not for sale)
- Powers: Can use ancient magic up to 7th level. However, you’ll be controlled and manipulated by the staff
- The wielder of this staff can use ancient magic up to level 7, as if they were a sorcerer.
- However, for each spell cast, they must make a _mental power resistance_ roll with a target score of 16. If they fail, they become controlled by the staff. Even with a successful resist, the _target score increases by +1_ with each spell cast. If the staff is released for more than 1 day, the _target score_ returns to its original value. When the staff controls its user, it will attempt to cunningly and ruthlessly slaughter people, targeting magic users first.
- If consciousness is taken away through magic or if the staff is wrested away, those controlled by the staff will return to normal.

#### Dark Crystal
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Spherical polished black crystal
- Base Trade Price: Remaining number of uses x 120 gamels

#### Staff of Fire
- Rarity: 15
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Mage’s staff
- Required Strength: 5 (Strike power 10)
- Base Trade Price: Remaining mental points x 350 gamels
- Powers: Can use fire-type ancient magic
- This staff stores 2Dx10 mental points. The user does not need to be a sorcerer. By waving the staff while chanting a password, you can use the _magic Tinder, Fire Weapon, Fireball,_ and _Suppress Elemental_ (salamander only). _Magic power_ is uniformly treated as 5 regardless of the user’s own magic power. The base mental power cost of the magic is then directly subtracted from the mental points stored in the staff.

#### Sorcerer Staff
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Mage’s staff
- Required Strength: 5 (Strike power 10)
- Base Trade Price: 60,000 gamels
- Powers: +2 to magic final scores
- If you cast a spell using this staff as a magical catalyst, it’ll add +2 only to the _final score of a success roll_. There are no other bonuses.

#### Delay Staff
- Rarity: 14
- Enchanter: Unknown
- Form: A beautifully carved wooden mage’s staff
- Required Strength: 5 (Strike power 10)
- Base Trade Price: 20,000 gamels
- Powers: Can store one spell
- This staff can be pre-imbued with magic that activates either when a specific password is spoken or after a predetermined time has elapsed. The spells that can be stored in this staff are limited to ancient magic with a consumed mental power (not the base mental power cost) of 3 or less. In other words, as a sorcerer reaches higher levels, they become capable of storing more powerful spells. To store a spell in this staff, you must expend the same amount of mental power as casting the spell normally would require. Furthermore,
- the mental power used to store a spell in this staff cannot be recovered by any means until the stored magic is used. Anyone who knows the password can activate the magic stored in the staff. If set to activate after a certain time instead of with a password, the spell will be centered on the staff. In this case, if the activating magic is an area-effect damage magic like _Fireball_, the staff will be destroyed upon activation. While this staff can be used as a normal magical catalyst, it provides no bonus when used as a weapon.

#### Power Wand
- Rarity: 17
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Wand
- Base Trade Price: 120,000 gamels
- Powers: Fires Energy Bolt
- By chanting a password, you can fire unlimited energy arrows (strike power 10, magic power 6) equivalent to the ancient magic _Energy Bolt_. This magic cannot be expanded.

#### Plant Command Wand
- Rarity: 15
- Enchanter: Unknown
- Form: A wand about 15cm long
- Powers: Can use plant spirit magic
- Base Trade Price: 100,000 gamels
- Those with this wand will be able to cast spirit magic sourced from the power of plant spirits up to 8th level, such as _Binding_ and _Charm_. The wand stores 100 mental points, and each time you use magic, the base mental power cost of the magic is then directly subtracted from the mental points stored in the wand.

#### Memory Ring
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Silver ring in the shape of a bird's wings
- Base Trade Price: 72,000 gamels
- Powers: +6 to agility when worn (agility bonus +1)
- Adds +6 to the wearer’s agility. At the same time, he or she will no longer be affected by magic that belongs to _physical ability_.

#### Linkage Ring
- Rarity: 15
- Enchanter: Unknown
- Form: A pair of (two) silver engagement rings
- Base Trade Price: 64,000 gamels
- Powers: Two people who wear these can freely use each other's mental points between them
- When characters of the same race and opposite gender wear these rings, they can freely use each other’s mental points. However, if one person has a spell, etc., that affects the mind cast on him or her, it’ll affect _both_ people. These rings will not work unless both people are within 1 km.

#### Rune Ring
- Rarity: 14
- Enchanter: Karla "The Powerful"
- Form: Small platinum ring
- Base Trade Price: 60,000 gamels
- Powers: +1 to magic power of ancient magic when you wear it
- Those who wear this will gain a +1 bonus to magic power of ancient magic. This ring also has the ability to act as a normal magical catalyst.

### Other

#### Weightless Bag
- Rarity: 14
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Lightly colored backpack
- Base Trade Price: 40,000 gamels
- Powers: Removes gravity from objects in the bag
- This is a backpack made of a mysterious material that repels water. The weight of objects inside can be ignored. However, you can _only_ put in as many objects as you can outwardly _see_ , so if you put in something of an _abnormal_ weight (the game master should make a suitable decision), the magical power will disintegrate and it’ll tear.
- Combining it with the Bulkless Bag mentioned below, makes it possible to transport extremely large amounts of objects. Put the Bulkless Bag _in_ the Weightless Bag, _then_ put the objects in it.

#### Explosive Bullet
- Rarity: 15
- Enchanter: Many
- Form: Sling Bullet
- Base Trade Price: 2500 gamels
- Powers: Explodes on impact, dealing strike power 30 damage
- This bullet explodes on impact, dealing strike power 30 damage regardless of the sling used. As usual, bonus damage is _fighter/thief/ranger skill level + strength bonus_ , and furthermore, your opponent can _only_ reduce this damage with adventurer level (monster level).

#### Old Man Liar Punisher
- Rarity: 15
- Enchanter: Unknown
- Form: Relief of an old man’s face with its mouth half open
- Base Trade Price: 150,000 gamels
- Powers: Sees through lies
- When someone puts his or her hand in the mouth and says something, and it’s a lie, the mouth will close and won’t let go of the hand until he or she admits _I lied_.

#### Card of Power
- Rarity: 13
- Enchanter: Void, "The Immoral Summoning Sorcerer"
- Form: Palm-sized card of unknown material
- Base Trade Price: Magic’s base mental power cost x 320 gamels
- Powers: User can use various ancient magic
- This item was developed so that even those without the sorcerer skill can use ancient magic. It does _not_ consume mental power when used. To use it, a manual written in low ancient is included in its box, and if you chant a spell name in low ancient while tearing up the card, the magical power previously bestowed on the card will be released. Magic intensity is 15, and the effect _cannot_ be expanded at all.
- This item is consumable.

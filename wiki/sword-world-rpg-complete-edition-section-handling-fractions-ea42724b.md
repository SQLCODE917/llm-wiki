# Handling fractions

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Due to the nature of the game, there are situations in Sword World RPG where math calculations must be made. If division results in a fraction, the fraction is either rounded up or rounded down. Which one is clearly stated for each situation. However, if it's not mentioned, the fraction should always be rounded up. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00089))_

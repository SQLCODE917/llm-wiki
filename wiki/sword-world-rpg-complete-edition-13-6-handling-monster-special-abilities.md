## Source record

This chapter outlines how to handle monster special abilities, including mental power and life force resistance rolls, special attack methods, and defensive immunities.

## Key supported claims

- Monsters that use magic are, without exception, more formidable enemies than their level suggests (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.235-240).
- When attacked by these monsters, you must make a life force resistance roll using the indicated toxicity score as the target score (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.235-240).
- Even in combat between monsters, those that only have attack methods similar to weapons (e.g., fangs or claws) cannot fight each other (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.235-240).
- Monsters with this ability can act underwater without any penalty, while others cannot (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.235-240).
- Depending on the monster, the penalty may be smaller, or it might be possible to still make sound; if this is the case it will be specified (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.235-240).

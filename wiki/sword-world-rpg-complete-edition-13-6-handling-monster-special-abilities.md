## Source record

This section outlines how to handle various monster special abilities in the Sword World RPG, covering attacks requiring mental power or life force resistance rolls, and defense abilities such as resistance to weapons and magic. Some details are not specified.

## Key supported claims

- Magic abilities allow monsters to use magic at indicated levels and intensity (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.235-239).
- Hypnotism requires mental power resistance roll (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.235-239).
- Gaze or ray attacks require mental power resistance roll (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.235-239).

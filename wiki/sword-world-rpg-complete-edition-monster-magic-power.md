Monster magic power is predetermined for each monster. The magic procedure is resolved the same way regardless of whether a _monster_ or an _adventurer_ is casting the spell. In other words, you roll 2D and add it to your _magic power_ . Using that as the target score, everyone on the side on which it was cast must attempt a mental power resistance roll.

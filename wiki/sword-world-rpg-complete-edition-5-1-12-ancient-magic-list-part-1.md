## Source record

Ancient Magic List part 1 lists first‑level spells such as Unlock, Energy Bolt, Enchant Weapon, Counterspell, and Sense Magic, with their costs and effects.

## Key supported claims

- Unlock: Instantly opens non‑magical locks like doors and lids as if the right key were used. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82)
- Energy Bolt: Fires a pure‑energy arrow that never misses, dealing strike power 10. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82)
- Enchant Weapon: Adds +5 to a weapon’s strike power; on a creature, bare‑handed attacks gain +5. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82)
- Counterspell: Boosts mental power resistance by +2 for any resistance roll. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82)
- Sense Magic: Reveals invisible magical auras; cannot discern exact powers (possible). (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82)

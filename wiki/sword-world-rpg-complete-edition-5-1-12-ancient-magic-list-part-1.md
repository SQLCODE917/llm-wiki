## Source record

Summary of the Ancient Magic List (part 1) from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering 1st, 2nd, and 3rd level spells.

## Key supported claims

- Ancient magic list includes 1st, 2nd, and 3rd level spells with specific costs and effects (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82).
- Spells such as Unlock, Energy Bolt, and Enchant Weapon have defined base mental power costs and effects (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82).
- Some spells like Increase Weight and Decrease Weight can be expanded to alter required strength by -1 to -3 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-82).

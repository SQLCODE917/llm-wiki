# Distance=10 meters

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- _Full Potential_ is a spell that enhances the effects of _Physical Enchantment_ and raises potential capabilities of the human body to maximum. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02057))_
- The result is the same effect as casting the _Sharpness_ , _Quickness_ and _Strength_ spells of _Physical Enchantment_ all at the same time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02057))_
- As a result, each ability bonus will be increased by +1, so for the duration, the baseline score of the abilities of each skill related to the increased ability score will also become +1. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02057))_
- If the target is expressed as a monster, its attack points, evasion points, and strike points will increase by 1 point, and at the same time its movement speed and action order will also increase by 6. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02057))_
- You _cannot_ expand this spell's effect (that is, increase ability scores by 12 points, 18 points, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02058))_

Axel Rauschmayer is a software developer, trainer, and author known for his work on ECMAScript standards and JavaScript. He wrote *Exploring ES6*, a comprehensive guide to ECMAScript 2015, and contributed a foreword to *JavaScript Allongé, the "Six" Edition*. His blog (2ality.com) and training materials focus on modern JavaScript practices and language evolution.

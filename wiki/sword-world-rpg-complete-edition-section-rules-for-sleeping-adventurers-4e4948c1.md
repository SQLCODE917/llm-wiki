# Rules for Sleeping Adventurers

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-10-5-9dea10a2]] - broader source section

## Statements

- If a sleeping adventurer is approached by a monster, the baseline score is adventurer level + intelligence bonus , and if you succeed on a resistance roll against target score 9, you can wake up before being attacked. If the monster has the ability to sneak up on you, the target score will be even higher. If they approach using stealthy step , etc. their final score will become the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04017))_
- When a monster, etc. attacks a sleeping adventurer, the attack will automatically hit. If the attack does not reduce life to 0, the adventurer wakes up immediately. Therefore, the quickest way to wake up your friends is to kick them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04020))_
- Therefore, the quickest way to wake up your friends is to kick them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04020))_

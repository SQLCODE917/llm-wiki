## Statements

- Monsters whose mental points are reduced to 0 (or less) by _Shade_ magic, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04652))_
- **Mental point resistance** is a score that's a substitute for a character's _mental power resistance roll_ , so when a character casts magic on a monster, this _mental point resistance_ becomes the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04652))_
- **Mental points** have the same meaning as a character's mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04652))_
- If the final score is equal to or greater than the monster's mental point resistance, the magic will successfully be cast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04653))_
- This is done purely for consistency, so that from the character's perspective, **tied success rolls are always successful** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04653))_
- Conversely, for a monster to resist magic, its mental point resistance must be _greater_ than the magic's final score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04653))_
- A _tie_ means the magic will successfully be cast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04653))_

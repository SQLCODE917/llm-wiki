# 《 **Magic Power Decrease** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Magic power can be optionally reduced. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01409))_
- Also, if the side casting the magic rolls double sixes, the resisting side will _also_ need double sixes, no matter _how much_ magic power was declared to be decreased (if it's a monster, it _cannot_ resist). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01409))_
- This will reduce the final score and, if it's damage magic, _also_ reduce bonus damage (it’s not possible to _only_ reduce the final score or _only_ reduce bonus damage). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01409))_
- If used on multiple targets, you must reduce it by the same amount for _all_ of them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01409))_

### Technical atom 1

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01409))_

> You must declare how many points you’ll reduce _before_ you roll the dice.

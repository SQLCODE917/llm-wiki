### Common Runes

- Common runes were invented by Mana Rai, the great sage of Oran, and it involves putting the power of ancient magic into items (mainly rings) and activating it using a password in common . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03380))_
- Theoretically, any ancient magic can be made into common runes, but the sorcerers' guild (academy of sages) limits what gets put on the market, to prevent the reputation of magic and sorcerers _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03381))_
- Common runes, like other magic, consume mental power when used. The amount of mental power consumed is determined by each common rune. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03382))_
- Common runes can be used by anyone who knows the password. Also, even if you can't use your hands or are wearing metal armor, you can use them as long as you can say the word. However, no matter how high your rune master skill is, you cannot reduce the amount of mental power consumed. When using common runes, if magic power is required for a game procedure, treat it as 0 regardless of the user. Common runes can also be expanded by consuming extra mental power. However, increasing the final score is the one thing you cannot do. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03383))_
- By expanding the effect of this spell, you can increase the weight of the target metal mass. You can increase the weight of metal that can be affected by 20 kilograms by consuming double the mental power, 40 kilograms by consuming quadruple the mental power...... etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03386))_

It only appears on the market when a character discovers it, or has already discovered it, through an adventure (which means it is no longer a lost spell). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03388))_

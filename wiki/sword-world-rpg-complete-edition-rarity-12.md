# Rarity=12

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about rarity=12:

## Statements

- This is an amber stone that comes from the body of the mythical beast lynx. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04513))_
- If you crush, brew, and drink this stone, you will receive a +4 bonus on progression/healing checks for illnesses that affect the mind (such as dance macabre). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04514))_
- Once affected, the target will not change their beliefs no matter how they are persuaded or what evidence they are shown. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06666))_
- The effect cannot manifest on more than one target at a time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06666))_
- The effect continues as long as the item is worn. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06666))_
- If the target fails a _mental power resistance roll_ with a _target score of 16_ , they become affected by the effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06666))_
- However, if the wearer of the necklace themselves stops believing in the truth, the necklace's effect will disappear. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06666))_
- Targets are limited to humans, fae, youma, titans, and animals. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06667))_
- When you wear this, you’ll be able to fly freely in the air. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06989))_
- Movement speed when flying is 50, with a maximum speed of about 50km per hour. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06989))_

## Statements

- This spell allows you to walk on water. Those who've had this spell cast on them can walk on water as if they were walking on solid ground. Of course, it doesn't matter how heavy their equipment is. However, when transporting someone else, you must always carry that person on your back. This spell is also a counter spell to _Sink_ , so if you cast this spell after _Sink_ has been cast, the effects of both will be negated, just as if they had been removed by a _Dispel Magic_ spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02350))_
- If you cast this spell on something underwater, it will immediately rise to the surface. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02351))_

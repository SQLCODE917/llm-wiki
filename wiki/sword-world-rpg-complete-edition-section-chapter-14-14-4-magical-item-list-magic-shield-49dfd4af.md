## Statements

- This is a price list for magical shields with enchantments. For shields with +2 or higher enchantment, small shields tend to be more expensive than large shields due to differences in demand. For the same reason, high-quality large shields +1/+2 are more expensive than large shields +2/+3. Shields requiring strength of 13 cannot be used by most adventurers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10573))_

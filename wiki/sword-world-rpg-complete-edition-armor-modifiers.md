## **Armor Modifiers**

" _Table 16-3 Armor Optional Rules_ " is a table of numerical armor modifications.

### **Evasion Modifier**

**Evasion speed modifier** is the **evasion speed penalty** you suffer while wearing that armor. Heavy metal armor slows down your movement and makes it more difficult to dodge your opponent's attacks.

With this optional rule, there are differences in the way small and large shields are handled ( _Table 16-2: Shield Optional Rules_ ).

### **Defense Modifier**

**Defense power modifier** is the **defense power bonus** you receive while wearing that armor. Metal armor generally has a higher defensive effect and grants a bonus to defense.

### **Critical Target Variation**

**Critical target variation** indicates that the armor _reduces_ the chance of being dealt a strong blow from some weapons while wearing it. If this field says _yes_, the _opponent's_ critical target will be +1 when they attack you with a sword, spear, etc.

### **Anti-Strangling Protection**

The _anti-strangling protection_ indicates whether the armor protects you when being strangled by a garrote or arms around the neck.

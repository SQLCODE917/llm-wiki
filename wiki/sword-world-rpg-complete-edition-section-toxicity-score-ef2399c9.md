# 《 **Toxicity Score** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Each poison has a **toxicity score** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04547))_
- If you fail, the effect will appear, but if you succeed, the effect will only appear incomplete or won't appear at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04547))_
- The toxicity scores listed here are just examples; by diluting the substance, the toxicity score can be made as low as desired, and by concentrating it, it can be made somewhat greater. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04547))_
- When you cast the holy magic _Cure Poison_ on someone who has been poisoned, compare the final score when casting the magic to this toxicity score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04547))_
- If the final score is equal to or greater than the toxicity score, the poison can be removed by the magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04547))_
- If you fail, the effect will appear, but if you succeed, the effect will only appear incomplete or won't appear at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04547))_

## Technical atoms

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04549))_

> This represents how famous the poison is. The higher this score, the rarer the poison. An adventurer must make a success roll using _sage skill level + intelligence bonus_ as the baseline score, and if they succeed, they will know about the poison. You can also _guess_ the name of the poison when you see the symptoms of the poisoned person. If your success roll is 4 or more above the **rarity** , you can also know how to _make_ the poison and its antidote (if it has one).

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04547))_

> When the poison enters the body, make a life force resistance roll (for monsters, a life _point_ resistance roll) using the toxicity score as the target score.

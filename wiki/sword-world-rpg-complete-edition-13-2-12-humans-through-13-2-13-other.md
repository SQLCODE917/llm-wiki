## Source record

Summary of humans, monsters, and other entities in Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, chapters 13.2.12 through 13.2.13.

## Key supported claims

- A normal human has standard stats for villagers and is not typically an enemy of adventurers. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-231)
- A Maligdorai is a daemon with an owl-like upper body and goat-like lower body, and has the ability to cast illusions that affect mental power. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-231)
- A Doppelganger is a greater daemon that can shapeshift, copying the appearance, abilities, and memories of an opponent. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-231)
- A Lycanthrope has the ability to change from human to beast form, with transformation affected by the moon cycle. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.232-232)
- A Dark Priest Warrior serves wicked gods and is often an enemy of player characters, sometimes trained as warriors. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.232-232)

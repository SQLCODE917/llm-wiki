# 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The basic rules for casting magic are the same, but the following conditions are required in order to use the shaman skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03664))_
- It must be in a place where the wind blows in its natural state. The spirit power of wind does not work inside buildings, deep caves, or even underground passages. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03672))_
- This is a power that's at work in places where the ground is exposed or underground. Particularly strong spirit power is at work in the mountains. Since the power of water spirits is strong on the ocean floor and the bottoms of lakes, the spirit power of earth does not work there. Likewise, this spirit power does not work on the floors of buildings (whether they're stone or wood). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03674))_
- The spirit power of water is at work wherever there is water, such as the ocean or a lake. Even if you carry water in a water bottle, etc., if you open the lid, the spirit power will work. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03676))_
- This spirit power is at work wherever there's fire burning. You can also use fire from a torch you're carrying with you. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03678))_
- This spirit power is at work wherever there is snow or ice. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03680))_
- This spirit power is at work wherever there are living things with functioning minds. Of course if the caster him or herself is like that, there's no problem. Some of these types of spirits include those that control the nature of the mind, and those that control fluctuation of emotions. It's said that if one emotion has too much of an outburst, it can cause phenomena such as alienation or complete loss of functions of other spirits, which can lead to mental disorders. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03682))_
- Very strong power is at work in forests, etc. but in places where plants and trees grow, they're only at work in small amounts. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03684))_
- These are the spirits that control light, darkness, and life. Explanations of where these are at work can be found under each individual spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03686))_
- Shamans have the following basic abilities, which they can use without any restrictions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03689))_
- The spirit power of wind does not work inside buildings, deep caves, or even underground passages. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03672))_
- The spirit power of water is at work wherever there is water, such as the ocean or a lake. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03676))_
- It's said that if one emotion has too much of an outburst, it can cause phenomena such as alienation or complete loss of functions of other spirits, which can lead to mental disorders. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03682))_

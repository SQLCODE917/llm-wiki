## Statements

- This is the dragon's special ability, to let out a roar that trembles the souls of those who hear it. It has no effect unless you hear it, but everyone who does hear it must make a mental power resistance roll. If you fail, you will suffer the effects specified on Table 5-5: Fears (see p. 89). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07943))_

# **[ Shape Change ]**

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about **[ shape change ]**:

## Statements

- However, the creatures they can change into are limited to about _10 times_ or _1/10th_ of the caster's weight. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01763))_
- You can never change into something whose origins are not clearly known, such as magical beasts, mythical beasts, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01763))_
- You can only change into humans, fae, youma, titans, and animals, and only those the caster knows in detail (you must succeed on a roll such as the sage skill's _monster check_ ). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01763))_
- This is a spell that allows the caster to change their form into another living creature at will. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01763))_
- Only the caster's body will change shape, the clothes and accessories they're wearing do not change. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01764))_
- Also, after changing form, the caster's physical abilities will become the same as the creature they changed into. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01764))_
- You can think of the maximum as a 6- meter tall titan and the minimum as a medium-sized cat. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01763))_
- The caster will not lose their sense of self, and can cancel the spell at will. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01764))_
- You will also gain special abilities such as flying. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01764))_
- They will fall off or break in the process of changing. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01764))_
- Life force and mental power will be taken from the caster's current scores ( _not_ their maximum scores), but other details such as attack power and defense power will be the same as a lesser dragon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04095))_
- Even if you suffer damage from magic while transformed into a dragon, the damage reduction will be 10 points, which is the lesser dragon's monster level, _not_ the caster's adventurer level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04095))_
- While transformed into a lesser dragon, your intelligence also decreases, making you unable to act rationally or use magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04097))_

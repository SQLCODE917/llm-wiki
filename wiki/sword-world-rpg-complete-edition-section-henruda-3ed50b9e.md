# [ Henruda ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is an herb that grows in the wilderness and is the staple food of the cockatrice, a magical beast. This herb is useful as a preventative against monsters with the ability to petrify and Petrification magic, but it's also a wonder drug for the dreaded stone skin . If you take this herb daily, you'll receive a +4 bonus to progression/healing checks against stone skin . One henruda plant is enough for one adult for one day. This herb cannot be stored and if it's not used within three days of being collected, it will have no effect at all. For this reason, it' s rarely sold in town apothecaries. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06802))_

## Statements

- This doll, which looks like a woman with one arm raised, was made to prevent the loss of important things, so it's endowed with the magical power to remember specific objects or people, and always points in the direction they're located (are). In a state where it does not have anything to remember , or if the thing it remembers has been lost, the doll cannot point anywhere and will just spin around. To make it remember something, you must touch the doll to the object and chant a password. What the doll remembers is displayed on its pedestal, so you can know this if you read low ancient. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08715))_

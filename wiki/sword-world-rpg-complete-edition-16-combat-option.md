# 16.5 Combat Options

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about 16.5 combat options:

## Statements

- These optional rules allow for a variety of attack methods, rather than just simple attacks _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07765))_
- Each throwing or shooting weapon has a **maximum range** , and they will _never_ hit a target farther away than this ( _Table 16-4: Maximum Ranges_ ). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07760))_
- If you use this optional rule, then if successfully hit by a monster whose attack method is _ram_ , a character must determine whether he or she has fallen _prone_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07841))_
- In the basic rules, the range limit is 20 meters for throwing weapons and 100 meters for shooting weapons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07756))_
- Hitting a distant target with a weapon is a nigh-impossible task. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07758))_
- Throwing weapons have a **-1 modifier to attack power for every meter beyond 20 meters** , and shooting weapons have the same for **every 5 meters beyond 100** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07758))_
- In return, you’ll gain a +2 bonus to your _evasion speed_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07767))_
- When focusing on defense, an adventurer _cannot_ attack or use any magic during that round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07767))_
- but to defend, an adventurer can put everything they have into their attack. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07770))_
- When the opponent is unable to evade, or the adventurer is cornered and there is no point in defending, the adventurer can use all his or her attacks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07771))_
- At this time, you can choose one of the following. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07772))_
- This action can be performed in combination with the _mow down_ , _ram_ , and _area aim_ actions (all described below). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07774))_
- This is because it's automatically assumed to be a strong blow. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07777))_
- When an adventurer is armed with a spear, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07779))_

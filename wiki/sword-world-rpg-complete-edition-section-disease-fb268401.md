# 《 **Disease** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The spirit power of a monster is balanced differently than that of a human, and can always cause illness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06813))_
- However, in most cases the infection power of this _information imbalance_ is weak, so it doesn’t pose much of a problem, but if a character is bitten by a bat or rat, it’s highly contagious, so there’s a high chance he or she will become ill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06813))_
- If your roll is less than the progression intensity, you will be infected with the disease. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06814))_
- Illnesses can be treated using the holy magic _Cure Disease_ and _Refresh_ , and the spirit magic _Restore Health_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06814))_
- For detailed rules regarding illnesses, please refer to 12.2.2: Illnesses (see p. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06815))_

- This is a backpack made of a mysterious material that repels water. The weight of objects inside can be ignored. However, you can only put in as many objects as you can outwardly see , so if you put in something of an abnormal weight (the game master should make a suitable decision), the magical power will disintegrate and it'll tear. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10861))_
- Combining it with the Bulkless Bag mentioned below, makes it possible to transport extremely large amounts of objects. Put the Bulkless Bag in the Weightless Bag, then put the objects in it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10862))_

# 【 Sage 】

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Those with the sage skill are commonly referred to as sages. They're knowledgeable and well-versed in all fields of study (academics in Forcelia are not differentiated, and one is rarely versed in only one specific field). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00268))_
- Although they are often thought to be confined to their study rooms, many of them do go out on adventures , because they believe that the best way to learn is to see and hear firsthand . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00269))_

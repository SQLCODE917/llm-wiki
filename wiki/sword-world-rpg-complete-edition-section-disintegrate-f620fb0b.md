## Statements

- Targets who have this spell cast on them and fail to resist will, after a moment of brightness, be broken down into the smallest unit of matter they were composed of, literally disappearing. It's impossible to regenerate a target that has become like this. If it was a living thing, it can never be revived again, and cannot be revived by Resurrection . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02183))_
- Usually it's limited to 1 cubic meter, but by expanding the volume, it's possible to cast it on a larger target. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02184))_

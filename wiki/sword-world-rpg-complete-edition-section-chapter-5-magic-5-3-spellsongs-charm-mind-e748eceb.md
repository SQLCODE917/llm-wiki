## Statements

- This spellsong has the effect of charming those who hear it. Those who are, are not charmed by the bard him or herself, but rather charmed by their singing voice and performance. As long as the spellsong continues, those who are charmed will listen to the song in ecstasy. However, if you suffer physical or mental damage, you can make another resistance roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03092))_
- With this spellsong, a bard can make those they charm take specific actions. What you want them to do must be carefully woven into the lyrics. This weaving of lyrics must be in a language that is understandable to those who hear it (conversely, it does _not_ have to be in high ancient). If forced to perform such an action, the charmed one can attempt _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03093))_

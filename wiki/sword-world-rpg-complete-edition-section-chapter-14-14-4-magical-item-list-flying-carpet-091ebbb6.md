- This is a magic carpet that moves with a password, carrying one person and flying freely in the air for an hour. However, it is best to avoid somersaults and sudden changes in course. Also, if you don't hold your baggage securely, it can easily fall off the carpet. In addition, movement speed during flight is 50, with a maximum speed of about 50km per hour. This carpet can only be used once per day. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11078))_

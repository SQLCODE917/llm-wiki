# Power

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about power:

## Statements

### 【 Mental Power 】

- Mental power represents the adventurer's willpower and perseverance. Using magic is hard work, and one cannot hope to cast much magic without a high mental capacity. Mental power is also related to magic resistance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00150))_

### Loss of Ability Scores and Its Impact

- While adventuring, adventurers often suffer physical and mental injuries and fatigue. In Sword World RPG , this is represented by a loss of life force and mental power (point reduction). If either life force or mental power fall to 0 or less, the adventurer will fall unconscious and be unable to act. The four other ability scores of dexterity, agility, intelligence, and strength are rarely reduced, but if this does happen and these ability scores do fall to 0 or less, the adventurer will still become disabled. At this time, whether they're conscious or not depends on the situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00152))_

- If life force and mental power are reduced by wounds or exhaustion, they will recover once the wounds are treated or the exhaustion is wiped away. There are various conditions for these reductions and recoveries, which you will discover as you read through this book. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00153))_

### 【 Shaman 】

- The powers controlled by spirits are diverse, ranging from the four major powers of earth , water , fi re , and wind to the spirit of humans , and there are many different types of spirit magic used by shamans. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00247))_

### 【 Priest 】

- Those who exercise their power as agents of the gods are priests. In the ancient days of myth, the gods who created the world were later divided into light and darkness and waged a great war. A great war that engulfed all the ancient races- including the legendary dragons of old --ended with all the gods losing their bodies. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00249))_

### 【 Half-Elf 】

- Half-elves are halfway between human and elf in terms of ability. Therefore, their dexterity, agility, intelligence, and mental power are slightly higher, while their strength and life force are slightly lower. However, they do not deviate very far from the average and are fully capable of handling all skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00277))_

### Scores Used in Weapon Combat

- Attack power , strike power , bonus damage , critical target , evasion speed , defense power , and damage reduction . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00770))_

### 【 Strike Power 】

- Strike power is the same number as the required strength of the weapon used. However, if the weapon is a two-handed weapon, it's instead required strength + 5 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00778))_

### 【 Defense Power 】

- Defense power is the same number as the required strength of the armor worn by the character. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00800))_

### 4.5.1 Hit Checks

- character's attack power + 2D < monster's evasion points → attack fails ※ Double sixes will automatically hit, and double ones will automatically fail. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00821))_

### Example:

- Ducard II is fighting a goblin. His attack power is 4 (fighter skill level 2, dexterity bonus 2) and the goblin's evasion points are 10. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00824))_

### Determining Base Damage (Strike Roll)

- On the table, look at the column where the key number equals your character's strike power . For example, if your character's strike power is 14, you only need to look at key number column 14. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00833))_

### 4.6.3 Defense Rolls

- Characters can reduce the damage they receive from monsters by means of armor. You can determine the amount of damage reduction by using Table 4-1: Rating Table. On the Rating Table , look at the column where the key number equals your character's defense power . For example, if your defense power is 7, you would look at key number column 7 with on the Rating Table . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00893))_

### Example:

- Ducard II suddenly found himself in a dispute with another adventurer, Bucky. Ducard II has higher agility, so he attacks first. Ducard II 's attack power is 4 , while Bucky's evasion speed is 5 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00929))_

### Silver Weapons and Armor

- However, silver weapons , due to their magic power, can deal damage to monsters that can only be wounded by magic or magical weapons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01012))_

### Magical Weapons and Armor

- Characters may obtain these magical weapons and armor through their adventures. The power of magical weapons and armor is represented by a +1 or +2 score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01018))_

- In Forcelia, the limit for this power is +3 . There are only a scant few weapons and armor in the world with +3 power, and any with +4 or more have yet to be found. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01019))_

### 【 Magical Armor 】

- Magical weapons and armor are not usually traded, but if you are very lucky you may be able to buy them if they're +1. A guide to prices can be found in Chapter 14: Treasure (see p. 240). Also, they offer basically no bonus to strike power or defense power, same as required strength. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01028))_

### Mithril Silver Weapons and Armor

- Mithril silver weapons and armor are always magical items and, like magical weapons and armor, have a power of +1 or +2. This power affects attack power, evasion speed, bonus damage, and damage reduction. The maximum power is +3. No one has yet discovered anything with a power of +4 or higher. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01034))_

- Mithril silver is also a very strong metal. For this reason, strike power and defense power are calculated by adding +5 to required strength. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01035))_

### Procedure When an Adventurer Casts Resistible Magic on an Adventurer

- In other words, you compare the magic power + 2D score of the one casting the magic with the mental power resistance + 2D score of the one on whom the magic is being cast. If the score of the one casting the magic is greater, the magic will take full effect, but if there is a tie or if the score of the one on whom the magic is being cast is greater, the magic is resisted and will only take an incomplete effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01158))_

### When an Adventurer Casts Magic on Adventurers

- First, the side casting the magic rolls 2D. The final score (magic power + 2D) must then be determined this one time only. Each resisting side must then make a mental power resistance roll, using that determined final score as the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01185))_

### Example:

- The evil sorcerer Gorman (magic power 5) unleashes Lightning on the three adventurers. First, Gorman rolls 2D and adds his own magic power. If we assume his 2D roll is 8 , each of the three adventurers must make a mental power resistance roll, with a target score of 5 (magic power) + 8 (roll) = 13 . Suppose that the baseline score of all three of their mental power resistance rolls is 4 . If their rolls are each 2 (double ones!), 6 , and 9 , then only the last one is able to resist the Lightning . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01187))_

### If the Target Does Not Resist

- Some magic can also have secondary effects. For example, the spirit magic Fanaticism increases attack power, but conversely decreases evasion speed. With such magic, the one on whom it is cast can choose whether or not to resist. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01193))_

### Consuming Mental Power

- Each magic has a score called base mental power cost . This base mental power cost, divided by the corresponding rune master skill level _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01199))_

### Example:

- A character with shaman skill level 2 uses the Fire Bolt spell. The base mental power cost for Fire Bolt is 5 . The character must consume 5 ÷ 2 = 2.5, rounding up to 3 points of mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01202))_

- A character can reduce their mental power to 0, but they cannot make it negative. For example, a character with 5 points of mental power remaining can use magic that costs 5 points of mental power, but cannot use magic that costs 6 or more points of mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01203))_

### Power

### 《 Recovery via Sleep 》

- If a character sleeps for at least 6 hours in a row, they will fully recover their mental power upon awakening. However, this method of recovery can only be done once a day. If you awaken within 24 hours of your previous awakening (when your mental power was last recovered), your mental power will not be recovered. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01213))_

### 5.1.7 Magic Expansion

- Magic expansion is done by doubling or tripling...... the amount of mental power consumed. At this time, you do not double or triple the base mental power cost....... Instead, you divide the mental power by your skill level, rounded up , or in other words, the mental power you would consume when not expanding is doubled, tripled...... etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01217))_

### 《 Targets Expansion 》

- If it's two or three targets...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01225))_

### 《 Area Expansion 》

- If the area is doubled or tripled ...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... You can also expand an area by a fraction (x1.5, x8/3, etc.), in which case the fraction is rounded up when determining the multiplier for mental power consumed. So for expansions such as x 1.5 and x 8/3 , the cost would be double and triple , respectively. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01230))_

### 《 Volume Expansion 》

- Some spells that affect a space or object can have their volume expanded. If the volume is expanded by two or three times...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... Rounding should be handled the same way you would for area expansion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01234))_

### 《 Duration Expansion 》

- For spells whose effects last a certain amount of time, you can expand that duration. If the duration is doubled or tripled ...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... Y ou can also increase it by a fraction, such as x1.5, in which case the fraction is rounded up when determining the multiplier for mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01241))_

### 《 Distance Expansion 》

- Magic basically only works up to a certain distance. However, there are spells that can extend their reach. If reach distance is doubled or tripled ...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... You can also increase it by a fraction, such as x1.5, in which case the fraction is rounded up when determining the multiplier for mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01244))_

### 5.1.7.2 Combined Expansion

- Some magic allows for multiple expansion types. In these cases, you can perform multiple expansions at the same time. However, at this time, the amount of mental power consumed will become extremely large. By multiplying the rates determined for each expansion, you can determine the total multiplied rate of mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01257))_

### 《 Magic Power Decrease 》

- Magic power can be optionally reduced. This will reduce the final score and, if it's damage magic, also reduce bonus damage (it's not possible to only reduce the final score or only reduce bonus damage). You must declare how many points you'll reduce before you roll the dice. If used on multiple targets, you must reduce it by the same amount for all of them. Also, if the side casting the magic rolls double sixes, the resisting side will also need double sixes, no matter how much magic power was declared to be decreased (if it's a monster, it cannot resist). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01274))_

### 《 Damage 》

- In either case, the caster's magic power can be added as bonus damage to the result of the strike roll. Also, the target's adventurer level (or the monster's monster level) is subtracted from the damage. However, there's an exception if you roll double ones during your strike roll. In this case, no matter how much magic power your character (monster) has, they cannot deal damage to the target at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01286))_

### 《 Weapon Enhancement 》

- Weapons that have been given power by ancient magic, silver weapons, or mithril silver weapons are assumed to already have non-removable (see below) weapon enhancement magic cast on them. Therefore, that power cannot be nullified, so even if you cast new weapon enhancement magic, it'll be useless . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01325))_

### 《 Non-removable 》

- Although, the ancient magic Perfect Cancellation is able to remove nonremovable magic. Also, sometimes there is counter magic that counters certain magic, which if specified, will be removed by that counter magic. It's also possible to remove non-removable magic by directly using the power of a god (wicked god) with the holy/dark magic Call God . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01328))_

### 《 Focus 》

- If the caster suffers damage to their life force or mental power, their focus will be interrupted and the magic's effect will be lost . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01342))_

### 《 Base Mental Power Cost 》

- The base mental power cost represents how much mental power is consumed when using that magic. As previously explained, this base mental power cost divided by the corresponding rune master skill level equals the actual mental power consumed by the caster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01374))_

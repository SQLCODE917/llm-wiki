Summary of undead types and rules in chapter 13.2.9 part 1 of the Sword World RPG Complete Edition.

- An ash is an undead created from the ashes of a burned corpse using ancient magic and dark magic. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221)
- Zombies, along with skeletons, are representative undead, created from corpses by ancient magic and dark magic. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221)
- A soul of someone who was greatly possessive in life will become undead as a sin. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221)
- Even _Turn Undead_ may not affect Phantoms. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221)

## Source record

This section covers various undead monsters and creatures found in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, including alraune, flowers, fungus, buau zombies, skeletons, zombies, ashes, ghouls, sins, wights, haunts (including ghosts, specters, and phantoms), jack-o-lanterns, and dullahans.

## Key supported claims

- Alraune are artificial plants created from human blood, with no fertility and no weakness to fire, unknown if they can be created from other sources (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Bloody petals are large red flowers resembling human blood, known as blood flowers or man-eating flowers, which can grow up to 10 meters in height and strangle prey (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Buau zombies are undead created by the lost spell _Create Buau Zombie_, possessing intellect and the ability to follow commands, unlike normal zombies (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).

# 5.1.12 Ancient Magic List / [ Magic Protection ]

## Statements

- This spell has the effect of giving magic resistance to objects that do not have it. If the targeted object is exposed to another spell that's not detection type, it will resist that spell unless the opponent succeeds on a success roll against a target score equal to the final score from when Magic Protection was cast. If a successful resist, the opponent's spell will either completely lose its effect, or will take only an incomplete effect. If the resist fails, the spell will take its full effect, and furthermore the Magic Protection spell itself will be removed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02915))_
- Even spells with a resist of none can be resisted by Magic Protection . In this case, the spell will take no effect at all. For example, if you cast Kindle on a bundle of straw that has Magic Protection cast on it, if Kindle cannot break through the protection of Magic Protection (the final score is insufficient), it won't be able to ignite. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02916))_
- This spell is powerless against spells that belong to detection . Therefore, objects with this spell cast on them will respond to Sense Magic . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02917))_
- Also, if you cast it on something that naturally has resistance (humans, fae, youma, etc.), it will have no effect at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02918))_
- If the targeted object is exposed to another spell that's not detection type, it will resist that spell unless the opponent succeeds on a success roll against a target score equal to the final score from when Magic Protection was cast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02915))_

- Therefore, objects with this spell cast on them will respond to Sense Magic . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02917))_

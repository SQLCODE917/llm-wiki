## Statements

- The scope of this ability is very wide. It's for checks regarding whether you know or don't know about things in all fields, from trivia to politics to science. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03626))_
- There are two methods of making checks. One is one where the game master judges whether or not the player knows based on their sage skill level and tells them, without providing a rarity . The other is to provide a rarity for each piece of knowledge, and ask for a check via success roll, similar to monster check . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03627))_
- Please use whichever method the game master deems appropriate for each situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03628))_

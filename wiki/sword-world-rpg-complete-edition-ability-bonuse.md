# 【 **Ability Bonuses** 】

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about 【 **ability bonuses** 】:

## Statements

- An ability score value is related to the success or failure of an action that an adventurer tries to perform, but in _Sword World RPG_ , success or failure is determined by a **bonus** derived from the ability score, rather than the ability score itself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00177))_
- The result of each ability score divided by 6 (rounded down) will be the bonus for that ability score (Table 1-1: Ability Bonuses). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00177))_
- Once ability scores have been calculated, the next step is the ability bonuses. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00501))_
- Each of these numbers is referred to as a _dexterity bonus_ , _intelligence bonus_ , etc., using the name of the ability score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00177))_
- A bonus is an ability score divided by 6 (rounded down). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00501))_
- Strength represents an adventurer's muscular strength. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00176))_
- However, temporary reductions do _not_ affect modifiers for only two scores: life force and mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00177))_

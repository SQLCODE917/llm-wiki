# **[ Dryad ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- 5th level spirit magic (Magic Intensity/Magic Power=14/7, dryad only) _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05204))_
- Normal weapon immunity Mental attack immunity Not affected by poison and illness Habitat=Woods _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05205))_
- Their figure is that of a completely naked elf woman, with long green hair wrapped around her entire body. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05207))_
- Dryads are plant spirits. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05207))_
- They usually reside in trees, but a shaman can summon them anywhere there are plants. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05207))_
- A mad dryad uses the spirit magic " _Charm_ " to cause division among allies, and tries to block their opponent's movements with " _Binding_ ". _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05208))_
- Using these spells does not consume any of the dryad’s mental points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05208))_
- To harm a dryad, it must be a magical or silver weapon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05210))_

## Statements

- This was created to allow working for long periods without sleep. Those who wear this pendant will never fall asleep. Neither the Sleep Cloud of ancient magic nor the Sleep of spirit magic will have any effect. However, it cannot reduce the fatigue and other effects that come with staying awake for long periods. If worn for too long, one might die from sleep deprivation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10659))_

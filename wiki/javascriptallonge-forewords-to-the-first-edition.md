## Key supported claims

- Fogus is a lifelong bibliophile excited by Reg’s writing, yet he remains conservative about books, preferring a scent like a "Used Book Store" before buying (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.14-29).
- Reg sent Fogus a copy of the book, which humbled him, contrasting Fogus’s skepticism about the ebook model (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.14-29).
- Reg’s Leanpub model facilitates rapid feedback loops, making the book improve continuously; Fogus describes this as efficient and transformative, noting it may change future publishing (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.14-29).

## Source record

This section details the list of spirit magic spells available in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, specifically covering 4th, 5th, and 6th level spirit magic spells, with some unknown aspects of their effects.

## Key supported claims

- Healing spell completely heals wounds by borrowing spirit power, but cannot regenerate severed limbs (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.88-91).
- Mute spell silences target's sounds but does not prevent spell use (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.88-91).
- Water Walking allows walking on water, negating Sink spell effects (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.88-91).

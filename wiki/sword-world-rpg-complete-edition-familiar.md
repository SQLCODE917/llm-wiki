The *Familiar* spell is a 15th level ancient magic spell detailed in the *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition* (see [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-chapter-5-1-12-ancient-magic-list]]). This spell allows a sorcerer to summon and control an animal to become their familiar, with specific abilities and limitations.

## **Effects**

- **Summoning**: The spell summons a specific animal to become the sorcerer's familiar. The animals that can become familiars are limited, such as frogs, crows, owls, cats, and doves.
- **Mental Contact**: The caster and familiar are always in mental contact, allowing communication similar to telepathy and sharing sensory organs.
- **Command**: The familiar obeys the commands of its maker, the sorcerer, and can be used for tasks or combat.
- **Mental Points Borrowing**: The caster can borrow mental points from their familiar to cast spells, reducing the mental power consumed by the caster.
- **Damage Sharing**: If the familiar loses life points or mental points due to an attack, the caster will suffer the same pain.

## **Limitations**

- **One Familiar**: A sorcerer can have only one familiar. Attempting to summon a second one will have no effect.
- **Ritual Requirement**: The spell must be performed as a ritual, requiring 3 full days of preparation followed by 12 hours of spell chanting.
- **Distance Limit**: The caster and familiar can only maintain contact if they are within 1 kilometer of each other.
- **Abilities**: Each familiar has specific abilities based on its animal type, such as seeing well in the dark or flying.

## Statements

### Chapter 7: General Skills / The Difference Between General Skills and Adventurer Skills

- Many of you may be thinking, _Then there's no need to separate the two, right?_ However, you can't do that. This is because the hunter skill and the ranger skill are completely different things. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03420))_

- The reason for this is that the hunter skill is a general skill, while the ranger skill is, as precisely called, an adventurer skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03422))_

### Chapter 7: General Skills

- The **hunter skill** is the skill needed to hunt. People who own this skill are called hunters, and people called hunters own this skill. Additionally, even those who do not specialize in hunting but do it as a hobby or side job still possess this skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03477))_

### Chapter 7: General Skills / [ ⑨ Pre-processing [Dexterity]]

- This is the ability to pre-process the prey obtained from hunting so that it can be sold as a product. It’s needed to separate the antlers from the carcasses of animals such as deer, etc. and to strip and tan the hides from prey. You can also use it to take apart and smoke meat. It concerns all other similar acts. If this pre-processing fails, the value of the product will _decrease_ to half of its normal value. For each pre-processing attempt, the game master should set a target score and have the player make a success roll.

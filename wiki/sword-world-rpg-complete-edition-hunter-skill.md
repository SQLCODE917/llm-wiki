The hunter skill is a general skill in _Sword World RPG_ needed to hunt. People who own this skill are called hunters, and people called hunters own this skill. Additionally, even those who do not specialize in hunting but do it as a hobby or side job still possess this skill. 

The hunter skill is similar to the ranger skill. The details will be explained later, but the abilities of the two actually overlap quite a bit, and a good ranger can also become a good hunter. Of course, the opposite is also true. Therefore, a good ranger has the potential to be called a hero by the people, but a hunter, no matter how good he is, has a hard time getting people to recognize him as a hero. Of course, being a hunter is also a respectable profession, so you should never look down on it. People with higher levels in general skills should also be given due respect. However, they are not called heroes. They don't even become the protagonist of the story. They are unrelated to these _adventures_.

## Abilities of the Hunter Skill

- ① Footprint Tracking 
- ② Find/Sense Trap 
- ③ Remove/Set Trap 
- ④ Stealthy Step 
- ⑤ Camouflage 
- ⑥ Listen 
- ⑦ Plant and Animal Check 
- ⑧ Projectile Use 
- ⑨ Pre-processing [Dexterity]
- ⑩ Value Check [Intelligence]

## **⑨ Pre-processing [Dexterity]**
This is the ability to pre-process the prey obtained from hunting so that it can be sold as a product. It’s needed to separate the antlers from the carcasses of animals such as deer, etc. and to strip and tan the hides from prey. You can also use it to take apart and smoke meat. It concerns all other similar acts. If this pre-processing fails, the value of the product will _decrease_ to half of its normal value. For each pre-processing attempt, the game master should set a target score and have the player make a success roll. It may not be difficult to cut off the horns, but tasks like skinning small animals and skillfully smoking meat can be quite difficult.

## **⑩ Value Check [Intelligence]**
Unlike rangers, hunters hunt and convert their prey into gold to earn their own income. Therefore, it's no good if you cannot determine the price of the game you've hunted. Hunters can see how much their prey and pre-processed products are worth. For prey and pre-processed items, set a difficulty and have them make a success roll. Game masters should not set a very high difficulty level for using this _ability_ unless you're trying to catch an extremely difficult animal.

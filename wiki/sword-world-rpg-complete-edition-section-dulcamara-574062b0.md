# [ Dulcamara ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a vine-like shrub that can be found in highlands. Its seeds are ground and mixed with oil to make an ointment that's used to treat skin diseases. When used on illnesses that cause severe inflammation of the skin, such as green slime rot, it provides a +2 bonus on progression/healing checks. Dozens of seeds can be harvested from one plant, and 2 to 3 seeds are enough for one adult per day. Seeds can be stored for long periods of time as long as they're in good condition. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06798))_

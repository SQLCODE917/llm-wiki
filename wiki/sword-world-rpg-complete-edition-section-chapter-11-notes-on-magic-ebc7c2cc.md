## Statements

- In Alecrast, there aren't that many beings called rune masters. Rune masters are respected in a sense by the general public because they have special powers called magic. However, public sentiment towards magic is far from good. This is because, in the days of the ancient kingdom, citizenship was not recognized for anyone _except_ rune masters, and all other people were used as slaves. Therefore, there is a great sense of crisis regarding the power of rune masters (especially sorcerers). Some believe that once their numbers become sufficient and they regain the power they had during the ancient kingdom, the age of darkness may come again. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03710))_

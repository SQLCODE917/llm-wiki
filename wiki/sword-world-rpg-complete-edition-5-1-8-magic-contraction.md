## Source record

This section details the mechanics of magic contraction in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, including area, volume, duration, magic power, and effect contractions.

## Key supported claims

- Area contraction narrows the area of effect, keeping the shape unchanged, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.62-62).
- Duration contraction shortens the duration of magic, only ending at the end of a round, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.62-62).
- Magic power decrease reduces final score and bonus damage, with declaration before dice roll, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.62-62).
- Effect contraction allows all magic that can expand effects to also contract them, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.62-62).
- Combined expansion and contraction is possible, but mental power is decided by expansion, not contraction, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.62-62).

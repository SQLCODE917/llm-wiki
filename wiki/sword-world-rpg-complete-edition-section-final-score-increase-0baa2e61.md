## Statements

- There's some magic whose effects are negated or reduced if they're resisted by the target, but will also let you increase the magic's final score (resist target score) in order to prevent this. This increase in final score may also take effect if a nullifying spell is cast later. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01250))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, fi nal score is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01252))_
- This expansion requires a deep proficiency in magic. Therefore, it cannot be done infinitely. Final score can only be increased up to a maximum of caster's rune master skill level ÷ 2 (rounded up) . That's up to +1 (double) for 1st~2nd level, +2 (triple) for 3rd~4th level, and +5 (sextuple) for 9th~10th level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01253))_

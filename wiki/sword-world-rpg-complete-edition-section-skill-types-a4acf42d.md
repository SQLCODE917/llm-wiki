# **Skill Types**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Skills are grouped into several types. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00237))_
- There are also two adventurer skills that _cannot_ be acquired by a player character: **dark priest** and **dragon priest** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00238))_
- First, skills are divided into two types: **adventurer skills** and **general skills** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00238))_
- There are eight of these: **fighter** , **thief** , **priest** , **sorcerer** , **shaman** , **sage** , **ranger** , and **bard** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00238))_
- Adventurer skills, as the name implies, are acquired by player characters, the adventurers, or by equivalent characters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00238))_
- Some of these are useful during an adventure, and it is well worth it for an adventurer to acquire general skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00239))_
- General skills are skills of an ordinary person as opposed to an adventurer, such as **merchant** , **farmer** , etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00239))_

## Statements

- As the name suggests, this is a spell that creates an illusionary beast to attack the target. In the round in which the spell is cast, an illusionary beast will appear in front of the target, and begin attacking on the next round. This attack can only target the person targeted by the spell. The attack will continue until the illusionary beast itself is defeated, this spell is removed, the duration expires, or the target dies. This illusionary beast can only be seen by the target. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03504))_
- In order for anyone other than the target to discover the illusion, they must have knowledge of this spell. Sorcerers can always discover it, but those who do not must attempt and succeed on a success roll with the sage skill's knowledge . Its target score is 16. However, even if you cannot discover it, it's possible to determine that the target is being attacked by an invisible monster, so although you cannot attack this monster directly , you can still support the target indirectly (casting an Enchant Weapon spell on the target's weapon, healing their wounds with a Healing spell, etc.). Damage dealt by an illusionary beast is not an illusion, but is actually treated as a reduction in life force. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03505))_
- This illusionary beast can only be seen by the target. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03504))_
- This attack can only target the person targeted by the spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03504))_

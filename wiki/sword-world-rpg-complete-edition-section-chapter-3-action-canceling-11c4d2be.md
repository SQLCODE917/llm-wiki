# Chapter 3: / Action Canceling

## Statements

- A character may cancel and not perform their announced action. For example, if an enemy that you intended to attack falls down due to the attack of a fellow player, no further attack is necessary. If you fall down yourself early in the order, you will not be able to act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01323))_
- Or, in the middle of a round, your companion may ask you to help out by giving money or goods. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01324))_
- In such a case, a character may cancel their intended action. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01325))_
- However, characters cannot switch their actions. Just because a companion has knocked down an enemy in front of you does not mean that you can attack a different enemy. You also cannot switch from magic to a weapon attack because you intended to cast a spell but were blocked from chanting halfway through. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01326))_
- The choice is basically this: either continue with the announced action or cancel it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01327))_

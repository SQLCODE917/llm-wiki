# 《 **Steal Mental Power** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is an ability that undead normally have, and a character whose mental power is reduced to 0 by this attack will die instantly and will be resurrected as an undead (wight, vampire, etc.) 24 hours later. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06839))_
- At the same time a normal attack hits, mental power will also be damaged. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06839))_
- This damage cannot be reduced by armor, only adventurer level and magical defense can help. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06839))_

## Statements

- This spell has the effect of accelerating the rate of decay and causing the target to rot faster. Objects that had this spell cast on them will decay very quickly. Decay, which normally takes two to three days, will be reduced to half an hour. As long as an object is perishable, it will decay, even if under conditions where decay would normally not progress, such as the environment being below freezing, or being soaked in a saturated brine. However, this has no effect on metals or rocks, and it has no effect on living creatures at all. Targets are limited to non-living meat, plants, fibers, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04221))_
- This spell can reduce the time it takes for decay to complete by consuming extra mental power. If double the normal amount of mental power is consumed, the target will decay in 15 minutes, and if triple , the target will decay in 10 minutes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04222))_
- Even if the spell is removed with Perfect Cancellation , etc. the target will not return to the state it was in before it decayed (it will return with the counter magic Preservation . See p. 102). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04223))_

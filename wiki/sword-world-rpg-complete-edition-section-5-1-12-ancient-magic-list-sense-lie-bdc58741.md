## Statements

- By casting this spell, the caster can discover parts that are untrue, exaggerated, and dramatized in conversations they overhear. During the duration, all words that the caster hears are subject to a true-or-false check. However, while you can discover lies, you cannot know truths. You can only know that if it's a lie, it's a lie, and if it's an exaggeration, it's an exaggeration. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03221))_

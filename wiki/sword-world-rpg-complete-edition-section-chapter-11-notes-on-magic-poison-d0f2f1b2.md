- This is a spell that creates a poison within the target's body, and its effect depends on the type of poison produced. There are two types of poison that can be created by this spell. The target can also attempt a resistance roll, and targets that succeed will not suffer any effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03760))_

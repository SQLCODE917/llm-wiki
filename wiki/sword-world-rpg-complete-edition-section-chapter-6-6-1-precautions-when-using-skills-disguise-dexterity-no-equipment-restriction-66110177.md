## Statements

- This is the ability to change one's face and appearance using special makeup. It's easy to just act like a completely different person, but it's difficult to disguise yourself as someone specific. Things like height and physique can be changed to a certain extent, but there are still limits. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04974))_
- If the thief is disguised as a specific person and they encounter an acquaintance of that person, they must make a success roll to determine success or failure. The game master should add modifiers to success rolls to account for the unnaturalness of the disguise (such as disguising as the opposite sex, etc.). Especially when disguised as someone whose characteristics the thief doesn't know in detail, it's best to add a difficult modifier. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04975))_

## Statements

- This is magic that affects the target's physical abilities (strength, etc.). Magic of this type can only take effect on humans , fae , and youma . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02675))_
- Also, with magic of the physical ability type, two or more cannot affect the same target at the same time. Please resolve them as counters (overpowers) magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02676))_

### 《 Physical Ability 》 / 《 Weapon Enhancement 》

- Magic that targets a weapon and enhances it. Two or more of this kind of magic cannot affect the same weapon at the same time. Please resolve them as counters (overpowers) magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02679))_
- Weapons that have been given power by ancient magic, silver weapons, or mithril silver weapons are assumed to already have non-removable (see below) weapon enhancement magic cast on them. Therefore, that power cannot be nullified, so even if you cast new weapon enhancement magic, it'll be useless . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02680))_

# **2. Strangle/Continue to Strangle/Escape**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- **Strangle** your opponent barehanded or with a garrote. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07815))_
- Once strangling, the strangler can automatically deal damage every round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07815))_
- If you’re barehanded, strike power is 0 (critical target 12), and if you’re using a garrote, strike power is 10 (critical target 10). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07815))_
- Opponents have a -2 penalty to evasion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07815))_
- While being strangled, you cannot make any sound at all, so you _cannot_ use magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07816))_
- If the final score of the one trying to break free is greater, or if there’s a tie, he or she will escape. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07816))_
- To resolve breaking free, the one being strangled must make a success roll using _adventurer level + strength bonus_ as the baseline score, while the strangler must make one using _fighter/thief skill level + strength bonus_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07816))_
- Also, even if your character doesn’t suffer damage, if or she remains strangled for a _number of rounds equal to his or her life force_ , life force will immediately fall to 0, he or she will fall _unconscious_ , and you’ll need to make a _death check_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07817))_
- You _must_ make a death check _every_ round so long as the strangling continues, even if you do _not_ suffer any damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07818))_
- However, you _cannot_ attack monsters that _don’t breathe_ (undead, etc.), monsters that are _much larger than your character_ (ogres, etc.), or those that wear _metal armor_ , by strangling. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07819))_

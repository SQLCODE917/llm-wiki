## Source record

Spirits are generally unstable and have little intellect, with most being neutral or violent in reaction. This section covers various spirits including Will-O-Wisp, Shade, Brownie, Undine, Salamander, Sandman, Sylph, Sprite, Gnome, Frau, Leprechaun, Dryad, and Mad Spirit of Life.

## Key supported claims

- Will-O-Wisp is a spirit of light, appearing as a blue-white ball of light about 50 cm in diameter, and its light illuminates a space with a 5-meter radius (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184).
- Will-O-Wisp is among the most unstable of spirits and has little to no semblance of intellect (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184).
- Will-O-Wisp attacks are always successful, dealing damage through a shockwave (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184).

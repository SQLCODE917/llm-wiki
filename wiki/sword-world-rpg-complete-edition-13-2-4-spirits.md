## Source record

Spirits in Sword World RPG - Complete Edition include will-o-wisp, shade, brownie, undine, salamander, sandman, sylph, sprite, gnome, frau, leprechaun, dryad, and mad spirit of life.

## Key supported claims

- Will-O-Wisp is a spirit of light, appearing as a blue-white ball of light about 50 cm in diameter, and is among the most unstable spirits with little intellect. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184)
- Shade is a spirit of darkness, controlling fear, and cancels out natural light within 5 meters, leaving the area in total darkness. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184)
- Brownie appears in old mansions, has illusions and telekinesis, and cannot leave the mansion. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184)

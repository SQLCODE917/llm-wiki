## Source record

This chapter outlines various spirit creatures, their traits, behaviors, and special abilities, with a focus on Will‑O‑Wisp, Shade, Brownie, Undine, Salamander, and other spirits.

## Key supported claims

- When they appear as mad spirits, there are two actions they can take (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184).
- If the humans living in the mansion disappear or die, the brownie may turn into a mad spirit (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184).
- Special Abilities are 4th‑level spirit magic (Magic Intensity/Magic Power=13/6, undine only) (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184).
- If anyone bathes or swims in a spring or river where this spirit resides, the mad undine may do everything it can to drown the opponent (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184).
- Salamanders shower you in a breath of fire, which has the same effect as the 1st‑level spirit magic spell Fire Bolt (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.181-184).

# Phantom

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- A phantom is a being who by now is merely a grudge bound to a specific place, object, etc. They have almost no power to interfere with others, but it's impossible to counter them through normal means. Even Turn Undead has no effect on Phantoms. Only Exorcism and Save Soul can be expected to be effective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06961))_
- A phantom's appearance is the same as a specter. It can also communicate its intentions while disappearing. At this time, it may cause rapping sounds or poltergeist phenomena. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06962))_

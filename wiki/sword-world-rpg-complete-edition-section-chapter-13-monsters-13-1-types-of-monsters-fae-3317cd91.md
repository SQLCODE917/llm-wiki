## Statements

- Fae are a demi-human race that originally lived in the fairy world, but for various reasons they came to live in this world and were unable to return to their original world. However, some races, such as high elves and fairies, still have the ability to travel between the two worlds. They are relatively friendly towards humans, but do not actively try to connect with them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04535))_
- Spirits can use an unlimited amount of spirit magic depending on their power. No matter how much they use it, it will not consume their mental points. However, they cannot expand the power of their spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04536))_
- Elves, dwarves, and grassrunners are also fae, and these three races can also be player characters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04537))_
- Regardless of a spirit's rarity (a score related to how well-known the monster is, which will be explained later), a shaman knows all spirits. When encountering a spirit, a shaman can recognize a spirit without the need for a success roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04538))_
- However, some races, such as high elves and fairies, still have the ability to travel between the two worlds. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04535))_

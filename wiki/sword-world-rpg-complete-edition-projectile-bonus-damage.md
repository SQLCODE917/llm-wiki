## **Projectile Bonus Damage**

In the basic rules, any weapon's bonus damage equals _fighter (or thief, ranger) skill level + strength bonus_, but for _projectiles_, an adventurer's strength is not fully utilized as-is.

Bonus damage added to a bow or crossbow equals **skill level + {bow's required strength ÷ 6 (rounded down)}**. It's wasteful to pull a _light_ bow with _so_ much strength that it breaks.

*(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.263)*

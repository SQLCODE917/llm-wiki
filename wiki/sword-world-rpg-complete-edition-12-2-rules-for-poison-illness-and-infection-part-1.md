## Source record

In _Sword World RPG_, illness is defined as a state in which the balance of spirit power within the body is disrupted. The concept of bacterial or viral contagion does not exist. There are two types of symptoms that can appear. One is due to poison and the other is due to illness. In addition, a wound may become infected, which is treated as an illness. Unknown spirit of life heals illness by showing other spirits in the body how to achieve balance.

## Key supported claims

- Illness disrupts spirit power balance in the body, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.159-167).
- Poisons disrupt spirit power and require detoxification, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.159-167).
- Infection occurs when the body's container is broken, causing imbalance, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.159-167).
- Poisons can be cleared using antidotes, holy magic, or spirit magic, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.159-167).
- Illness is healed by the unknown spirit of life showing how to achieve balance, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.159-167).

## Source record

Overview of the world of Forcelia and the Alecrast continent, including its history, geography, and social structure in the Sword World RPG. Some aspects remain unknown.

## Key supported claims

- Forcelia is a flat, square world with four gates sealing it, and the concept of a world outside Forcelia barely exists (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.280-290)
- The age of mythology began with the death of a Titan, creating gods and the world (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.280-290)
- The Age of Magic saw the rise of the Kingdom of Kastuul ruled by wizard-kings, which was destroyed by its own magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.280-290)

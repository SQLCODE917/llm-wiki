# Chapter 2: Success Rolls / Resistance Rolls

## Statements

- A success roll made using _adventurer level + life force bonus_ as the baseline score is specifically referred to as a **life force resistance roll** . Similarly, a success roll made using _adventurer level + mental power bonus_ as the baseline score is specifically called a **mental power resistance roll** , and the two are collectively called simply **resistance rolls** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00645))_
- The reason for the name change and the distinction between the two in particular, is that they are frequently performed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00646))_
- Therefore, these baseline scores are also defined by the terms **life force resistance** and **mental power resistance** , among others. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00647))_
- Life force resistance rolls are made to avoid or reduce the effects of physical dangers such as poison, while mental power resistance rolls are made to avoid or reduce the effects of magic or curses. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00648))_
- In particular, all checks against magic, even if it causes physical damage, are made with mental power resistance rolls. Checks against special attacks by monsters that are magical in nature are also made with mental power resistance rolls. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00649))_
- master asks us to make a life force resistance roll to see if Ducard II is poisoned by the ghoul. The _toxicity score_ of ghoul poison is 10, which is the target score. Our 2D roll is 5, so with 5+5= _10_ , he escapes paralysis, if barely. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00650))_

In functional programming, **functional iterators** are used to process collections (like arrays) in a declarative manner, often using higher-order functions such as `map`, `filter`, and `reduce`. These iterators avoid side effects and are discussed in *JavaScript Allongé* (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.273-288).

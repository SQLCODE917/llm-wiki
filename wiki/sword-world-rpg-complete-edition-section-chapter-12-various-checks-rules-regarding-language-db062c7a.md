# Chapter 12: Various Checks / Rules Regarding Language

## Statements

- The characters and intelligent monsters of Forcelia each have a means of communicating, that is, a language. In Forcelia (as in our world), there are multiple language systems. They vary depending on region and on race. Language includes spoken words and written words. In Alecrast, there are not many written languages, and there are quite a few languages that only have spoken words. There are not many races other than humans that have the habit of writing and reading. If you have a written language, you can not only have conversations with it, but also read it, but with a language that only has spoken words, you cannot read it, and can only have conversations with it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04636))_

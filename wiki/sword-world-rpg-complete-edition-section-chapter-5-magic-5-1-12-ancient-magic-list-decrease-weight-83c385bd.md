## Statements

- Distance=10 meters Area=One object Duration=18 rounds Effect=Reduce the weight of an object Type=Lost Counters (Negates): _Increase Weight_ Countered (Negated) By: _Increase Weight_ Expansion=Duration, distance, targets, effect (see description) Resist=None _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01550))_
- This spell is used on a single object and has the effect of reducing the target’s weight. If cast on a weapon or armor, it has the effect of reducing the target's required strength by -1 _without_ changing its strike power or defense power. When used on other objects, it has the ability to reduce their weight by about 90%. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01551))_
- This spell’s effect can be expanded. If you _double_ or _triple_ the amount of mental power you consume...... the required strength can become _-2_ or _-3_ ...... for weapons and armor. For other objects, it’ll be reduced by 10% each time. When reducing the weight of an object, it’s reduced by 90% each, the rate of _double_ expansion would be _0.9 x 0.9 = 0.81 times_ . For _triple_ , it’d be _0.9 x 0.9 x 0.9 = 0.721 times_ . To reduce the weight by _half_ , you must expand it _7 times_ , and to reduce it to _1/10th_ , you must expand it _22 times_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01552))_
- Even if you reduce the required strength, the strike power or defense power of weapons and armor will _not_ change. Also, in this case, required strength can only be reduced to 1. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01553))_
- Also, in this case, required strength can only be reduced to 1. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01553))_

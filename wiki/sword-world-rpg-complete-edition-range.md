## Statements

### 【 Dwarf 】

- They are about 120 cm tall, round and plump, and have bulbous noses. Men often have long beards on their chins, while women do not. Their hair ranges from brown to dark brown and their eyes are a similar color. Their skin color is dark, almost brown. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00162))_

### 【 Ranger 】

- Rangers are also excellent with projectile weapons and have the same offensive capabilities as fighters, especially when it comes to projectiles. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00231))_

### 1.4.5 Character Growth via Starting Experience Points

- Characters can spend their starting experience points to gain skill levels. For example, if you want to acquire 1 level in ranger, you can do so by subtracting 500 from your starting experience points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00346))_

### [ Ranger ]

- For weapons, there are no restrictions. Y ou should buy a weapon whose required strength is the same as your own strength. Ranger does not include the ability to fight with melee weapons, but it does include the ability to handle projectiles . Therefore, it is a good idea to choose a projectile, if you have to choose a weapon. On Table 1-8: Weapons, they are indicated as throwable made for throwing , and shooting . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00397))_

- Ducard II has 2 levels in the fighter skill and 1 level in the ranger skill. There are no restrictions on either skill when choosing a weapon. Ranger has an advantage in projectiles, but this is consequently irrelevant because of the higher level of the can use any weapon fighter. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00408))_

- Now let's buy some parchment (10 sheets for 10 G) and quill pen ink (5 G). Ducard II's ranger skill includes the map creation ability. It may be useful at some point. The thieves' tools are for a thief and the musical instruments are for a bard, so they're not necessary for Ducard II. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00429))_

### 【 Ranger Skill 】

- The ranger skill cannot be used for defense. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00764))_

### 【 Attack Power 】

- If fighter (or thief, ranger) skill level is 0 , then attack power is 0 regardless of the character's dexterity bonus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00775))_

### 【 Bonus Damage 】

- If fighter (or thief, ranger) skill level is 0 , bonus damage is 0 regardless of the character's strength bonus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00785))_

### Chapter 6: / Ranger Skill

- Owners of the ranger skill demonstrate a variety of abilities in outdoor activities, as explained below. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03545))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ② First-Aid [-] ] (No equipment restriction)

- The ranger skill is also limited by equipment, just like the thief skill, if you want to make full use of its abilities. The armor you wear must have a required strength of up to half your own strength, and must be ring mail or non-metal _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03547))_

- The ranger skill has some similar abilities to the thief skill. However, while the thief skill can be used both outdoors and indoors, the ranger skill is mostly useful only outdoors. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03548))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ④ Listen [Intelligence] ] (Equipment restriction)

- Rangers can listen to even the smallest sounds and can choose the sound they want to hear from a wide range of sounds. This is handed the same way as the thief's ability. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03573))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ⑤ Sense Danger [Intelligence]] (No equipment restriction)

- Rangers acquire a good intuition, which can be called a sixth sense. This ability is the ability used to sense when danger is approaching. For example, when you walk through a ravine and see a pebble fall, you may feel like a landslide is about to occur, or you may feel an arrow flying from the darkness. The target score is determined by the game master depending on the situation, but in general it should be set high. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03575))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ⑥ Stealthy Step [Agility] ] (Equipment restriction)

- Rangers can walk silently and unnoticed. This is handled the same way as the thief skill's ability. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03577))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ⑦ Survivalism [Life Force] ] (No equipment restriction)

- Rangers have acquired the ability to endure difficult situations brought about by disaster, such as surviving with only a small amount of water or avoiding freezing to death in winter mountains, and either survive until help arrives or escape from a predicament on their own. When an adventurer's survival is in danger, the game master should set a target score and have them make a success roll to overcome the situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03579))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ⑨ Map Creation [Intelligence]] (No equipment restriction, not retriable)

- Rangers have a good sense of direction and can map out the places they've been. You can also determine direction from the position of the sun or stars. When creating a map, the game master determines a difficulty, and a success roll must be made to determine whether or not the map was drawn correctly. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03584))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ⑩ Weather Prediction [Intelligence] ] (No equipment restriction, not retriable)

- Rangers have the ability to predict the weather. Rangers can measure the humidity using their own senses, and they also use small knowledge such as birds that eat small insects such as flies fly close to the ground = no updraft = thick clouds = rain . From these combinations, you can roughly predict the weather. If a ranger succeeds on a success roll against a set target score using ranger skill level + intelligence bonus as the baseline score, the ranger can roughly predict the weather (sunny, cloudy, rain, snow, storm, strong wind...... etc.) The game master must decide whether to use a difficulty or a target score. Normally you would use a target score. This is because failure means that the weather is unpredictable, and this is also very clear to the characters as well. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03586))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ⑫ Projectile Use [Dexterity, Strength] ] (No equipment restriction)

- The ranger skill is treated the same as the fighter skill (attack power, bonus damage modifiers) only when attacking with projectiles. You can use any projectile as long as your strength allows it. The ranger skill is not relevant to the use of or defense from melee weapons. Adventurers attack with the ranger skill and defend with the fighter or thief skill. When using the thief skill for defense, you must be lightly armed (required strength of weapons and armor must be half your own strength or less). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03592))_

### Chapter 6: / [ ⑭ Surprise Attack [Intelligence]] (No equipment restriction)

- The ranger skill is used for surprise attacks. For details, see 10.3: Surprise Attacks (see p. 138). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03596))_

### Chapter 7:

- Therefore, a good ranger has the potential to be called a hero by the people, but a hunter, no matter how good he is, has a hard time getting people to recognize him as a hero. Of course, being a hunter is also a respectable profession, so you should never look down on it. People with higher levels in general skills should also be given due respect. However, they are not called heroes. They don't even become the protagonist of the story. They are unrelated to these adventures . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03753))_

### Chapter 7: / Hunter Skill

- Regarding the abilities from ① to ⑧ , ranger has an ability with the same name, and they are exactly the same. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03824))_

### [ Ceiling Hanger ]

- Ceiling hangers primarily attack sleeping prey, but when hungry they may attack active prey as well. In addition to its protective color, it also stretches its body out from the ceiling in an instant to attack, so it has a high potential for surprise attacks. Rangers have a chance to notice this surprise attack with their sense danger ability. To do so, you must succeed on a success toll against target score 12 using ranger skill level + intelligence bonus as the baseline score. Failure will result in a surprise attack and the targeted character will suffer a -4 penalty to evasion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-05648))_

### Larva

- Those with the ranger skill may be able to sense an attack from this dangerous creature with their sense danger ability. Make a success roll against target score 16, using ranger skill level + intelligence bonus as the baseline score. If you succeed, you'll be able to sense the hole just before it opens under your feet and jump to the side. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06501))_

### Chapter 16: / Projectile Bonus Damage

- In the basic rules, any weapon's bonus damage equals fi ghter (or thief, ranger) skill level + strength bonus , but for projectiles , an adventurer's strength is not fully utilized as-is. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08904))_

### Chapter 16: / Projectile Range Extension

- In the basic rules, the range limit is 20 meters for throwing weapons and 100 meters for shooting weapons. If you follow these optional rules, you can extend these even further. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08909))_

### Net

- Even if this hits, it cannot deal damage. It's used in melee, but you can also use it by throwing it. However, its range is limited to 5 meters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09084))_

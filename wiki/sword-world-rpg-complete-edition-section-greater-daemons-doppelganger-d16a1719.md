# **[ Greater Daemons ] Doppelganger**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Its has a bright red straight open mouth, but no other features besides that. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06060))_
- To shapeshift or shapeshift back requires one full round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06060))_
- A doppelganger can copy the appearance, abilities, memories, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06060))_
- Then, if they observe them for over one hour, they will be able to completely copy their abilities and memories. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06060))_
- Languages=Low ancient, languages of the person it shapeshifts into Perception=Five senses (Darkvision) The doppleganger, which is in the same family as the doubleburg, albeit a greater species, has the appearance of a 3 meter tall giant with an entirely black body. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06060))_
- At a glance, all they can capture is their appearance (including their belongings). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06060))_
- Then, if they observe them for over one hour, they will be able to completely copy their abilities and memories. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06060))_
- In addition, a doppelganger can use its own ability, ancient magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06061))_
- However, they cannot use holy magic or dragon roar magic (they _can_ use other magic). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06061))_
- If they transform into a dragon, they can breathe fire, and if they transform into a 10th level fighter, they can fight with their attack power, strike power, and bonus damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06061))_
- If combat occurs without them shapeshifting, a doppelganger will fight primarily using ancient magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06062))_

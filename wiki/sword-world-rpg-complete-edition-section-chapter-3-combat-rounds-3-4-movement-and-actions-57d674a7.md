## Statements by subsection

### Chapter 3: Combat Rounds / 3.4 Movement and Actions / Actions You Can Take While Standing Still

- Although stationary, you do not have to remain completely still; you can move up to **3 meters** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00786))_
- In this state, all _actions you can take during normal movement_ described in the previous section can be performed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00787))_

### Chapter 3: Combat Rounds / 3.4 Movement and Actions / Use Magic and Projectiles

- Above all, the advantage of _standing still_ is the ability to use magic and attack with projectiles. Conversely, if you are not _standing still_ , you _cannot_ use magic or projectiles. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00789))_
- If you want to attack with a projectile, please follow the process of _actions you can take during normal movement_ . If you chose _stand still_ , then _attack with that weapon_ can be read as _attack with that projectile_ . However, when you're _engaged_ (being attacked by a melee weapon), you _cannot_ use projectiles. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00790))_
- If you chose _stand still_ , then _attack with that weapon_ can be read as _attack with that projectile_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00790))_

### Chapter 3: Combat Rounds / 3.4 Movement and Actions / Stand Up and Ready Yourself

- From a state of falling down, stand up and ready yourself. You can only ready _one_ weapon, etc. that you’re wearing. You _cannot_ pick up any items on the ground. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00793))_
- You can only ready _one_ weapon, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00793))_

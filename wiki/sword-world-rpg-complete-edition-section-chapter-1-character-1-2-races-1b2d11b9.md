### Chapter 1: Character / 1.2 Races / 【 Dwarf 】

- Dwarves were once members of the fae tribe who lived in the fairy world, but now they have fully immersed into the world inhabited by humans (which we call the material world) and no longer have the ability to travel to and from the fairy world. Their lives are no different from those of humans. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00187))_
- They are about 120 cm tall, round and plump, and have bulbous noses. Men often have long beards on their chins, while women do not. Their hair ranges from brown to dark brown and their eyes are a similar color. Their skin color is dark, almost brown. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00188))_
- They can see things in the dark as well as they can in daylight. They are also very dexterous with their hands, and excel in such techniques as craftsmanship and blacksmithing. Many of their works are stunning and cannot be imitated by humans. Dwarves usually live in human cities and villages using their own skills, but there are several cities and villages where only dwarves live. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00189))_
- They have a very strong sense of tribalism, respecting the rules of their tribe and seldom violating them. They mainly worship the gods of the earth, with **Braki** as their main deity, who is also the god of smithing. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00190))_
- The life span of a dwarf is longer than that of a human, thought to be around 200 years. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00191))_
- Dwarves usually live in human cities and villages using their own skills, but there are several cities and villages where only dwarves live. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00189))_

### Chapter 1: Character / 1.2 Races / 【 Grassrunner 】

- Grassrunners were originally members of the fae tribe who lived in the meadows. However, they are very curious and many have abandoned the fairy world to become residents of human society. They have already lost the ability to return to the fairy world, but as fae of the meadows, they have a truly diverse range of abilities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00196))_
- They are about 1 meter tall and have the appearance of a human child. Other than their slightly pointed ears, they are not much different from humans. Their hair varies from golden to tan. Their eyes have a similar color. Their skin color is predominantly black or reddish. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00197))_
- They’re not in the habit of settling in one place. They only stay in one place for a while only to raise a few children, and once the children are grown, they travel again. They usually lead a nomadic lifestyle, either alone or in family units. Of course, they have no concept of tribes, nor do they like to live in cities. Their personalities are very cheerful and simple. Therefore, they have no custom of believing in gods at all. What they desire are the various experiences and incidents one encounters while traveling, so for this reason they may join groups of adventurers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00198))_
- Grassrunners have a life span of about 200 years. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00199))_
- They only stay in one place for a while only to raise a few children, and once the children are grown, they travel again. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00198))_
- Therefore, they have no custom of believing in gods at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00198))_

### Chapter 1: Character / 1.2 Races / 【 Elf 】

- Elves are members of the fae tribe who live in the forests, and are the envy of humans because of their beautiful appearance and immortality (no life span). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00205))_
- Their most distinctive feature is their long, slender, pointy ears. They're about 160 cm tall and slim. Their body hair is thin, and even men do not grow beards. Their hair color ranges from golden to chestnut, sometimes silver. Their skin color is near white and their eyes are pale blue or green. They are also close as a species to humans and both can produce offspring. These children are called **halfelves** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00206))_
- They live a very closed-off life in the forest. They do not like outsiders, nor do they want to be ruled by others. As such, they may act aggressively toward intruders into the forest. They rarely leave the forests where they live, but occasionally the odd elf will show up in town. They do not believe in any gods. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00207))_
- One of the reasons humans envy elves is their _immortality_ , which is a characteristic of an elite (ancient) race called **high elves** . However, these high elves avoid contact with humans even more so than common elves. Elves who appear in the cities of humans are for the most part ordinary (newer species) elves, bound to a life span of about 1000 years rather than immortality. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00208))_
- Elves are members of the fae tribe who live in the forests, and are the envy of humans because of their beautiful appearance and immortality (no life span). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00205))_

# Giant Ant Queen

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The giant ant queen is the queen of all giant ants. Her head and chest are almost the same as those of a normal giant ant, but her white abdomen is full of eggs and bloated. Therefore, her body length can reach over 10 meters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06417))_
- A giant ant queen is always protected by giant ant soldiers, but she also has her own means of attack. Giant ant queens can spit out mucus from their mouths that's used to alter the food they feed to their young. Those hit by this mucus will be deprived of their bodily freedom. If you're hit by the mucus, you won't suffer damage, but you will become more and more entangled. While entangled in the mucus, attack and evasion suffer a -4 penalty, and ancient magic and spirit magic cannot be used. You can escape from the mucus by succeeding on a success roll against target score 12 using adventurer level + strength bonus as the baseline score. However, if you're unable to escape, the target score for the success roll required to escape increases by 2 points each time you're hit by the mucus, increasing to 14, 16…… and so on. If the target score is 13 or more above the baseline score, the prey will be completely entangled and unable to move. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06418))_
- A giant ant queen has some intellect. With a certain scent (pheromone), she can command other giant ants, devise some strategy, and make them fight. Once a giant ant queen is defeated, the giant ants under its command will begin to attack everything around them indiscriminately (they will not attack each other). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06419))_

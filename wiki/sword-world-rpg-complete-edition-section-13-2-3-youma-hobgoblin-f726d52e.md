- Hobgoblins are a race of youma that are closely related to goblins, described later. They have reddish-brown skin and very little hair on their heads, but they do have thick hair all over their bodies. They are a feared monster with a physique comparable to that of a human, strong and powerful. When fighting, they use either a crude club or the fangs that protrude from their jaws. Although they have a belligerent personality, they are also surprisingly cowardly, and will run away as soon as they see that the situation is not going well. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07037))_
- Although they may live in a goblin village, they usually live alone or in groups of several, frequently changing locations and living relatively far from human villages. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07038))_

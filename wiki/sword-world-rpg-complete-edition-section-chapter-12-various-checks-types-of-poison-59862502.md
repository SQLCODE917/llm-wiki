# Chapter 12: Various Checks / 《 Types of Poison 》

## Statements

- Poisons are sometimes labeled as magic , mind , or damage , depending on how they affect you. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04767))_
- Those that say magic are poisons that have magical effects. These are basically magical items, so they respond to the ancient magic called Sense Magic . Magical poisons and their effects can be removed with Dispel Magic as well as Purification , Cure Poison , etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04768))_
- Those that say mind are poisons that affect the target's mind. The holy magic Sanity is effective. The poison's effect also vanishes when another effect appears due to magic that belongs to mind . Poisons that are both magic and mind are equivalent to magic that has a target score . The target score of a poison is the same as its toxicity score (described later). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04769))_
- Those that say damage are poisons that damage life force (points), but there are common rules for these types of poisons. Each poison has its own unique strike power and bonus damage. If this type of poison enters your body, you will suffer this damage. Make a strike roll as you would for any magic that deals damage. If you roll 10 or greater, it becomes a critical. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04770))_
- Those affected by the poison can reduce this damage. For characters, the score is adventurer level, and for monsters, it's monster level. After deducting this score, if 1 or more damage remains, your life force (points) will be subtracted by the amount of damage that remains. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04771))_
- Those affected by the poison can attempt a life force resistance roll. If you successfully resist, the damage will be reduced (in this case, strike power will be -10 and no criticals will occur) or you will not suffer any damage at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04772))_
- After deducting this score, if 1 or more damage remains, your life force (points) will be subtracted by the amount of damage that remains. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04771))_

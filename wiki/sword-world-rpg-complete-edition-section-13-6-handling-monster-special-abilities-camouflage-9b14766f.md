## Statements

- Monsters with this ability disguise themselves in a particular way, wait for a victim to approach, then strike when he or she is not careful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06314))_
- To discover this, you must succeed on a resistance roll against the indicated target score using _ranger skill level + intelligence bonus_ as the baseline score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06314))_
- From the second round onwards, it’ll be normal combat. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06314))_

## Statements

- This spell has the effect of taking away the target's hearing. The target can attempt to resist, but a target who fails will no longer be able to hear any sounds. If they're successful, they won't be affected at all. The penalty that'll be imposed depends on the situation, so the game master should act on the assumption that the target cannot hear. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05792))_

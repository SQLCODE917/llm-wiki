## Source record

Rules for destroying structures in Sword World RPG, including structure strength tables and material-specific destruction rules.

## Key supported claims

- Characters cannot destroy walls or doors without game master discretion, but specific rules are provided for numerical handling (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.277-279).
- A wooden wall with 20 cm thickness has maximum defense points of 7 and destruction points of 15 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.277-279).
- If damage is greater than or equal to destruction points, the structure is destroyed; if damage is greater than 1 but less than destruction points, defense points are reduced by 1 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.277-279).

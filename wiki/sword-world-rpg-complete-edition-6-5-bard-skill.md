## Source record

Bard skill rules and abilities in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering performance, knowledge, and musical instruments. Some aspects of bard abilities are unknown.

## Key supported claims

- Bards perform folklore and poetry for a living (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.119-119).
- Bards choose one instrument type (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.119-119).
- Bard skill includes singing, playing, and folklore knowledge (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.119-119).
- Higher bard level increases instrument skill (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.119-119).
- Bards earn silver coins from performances (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.119-119).

## Source record

Section 6.6 Common Adventurer Abilities from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering skills like Riding, Swim, Ship Steering, Climb, and Jump abilities.

## Key supported claims

- Adventurers naturally acquire various abilities due to their adventurous lifestyle, called common adventurer abilities (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-123).
- Common adventurer abilities use adventurer level + related ability bonus as the baseline score when making success roll checks (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-123).
- Trying to control your horse without reins incurs a -4 penalty, and failing results in falling from a height of 3 meters (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-123).

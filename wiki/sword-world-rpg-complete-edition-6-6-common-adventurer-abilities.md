## Source record

Section 6.6 Common Adventurer Abilities from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition covers a range of abilities that adventurers can acquire through their lifestyle, including Riding, Swim, Ship Steering, Climb, and Long/High Jump. It also details specific abilities like Folklore Knowledge and Spellsongs, which are tied to the bard class.

## Key supported claims

- Folklore Knowledge [Intelligence] is a bard ability that provides familiarity with myths, legends, and ancient poetry, which can offer clues for adventures, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-125).
- Spellsongs [Mental Power] are magical songs from the ancient kingdom period, requiring musical instruments to perform. Bards can learn one spellsong per skill level, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-125).
- Common adventurer abilities use _adventurer level + related ability bonus_ as the baseline score when making success roll checks, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-125).

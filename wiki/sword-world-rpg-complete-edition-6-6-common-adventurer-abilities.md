## Source record

These rules cover baseline checks, limitations, and tables for common adventurer abilities such as riding, swimming, and climbing.

## Key supported claims

- Common adventurer abilities are defined as any ability an adventurer can acquire, with baseline checks based on adventurer level plus relevant ability bonus. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-125) may
- The player must make a success roll using adventurer level + agility bonus as the baseline; failure may cause drowning. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-125) might
- An adventurer can hold breath underwater for rounds equal to their life force; breathing beyond that may lead to drowning. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-125) may
- Table 6‑1 defines climb by skill: with rope, climb with thief skill almost always succeeds; with common adventurer ability requires a success roll and may fail. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-125) might
- Tables 6‑3 and 6‑4 provide long and high jump target scores, factoring in skill level, armor, and environmental conditions; successful rolls are based on adventurer or thief skill plus agility bonus. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-125) may

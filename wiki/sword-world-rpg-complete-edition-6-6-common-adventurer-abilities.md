## Source record

Section 6.6 covers common adventurer abilities, including Riding, Swim, Ship Steering, Climb, and Long/High Jump. It also details specific abilities like Folklore Knowledge and Spellsongs, which are tied to the bard class.

## Key supported claims

- Folklore Knowledge [Intelligence] is a bard ability that provides familiarity with myths, legends, and ancient poetry, which can offer clues for adventures, (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.120-125).

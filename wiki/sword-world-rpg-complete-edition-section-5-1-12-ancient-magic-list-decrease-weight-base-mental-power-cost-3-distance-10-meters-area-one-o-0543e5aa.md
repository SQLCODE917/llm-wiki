## Statements

- This spell is used on a single object and has the effect of reducing the target's weight. If cast on a weapon or armor, it has the effect of reducing the target's required strength by -1 without changing its strike power or defense power. When used on other objects, it has the ability to reduce their weight by about 90%. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02873))_
- This spell's effect can be expanded. If you double or triple the amount of mental power you consume...... the required strength can become -2 or -3 ...... for weapons and armor. For other objects, it'll be reduced by 10% each time. When reducing the weight of an object, it's reduced by 90% each, the rate of double expansion would be 0.9 x 0.9 = 0.81 times . For triple , it' d be 0.9 x 0.9 x 0.9 = 0.721 times . To reduce the weight by half , you must expand it 7 times , and to reduce it to 1/10th , you must expand it 22 times . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02874))_
- Even if you reduce the required strength, the strike power or defense power of weapons and armor will not change. Also, in this case, required strength can only be reduced to 1. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02875))_

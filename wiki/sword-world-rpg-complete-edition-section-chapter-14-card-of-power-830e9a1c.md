## Statements

- This item was developed so that even those without the sorcerer skill can use ancient magic. It does not consume mental power when used. To use it, a manual written in low ancient is included in its box, and if you chant a spell name in low ancient while tearing up the card, the magical power previously bestowed on the card will be released. Magic intensity is 15, and the effect cannot be expanded at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08584))_
- You can tell the effect of the card by the picture drawn on it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08586))_

## Statements

- This spell is almost the same as the divine magic of the same name, except that it uses the body of a sacrifice instead of the caster to bring the dead back to life. Only those that the caster knows well (or at least is acquainted with) can be resurrected. Also, those who have lost their souls and are destroyed, or those who have become undead, cannot be resurrected. The person who was sacrificed loses their soul container and cannot be resurrected. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03894))_
- This spell requires a final score of 20 or greater while also defeating the sacrifice's resist for it to be fully effective. Even if you defeat the sacrifice's resist, if you do not attain a final score of 20 or greater, the dead who was resurrected will lose their memories. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03895))_

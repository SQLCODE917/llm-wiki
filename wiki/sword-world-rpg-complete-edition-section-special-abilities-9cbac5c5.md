## Statements

- Monsters may have various special abilities . Some breathe fire, and others are not affected by mental attacks. There are some that cannot be hurt by normal weapons, and require magic or magical weapons to defeat them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-05183))_
- When a monster has such special abilities, they are briefly expressed here. Since the amount of text would be enormous, we will avoid explaining each one individually here. When necessary, refer to the main text of the monster catalog and 13.6: Handling Monster Special Abilities (see p. 235). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-05184))_

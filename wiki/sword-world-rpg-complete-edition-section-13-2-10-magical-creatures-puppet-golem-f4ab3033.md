- Depending on the material, there are four different types of puppet golems that can be created. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05845))_
- They are oak, stone servant, bone servant, and skeleton warrior. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05845))_
- A puppet golem is a small, simple version of a golem that is created by giving temporary life to a tree branch, stone, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05845))_

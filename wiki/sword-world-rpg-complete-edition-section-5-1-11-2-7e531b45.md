### 《 Spellbook 》

- A sorcerer must somehow preserve the ancient magic spells they've learned. Otherwise, even those with the greatest minds cannot possibly memorize all the words, actions, etc. needed to cast spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01418))_
- For this reason, it is customary for sorcerers to write these spells down in a book called a spellbook . A spellbook is, so to speak, just a handwritten notebook, and the spellbook itself has no magical power of its own. However, because it's a very important thing, it would be a disaster if it were lost or stolen, so some sorcerers may keep several copies in reserve. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01419))_
- When you go on an adventure, or you're in a situation where you'd need to use magic, you'd usually bring your spellbook with you. However, no sorcerer looks at their spellbook every time they try to cast a spell. This is because the words and actions of a spell are usually sharply memorized in your head. A spellbook is used to review them, always confirming and ensuring your memory. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01420))_
- There are no special restrictions on spellbooks according to the rules. Nevertheless, keep in mind that sorcerers in Sword World always keep their spellbooks with them, and remember to take very good care of them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01421))_

### 《 Magical Catalyst 》

- Sorcerers require a special medium to cast spells. This is a magical catalyst , and it is through this that a sorcerer exercises the power of a spell. Usually, a specially-made mage's staff is used as a magical catalyst, made from a wooden staff with a Create Device spell cast on it. This is why you must have a mage's staff as a restriction when using ancient magic. However, some sorcerers substitute other things besides staves. For example, Laverna, the witch of Orphan, uses an amethyst ring she wears on her right middle finger as her magical catalyst, and Cyanos, leader of the magic knights of Ramliearth, carries a small twig wand in his left hand as a magical catalyst. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01423))_

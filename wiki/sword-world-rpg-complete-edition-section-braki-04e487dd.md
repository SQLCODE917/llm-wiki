# 《 Braki 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- He is known as the god of smiths. However, he was originally the god of volcanoes, and because he told people that when you heat a stone, it melts like water, he was worshiped as the god of smiths. He has many believers among those who work in the mines, and he even has the personality of a chief god, especially among the dwarves. As a result, most dwarven settlements have built shrines to Braki. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02905))_
- However, his temples are rarely seen in human cities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02906))_

# Designer’s Notes

This section provides insights and explanations from the designers of the Sword World RPG, offering context and guidance for interpreting the rules and mechanics of the game.

## Key Points

- The Designer’s Notes offer guidance on how to interpret and apply the rules in a way that enhances gameplay.
- These notes are intended to help players and game masters understand the intent behind certain mechanics and rules.
- The notes may also provide clarification on how to handle edge cases or unusual situations that arise during gameplay.

# 4.11.3 Natural Recovery

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- If a character is conscious and at rest (full rest in a safe place), that character recovers 1 point of life force per day. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01066))_
- If a character is not at rest (e.g., traveling, fighting monsters in a labyrinth, not getting much sleep at night due to keeping watch), that character cannot recover any life force at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01068))_

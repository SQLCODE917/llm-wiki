# Monster Level=3

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- They are ferocious by nature and will attack indiscriminately if they are hungry. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05291))_
- Their standard body length is 1.5 meters, but larger ones can exceed 3 meters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05291))_
- Attacks with their large jaws are powerful, and once they bite an opponent, they will not let go and continue to deal damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05291))_
- Crocodiles are large carnivorous reptiles. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05291))_
- Such giant crocodiles are also called alligators. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05291))_

# Chapter 11: Notes on Magic / [ Mental Attack ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-chapter-11-notes-on-magic-84c50fb0]] - broader source section

## Statements

- This is a very special spell. Before using this spell, you must declare in advance how many points of mental power (points) you will use. You can reduce the target's mental power (points) by _twice_ the mental power (points) consumed. The caster can use any number of points up to the amount of mental power they have. As a result, if the target's mental power (points) 142 reaches 0, the opponent will lose consciousness. However, mental power (points) will _not_ fall below 0. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03744))_
- Note that this spell does _not_ allow the target to reduce damage using adventurer level. Also, the caster _cannot_ add their magic power to the damage dealt, and _cannot_ divide the mental power consumed by the spell by their level (for both a caster with a 1st level dark priest skill and a caster with a 3rd level dark priest skill, the mental power consumed is the same). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03745))_

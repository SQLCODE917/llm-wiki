## Statements

- In Sword World , all production is done by hand. Therefore, the quality of the item depends entirely on the skill of the craftsman. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05319))_
- Products made by craftsmen are picked up by merchants and displayed in their shops. Of course, items made by skilled craftsmen will be more expensive, and items that are not made by skilled craftsmen will be cheaper . Naturally, there are many different types of craftsmen. There are craftsmen who make furniture, and there are also blacksmiths who make weapons. These techniques are secretly taught from master to apprentice, and care is taken to ensure that no secret techniques are leaked. Also, a craftsman must belong to a guild. It is illegal for anyone outside a guild to make products handled by that guild. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05320))_

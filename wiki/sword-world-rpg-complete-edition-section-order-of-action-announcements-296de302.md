- In combat, there are basically two opposing groups. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00697))_
- **Action announcements** are made by each group. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00697))_
- If there's an enemy in front of you that’s attacking you, attacking another enemy several meters away is not possible, just by common sense. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00698))_
- Casting a spell while running at full speed is prohibited by the rules. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00698))_
- These impossible actions cannot be declared, as a matter of course. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00698))_
- In this case, the order of action announcements is **A → B** or **B → A** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00699))_

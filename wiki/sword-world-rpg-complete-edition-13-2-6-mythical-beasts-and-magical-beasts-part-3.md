## Source record

This section presents the most feared beasts in Forcelia, including the gigantic roc, the fire‑spearing humbaba, and various dragon species.

## Key supported claims

- The most feared beast second only to the dragon, the roc is a gigantic bird resembling an eagle, with a wingspan of 20 meters, and may occasionally be swept close to the continent by wind. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198)
- There is an outlook that they are like giant beasts, living according to instinct rather than thought. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198)
- Those hit by a roc's talon attacks will be grabbed by the talons, lifted into the air, and carried to its nest. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.188-198)

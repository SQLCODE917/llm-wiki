## Statements

- This spell is used on a single object and has the ability to increase the target's weight. If cast on a weapon or armor, it has the effect of increasing required strength by +1 (strike power does not change). When used on other objects, consider it to have the effect of increasing their weight by 10%. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02984))_
- This spell's effect can be expanded. If you double or triple the amount of mental power you consume...... the required strength can become +2 or +3 ...... for weapons and armor. For other objects, it'll be increased by 10% each time. When increasing the weight of an object, it's increased by 10% each, the rate of double expansion would be 1.1 x 1.1 = 1.21 times . For triple , it' d be 1.1 x 1.1 x 1.1 = 1.331 times. To double the weight, you must expand it 8 times , and to increase it tenfold , you must expand it 25 times . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02985))_
- If this spell would cause a weapon or armor's required strength to exceed its owner's strength, that character cannot act. However, if you're using the optional rules under 16.8: Weapons and Armor That Are Too Heavy (see p. 268), they can still act, but they'll receive a penalty to attack power, evasion speed, etc. Even if you increase the required strength, the strike power or defense power of weapons and armor will not change. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02986))_

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- An octopus is a sea-dwelling mollusk that uses its eight tentacles to entangle and strangle its prey to death. On rare occasions, they may come ashore, in which case they suffer a -4 penalty to hit and evasion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06648))_
- There are many different types of octopus, but most of them are small and harmless. Here we will only explain the two dangerous types known as killer and giant . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06649))_

## Statements

- However, large shields with _magical power of +1 or greater_ will _no longer_ have this -1 modifier. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07306))_
- In other words, a _small_ shield is **±0** and a _large_ shield is **-1** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07306))_
- The shield's attack power modifier must be used _as is_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07306))_
- However, in this case, the shield's evasion speed modifier _cannot_ be added to evasion speed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07306))_
- The strike power is the same as the shield’s required strength (strike power modifier ±0), and the critical target is 12. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07307))_
- In this case, the adventurer _cannot_ use the weapon in their _dominant_ hand, but there will be _no_ penalty for using the _opposite_ hand. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07308))_
- You can use the shield's strike power for a damage check while at the same time making a _prone_ check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07308))_

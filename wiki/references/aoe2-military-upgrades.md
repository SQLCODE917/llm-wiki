# AoE2 Military Upgrades

## Summary

This reference page documents military upgrade facts from the source that affect Age of Empires II combat, especially archer, crossbow, bodkin, fletching, and knight armor interactions.

## Reference data

| Item | Supported fact | Evidence | Locator | Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Knight armor upgrade | Knight +1/+1 armor reduces crossbow damage from 5 damage to 4 damage, described as a 20% effective reduction. | "We get +1/+1 armor on the knights, we reduce the damage from the xbows by 20% effectively 5dmg -> 4dmg." | `normalized:L405` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Archer to crossbow upgrade | Crossbowman has 5 damage, one more than a standard archer, and the count goes to 25 crossbows. | "Crossbowman has 5 damage, so one more than standard archer, the number goes up again and now we're dealing 4 dmg (+33% increase), so **25 crossbows**." | `normalized:L398` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Bodkin upgrade | Bodkin adds 1 more damage to crossbows and reduces the described number needed to kill a knight from 25 to 17. | "Now we get bodkin which adds 1 more damage (+25% increase) and that leaves us at **17 crossbows**." | `normalized:L399` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Fletching upgrade | With fletching, archers deal 3 damage per shot and need only 34 archers to kill a knight with a single volley. | "Now we get fletching to the archers, we're suddenly dealing 3 damage per shot (+50% increase), so we need only **34 archers** to kill a knight with a single volley." | `normalized:L397` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Knight base stats | In the source's example, a knight has 100 HP, 2 armor, and 2 pierce armor; an archer has 4 pierce damage, so 50 archers are needed to kill a knight in one volley. | "Knight has 100HP, 2 armor and 2 pierce armor. Archer has 4 pierce damage. So an archer deals 2 damage to a knight, you'd need **50 archers** to kill a knight in a single volley." | `normalized:L396` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |

## Source-backed details

| Claim | Evidence | Locator | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Upgrade timing can create a power spike. | "This is just one type of timing, there are upgrade timings as well, like having 35 archers being upgraded with xbow and bodkin from feudal is a huge power spike." | `normalized:L437` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Feudal-age damage and armor upgrades can delay Castle Age timing because they cost food. | "Damage upgrades and armor upgrades during feudal age are problematic because they do cost food which delays your castle age time." | `normalized:L411` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| The crossbow upgrade increases the listed unit damage from the standard archer's 4 damage to 5 damage. | "Crossbowman has 5 damage, so one more than standard archer, the number goes up again and now we're dealing 4 dmg (+33% increase), so **25 crossbows**." | `normalized:L398` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |

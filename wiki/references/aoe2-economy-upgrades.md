# AoE2 Economy Upgrades

This reference page lists economy-upgrade facts from the AoE2 basics guide.

## Reference data

| Item | Supported fact | Evidence | Locator | Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Economy upgrades | Economy upgrades can be researched without stopping villager production. | "Best thing about these upgrades is that they do not cost TC time, so you do not need to stop producing villagers for them." | `normalized:L288` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Lumbercamp upgrades | Lumbercamp upgrades should be prioritized highly. | "All lumbercamp upgrades are extremely important and should be prioritized very highly." | `normalized:L290` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Farm upgrades | Horse collar, heavy plow, and crop rotation are wood-saving farm upgrades. | "Farm upgrades like horse collar, heavy plow and crop rotation however are effective wood saving upgrades." | `normalized:L290` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Mining camp upgrades | Feudal-age mining camp upgrades are usually delayed until age-up research. | "during feudal age the mining camp upgrades are usually left until you're researching age up already" | `normalized:L292` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Wheelbarrow | Wheelbarrow increases farm food income efficiency by 25-35%. | "Wheelbarrow effectively increases the efficiency of your farms (food income) by 25-35%." | `normalized:L294` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Handcart | Handcart has a 500 total resource cost. | "Handcart however is a whole different ordeal, with the whopping 500 total resource cost" | `normalized:L294` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |

## Source-backed details

| Claim | Evidence | Locator | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Economy upgrades can improve resource income without interrupting villager production. | "Best thing about these upgrades is that they do not cost TC time, so you do not need to stop producing villagers for them." | `normalized:L288` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Lumbercamp upgrades are given high priority in the source. | "All lumbercamp upgrades are extremely important and should be prioritized very highly." | `normalized:L290` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Farm upgrades save wood by increasing the amount of food in each farm. | "this means you do not have to spend 60 wood as often to refresh farms." | `normalized:L290` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Mining camp upgrades are usually delayed in feudal age because their food cost can slow castle-age timing. | "75 food is nearly 10% of the food cost of castle age upgrade, so it does indeed slow you down." | `normalized:L292` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Wheelbarrow has an efficiency benefit but also costs town-center time. | your TC "idles" for 75s which is effectively worth of 3 villagers. | `normalized:L294` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |
| Handcart is delayed because its cost competes with army production or imperial-age timing. | "you'd have to cut your army numbers or your imperial timing significantly to be able to afford this upgrade during castle age." | `normalized:L294` | [Noobs guide for AoE2](../sources/aoe2-basics.md) |

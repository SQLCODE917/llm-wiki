# **Dark Elf Leader**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The data listed here is based on the strength needed to lead a group of 5 to 6 people. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04762))_
- If a character is poisoned and also fails a life force resistance roll (target score 13), the character will be paralyzed at the end of the next round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04762))_
- Those who lead larger groups can be even stronger. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04762))_
- The poison they use has a higher toxicity score than the usual kind, and its effect is paralysis. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04762))_
- Leaders also often apply and use poison on their weapons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04762))_

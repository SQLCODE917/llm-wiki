# **Resistance Rolls**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- A success roll made using _adventurer level + life force bonus_ as the baseline score is specifically referred to as a **life force resistance roll** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00645))_
- Similarly, a success roll made using _adventurer level + mental power bonus_ as the baseline score is specifically called a **mental power resistance roll** , and the two are collectively called simply **resistance rolls** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00645))_
- The reason for the name change and the distinction between the two in particular, is that they are frequently performed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00646))_

## Source record

Chapter 12.3 of the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition covers work requests to NPCs, detailing costs, hiring, and payment terms.

## Key supported claims

- For simple tasks, the cost is calculated as NPC skill level x skill level x 30 gamels (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.168-168).
- For complex tasks, use multipliers of x40 or x50 instead of x30 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.168-168).
- If the task completely restrains the NPC, the adventurer must hire the NPC, with a daily rate of NPC's skill level multiplied by 50 to 200 gamels (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.168-168).
- Adventurers cannot charge these rates themselves; they must rely on clients for payment (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.168-168).
- A 10% to 30% advance payment is typical, and if the work fails, no remainder is paid (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.168-168).

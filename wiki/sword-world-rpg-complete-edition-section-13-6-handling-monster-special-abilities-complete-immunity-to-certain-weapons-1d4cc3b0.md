## Statements

- There are types of weapons that are completely ineffective due to their form, material, etc. Attacks with the indicated weapon, no matter how powerful or magical it is, will have no effect at all. Even in combat between monsters, if an opponent monster's attack method is equivalent to an ineffective weapon, it will not deal damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09882))_

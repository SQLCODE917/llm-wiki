## Source record

This section describes various animals found in the Sword World RPG setting, including sand worms, giant seals, snakes, spiders, and octopuses.

## Key supported claims

- Sand worms usually burrow underground, but are always hungry, so when other creatures pass by on the ground, they leap out to attack them for food. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211)
- Giant seals live in the frozen seas of the north, diving under the ice to feed on fish. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-211)
- There are many types of snakes, including vipers, pythons, and hypnopythons. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.209)
- Giant spiders attack by shooting sticky string and biting with fangs. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.210)
- Octopuses use tentacles to entangle and strangle prey, with killer octopuses being particularly dangerous. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.211)

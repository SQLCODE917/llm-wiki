The *Magic Protection* spell is a 4th level ancient magic spell detailed in the *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition* (see [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-chapter-5-1-12-ancient-magic-list]]). This spell grants an object resistance to magic, creating a magical barrier that can negate spells cast upon it.

## **Effects**

- **Object Resistance**: The spell gives the targeted object resistance to magic, allowing it to resist spells that are not of the *detection* type.
- **Target Score**: The resistance is determined by a target score equal to the final score from when *Magic Protection* was cast. If a spell's power is less than this score, the spell will either lose its effect completely or take only an incomplete effect.
- **Non-Removable**: The spell's effect is non-removable, meaning it cannot be dispelled by *Dispel Magic* unless it's a curse or non-removable magic.

## **Limitations**

- **Detection Spells**: The spell is powerless against *detection* spells, such as *Sense Magic*, which will still detect the object.
- **Natural Resistance**: If the object already has natural resistance (e.g., humans, fae, youma), the spell will have no effect.
- **Spell Failure**: If the spell fails to resist, the *Magic Protection* spell itself will be removed.
- **Non-Detection Spells**: Even spells with a *resist* of *none* can be resisted by *Magic Protection*.

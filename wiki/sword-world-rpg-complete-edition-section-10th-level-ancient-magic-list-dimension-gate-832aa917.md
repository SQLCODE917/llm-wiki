## Statements

- This spell creates a gate between two distant places, allowing you to instantly move to a destination. The gate is a silver disc 5 meters in diameter, which appears on the floor. A disc of the same shape will also appear above the destination. The disc can be penetrated like water, and those who jump into it will jump out of the other disc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02217))_
- There is no restriction on the travel distance, but the destination must be a place the caster knows. If the caster knows about the underworld, it's also possible to open a gate to the underworld. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02218))_
- A normal Dimension Gate lasts for only 3 minutes, but the sorcerers of the ancient kingdom knew the technique for fixing gates semi-permanently, and so used them as a way of moving between distant places or within labyrinths. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02219))_

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Any damage is first absorbed by this protective light, so until it vanishes, virtually no damage can be dealt directly to the body. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02546))_
- When the protection runs out, any damage beyond that (such as suffering 20 damage when the protection has only 10 remaining) will not reach the target. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02546))_
- The protective light absorbs all damage up to 50 points in total, whether it's caused by weapons or by magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02546))_

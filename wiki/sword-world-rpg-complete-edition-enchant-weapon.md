The *Enchant Weapon* spell is a 1st level ancient magic spell detailed in the *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition* (see [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-chapter-5-1-12-ancient-magic-list]]). This spell enhances a weapon's strike power by +5, and can also be applied to living creatures' bare hands, claws, or fangs.

## **Effects**

- **Weapons and Armor**: Adds +5 to the weapon's strike power. For example, a weapon with a base strike power of 10 becomes 15. Attack power and critical target remain unchanged. If cast on armor, it reduces the required strength by -1 without changing strike or defense power.
- **Living Creatures**: When cast directly on a living creature, it adds +5 to the strike power of bare hands, claws, or fangs (or +1 if strike points).
- **Projectiles**: If applied to a projectile such as a bow, everything fired from it will gain +5 to strike power, making it effective against monsters that normal weapons cannot affect.

## **Limitations**

- The spell cannot be used on weapons or armor that already have non-removable weapon enhancement magic, as such enhancements cannot be nullified.
- The spell's effect does not change the required strength of the weapon or armor, only the strike power.
- When applied to weapons or armor, required strength can only be reduced to 1.

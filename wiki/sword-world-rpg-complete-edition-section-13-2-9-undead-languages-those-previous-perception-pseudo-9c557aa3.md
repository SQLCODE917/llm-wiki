## Statements

- A specter is a very troublesome entity, a grudge that has lost its physical body and remains in this world. The faint, transparent appearance it had in life takes on the form of a hazy shadow. Since it does not have a physical body, attacks such as weapons and damage magic are ineffective, and the only effective countermeasure is magic that has a mental effect. Those who had rune master skills previously can use magic. However, holy magic cannot be used. A magical catalyst is not required when using ancient magic. Also, after performing _possession_ , they can freely use the skills they had previously. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05697))_

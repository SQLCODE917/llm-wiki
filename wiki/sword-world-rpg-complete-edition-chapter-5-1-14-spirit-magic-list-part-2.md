## 5.1.14 Spirit Magic List (part 2)

This section details spirit magic spells, organized by level.

### 5th Level Spirit Magic List

#### **[ Fear ]** 

## **(Shade/Dark Spirit)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=12 Distance=10 meters Area=One Duration=18 rounds 

Effect=Puts fear into an opponent Type=Mind Expansion=Final score, duration, distance, targets, effect Resist=Negated effect 

This spell has the effect of instilling fear in the target. An opponent who suffers the effect of this spell will feel an immeasurable fear towards the caster. For those affected, please determine the specific effect according to Table 5-5: Fears. 

This spell's effect can be expanded. If expanded, make an equivalent number of checks on the _Fears_ table, then apply which result the caster finds most desirable. 

## **Table 5-5: Fears** 

- 2D Result 

- 2[The target goes berserk from a state of panic. Attack points +2, evasion points -4. Even those with intelligence can no longer act rationally and ] cannot use magic, etc. 

- 3 Same as above 

- 4 Target is struck by panic and is prevented from taking action. Take a -1 penalty on all success rolls 

- 5 Same as above, but penalty is -2 

- 6 Same as above, but penalty is -3 

- 7 Target runs away. They go berserk if that's not possible. 

- 8 Target runs away. If that's not possible, they act with a -3 penalty 

- 9 Target runs away. If that's not possible, they freeze in fear and become unable to act 

- 10 Target freezes in fear. They become unable to act 

- 11 Same as above 

- 12 Target loses consciousness from fear. It takes 1 hour to regain consciousness naturally 

#### **[ Restore Health ] (The Unknown Spirit of Life)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=20 

Distance=Touch 

Area=One Duration=Instant 

Effect=Heals the target from poison or illness Type=Expansion=Final score Resist=None 

This spell can only be used _by_ women. Of course, it's okay if the _target_ is a man. 

With this spell, the target will be completely free from the effects of poison and illness. You could say it's an effect that combines the holy magic of _Cure Poison_ and _Cure Disease_ . You can heal multiple poisons and illnesses at the same time. 

#### **[ Control Plant ]** 

## **(Ent/Greater Plant Spirit)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=20 

Distance=10 meters 

Area=1 plant Duration=Until focus ends Effect=Can control plants as one Type=Focus, target score Expansion=Final score, distance Resist=None 

This is a spell that allows you to control plants by borrowing the power of ent, which is the greater spirit of plants. This allows you to control all plants at will. It is also effective against plant-type monsters, but if the opponent has a mental point resistance score, you must make a success roll using magic power as the baseline score and attain a final score greater than or equal that resistance score. This spell can also move plants that normally wouldn't move, allowing them to directly participate in attacks. In this case, the ent will directly possess the plant and exert physical power, so its 

attack points, evasion points, etc. will always be the following scores. 

Monster Level =5 Rarity=15 Agility=6 Movement Speed=6 Attack Points=12 (5) Evasion Points=12 (5) Mental Points/Resistance=-/12(5) Special Abilities=Mental attack immunity, Weak to fire 

The scores for strike points, defense points, and life points/resistance will depend on the size of the plant being controlled, but for a normal tree they will be about as follows. 

Strike Points=10 Defense Points=7 Life Points/Resistance=30/15(8) 

The game master should modify these scores according to the size of the plant being controlled. Additionally, if the spell is removed by _Dispel Magic_ , etc. the controlled plants will immediately return to their original tree state. 

#### **[ Shoot Arrow ] (Sylph/Wind Spirit)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=20 Distance=30 meters 

Area=One Duration=Instant 

Effect=Automatically hit the target with a strike power 20 arrow 

Type=Counters (Negates): _Missile Protection_ 

Expansion=Distance, targets Resist=None 

When you cast this spell, the caster's arrow will be carried by a sylph, ensuring that it hits the target. The strike power of the arrow is a uniform 20, the magic power of the caster is used as bonus damage. Also, if you roll 10 or greater on your damage check, a _critical_ will occur. The target cannot resist this spell. However, armor protection is effective. 

This magic is a counter spell to the spirit magic _Missile Protection_ , so when the target of the arrow is under the influence of _Missile Protection_ , the wind spirit powers of the same nature will interfere with each other, nullifying both. 

It is possible to expand the effect of this spell to launch multiple arrows and attack multiple targets at once. However, you cannot launch multiple arrows at a single target at once. Note that this spell cannot deal damage to monsters that can only be wounded by silver or magical weapons, such as werewolves, etc. (unless the arrow itself is magical or silver). 

#### **[ Charm ] (Dryad/Plant Spirit)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=25 Distance=20 meters Area=One Duration=1 week Effect=Charms the target Type=Mind, target score Expansion=Final score, duration, distance, targets Resist=Negated effect 

Dryad is a plant spirit which at the same time has the nature of a mind spirit. This spell has the effect of using the dryad's mind spirit power to charm the target. At this time, if both your intentions are _mutual_ , you can make them follow your will in the form of a proposal, and if your intentions are _not_ mutual, you will deprive them of hostility. In either case, the target under the spell will attempt to protect the caster from danger. If the target successfully resists this spell, they will not suffer any effects. Additionally, if the caster loses their life 

for any reason, the spell's effect will be canceled immediately. 

This spell cannot be used if there are no plants in your surroundings. Dryads are not spirits that are always present in the minds of humans (or other creatures). 

#### **[ Valkyrie Javelin ] (Valkyrie/Mind Spirit)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=25 

Distance=20 meters 

Area=One 

Duration=Instant 

Effect=Create a strike power 30 energy javelin 

Type=Damage (Pure energy-type) Expansion=Final score, distance, targets, damage certainty Resist=Reduced effect 

By casting this spell, the caster can strike the target with a javelin of light. Its strike power is 30. This javelin will always hit the target as long as they're within the caster's view, even if they're not in the line of fire. 

Please note that this spell can only be used _by_ men. 

#### **[ Missile Protection ] (Sylph/Wind Spirit)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=20 Distance=20 meters 

Area=A space with a 5 meter radius Duration=18 rounds Effect=Protect yourself from projectile attacks 

Type=Countered (Negated) By: _Shoot Arrow_ Expansion=Duration, distance, area Resist=None 

This spell specifies a point in space and affects the space within a 5 meter radius from it. The effect of this spell can be expanded regarding the size of the area being protected (target volume). 

This spell has the effect of protecting the target from projectile attacks. It is a counter spell to _Shoot Arrow_ , so if _Shoot Arrow_ is cast on a target under the influence of _Missile Protection_ , the effects of both spells will be negated, just as if they had been removed by a _Dispel Magic_ spell. 

### 6th Level Spirit Magic List

#### **[ Control Sound ] (Various lesser spirits)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=24 

Distance=10 meters 

Area=One Duration=Until focus ends Effect=Control the target's sound Type=Focus Expansion=Final score, distance, targets 

Resist=Negated effect 

This spell has the effect of controlling the sound that the target makes. The target can be made to speak or remain silent, and the sound they make can be altered. The sound can be changed to any sound the caster wishes, but it must be a sound that the target is capable of producing. The target cannot be made to speak in a language they do not know, or to make sounds they are not capable of producing. 

#### **[ Spirit Wall ]** 

## **(Various lesser spirits)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=24 

Distance=10 meters 

Area=A space with a 5 meter radius Duration=1 hour 

Effect=Creates a wall of spirits that blocks movement and attacks Type=Expansion=Final score, distance, area Resist=Reduced effect 

This spell creates a wall of spirits that blocks movement and attacks. The wall is made of spirits of various types, and it can block both physical and magical attacks. The wall is made of spirits that are no more than level 4, and it can be dispelled by a spell of the same type or by a _Dispel Magic_ spell. 

#### **[ Decrease Water Pressure ] (Undine/Water Spirit)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=24 

Distance=10 meters 

Area=One 

Duration=1 hour Effect=Relieves water pressure when diving Type=Target score 

Expansion=Final score, duration, distance, targets 

Resist=None 

Targets under this spell are saved from the effects of water pressure when diving deep underwater. All water pressure exerted on the body by the spell will be alleviated regardless of its strength, and reduced to a point where normal actions are no longer a problem. If you cast this spell, you can fight underwater without any penalties at all. 

In addition, to actually dive, it is essential to use _Water Breathing_ in order to be able to breathe underwater. 

#### **[ Preserve Sound ] (Sylph/Wind Spirit)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=30 

Distance=10 meters 

Area=A space with a 5 meter radius Duration=Until sound is transmitted 

Effect=Record and transmit sound to a specific location Type=- 

Expansion=Distance, area Resist=None 

This spell has the effect of recording a sound and transmitting it to a specific location. It secures a sylph in place and allows it to transmit recorded sounds to others. You can optionally determine the person to whom the sound is transmitted to some extent, such as the first person to step into that space. However, in order to specify a specific person, the caster must be familiar with the person's features and appearance. Also, at the same time you cast the spell, you must also generate the sound you want to transmit. It's also _not_ possible to use this to record spells and activate them. Even if _Silence_ is cast later, this spell still takes effect. 

Once the sound is transmitted, the effect of this spell vanishes. 

### 7th Level Spirit Magic List

#### **[ Windstorm ]** 

## **(Djinn/Greater Wind Spirit)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=35 Distance=30 meters 

Area=A space with a 10 meter radius Duration=18 rounds 

Effect=Create a tornado, knocking targets prone within range and dealing strike power 10 damage. 

Type=Target score, damage (Wind cuttertype) 

Expansion=Final score, duration, distance, area, damage certainty Resist=Reduced effect 

This spell creates a tornado in a 10 meter radius around a point in space. Anyone except the caster within the spell's area of effect, or who attempts to enter the tornado during the spell's duration must attempt to resist. Those who successfully resist will move at half their normal movement speed (there is no change in their action order) and can take actions with a -2 penalty on success rolls such as attack, evasion, etc. However, those who fail to resist are slammed to the ground and can only move 3 meters per round while crawling on the ground for the duration of the spell. When attacking, evading, etc. in this state, all will suffer a -4 penalty. 

Additionally, the tornado creates a vacuum that cuts the skin and flesh of those within its area of effect, dealing strike power 10 damage. The cutting damage continues to have an effect until the target leaves the area of effect. Damage is determined by making a strike roll each round. Only the first attempt to resist this spell counts. Objects weighing less than 10 kilograms or equipment held in the hand or not securely attached to the body will be blown out of the area of effect. 

#### **[ Crack ] (Behemoth/Greater Earth Spirit)** 

Base Mental Power Cost=35 Distance=30 meters Area=One Duration=Instant 

Effect=Trap the target between a crack in the ground, dealing strike power 30 damage Type=Damage (Earthquake-type) Expansion=Final score, distance, targets, target volume, damage certainty Resist=Negated effect 

This is a spell that creates deep cracks, causing the ground underfoot to cave in, and swallowing the target in a deep chasm created this way. If a target falls into the cracks, they will first be buried in the ground up to their chest, and then be caught between the closing bedrock. Then, by being crushed between the bedrock, they will suffer strike power 30 damage. The effect of the spell is momentary, but once something is swallowed up by the crack, it cannot climb out of it on its own. It remains trapped in the crack until someone else pulls it out, which takes one round. When trapped in the ground, there is a - 4 penalty to attack power (points) and evasion speed (points). If you successfully resist the spell, you will avoid being swallowed by the crack and will not be affected. 

Note that this spell can also be used on targets larger than human size by expanding its effect.

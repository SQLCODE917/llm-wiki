# **Accomplishing Missions**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- If you're going into ruins to search for treasure, the goal is probably the treasure hidden there, and if you're rescuing a captured dignitary, it’s to bring that person back safely. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07665))_
- Most experience points are earned by accomplishing missions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07665))_

## Technical atoms

> If the game master deems it to be an incomplete success, but not _exactly_ a failure, he or she may decide to award experience points somewhere in between, such as 700 or 800.
_(source: Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07666))_

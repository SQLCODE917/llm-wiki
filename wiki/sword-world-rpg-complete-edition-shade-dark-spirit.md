# **[ Shade ] (Shade/Dark Spirit)**

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about **[ shade ] (shade/dark spirit)**:

## Statements

- A wall of darkness is a collection of shades. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02479))_
- The wall itself is pitch black, and the space up to 5 meters on either side of the wall will also be closed in darkness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02479))_
- However, if you touch or try to pass through the wall, you will destroy the shades that form the wall, and you will suffer strike power 10 damage to your mental power (points). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02479))_
- Your gaze cannot pass through a wall of darkness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02479))_
- However, it has no means of preventing the passage of objects, so you can pass through it without any resistance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02479))_
- For those affected, please determine the specific effect according to Table 5-5: Fears. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02393))_
- An opponent who suffers the effect of this spell will feel an immeasurable fear towards the caster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02393))_
- This spell has the effect of instilling fear in the target. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02393))_
- If expanded, make an equivalent number of checks on the _Fears_ table, then apply which result the caster finds most desirable. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02394))_
- This spell's effect can be expanded. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02394))_

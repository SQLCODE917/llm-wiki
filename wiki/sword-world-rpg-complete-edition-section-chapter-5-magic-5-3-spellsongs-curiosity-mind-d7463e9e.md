## Statements

- This spellsong has the effect of arousing the curiosity of those who hear it. The resistance roll is made on the fourth round after the song begins (count the _beginning_ of the song as the _first_ round, and make the roll when the bard's _action order_ occurs on the _fourth_ round). Those who fail to resist will be tempted to catch a glimpse of the performer. The result is that they'll come out of their hiding place and reveal themselves to the performer. It has no effect on those who can already see the performer. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03083))_

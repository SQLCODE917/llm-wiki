- This spellsong has the effect of arousing the curiosity of those who hear it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03083))_
- Those who fail to resist will be tempted to catch a glimpse of the performer. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03083))_
- The result is that they'll come out of their hiding place and reveal themselves to the performer. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03083))_
- It has no effect on those who can already see the performer. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03083))_
- The resistance roll is made on the fourth round after the song begins (count the _beginning_ of the song as the _first_ round, and make the roll when the bard's _action order_ occurs on the _fourth_ round). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03083))_

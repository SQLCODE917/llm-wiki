# Served by the Pot: Collections > Iteration and Iterables

## Collections as Boxes

A collection in JavaScript can be thought of as a box that holds multiple items. _(raw/javascriptallonge.pdf (p. 206))_ 

## Iterating Over Collections

Iterating over a collection means going through its elements one by one. _(raw/javascriptallonge.pdf (p. 206))_ 

## Operations on Collections

Examples of operations on collections include labeling all items or filtering them based on specific criteria. _(raw/javascriptallonge.pdf (p. 206))_

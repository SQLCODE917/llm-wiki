### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1 Basic Rules of Magic / Consuming Mental Power

- When using magic, an adventurer must temporarily _reduce_ their mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01272))_
- Each magic has a score called **base mental power cost** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01273))_
- This base mental power cost, divided by the corresponding rune master skill level (rounded up), equals the actual mental power consumed by the character. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01273))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1 Basic Rules of Magic / Example:

- A character with shaman skill level 2 uses the _Fire Bolt_ spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01275))_
- The base mental power cost for _Fire Bolt_ is _5_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01275))_
- The character must consume 5 ÷ 2 = 2.5, rounding up to _3_ points of mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01275))_
- A character can reduce their mental power to 0, but they _cannot_ make it negative. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01276))_
- For example, a character with 5 points of mental power remaining _can_ use magic that costs 5 points of mental power, but _cannot_ use magic that costs _6 or more_ points of mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01276))_
- For example, a character with 5 points of mental power remaining _can_ use magic that costs 5 points of mental power, but _cannot_ use magic that costs _6 or more_ points of mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01276))_
- If a character’s mental power reaches _0_ , they will _lose consciousness_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01277))_

## Technical atoms

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01269))_

> If the one on whom the magic is being cast chooses _not_ to resist the magic, it will _definitely_ take effect so long as the one casting the magic does _not_ roll double ones. You’re also allowed to choose to _not resist_ magic like _Fire Bolt_ , which will definitely result in a loss if it's cast on you....... but that probably won't benefit you in any way.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01272))_

> Adventurers cannot use magic endlessly.

### Technical atom 2

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01276))_

> A character can reduce their mental power to 0, but they _cannot_ make it negative. For example, a character with 5 points of mental power remaining _can_ use magic that costs 5 points of mental power, but _cannot_ use magic that costs _6 or more_ points of mental power.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01277))_

> The character cannot regain consciousness unless they recover their mental power to _1 point or more_ in some way.

### Technical atom 3

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01276))_

> A character can reduce their mental power to 0, but they _cannot_ make it negative. For example, a character with 5 points of mental power remaining _can_ use magic that costs 5 points of mental power, but _cannot_ use magic that costs _6 or more_ points of mental power.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01278))_

> Even if magic _fails_ , such as by rolling double ones or being resisted by the opponent, a character must _still_ consume their mental power, same as if they had actually used the magic.

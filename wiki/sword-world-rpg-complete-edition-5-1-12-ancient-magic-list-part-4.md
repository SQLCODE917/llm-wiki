## Source record

This chunk covers a selection of ancient magic spells from the *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition*, detailing their properties, effects, and usage.

## Key supported claims

- The spell *Petrify* turns targets into stone, with specific restrictions on who can be affected (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-83).
- The *Rune Shield* spell creates a magical barrier that blocks all magic, except for non-removable spells, and allows the caster to still cast spells (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-83).
- The *Dimension Gate* spell creates a gate between two distant places, allowing instant travel, and can be semi-permanently fixed by ancient sorcerers (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-83).

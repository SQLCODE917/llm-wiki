## Source record

Chunk covering spells and spirit magic rules from the Sword World RPG Complete Edition.

## Key supported claims

- Spell is a powerful version of Dispel Magic that removes all spell effects (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-83)
- Casting rules are unchanged, but to use the shaman skill you must avoid metal armor, free both hands, and may have active spirit power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-83)
- Counters (Overpowers) include curses on the caster; all magic except non-removable types is countered (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-83)
- Rune Shield can be removed by Dispel Magic, but other spells are blocked by the barrier (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-83)
- Skill for spirit magic is the shaman skill; those with it are shamans (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.68-83)

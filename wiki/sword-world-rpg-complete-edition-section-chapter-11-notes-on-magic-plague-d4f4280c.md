- Regardless of the symptoms specified by the game master, the plague's _rarity_ is 16. Also, the _progression speed/progression Intensity/ Healing score_ is 3 days/8/11 (22). It has a _lethal depth_ of 3 and is _very highly_ contagious. Note that the caster of the spell _also_ becomes a target of the contagion immediately after the target contracts the illness.

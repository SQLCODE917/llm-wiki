## Source record

Section 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition covers the rules and restrictions for spirit magic, the shaman skill, and basic abilities.

## Key supported claims

- The shaman skill allows use of spirit magic up to the caster's skill level (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.83-83).
- Metal armor restrictions apply, except for silver and mithril silver (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.83-83).
- One hand must be free for spell casting, and two-handed weapons can be used with one hand (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.83-83).
- Spirit magic cannot be cast without the corresponding spirit power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.83-83).
- Spirit power does not work inside buildings or deep caves (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.83-83).

# 《 Volume Expansion 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Some spells that affect a space or object can have their volume expanded. If the volume is expanded by two or three times...... the mental power consumed will also be doubled or tripled ....... Rounding should be handled the same way you would for area expansion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02585))_
- Since it' s the volume that gets expanded, the radius of a sphere, a side of a cube, etc., can only be increased by the cube root of the expansion rate. Please see Table 5-1: Area and Size Expansion. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02586))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, target volume is written in the expansion section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02587))_
- Note: On the table, expansion rate is the multiplier for mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02597))_
- When you cast a spell by consuming that multiplied amount of mental power, multiply the number in area expansion length or volume expansion length by the number (radius, side) that represents the spell's area of effect , and the result will be the spell's area . For spells cast on a flat surface, use the number in the area expansion length column, and for spells cast into space, use the number in the volume expansion length column. For example, when you cast the ancient magic Light , whose area is A space with a 10 meter radius , by consuming double the mental power, the resulting area will be a spherical space with a radius of 10 x 1.3 = 13 meters . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02598))_

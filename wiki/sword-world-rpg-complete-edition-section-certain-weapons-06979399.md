# **Certain Weapons** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- There are types of weapons that are completely ineffective due to their form, material, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06854))_
- Attacks with the indicated weapon, no matter how powerful or magical it is, will have no effect at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06854))_

## Technical atoms

> Even in combat between monsters, if an opponent monster's attack method is equivalent to an ineffective weapon, it will not deal damage.
_(source: Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06854))_

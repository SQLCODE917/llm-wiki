### Pole Ax

- This is shaped like a combination of an ax and a spear. It can be used in two ways: thrust and cut . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09062))_

### Halberd

- The halberd is an advanced version of the pole ax, and is shaped like a combination of an ax and a spear, with the other side of the ax also shaped like a hook. It's the most sophisticated pole weapon, and can be used to thrust , cut , stab , and sweep . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09064))_

### Lucerne Hammer

- The lucerne hammer has a similar shape to a halberd, but the tip of the spear is longer than a halberd, and the striking part is a hammer rather than an ax. On the other side, there's a hookshaped protrusion. This weapon can be used to thrust , cut and beat . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09066))_

### Glaive

- The glaive is a weapon with a knifelike blade attached to the end of a long handle. This weapon can be used in two ways: thrust and cut . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09068))_

### Bill

- Pole weapons cannot reach their full power in tight spaces. The length of a pole weapon is required strength x 15 centimeters. If you don't have a space with this radius, you cannot use attack methods other than thrust . It would not be a good idea to bring them into underground labyrinths, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09071))_

### Main-gauche/Sword Breaker

- A main-gauche is a dagger held in the left hand (right hand for left-handed adventurers) for parrying. Y ou'll gain a +1 bonus to evasion speed only if your opponent's weapon is a dagger , rapier , or short sword . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09073))_
- A sword breaker is a type of maingauche with serrated blades lined up along the back. When you roll 2D for evasion, if you get double sixes , you can break your opponent's weapon. If you roll an 11 , and you evade your opponent's attack, you can make your opponent drop his or her weapon. Of course, your opponent's weapons are limited to daggers , rapiers , and short swords . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09074))_

### Whip, Net, Bola

- These weapons can perform attacks that entangle opponents. If an attack with one of these weapons hits, the evader must make a success roll using adventurer level + agility bonus as the baseline score and attacker's final score at this time as the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09077))_
- If he or she fails, they'll be entangled and fall prone . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09078))_
- Once entangled with an opponent, the weapon is no longer at the attacker's disposal. If the opponent is of a larger race than him or her, the attacker must release the whip or net. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09079))_
- Once entangled, an escape check is required every round. Make a success roll using adventurer level + strength bonus ( attack power -7 for monsters ) as the baseline score, and attacker's final score when entangling (attack power + 2D roll at that time) as the target score. If the entangled target has hands or similar organs, the target score will decrease by 1 each round. While entangled, there is always a -4 penalty evading your opponent's attacks. To escape, you must first release any armaments you're holding. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09080))_

### Whip

- If the attack hits and the opponent is not entangled, the whip will return to the attacker's hand. The attacker may choose , even if it hits , to return the whip to his or her hand instead of letting it entangle the opponent. At this time, roll 2D, and it'll only entangle the opponent if you roll double ones . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09082))_

### Net

- Even if this hits, it cannot deal damage. It's used in melee, but you can also use it by throwing it. However, its range is limited to 5 meters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09084))_
- However, its range is limited to 5 meters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09084))_

### Shield Attack

- Adventurers may use shields for attacks. However, in this case, the shield's evasion speed modifier cannot be added to evasion speed. The shield's attack power modifier must be used as is . In other words, a small shield is ±0 and a large shield is -1 . However, large shields with magical power of +1 or greater will no longer have this -1 modifier. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09088))_
- The strike power is the same as the shield's required strength (strike power modifier ±0), and the critical target is 12. Since you're probably not holding it in your dominant hand, you'll still suffer a 2/-4 penalty. Even if it's a magic shield, the only bonus damage you get is the usual (fi ghter/thief skill level + strength bonus ). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09089))_
- If you're a fighter , you can also perform a shield attack as a ram . In this case, the adventurer cannot use the weapon in their dominant hand, but there will be no penalty for using the opposite hand. You can use the shield's strike power for a damage check while at the same time making a prone check. Please refer to the process for ram (see p. 266). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09090))_

### Cranequin Crossbow

- A hoist is attached to a crossbow so that the string can be tightened strongly with a small amount of force. However, it takes time to wind up, so there are significant restrictions on continuous firing. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09092))_

### Ballista

- A general term for extremely large wind-up crossbows that are installed on castles, ships, etc. It's not carried or used by characters. Hoisting requires multiple people. With 2~3 characters, when the total strength of the characters reaches the required strength , it can be wound up in three rounds. Once hoisted, shooting is done by one person. Both the fighter and ranger skills can be used to attack (i.e. attack power is the archer's fighter/ranger skill level + dexterity bonus ). The thief skill does not allow for ballista shooting. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09096))_
- When using a ballista, the strike power can often exceed 50, but even in that case, the damage check should still be made on column 50 of the Rating Table . Even so, the bonus damage will be shooter's skill level + (ballista's required strength ÷ 3) , so a ballista with a greater required strength will be more powerful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09097))_

### Lance

- A lance is a long weapon used by cavalry. It's held under your arm with one hand. It must be used on horseback and can only be used in a charge (lance charge) . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09099))_
- When performing a lance charge, the movement distance must be within lance length (required strength x 15 cm) x 2 and 90 meters (horse's full movement distance) . During this time, any attacks made against the horse or mounted adventurer must be evaded with a -4 penalty. Also, even if the horse is a war horse, you cannot make the horse itself attack. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09100))_
- If you both have lances, or if your opponent is using a spear or a pike , follow the rules for charging each other. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09103))_
- In the case of lance charge, it is also possible to run straight through without entering an engagement. However, keep in mind that there is a possibility that you'll face a counterattack from your opponent each time you use a lance charge. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09104))_
- It must be used on horseback and can only be used in a charge (lance charge) . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09099))_

### Boomerang

- When this weapon is thrown at an enemy and misses, it returns to the user's hand at the end of the round. However, it will not return if you roll double ones on your hit check or if the boomerang hits the enemy. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09106))_

### Shotel

- This weapon has a large S-shaped curve at the tip of the blade. Therefore, you can attack through an enemy's shield. When attacking a shield-wielding enemy with a shotel, you can ignore the shield's evasion bonus (even if it's a magic shield!). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09109))_

### Cestus

- These are combat gloves . This weapon allows you to use your hands freely while it's equipped. After putting on a cestus, you can also hold a weapon in your hand. Although you cannot add the strike power of a cestus to another weapon, nor can you make two attacks, it can be useful when your weapon is knocked away. However, it becomes a hindrance when dealing with ancient magic and spirit magic. It'll also be a hindrance to the thief skill's lockpicking and remove trap . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09111))_
- After putting on a cestus, you can also hold a weapon in your hand. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09111))_

### Sap (Blackjack)

- This weapon has the potential to cause fae, youma, or animals of human size or smaller to lose consciousness when you attack them unnoticed from behind. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09113))_
- When struck by such an attack, the target must make a life force resistance roll. The target score is the final score from when the attack hit . If he or she fails, they'll fall unconscious and will not wake up for 2D x 10 minutes . Normal damage is dealt regardless of success or failure. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09114))_
- The attacked opponent gains a +4 bonus to his or her resistance roll if he or she is wearing metal armor. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09115))_

### Garrote

- Garrotes may only be used during a grapple . They're completely ineffective against things that don't breathe , are huge , or wear metal armor . For detailed rules, see Grapple (see p. 265). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09117))_
- Garrotes may only be used during a grapple . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09117))_

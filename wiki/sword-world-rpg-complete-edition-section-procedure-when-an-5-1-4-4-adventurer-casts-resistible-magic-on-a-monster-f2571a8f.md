# **Procedure When an 5.1.4.4 Adventurer Casts Resistible Magic on a Monster**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- If the 2D roll comes up _double ones_ , that means there was some mistake and the magic did _not_ work. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01201))_
- You must make a success roll using _caster’s magic power_ as the baseline score and the monster’s **mental point resistance** as the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01201))_
- If the resistance score is _not_ met, the magic will _not_ work or will only have a _weak_ effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01201))_
- Even if the magic doesn’t normally lose its effect when resisted, in this case it is completely nullified. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01201))_
- If _2D roll + magic power_ is greater than or equal to the monster’s resistance score, the magic will be cast with its full effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01201))_
- If the 2D roll comes up _double ones_ , that means there was some mistake and the magic did _not_ work. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01201))_
- If the resistance score is _not_ met, the magic will _not_ work or will only have a _weak_ effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01201))_
- In this case, there is no need to roll the dice, and the magic does not take effect). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01202))_

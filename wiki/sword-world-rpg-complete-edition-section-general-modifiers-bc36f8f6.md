- Success rolls may have modifiers applied to them. For example, trying to find things in situations with little light can be difficult. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03947))_
- This situation can be reflected in the target score or difficulty, but there is also a way to deal with it by modifying the final score of the success roll. The advantage of dealing with this through modifiers is that it is simpler and easier to maintain fairness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03948))_

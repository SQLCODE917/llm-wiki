# 5.1.12 Ancient Magic List / [ Seal Enchantment ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-5-1-12-ancient-magic-list-19f66f88]] - broader source section: 5.1.12 Ancient Magic List

## Statements

- This spell is used on objects imbued with magical power to hide the presence of that magical power. For this reason, Sense Magic or Sense Aura will no longer be able to distinguish them. When discerning magic power with Analyze Enchantment , you'll need a final score greater than or equal to the Seal Enchantment that was cast on it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03336))_
- Sometimes there are items from the ancient kingdom period that have this cast on them. Many of them are protected by Seal Enchantment with a final score as high as 30 or greater. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03337))_
- This spell has no effect when using the sage skill to appraise an item (however, if you don't think it has magical power, you might not try to appraise it). This spell cannot be cast on living things. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03338))_

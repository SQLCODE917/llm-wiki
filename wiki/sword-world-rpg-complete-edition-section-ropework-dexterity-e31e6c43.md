# **[** ⑦ **Ropework [Dexterity] ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- A sailor can untie a knot by succeeding on a success roll using _sailor skill level + dexterity bonus_ as the baseline score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03718))_
- At this time, the game master should decide the difficulty or target score, taking into consideration the tightness of the knot and the time it'll take. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03718))_
- Sailors have the ability to tie ropes at will so that they do not unravel or, conversely, quickly unravel. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03718))_
- A rope that a sailor tied to keep it from coming undone should have a difficulty equal to the sailor's skill level if someone attempts to untie it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03719))_

# Pick a Weapon Up off the Ground and Ready It

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- If a weapon or shield has fallen on the ground, you can pick it up and ready it. However, that weapon cannot be used to attack this round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01381))_

## Statements

It's not useful for treating illness, but is effective when accidentally taking other poisons, giving you a +2 bonus to resistance rolls. However, it can be dangerous if used too much. A large amount of bark can be removed from one tree and can also be stored. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-05029))_

## Statements

- She is feared as the goddess of destruction and the goddess of the undead, and is considered to be the goddess who controls the _end_ . Believers consider destruction and slaughter to be their mission anyway, as they believe that without destruction there can be no new beginning. Due to the nature of her faith, she has no temples, etc. but her doctrines have been continually passed down throughout the past and present, so she has believers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02698))_

# Ram

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is an attack method where you knock an opponent **prone** by charging forward. This can _only_ be performed with the _fighter skill_ , it _cannot_ be used with the _thief skill_ . If your attack hits, make a strike power 0 and critical target 12 damage check. At the same time, the opponent must determine whether he or she fell _prone_ . The attacker must make a _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07206))_
- 266 success roll using _fighter skill level + strength bonus_ as the baseline score, while the defender must make one using either _adventurer level + strength bonus_ _**or** adventurer level + life force bonus_ ( _life point resistance - 7_ for monsters), whichever is greater. If the attacker’s final score is greater, the opponent will fall _prone_ . Conditions such as poor footing, etc. will impose a penalty on the attacker's success roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07207))_
- If you use this optional rule, then if successfully hit by a monster whose attack method is _ram_ , a character must determine whether he or she has fallen _prone_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07209))_

# 1.3.2 Skill Types

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Skills are grouped into several types. Below is an explanation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00203))_
- First, skills are divided into two types: adventurer skills and general skills . Adventurer skills, as the name implies, are acquired by player characters, the adventurers, or by equivalent characters. There are eight of these: fighter , thief , priest , sorcerer , shaman , sage , ranger , and bard . There are also two adventurer skills that cannot be acquired by a player character: dark priest and dragon priest . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00204))_
- General skills are skills of an ordinary person as opposed to an adventurer, such as merchant , farmer , etc. Some of these are useful during an adventure, and it is well worth it for an adventurer to acquire general skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00205))_
- Each skill consists of a number of abilities appropriate to the skill name, and having a skill level (1 skill level or higher) means that you can demonstrate those abilities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00206))_

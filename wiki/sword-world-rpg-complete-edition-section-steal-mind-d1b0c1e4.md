# **[ Steal Mind ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- First make a strike power 20 check to deal damage to the target, then the target's mental power (points) will be reduced while at the same time the caster's mental power (points) will be recovered by the same amount. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01997))_
- However, you cannot make your mental power (points) higher than your original score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01997))_
- This also has no effect on opponents that do not have mental power (points), such as golems and skeletons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01997))_
- This spell allows you to steal mental power (points) from the target and make it your own. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01997))_
- Also, it has no effect at all if your opponent successfully resists. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01997))_

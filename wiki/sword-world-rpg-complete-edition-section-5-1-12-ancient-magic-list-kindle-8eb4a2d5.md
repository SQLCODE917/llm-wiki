## Statements

- This is a spell used on flammable objects several centimeters in size, causing them to ignite. The effect of the spell will disappear in an instant, but the object should burn on its own afterwards. This spell's effect only works on highly flammable objects such as oil, dead grass, and sawdust, so it's completely useless if used on live trees or non-flammable materials. It has almost no effect on monsters either. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02893))_

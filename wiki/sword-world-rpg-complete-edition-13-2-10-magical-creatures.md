## Source record

Magical creatures from the ancient kingdom period, including simulacra, homunculi, blobs, puppet golems, imitators, things, obsidian dogs, gargoyles, sí, and stalkers, created through ancient magic with specific abilities and behaviors.

## Key supported claims

- Simulacra duplicate appearance and abilities of enemy humans, but not magical powers. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.222-225)
- Homunculi are pseudo-life forms with human-like intelligence and speech, created through ancient magic. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.222-225)
- Blobs are failed homunculus creations, turned feral, with iron-corroding liquid inside and cannot critical. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.222-225)

## Statements

- This is a spell that inflicts a plague on the target. There are various types of this plague. Each game master should specify the symptoms. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05966))_
- Regardless of the symptoms specified by the game master, the plague's rarity is 16. Also, the progression speed/progression Intensity/ Healing score is 3 days/8/11 (22). It has a lethal depth of 3 and is very highly contagious. Note that the caster of the spell also becomes a target of the contagion immediately after the target contracts the illness. Also, if the target successfully resists the spell, they'll suffer no effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05967))_

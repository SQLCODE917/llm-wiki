Those with the bard skill are knowledgeable about ancient folklore and poetry, and make a living by performing them. Bards don't just play instruments and sing songs, they also know information about strange lands and local legends and folklore.

The bard skill includes the following abilities:

- ① Sing [Mental Power]
- ② Play Instrument [Dexterity]
- ③ Languages (Spoken)
- ④ Spellsongs
- ⑤ Folklore Knowledge

## ① Sing [Mental Power]

This is the art of singing songs. Although they're called songs, unlike modern pop songs, most of them are melodies attached to legends and folklore. Heroic tales and love poems are used as subjects.

Each bard carries their favorite instrument in hand. Bards are free to choose any instrument, but only _one_ type. If you use other instruments, you _won't_ be able to use abilities that require an instrument. Famous musical instruments include the lyre and the lute.

## ② Play Instrument [Dexterity]

This is the art of playing a musical instrument. The higher the bard's level, the more skillful they will be at playing an instrument.

## ③ Languages (Spoken) [Intelligence]

This ability is almost the same as the sage skill's ability of the same name, but it _only_ applies to speaking, not reading. In other words, bards can converse in languages other than their native language, but they _cannot_ read (or write, of course) text. Unlike the sage skill, there is _no_ restriction on the languages that can be learned. However, of course, you cannot learn runes.

## ④ Spellsongs

Bards also have the ability to sing magical songs called **spellsongs** . These songs have magical effects on all who hear them.

## ⑤ Folklore Knowledge

Bards are familiar with legends and folklore from strange lands and local areas.

Bards can earn _bard skill level x bard skill level x 2D_ silver coins by singing and playing an instrument for about an hour in a suitable location (such as a tavern, etc.).

### 《 Damage 》

- A spell that deals damage to the target's life force or mental power. Spells of the damage type always have a clearly defined strike power . If you manage to break through the target's resist, use the spell's strike power to determine damage by making a strike roll using the Rating Table, same as if you had hit with a weapon in weapon combat. The key number is, of course, the indicated strike power . If you roll 10 or greater during the strike roll, a critical will occur and deal even more damage, same as in weapon combat. Conversely, If you roll double ones, it does not deal any damage at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02639))_

### 《 Mind 》

- This is magic that affects the target's mental state. This kind of magic can only take effect on one target at a time. If magic of the mind type is cast on a target, and other magic also of the mind type is cast on that target, the new magic will take effect, and the prior magic will be nullified . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02672))_

### 《 Physical Ability 》

- This is magic that affects the target's physical abilities (strength, etc.). Magic of this type can only take effect on humans , fae , and youma . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02675))_

- Also, with magic of the physical ability type, two or more cannot affect the same target at the same time. Please resolve them as counters (overpowers) magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02676))_

### 《 Non-removable 》

- Some magic, such as Dispel Magic (ancient magic) and Remove Curse (holy magic), can be used as counter magic that removes other magic. However, magic of the non-removable type cannot be removed by other magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02682))_

### 《 Illusion 》

- A spell that deceives the five senses of the target. These spells deceive the opponent's sensory organs and give them false sensations. There are three types of senses through which monsters perceive their surroundings: fi ve senses , pseudo senses, and magic senses. Magic of the illusion type is effective on those that perceive their surroundings with fi ve senses or pseudo senses. Humans and fae perceive their surroundings through fi ve senses , so they can all be targeted by illusions . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02700))_

- The effects of spells of the illusion type can be detected by a resistance roll. However, in order to do this, you must first be suspicious of the illusion. The game master should decide in which cases it seems suspicious. In the case of player characters (adventurers), this may be when the players themselves suspect an illusion. For NPCs and monsters, the game master should decide based on their intelligence. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02702))_

## Statements

- Area=One, or a boat-sized object Duration=1 hour Effect=Eliminates the buoyancy of water, causing floating people and objects to sink Type=Counters (Negates): _Water Walking_ Countered (Negated) By: _Water Walking_ Countered (Overpowered) By: _Create Ghost Ship_ _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02355))_
- Those who've had this spell cast on them lose their liquid buoyancy and sink completely to the bottom. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02357))_

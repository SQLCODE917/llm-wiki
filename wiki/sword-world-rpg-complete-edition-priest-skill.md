The priest skill in *Sword World RPG* allows characters to cast holy magic by praying to gods. Priests must have enough faith to hear the voices of the gods, as the gods' thought forms are completely different from humans and cannot be fully understood. Holy prayers are runes created during the ancient kingdom period to give words to the gods' complex symbols and cause miracles.

## Requirements and Restrictions

There are no restrictions on armor or weapons to use the priest skill, and priests can cast magic simply by chanting holy prayers. However, this action must be in accordance with the _will_ of the god you believe in. If you try to use magic for a purpose that is far _removed_ from the god's will, it may not work at all. Also, a priest who loses _faith_ immediately loses their priest skill.

Also, one character _cannot_ worship multiple gods. Those with the priest skill must choose only _one_ god to worship.

## Abilities

Priests can use the following abilities:

### Divination

This is the ability to pray and communicate with a god, asking whether or not one's actions are right. Occasionally a god will give you a **revelation** (given at the game master's discretion). However, a god's _words_ can only be understood abstractly by humans, and you _cannot_ ask detailed questions to the gods.

Revelations should not be used too conveniently for the characters (players). It must be done _only_ when they absolutely need a word from their god to protect their faith.

There is no need for a god (the game master) to respond to nonsense such as, _If I die in a trap here, I won't be able to believe properly, so let's ask if there is a trap at this door._

### Vow

This is a voluntary vow. When a priest wants to do something, they can make a _vow_ to their god. When a priest's actions contradict or move away from their vows, their god will give them some form of punishment, such as losing the ability to cast spells, or even losing their life.

### Healing

This is the ability to heal wounds and cure ailments. The priest can use various spells to heal injuries, restore senses, and counteract poisons and diseases.

### Combat

Priests can use holy magic in combat to protect themselves and allies, as well as to harm undead enemies. Spells such as _Holy Weapon_ and _Turn Undead_ are particularly useful in combat situations.

### Protection

Priests can cast protective spells such as _Protective Circle_ to shield themselves and others from environmental hazards such as extreme temperatures.

### Resurrection

Priests can use spells such as _Resurrection_ and _Recall Spirit_ to bring the dead back to life, though these spells have strict requirements and limitations.

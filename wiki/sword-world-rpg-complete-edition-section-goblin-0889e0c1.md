## Statements

- Goblins are a type of youma that live near human villages, and are extremely common monsters in Alecrast. Their height is slightly smaller than that of a human, and their skin is reddish-brown. They usually live in groups of several to several dozen in forests and hills, but they sometimes appear at people's homes and attack livestock or steal. They often use weapons (mostly small ones such as shortswords, daggers etc.) stolen from humans. In their villages, they may use kobolds and human children as slaves. They may also live with a hobgoblin who acts as their bodyguard. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-05276))_

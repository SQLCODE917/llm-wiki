## Source record

Section 4.11 of the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition covers methods of recovering life force after damage, including magical recovery, first-aid, and natural recovery.

## Key supported claims

- Magic or items recover life force after damage (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.54-54).
- First-aid recovers up to 1 point in 30 minutes (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.54-54).
- Natural recovery rate is 1 point per day while at rest (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.54-54).

## Technical details

### `technical-atom-b395054ce5a269aa` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.54)

It can also instantly recover life force to 1 point, no matter how great the negative, but it _cannot_ recover life force to 2 or more points.

### `technical-atom-a7e542a29ec4713d` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.54)

If a character is _not_ at rest (e.g., traveling, fighting monsters in a labyrinth, not getting much sleep at night due to keeping watch), that character cannot recover any life force at all.

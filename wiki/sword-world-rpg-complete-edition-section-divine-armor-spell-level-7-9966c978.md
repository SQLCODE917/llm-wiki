# **[ Divine Armor ] (Spell Level=7)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Even if the caster is already wearing armor, their defense power does _not_ stack (the defense power of the armor you were wearing will be nullified). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03109))_
- There is no required strength, and it does not hinder the caster's movement at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03109))_
- This armor appears to glow vaguely. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03109))_

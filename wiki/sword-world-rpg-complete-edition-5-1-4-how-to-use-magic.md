## Source record

Summary of the How to Use Magic chapter, covering spellcasting mechanics and resistance rules.

## Key supported claims

- Magic power is limited to a defined set of effects achievable by weaving runes called spells (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).
- A double‑sixes roll is an automatic success; a double‑ones roll is an automatic failure, regardless of resistance (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).
- Each rune master can use all magic up to their skill level, but cannot exceed that level (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).
- Holy prayer and daemon scream share the same underlying power, differing only in magical nature (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).
- Even enemies built from player character data may cast magic and be resisted, meaning magic checks apply between all combatants (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).

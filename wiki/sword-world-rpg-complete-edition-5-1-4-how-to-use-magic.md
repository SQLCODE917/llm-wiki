## Source record

This section details the rules for using magic in _Sword World_, including magic power calculation, the distinction between resistible and non-resistible magic, and the procedures for casting spells.

## Key supported claims

- Magic uses runes and specific procedures (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-59).
- Magic power equals skill level plus intelligence bonus (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-59).
- There are resistible and non-resistible magic types (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-59).
- Magic power determines spell effectiveness (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-59).
- Magic casting requires resistance checks (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-59).

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-59)

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-59)

He cannot use any ancient magic, nor any holy magic of 2nd level or higher, nor any spirit magic of 3rd level or higher.

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-59)

### `technical-atom-cce38861ccc5d65d` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-59)

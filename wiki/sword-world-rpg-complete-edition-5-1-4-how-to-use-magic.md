## Source record

Section 5.1.4 How to Use Magic (pages 56-60) from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.

## Key supported claims

- Magic in Sword World is performed using runes and specific procedures, with five main types: high ancient, silent spirit, holy prayer, daemon scream, and dragon roar (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).
- Adventurers must make a skill check to use magic, with magic power calculated as magic skill level plus intelligence bonus (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).
- Attempting to cast magic requires a roll, and magic can be either resistible or non-resistible, with different procedures for each (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).

## Technical details

### `technical-atom-b2afce67e30ea424` formula

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60)

**magic power** = magic skill level + intelligence bonus

### `technical-atom-dd38c330b55ed530` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60)

However, this also has some limitations.

### `technical-atom-7324a33664151774` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60)

Each has a different source of magical power and a completely different procedure (although _holy prayer_ and _daemon scream_ are essentially the same power, the only difference being the nature of the magic).

### `technical-atom-4f804a0b369365a7` worked-example

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60)

Suppose we have an adventurer with priest skill level 1 and shaman skill level 2.

### `technical-atom-20fcf7b8a95c6a76` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60)

He cannot use any ancient magic, nor any holy magic of 2nd level or higher, nor any spirit magic of 3rd level or higher.

### `technical-atom-18720b952b8cb263` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60)

The other, is that you _cannot_ have more than _one each_ of holy, dark, and dragon roar magic at the same time.

### `technical-atom-4c3c8efc4cd74bfb` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60)

A character cannot hold more than _one_ of these beliefs at the same time.

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60)

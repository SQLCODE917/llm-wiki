## Source record

Section 5.1.4 How to Use Magic (pages 56-60) from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.

## Key supported claims

- Magic in Sword World is performed using runes and specific procedures, with five main types: high ancient, silent spirit, holy prayer, daemon scream, and dragon roar (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).
- Adventurers must make a skill check to use magic, with magic power calculated as magic skill level plus intelligence bonus (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).
- Attempting to cast magic requires a roll, and magic can be either resistible or non-resistible, with different procedures for each (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.56-60).

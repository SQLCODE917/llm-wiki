## Source record

This section outlines the rules for language in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering normal languages, runes, regional and racial languages, and adventurer language abilities.

## Key supported claims

- In Alecrast, there are not many written languages, and many languages only have spoken words (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158).
- Common is the most widely used language in the world of Sword World (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158).
- Adventurers can learn languages through bard or sage skill levels (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158).

## Technical details

### `technical-atom-2994677752414f52` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158)

In Alecrast, there are not many written languages, and there are quite a few languages that only have spoken words.

### `technical-atom-caed43b93f3800ad` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158)

If you have a written language, you can not only have conversations with it, but also read it, but with a language that _only_ has spoken words, you _cannot_ read it, and can _only_ have conversations with it.

### `technical-atom-97aa1103cdd272c7` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158)

Silent spirit is an exception among runes and _can_ be used for communication.

### `technical-atom-2d985ce87d254830` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158)

Language was a major barrier for peddlers without borders, traveling bards, mercenaries and adventurers.

### `technical-atom-940fde453b9fe098` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158)

These languages are widespread not only among humans, but also among demihumans and intelligent monsters living in the regions.

### `technical-atom-2fa38b5f7b210d2d` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158)

Strictly speaking, each kingdom should have its own regional language, but we don't want to place too many restrictions on language, so the rules only allow for two broad categories: **mārdan** and **gundar** .

### `technical-atom-92a9453e5cdac32d` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158)

Its western border is the kingdom of Eremire, but with the exception of a small village to the west called _Palmer_ , mārdan is also spoken.

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.157-158)

For example, there is the northeast region, where three kingdoms are at war, and the central plains, which is crowded with major powers such as Orphan and Ramliearth.

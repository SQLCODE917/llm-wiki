## Source record

Plants section from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering killer octopus, giant octopus, creeping tree, ethnoa, killer creeper, mandrake, and alraune.

## Key supported claims

- Killer octopus has tentacles up to 2 meters long and attacks by constricting opponents (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.212-213).
- Giant octopus has tentacles up to 10 meters long and can attack with each tentacle separately (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.212-213).
- Despite being plants, ethnoa and mandrake are weak to fire and can use spirit magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.212-213).

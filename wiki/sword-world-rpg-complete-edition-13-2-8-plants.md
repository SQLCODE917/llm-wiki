## Source record

Plant-based monsters in Sword World RPG, including killer octopus, giant octopus, creeping tree, ethnoa, killer creeper, mandrake, and alraune.

## Key supported claims

- Killer octopus uses tentacles to strangle prey (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.212-213); Giant octopus attacks with 10-meter tentacles (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.212-213).
- Creeping trees move on roots and attack with tentacles (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.212-213); Ethnoa entangles and digests prey in acid pouch (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.212-213).
- Killer creeper entangles prey with vines (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.212-213).
- Mandrake screams to disable opponents (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.212-213).
- Alraune are human-like plant monsters (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.212-213).

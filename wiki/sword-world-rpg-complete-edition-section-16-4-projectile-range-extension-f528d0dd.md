## Statements by subsection

### 16.4 Projectile Range Extension / Long Distance Modifiers

- Hitting a distant target with a weapon is a nigh-impossible task. Throwing weapons have a -1 modifier to attack power for every meter beyond 20 meters , and shooting weapons have the same for every 5 meters beyond 100 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11449))_

### 16.4 Projectile Range Extension / Maximum Range

- There's no such thing as a bow with infinite reach. Each throwing or shooting weapon has a maximum range , and they will never hit a target farther away than this ( Table 16-4: Maximum Ranges ). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11451))_

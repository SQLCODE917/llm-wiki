## Source record

This section covers the rules for unconsciousness and death checks in the Sword World RPG. When a character or monster's life force (points) is reduced to 0 or below, they fall unconscious and are in danger of dying. Death checks are required to determine survival, with different rules for adventurers and monsters. Mercy can be declared when attacking, which affects the death check outcome. The section also details how life force can be recovered through magic, first aid, or natural recovery.

## Key supported claims

- If a character's life force becomes 0 or negative, they fall unconscious and are in danger of dying. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-56)
- Death checks are made using life force resistance for adventurers. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-56)
- Monsters use life point resistance in death checks. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-56)
- Mercy can be declared before an attack, affecting death check outcomes. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-56)
- Life force can be recovered through magic, first aid, or natural recovery. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-56)

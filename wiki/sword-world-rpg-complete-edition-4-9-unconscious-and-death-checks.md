## Source record

Section 4.9 covers unconsciousness, death checks, mercy, and life force recovery rules.

## Key supported claims

- A character who falls unconscious with 0 or negative life force from a mercy‑declared attack fails a death check only on double ones (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-56).
- Ducard II, life force ‑4, must roll a death check (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-56).
- Failure on a death check leaves a character unconscious unless double ones are rolled (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-56).
- Increasing life force by at least 1 point lets a character regain consciousness; otherwise they may die (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-56).
- Damage lowers life force, and more damage lowers it further (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.50-56).

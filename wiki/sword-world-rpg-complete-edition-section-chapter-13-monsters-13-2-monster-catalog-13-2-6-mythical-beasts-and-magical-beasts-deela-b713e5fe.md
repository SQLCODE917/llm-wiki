- Deelas live on the coast, are beautiful, and can speak. They are neutral toward humans. Deelas mate with human males and lay eggs. At this time, deelas use the ability to _charm_ by singing. Anyone who hears a deela's song must make a mental power resistance roll against target score 10. If you fail, you will be charmed and must do what the deela tells you to do. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04953))_

## Statements

- Monsters, etc. that do not have intellect or mind have this ability, so magic with mental effects ( Fear , etc.) is ineffective. If they do not have mental points (expressed as -), magic that deals damage to mental points ( Shade , etc.) is likewise ineffective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09901))_

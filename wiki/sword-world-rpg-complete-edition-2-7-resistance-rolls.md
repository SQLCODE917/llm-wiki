## Source record

This chunk outlines rules for resistance rolls and action cancellation, including restrictions on declaring spell names and the possibility of canceling actions.

## Key supported claims

- If an enemy in front of you attacks, attacking another enemy several meters away is not possible (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.33-36).
- In such a case, a character may cancel their intended action (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.33-36).
- You may only declare ‘I’m preparing to cast a spell’ without naming the spell (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.33-36).
- In the middle of a round, a companion may ask you to help with money or goods (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.33-36).
- A character has only two choices: carry out their action as is or cancel it (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.33-36).

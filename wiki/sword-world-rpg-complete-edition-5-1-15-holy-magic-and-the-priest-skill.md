Summary of 5.1.15 Holy Magic and the Priest Skill from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.

## Key supported claims

- Holy prayers are runes used to contact gods and use their power to perform miracles (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98).
- The skill for using holy magic is called the priest skill, and those who have it are called priests (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98).
- There are many gods in Sword World, each drawing worship from humans and other intelligent races (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98).
- Game masters should not allow characters to become priests of wicked gods (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98).
- Priests can use abilities such as divination and vow (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98).

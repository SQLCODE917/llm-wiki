## Source record

This chapter explains the priest skill for holy magic, its requirements, special god‑specific spells, faith dependence, and disciplinary rules.

## Key supported claims

- The skill for using holy magic is called the **priest skill**, and those who have it are called **priests** (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98)
- Priests must choose only **one god** to worship; multiple gods are not allowed (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98)
- Each holy magic has **special spells** that can only be used by believers of a specific god (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98)
- If a priest attempts to use magic **far removed** from their god’s will, it **may** fail (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98)
- The priest skill can be **confiscated** when a character repeatedly commits unjust acts or shows no remorse (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98)

## Source record

Section 5.1.15 Holy Magic and the Priest Skill from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering holy prayers, priest skill, god teachings, and restrictions.

## Key supported claims

- Holy magic uses runes called holy prayers to contact gods and perform miracles (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98).
- Priests must have faith and worship only one god; they cannot use magic against the god's will (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98).
- Wicked gods teach anti-social behavior and their followers are called dark priests (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98).
- Holy magic has special spells usable only by believers of a specific god (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98).
- Holy prayers are used to contact gods and activate spell power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.96-98).

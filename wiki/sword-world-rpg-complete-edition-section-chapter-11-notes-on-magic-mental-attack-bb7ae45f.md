## Statements

- This is a very special spell. Before using this spell, you must declare in advance how many points of mental power (points) you will use. You can reduce the target's mental power (points) by twice the mental power (points) consumed. The caster can use any number of points up to the amount of mental power they have. As a result, if the target's mental power (points) _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05777))_
- reaches 0, the opponent will lose consciousness. However, mental power (points) will not fall below 0. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05778))_
- Note that this spell does not allow the target to reduce damage using adventurer level. Also, the caster cannot add their magic power to the damage dealt, and cannot divide the mental power consumed by the spell by their level (for both a caster with a 1st level dark priest skill and a caster with a 3rd level dark priest skill, the mental power consumed is the same). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05779))_

## Statements

- The fact that there are countless unseen masses of rock floating in the sky is considered a matter of course among sages. These rocks fall to the ground at a rate of about once every hundred years. These rocks floating in the sky are called meteorites. The spell Meteor Strike is a spell that allows the caster to control a meteorite (a very small one) at will, and make it fall to the ground. The meteorite will fall the moment you finish the spell, causing a large explosion. Targets within the area of effect will be hit by shockwaves and fragments of the meteorite, suffering strike power 50 damage. Of course, its destructive power would be enough to destroy buildings and ramparts. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03658, source-range-0b9dec90-03659))_

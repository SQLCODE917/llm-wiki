## Key supported claims

- Café Molinari, also called Espresso Lungo, is a drink midway between an Espresso and Americano in strength (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.6-6).
- The first method of preparing Café Molinari is to add a small amount of hot water to a double or quadruple Espresso Ristretto (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.6-6).
- The second way is to pull an extra long double shot of Espresso, achieving approximately the same ratio of oils to water as the dilution method (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.6-6).

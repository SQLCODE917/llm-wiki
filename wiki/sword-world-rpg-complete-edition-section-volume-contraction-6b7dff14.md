## Statements

- Similar to area contraction, you can also reduce the volume of the targeted object or space. Again, the shape cannot be changed, and you cannot directly aim it at the enemy's eyes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02629))_

## Statements by subsection

### 《 Volume Contraction 》 / 《 Duration Contraction 》

- Magic with a duration can be optionally shortened. However, you cannot set a time that causes the effect to wear off in the middle of a round. You may only set it to wear off at the end of a round. You must also decide how long you want the magic to last before you cast it. Magic that's already been cast cannot be canceled at will. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02631))_

### 《 Volume Contraction 》 / 《 Magic Power Decrease 》

- Magic power can be optionally reduced. This will reduce the final score and, if it's damage magic, also reduce bonus damage (it's not possible to only reduce the final score or only reduce bonus damage). You must declare how many points you'll reduce before you roll the dice. If used on multiple targets, you must reduce it by the same amount for all of them. Also, if the side casting the magic rolls double sixes, the resisting side will also need double sixes, no matter how much magic power was declared to be decreased (if it's a monster, it cannot resist). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02633))_
- You must declare how many points you'll reduce before you roll the dice. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02633))_

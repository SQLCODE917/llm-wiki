## Statements

- This score is used when making a check to see if you know the illness. If an owner of the sage skill or healer skill succeeds on a success roll using skill level + intelligence bonus as the baseline score and rarity as the target score, they can look at a sick patient and identify which illness they have. This means not only knowing the name of the disease, but also its characteristics and how to deal with it. If a disease can be identified, owners of the healer skill can provide appropriate treatment. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04915))_

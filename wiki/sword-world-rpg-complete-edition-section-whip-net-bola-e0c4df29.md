# Whip, Net, Bola

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-16-9-e9b13c9d]] - broader source section: 16.9

## Statements

- These weapons can perform attacks that entangle opponents. If an attack with one of these weapons hits, the evader must make a success roll using adventurer level + agility bonus as the baseline score and attacker's final score at this time as the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09077))_
- If he or she fails, they'll be entangled and fall prone . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09078))_
- Once entangled with an opponent, the weapon is no longer at the attacker's disposal. If the opponent is of a larger race than him or her, the attacker must release the whip or net. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09079))_
- Once entangled, an escape check is required every round. Make a success roll using adventurer level + strength bonus ( attack power -7 for monsters ) as the baseline score, and attacker's final score when entangling (attack power + 2D roll at that time) as the target score. If the entangled target has hands or similar organs, the target score will decrease by 1 each round. While entangled, there is always a -4 penalty evading your opponent's attacks. To escape, you must first release any armaments you're holding. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-09080))_

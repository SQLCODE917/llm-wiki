## Statements

- Products made by craftsmen are picked up by merchants and displayed in their shops. Of course, items made by _skilled_ craftsmen will be _more_ expensive, and items that are _not_ made by skilled craftsmen will be _cheaper_ . Naturally, there are many different types of craftsmen. There are craftsmen who make furniture, and there are also blacksmiths who make weapons. These techniques are secretly taught from master to apprentice, and care is taken to ensure that no secret techniques are leaked. Also, a craftsman must belong to a guild. It is illegal for anyone outside a guild to make products handled by that guild. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03452))_
- To put it simply, this is a technique for creating items. If a craftsman devotes the appropriate amount of time to production (which varies depending on the price and quality of the item they're making), they will be able to create a product that complies with their level. Of course, depending on the item, you may need tools and equipment. The finished product will be picked up by a merchant at half the price listed on the price list. Items made by high level craftsmen are naturally of high quality. Of course, prices will _also_ go up. It’s up to the game master to decide what bonuses to give to these high-quality, expensive items. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03453))_
- Success rolls are _not_ required to create items of _normal_ quality, but success rolls _are_ required for weapon _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03454))_
- The price will increase or decrease by about 3% for every 1 point of difference in the final score. Please round up any fractions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03456))_

## Source record

Chapter 1.4 of the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition covers character creation, including race selection, ability scores, skills, experience points, and equipment.

## Key supported claims

- Players choose a race, such as human, dwarf, grassrunner, elf, or half-elf, each with unique traits and limitations. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-28)
- Each race has specific ability score averages, with humans having the most average scores. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-28)
- Starting skills and experience points are determined by race and background, with humans using Table 1-5 and others using Table 1-6. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-28)
- Characters must have a name, age, and gender, with age ranges varying by race. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-28)
- Weapons and armor are purchased using starting money, with required strength matching the character's strength. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-28)

## Source record

This chapter explains character creation mechanics including race selection, ability scoring, skill and experience point allocation, and equipment purchase guidelines.

## Key supported claims

- Starting experience points may function equivalently to character growth experience, enabling skill advancement. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-28).
- A character may not purchase a weapon whose required strength does not match their own strength. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-28).
- Dwarves may not acquire the sorcerer or shaman skills. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-28).
- Certain races may have specific skill selection restrictions, such as the inability to choose priest for elves. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-28).
- Age and gender may not affect a character’s abilities in Sword World RPG. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.17-28).

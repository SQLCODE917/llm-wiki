## Statements

- If by a stroke of bad luck, an opponent's attack hits, a character can still reduce the damage by means of armor. It is defense power that represents this. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00799))_
- Defense power is the same number as the required strength of the armor worn by the character. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00800))_
- If a character is not wearing any armor (just clothes or nothing at all), then defense power is 0 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00802))_

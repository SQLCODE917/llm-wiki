# **If a Weapon or Shield’s Required Strength Exceeds Your Strength**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- For each point of required strength, reduce _attack power_ by -1. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07894))_
- If one in _one hand_ is too heavy, you will receive a penalty, but there will be _no_ stacking. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07894))_
- For example, if your sword is 3 too heavy in required strength and your shield is 2 too heavy in required strength, your penalty is **-3** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07894))_

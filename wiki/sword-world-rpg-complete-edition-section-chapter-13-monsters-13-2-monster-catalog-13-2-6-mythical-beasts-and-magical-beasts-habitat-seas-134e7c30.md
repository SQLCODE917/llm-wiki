## Statements

- Languages=None Perception=Five senses (darkvision) It is said that the sea monster, the sea dragon, is a creature that evolved from a dragon to adapt to living in the water, but this is not known for sure. Its face looks like that of a dragon, but its body is long and slender and has no wings, making it more like a snake. At a total of 30 meters long, it is feared by sailors along with the giant octopus (see p. 212). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05126))_
- A sea dragon can attack with its fangs and tail, and at the same time, it can also wrap its long body around its opponent and perform a strangling attack (see p. 236). Even a large ship can sink within minutes if it's ensnared by a sea dragon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05127))_

## Statements

- This spell can be cast on rivers, canals, lakes, oceans, etc. to adjust the flow of water there. Casting this spell while on a ship makes it easier to go against the natural flow. Movement speed is limited to 50 meters per round, and can be adjusted by the caster, but the flow cannot be changed in a very complicated way. Also, this spell cannot be used to dam the flow or divide the surface of the water to open a path. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02774))_

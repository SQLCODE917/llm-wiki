## Statements

- Represents the type of magic. Please see 5.1.9: Magic Types. **Counters (negates):** and **counters (overpowers):** describe magic that can be negated _by_ this magic, while **countered (negated) by:** and **countered (overpowered) by:** describe magic that can negate _this_ magic. However, _Dispel Magic_ , _Counter Sense_ , _Suppress Elemental_ , _Dispel Order_ , _Rune Shield_ , _Perfect Cancellation_ , _Neutralize Elemental_ , and _Remove Curse_ omit this. Magic of the **mind** , **physical ability** , and **weapon enhancement** types also omit _counters_ and _countered by_ , since they are sufficiently described. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01467))_

## Statements

- It has been noted before that _double sixes_ and _double ones_ are _automatic_ successes or failures and do not imply _definitive_ successes or failures. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00631))_
- It is especially bad to misinterpret an automatic failure as a _definitive_ failure. For example, if a player is trying to find a trap on a door and has rolled double ones, the game master will of course tell the player _you didn't find any traps_ . When the player hears this, if they reply like the following, it's a prime example of this misinterpretation: _Oh, I screwed up big time. Which means I can say for sure “There are definitely no traps on this door”. And yet I'll carelessly open it and get hit._ *laughs* _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00632))_

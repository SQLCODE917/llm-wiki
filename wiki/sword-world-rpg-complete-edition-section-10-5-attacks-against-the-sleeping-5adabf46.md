### 10.5 Attacks Against the Sleeping / Rules for Sleeping Monsters

- To get within 5 meters of a sleeping monster, you must successfully perform the thief skill stealthy step . If you succeed on a success roll using thief skill level + dexterity bonus as the baseline score, the monster will not wake up. If you fail, the monster will sense your presence and wake up immediately. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05712))_
- Attacks against sleeping monsters are always a success unless you roll 2D and get double ones on your hit check (even when attacking a sleeping opponent, double ones is always a failure). Furthermore, when making a damage check, you can reduce the weapon's critical target by -1. If you are attacking with the thief skill, your critical target will be -2. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05714))_
- If you attack a sleeping monster but do not deal any damage, or if you deal damage but are unable to reduce its life points to 0, the monster will wake up immediately. It will probably become angry and counterattack the adventurer starting on the next round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05715))_
- If there is combat, etc. going on in the surrounding area or a loud noise is made, the monster will wake up immediately. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05716))_

### 10.5 Attacks Against the Sleeping / Rules for Sleeping Adventurers

- If a sleeping adventurer is approached by a monster, the baseline score is adventurer level + intelligence bonus , and if you succeed on a resistance roll against target score 9, you can wake up before being attacked. If the monster has the ability to sneak up on you, the target score will be even higher. If they approach using stealthy step , etc. their final score will become the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05723))_
- When a monster, etc. attacks a sleeping adventurer, the attack will automatically hit. If the attack does not reduce life to 0, the adventurer wakes up immediately. Therefore, the quickest way to wake up your friends is to kick them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05726))_
- Therefore, the quickest way to wake up your friends is to kick them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05726))_

### 10.5 Attacks Against the Sleeping / 《 Damage 》

- If the attack hits, roll 2D. If it's double ones, the damage will be equal to the strike points of the attacking monster. Otherwise, the damage equals the attacking monster's strike points minus the defending monster's defense points. If the difference is 0 or less, only 1 point of damage will be dealt. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05734))_

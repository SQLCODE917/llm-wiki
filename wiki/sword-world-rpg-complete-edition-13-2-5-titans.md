## Source record

Chapter 13.2.5: Titans from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering various titan monsters and their traits.

## Key supported claims

- Ogres, also known as jikininki, are ferocious, carnivorous titans who particularly enjoy human flesh (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.185-187).
- Although they are smaller than other titans, ogres are still over 2 meters tall and have strong bodies (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.185-187).
- Giants are among the ancient races like dragons and gods, originating from the primordial titan (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.185-187).
- Hecatoncheir, despite their name, actually have only 12 arms (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.185-187).
- Titans are given a chance to be among the ancient races, but are not necessarily evil (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.185-187).

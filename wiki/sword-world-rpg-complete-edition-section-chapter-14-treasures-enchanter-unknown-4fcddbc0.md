A shapeshifted form can return to his or her original state by either touching the snake statue or with _a final score_ 15 _Dispel Magic_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06901))_

Crocodile=Crocodile Bear=Grizzly Ape=Boss Ape immediately if they fail a mental power resistance roll against target score 16.

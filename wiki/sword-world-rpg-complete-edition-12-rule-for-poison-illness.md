# 12 Rule For Poison Illness

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about 12 rule for poison illness:

## Statements

- In _Sword World RPG_ , illness is defined as a state in which the balance of spirit power within the body is disrupted. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04504))_
- To remove a poison with magic, the caster's final score (magic power + 2D roll) must be greater than or equal to the poison's **toxicity score** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04516))_
- _Injected_ poisons are poisons that need to be delivered directly into the bloodstream, usually in liquid form. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04530))_
- points of life force when you are hurt by a poisoned blade. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04531))_
- _Inhaled_ poisons are in gas or powder form and take effect when inhaled through the respiratory tract. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04532))_
- _Dose_ poison is used by mixing it into food or drink and comes in powder or liquid form. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04534))_
- Magical poisons and their effects can be removed with _Dispel Magic_ as well as _Purification_ , _Cure Poison_ , etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04538))_
- For characters, the score is adventurer level, and for monsters, it’s monster level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04542))_
- When you cast the holy magic _Cure Poison_ on someone who has been poisoned, compare the final score when casting the magic to this toxicity score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04547))_
- If, as an illness progresses, its _depth_ reaches the **fatal depth** , the character will die from the illness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04662))_
- This illness, which turns humans into lycanthropes, is thought to be different from normal illnesses caused by abnormalities in spirit power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04727))_
- However, if the poison itself is cleared away, the spirit power within the body will naturally return to a balanced state and restore health. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04506))_
- On the other hand, illness is a state where the spirit power within the body remains unbalanced and in a fixed state. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04507))_
- Just like in our world, in Alecrast, using poison as a weapon is an immoral act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04514))_

> Context: Just like in our world, in Alecrast, using poison as a weapon is an immoral act. If a player character uses poison to defeat an enemy, they are sure to be treated as a coward and looked down upon by the public. Therefore, a proud warrior does _not_ want to use poison. On the other hand, assassins, evil rune masters, dark priests, etc. _do_ like to use poison.

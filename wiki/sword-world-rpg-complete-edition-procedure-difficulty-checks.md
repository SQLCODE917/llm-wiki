# Difficulty Checks

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Goal

- Complete Difficulty Checks.

## Procedure Steps

1. **Difficulty** (`step`) - evidence section [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-2-5-difficulty-checks-2-5-1-difficulty-b2b0e076]].
   - Difficulty is a score determined by the game master based on the difficulty of the actions the character attempts. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01230))_
   - What makes this different from a target score is that this is not the direct number the player is aiming for. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01230))_
2. **How to Make a Difficulty Check** (`validate`) - evidence section [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-2-5-difficulty-checks-2-5-2-how-to-make-a-difficulty-check-778f9a94]].
   - The game master does not reveal their roll nor the target score that results from their calculation, to the players. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01232))_
   - Whether the base difficulty is revealed or not depends on the situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01232))_
   - To make a difficulty check , the game master also rolls the dice (2D). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01232))_
3. **Analogy of Success and Failure** (`generate`) - evidence section [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-2-5-difficulty-checks-2-5-3-analogy-of-success-and-failure-211ef947]].
   - If a success roll is made for a difficulty check, the success or failure of the action is strictly unknown to the player. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01241))_
   - That is precisely the reason why we use difficulty checks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01241))_
   - However, a player is given only one criteria to determine whether the success roll they made for a difficulty check is a success or a failure. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01242))_

- These checks-- which are often made mainly when finding something --change the method of success rolls considerably. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01227))_

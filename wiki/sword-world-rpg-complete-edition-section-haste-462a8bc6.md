# **[ Haste ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a spell that doubles the target's agility from its original score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01926))_
- Regarding the evasion speed (points) bonus, the effect differs depending on the target's original agility. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01926))_
- Please refer to Table 5-4: Haste: Agility and Evasion Bonuses. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01926))_
- The target's action order and movement speed will be doubled, and their evasion speed (points) will also receive a bonus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01926))_

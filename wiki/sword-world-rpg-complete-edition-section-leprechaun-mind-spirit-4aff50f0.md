From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- At this time, even if an ally casts magic on you in order to help you, you will consider it a _hostile_ act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02351))_
- If you have this spell cast on you and you fail to resist, you'll be unable to understand your current situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02351))_
- As a result, you won't be able to take any assertive action, and will be completely unable to attack or use magic on your opponents. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02351))_
- You can make similar judgments about those who cast magic on you. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02351))_
- Of course, this is not the case if _Confusion_ is removed by that magic's effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02351))_
- You'll also lose your ability to make accurate judgments. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02351))_
- You can also cast _Dispel Magic_ , _Sanity_ , or other magic that belongs to _mind_ on yourself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02352))_

# 《 **Non-removable** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- However, magic of the **non-removable** type _cannot_ be removed by other magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01464))_
- Some magic, such as _Dispel Magic_ (ancient magic) and _Remove Curse_ (holy magic), can be used as counter magic that _removes_ other magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01464))_
- Some magic, such as _Dispel Magic_ (ancient magic) and _Remove Curse_ (holy magic), can be used as counter magic that _removes_ other magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01464))_
- Also, sometimes there is counter magic that counters _certain_ magic, which if specified, will be removed by _that_ counter magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01465))_
- It's also possible to remove _non-removable magic_ by directly using the power of a god (wicked god) with the holy/dark magic _Call God_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01465))_
- Although, the ancient magic _Perfect Cancellation_ **is** able to remove _nonremovable_ magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01465))_

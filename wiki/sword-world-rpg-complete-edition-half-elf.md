A half-elf is one who is born of a human and an elf. Humans and elves are close as species and are able to have children, as explained in the elf section.

Half-elves combine characteristics of both humans and elves. Their ears are slightly pointed, but they also have a spot where a beard grows. They are almost the same height as humans, but are more delicate.

Half-elves have a longer life span than humans due to their elven characteristics, with a life span of around 200 years.

Half-elves vary in their traits depending on whether they were raised by humans or by elves. When raised by elves, they're similar to those of elves. When raised by humans, they're not all that different from humans.

## Changelings

Half-elves are not a single generation of mixed blood, but are free to have offspring. The child will be a full elf, a half-elf, or a human. There is no such thing as inheriting only 1/4 of the characteristics of one or the other.

Even among humans, elves and halfelves can be born from humans who have had elven blood in their past due to intermittent inheritance. These children are called **changelings** and are looked down upon in human society.

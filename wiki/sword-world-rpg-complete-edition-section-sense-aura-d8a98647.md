## Statements

- This ability is used to know what kind of spirit power is at work in your surroundings. You cannot use spells that belong to a specific spirit in places where that spirit power is not at work. When a player declares that they will use this ability, the game master must tell the player what spirit power is at work in that location. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03693))_
- Since there are spirits of life and spirits of undeath (which control negative life force), the sense aura ability can be used to distinguish between living things and undead. Ordinary corpses and monsters created by magic (magical creatures) such as gargoyles do not emit either aura. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03694))_
- Sense aura is a subtle sensation, more like what we can smell than see . Even if you know that spirit power is at work, you won't be able to tell where that spirit power is coming from unless you get very close. Also, if there's extremely strong spirit power in your surroundings, you may not be able to sense weak spirit power at work. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03695))_
- Ordinary corpses and monsters created by magic (magical creatures) such as gargoyles do not emit either aura. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03694))_

## Statements

Your opponent must make a life force (points) resistance roll against a target score of 10 and with _damage dealt to life force (points)_ as a penalty. If he or she fails, they will fall _prone_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07222))_

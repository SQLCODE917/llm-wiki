# 10th Level Basic Holy Magic List [ Call God ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- When you chant this spell, you can summon a major god into your own body. Even gods who have lost contact with the material world will be able to use their full power if summoned with this spell. However, the effect of the spell is temporary, and moreover, humans' frail bodies and minds cannot withstand the great power of a god, so after the spell's duration has expired, the caster's soul is destroyed, and even if you use spells such as Resurrection etc. you will not be able to bring them back to life again. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04613))_

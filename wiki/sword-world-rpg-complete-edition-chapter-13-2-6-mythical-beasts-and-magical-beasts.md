## Chapter 13.2.6: Mythical Beasts and Magical Beasts

This section describes various mythical and magical beasts found in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, including their characteristics, abilities, and behaviors. It covers creatures such as satyrs, ceiling hangers, harpies, hippocampi, medusas, and others, detailing their monster level, rarity, movement speed, attack points, and special abilities.

### Key Concepts

- **Mythical Beasts**: Creatures that have existed long before humans, such as dragons.
- **Magical Beasts**: Artificial creatures created through the experiments of ancient sorcerers.
- **Monster Level**: Corresponds to a character's adventurer level, indicating the monster's general strength.
- **Rarity**: Indicates how well-known the monster is.
- **Special Abilities**: Unique abilities the monster possesses that may affect combat and magic interactions.
- **Habitat**: The environment where the monster is typically found.
- **Perception**: The monster's sensory abilities.
- **Languages**: The languages the monster can speak.

### Key Entities

- **Satyr**: A forest-dwelling race with the upper body of a hairy human, pointed ears, short horns, and the lower body of a goat. They can manipulate human emotions with a flute.
- **Ceiling Hanger**: A magical beast with arms of different sizes, able to move along walls and ceilings. It attacks sleeping prey with a surprise attack and strangles them.
- **Harpy**: A carnivorous creature with the head and chest of a human female, and the rest of its body that of a bird. Includes deela and furia races.
- **Hippocampus**: A horse that lives in the sea, with the lower half of its body that of a fish. It can move quickly underwater but suffers a penalty on land.
- **Medusa**: A magical beast that takes the form of a human woman with dozens of snakes growing out of her head instead of hair. It has the magical power to petrify humans.
- **Androscorpio**: A mythical beast that lives in the desert, with the upper body of a handsome young man and the lower body of a brightly colored scorpion. It can use spirit magic.
- **Centaur**: A meadow-dwelling race that is human from the waist up and horse from the waist down. They are hunters and can use magic.
- **Cockatrice**: A flightless bird about the size of a human with the legs and tail of a lizard. Its beak has the ability to petrify living creatures.
- **Scylla**: A beautiful woman from the waist up, but six serpent heads and 12 octopus tentacles grow from her lower body. It can lure men with only its upper body above the water, then drag them underwater to kill them.
- **Chimera**: A creature with a black lion for the front half of its body and a demonic black goat for the rear half. Its tail is a snake and a goat's head grows out of its back.
- **Twintail Cat**: A mythical beast that looks like a normal black cat but has two tails. It has an intellect greater than humans and can use magic.
- **Telchines**: A creature with the upper body of a human male but his lower body has two large snake tails instead of legs. They live quietly on the coast and have deep knowledge of ancient magic and poisons.
- **Hippogriff**: A flying mythical beast, the front half of its body is an eagle and the back half is a horse. It lives in grasslands and feeds on small herbivores.
- **Pegasus**: A winged white horse that can fly. It lives on steep mountains and comes down to meadows to feed on grass.
- **Head Displacer**: A creature that looks like a severed human head with four vermillion tentacles growing out of its neck. It can devour large numbers of humans without being noticed.
- **Lamia**: A creature with the upper body of a human female and the lower body of a serpent. It can strangle its prey.

### Combat and Magic

- **Monster Level**: Similar to a character's adventurer level, indicates how difficult it is for a monster to die.
- **Special Abilities**: Unique abilities the monster possesses that may affect combat and magic interactions.
- **Petrify**: A special ability that turns a character to stone.
- **Charm**: A special ability that makes a character do what the monster tells them to do.
- **Strangle**: A special ability that can cause a character to suffocate.
- **Poison**: A special ability that can cause a character to take damage over time.
- **Magic**: Magical beasts can use magic, including ancient and spirit magic.

### Experience Points

- **Experience Points**: Used to increase character abilities and skills over time.

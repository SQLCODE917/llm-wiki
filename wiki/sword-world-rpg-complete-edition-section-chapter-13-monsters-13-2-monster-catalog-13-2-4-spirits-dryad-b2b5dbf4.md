Their figure is that of a completely naked elf woman, with long green hair wrapped around her entire body. Their skin is also green. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04856))_
- A mad dryad uses the spirit magic " _Charm_ " to cause division among allies, and tries to block their opponent's movements with " _Binding_ ". Using these spells does not consume any of the dryad’s mental points. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04857))_

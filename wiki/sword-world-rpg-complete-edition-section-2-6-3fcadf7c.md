### Resistance Rolls

- A success roll made using adventurer level + life force bonus as the baseline score is specifically referred to as a life force resistance roll . Similarly, a success roll made using adventurer level + mental power bonus as the baseline score is specifically called a mental power resistance roll , and the two are collectively called simply resistance rolls . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00593))_
- The reason for the name change and the distinction between the two in particular, is that they are frequently performed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00594))_
- Therefore, these baseline scores are also defined by the terms life force resistance and mental power resistance , among others. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00595))_
- Life force resistance rolls are made to avoid or reduce the effects of physical dangers such as poison, while mental power resistance rolls are made to avoid or reduce the effects of magic or curses. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00596))_
- In particular, all checks against magic, even if it causes physical damage, are made with mental power resistance rolls. Checks against special attacks by monsters that are magical in nature are also made with mental power resistance rolls. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00597))_

### Example 1:

- Since Ducard II has an adventurer level of 2 and a +3 life force bonus, the baseline score of his life force resistance roll (life force resistance power) is 5 . During the adventure, he is attacked and wounded by a ghoul. Paralyzing poison is a special ability of ghouls. The game master asks us to make a life force resistance roll to see if Ducard II is poisoned by the ghoul. The toxicity score of ghoul poison is 10, which is the target score. Our 2D roll is 5, so with 5+5= 10 , he escapes paralysis, if barely. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00599))_

### Example 2:

- Since Ducard II has an adventurer level of 2 and a +2 mental power bonus, the baseline score of his mental power resistance roll (mental power resistance power) is 4 . While touring an underground cave on an adventure, he wanders into a mysterious room where all four walls are painted in extreme colors. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00601))_
- In fact, there is a magic trap set in this room that will put anyone who enters it into a deep sleep. The richly colored patterns in the room begin to move strangely, and Ducard II is struck by a strong sense of drowsiness. The game master asks us to make a mental power resistance roll against target score 17. Normally, this would never succeed, but our 2D roll is 12 ! _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00602))_

### Ability Scores

- When a character with adventurer level 0 (a character with no adventurer skills) makes these checks, the baseline score is 0 regardless of their ability scores. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00606))_

### Filling Out the Character Sheet

- The two resistance rolls are frequently performed. Therefore, it' s advisable to make a note of the baseline score ( resistance ) so that it is readily available. The Character Sheet provides a field for this purpose. Look at the life force and mental power fields under ability scores. To the right of each of these is a field to write down the bonus, and further to the right of that should be a field labeled resistance . Write the baseline scores of each resistance roll here . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00609))_

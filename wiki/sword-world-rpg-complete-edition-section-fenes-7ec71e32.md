From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- During the war of the gods, it is said that he did not join the forces of darkness _nor_ the forces of light led by his older brother Pharis, choosing instead to take the position of king of the _neutral_ gods. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02686))_
- Belief in him is not popular in Alecrast, and he has almost no temples. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02686))_
- It is sometimes said that he is the true form of the _Nameless God of Madness_ (a wicked god, explained later), and he is also said to be the god of beastmen such as werewolves. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02686))_

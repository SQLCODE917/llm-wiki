Fae are demi-human races from the fairy world, generally friendly towards humans but not actively seeking connection. They include pixies, fairies, pookas, and featherfolk.

## Characteristics

Fae are a group of races that originally lived in the fairy world. They are generally friendly towards humans, but do not actively interact with them and rarely appear in public. They have a close relationship with small spirits called sprites and possess abilities related to spirit magic.

## Key Entities

- **Pixie**: A small, friendly fae creature with insect-like wings, about 30 cm tall. They are known for playing pranks on humans. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Fairy**: A representative race of fae, often seen where the fairy world and material world meet. They appear as beautiful elf women with thin but strong wings. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Pooka**: A member of the fairy family that lives in the forest, resembling a grassrunner but smarter. They can shapeshift into forest animals. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Featherfolk**: A race that lives in rugged mountainous areas, resembling humans with bird wings growing from their shoulders. They have gentle personalities and use spirit magic up to 4th level. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)

## Key Concepts

- **Spirit Magic**: Fae have a deep connection with spirits and can use spirit magic, with levels ranging from 2nd to 4th level depending on the creature. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Invisibility**: Pixies possess the ability to use invisibility, which they can maintain without consuming mental points, but requires focus. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Shapeshifting**: Pookas can transform into forest animals, such as foxes, fawns, rabbits, and squirrels, without detection. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Fairy World**: The realm where many fae live, often connected to the material world through specific places. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)

## Source record

This section details how to acquire and increase levels for rune master skills, including sorcerer, shaman, priest, dark priest, and dragon priest skills. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.154-155)

## Key supported claims

- Sorcerer skill requires joining guild or apprenticing to 6th level sorcerer. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.153-153)
- Sorcerer training requires 2000 experience points. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.153-153)
- Shaman skill requires teaching from 1st level shaman. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.153-153)

### `technical-atom-5e41968cfeea9a4e` procedure

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.153-155)

If a final score of 20 or greater _is_ attained, then after the caster's death, their corpse will be wrapped in a dragon egg, and a few years later, will be reborn as a lesser dragon.

### `technical-atom-da6eace0f512c872` requirement

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.153-155)

At this time, the caster must make a success roll to determine a final score.

### `technical-atom-2e0d48e1299711fc` exception

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.153-155)

Although greater than a wyvern, a lesser dragon's intellect is also not very high, so it can only be used to fight enemies or to carry you on its back.

### `technical-atom-da1b0cf760f57789` exception

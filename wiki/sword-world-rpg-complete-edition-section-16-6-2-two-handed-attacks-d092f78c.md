## Statements

- If a character has weapons in both hands, he or she has three options for attacking. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11548))_
- Attack with both hands at the same time = -2 penalty to attack with dominant hand, -4 penalty to attack with opposite hand. Also, the required strength for one of the weapons is limited to half your own strength (rounded up) . If you're fighting with the thief skill, the required strength for both weapons is only half the character's strength. There's no need to reduce one to a quarter. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11551))_
- When a character is ambidextrous, there is no opposite hand. If you attack with only one hand, there is no penalty, and if you attack with both hands, the penalty is only -2 for each. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11552))_

### 16.6.2 Two-Handed Attacks / Throwing Multiple Darts or Daggers

- The number thrown at the same time becomes a penalty to attack power. For example, if you throw three at the same time, that'll incur a -3 penalty to your attack power for each hit check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11555))_
- This optional rule increases the chance that a high-level character will defeat a low-level character or monster in one blow. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11556))_

### 16.6.2 Two-Handed Attacks / Weapons and Armor That Are Too Heavy

- A Character can also use weapons and armor whose required strength exceeds his or her own strength. However, at this time, you'll suffer a penalty to your attack power or evasion speed . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11561))_
- For each point of required strength, reduce attack power by -1. If one in one hand is too heavy, you will receive a penalty, but there will be no stacking. For example, if your sword is 3 too heavy in required strength and your shield is 2 too heavy in required strength, your penalty is -3 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11563))_
- For each point of required strength, reduce evasion speed by 1. At the game master's decision, any success roll that uses agility bonus as a baseline score may be subject to an equal penalty _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11565))_
- For example, if your sword is 3 too heavy in required strength and your shield is 2 too heavy in required strength, your penalty is -3 . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11563))_

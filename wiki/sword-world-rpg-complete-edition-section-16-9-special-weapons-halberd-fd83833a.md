# 16.9 Special Weapons / Halberd

## Statements

- The halberd is an advanced version of the pole ax, and is shaped like a combination of an ax and a spear, with the other side of the ax also shaped like a hook. It’s the most sophisticated pole weapon, and can be used to _thrust_ , _cut_ , _stab_ , and _sweep_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07279))_
- It has an ax-like blade. Treat its scores as _ax/2H_ . Enables _mow down_ attacks. **Stab** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07280))_
- It has a tip like a pickax. Its scores and handling are based on _warhammer/2H_ . **Beat** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07281))_
- It has a hammer-like tip. Please use the same scores and handling as _flail/2H_ . It _cannot_ be used to declare _mercy_ . **Sweep** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07282))_

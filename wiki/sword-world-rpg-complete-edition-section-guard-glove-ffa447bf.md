# **[ Guard Glove ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- When the wearer grasps tightly while holding nothing in their hand, a force field is generated around his or her arm, which has the same effect as holding a shield. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06855))_
- Also, if you’re holding something in your dominant hand, you _cannot_ use spirit magic either. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06855))_
- You _cannot_ use ancient magic while one hand is clenched. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06855))_

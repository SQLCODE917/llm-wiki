## Statements

- _Hard Lock_ is a spell that's more powerful than _Lock_ . You can place a magical lock on an object that opens and closes, such as doors, gates, treasure chest lids, etc. to prevent them from opening. Once cast, this spell's effect is permanent until it's removed. Also, no matter what material it's made of, this gives it the same strength as iron. In other words, opening a door or lid with _Hard Lock_ requires the same effort as destroying an iron wall or an iron box. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01674))_

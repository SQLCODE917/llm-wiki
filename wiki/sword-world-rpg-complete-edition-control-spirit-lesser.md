# **[ Control Spirit ] (Various lesser spirits)**

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about **[ control spirit ] (various lesser spirits)**:

## Statements

- Area=One lesser spirit Duration=Permanent Effect=Summon and control one type of spirit Type=Target score Counter (Overpowers): _Full Control Spirit_ Countered (Overpowered) By: _Full Control Spirit_ Expansion=Final score, distance Resist=None _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02305))_
- The only spirits you can summon are those that belong to the lesser spirits (monster level 4 or lower). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02306))_
- Spirits that have been successfully summoned and controlled can be sealed in items, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02307))_
- The biggest advantage of controlling spirits is that you can cast spirit magic that belongs to them, even in places where spirit power would not normally be at work. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02308))_
- The main spirits that can be controlled are gnome (earth spirits), undine (water spirits), salamander (fire spirits), sylph (wind spirits), brownie (building spirits), leprechaun (mind spirits), sprite (mind spirits), etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02309))_
- Counters (Overpowers): _Control Spirit_ Countered (Overpowered) By: _Control Spirit_ Expansion=Final score, distance Resist=None _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02559))_
- This is a spell that summons a spirit and lets you control it as your servant (you're free to call it your friend). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02306))_
- Of course, it can only be summoned in a place where spirit power is at work, and summoning also requires a three-hour magical ritual. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02306))_
- A shaman can only control one kind of spirit at a time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02307))_
- It is up to the game master's discretion to decide what kind of item the spirit will be sealed in (for example, an undine in a water bag, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02307))_
- A controlled spirit can communicate with its caster using silent spirit, and will carry out simple commands, but cannot do much. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02307))_
- It’s always possible to release a controlled spirit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02308))_
- A controlled spirit can only be used by its controller. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02308))_
- It cannot attack others directly. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02307))_

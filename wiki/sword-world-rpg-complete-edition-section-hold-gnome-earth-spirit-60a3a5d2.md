## Statements

- This spell causes a gnome to rise from the earth and wrap itself around the lower body of a target that fails to resist, sealing their movement. As a result, the target will be unable to move and must take a -2 penalty to attack power (points) and evasion speed (points). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02430))_

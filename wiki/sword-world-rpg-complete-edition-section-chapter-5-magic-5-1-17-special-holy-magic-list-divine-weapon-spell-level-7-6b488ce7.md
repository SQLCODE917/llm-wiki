## Statements

- When you cast this spell, a vaguely glowing weapon-like force field appears in the caster's hand. The shape depends on the caster's preference. There is no required strength, and its strike power is treated as 30 (if the caster uses it as a twohanded weapon, its strike power is _35_ ). This spell has no effect if the caster already has something else in their hand. Also, it's not possible to give this _weapon_ to someone else. This _weapon_ can also take effect against monsters that can only be wounded by silver or magic weapons. When you create a bow, etc. with this spell, arrows are automatically generated as well. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02969))_

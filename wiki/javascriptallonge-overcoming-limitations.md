## Source record

Chapter on overcoming limitations in JavaScript, including left-variadic functions and the leftVariadic decorator.

## Key supported claims

- Left-variadic functions have one or more fixed arguments, with the rest gathered into the leftmost argument. (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.91-92)
- The leftVariadic function is a decorator turning functions into left-variadic ones, gathering parameters from the left instead of the right. (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.91-92)
- A left-variadic function cannot be written directly in JavaScript; a decorator is needed to achieve this behavior. (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.91-92)

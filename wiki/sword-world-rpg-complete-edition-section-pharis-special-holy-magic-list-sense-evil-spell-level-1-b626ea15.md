# **Pharis Special Holy Magic List [ Sense Evil ] (Spell Level=1)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- _Sense Evil_ is a spell that allows you to see through those who harbor (so-called evil) feelings that would destroy the justice and order taught by Pharis. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03075))_
- Think of this spell _not_ as constantly detecting people called _villains_ , but only the _moment_ when someone is thinking _evil thoughts_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03075))_
- In other words, this spell works on the _evil intent_ of those that have minds, but is _not_ a spell that detects _people_ who commonly have evil intent (these kinds of people are usually called villains). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03075))_
- Think of this spell _not_ as constantly detecting people called _villains_ , but only the _moment_ when someone is thinking _evil thoughts_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03075))_
- This is a spell that erases unnatural beings belonging to spirits, undead, daemons, and magical creatures from the material world. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03081))_
- However, creatures summoned by _summon_ dragon roar magic can be targeted by this spell _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03084))_
- This spell has no effect on humans, fae, youma, titans, mythical beasts, magical beasts, animals, titan creatures, plants, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03084))_

## Statements

- Marfa, as the earth mother and the goddess of affection, preaches strong warnings against unnecessary conflict. Therefore, priests of Marfa can use this spell to take away the feelings of hatred that have arisen in each other's hearts and prevent them from fighting. Those who fail to resist will lose their hostility and attempt to stop the fight. At this point, if the opponent attempts to take hostile action again, this becomes a natural battle for Marfa, so you can counterattack. This spell has no effect on those without a mind, or when it is natural for them to attack another due to hunger, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03356))_

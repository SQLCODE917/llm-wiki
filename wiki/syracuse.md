Syracuse was an ancient Greek city-state in Sicily, known as the birthplace of Archimedes and a hub for engineering and mathematics. The Antikythera mechanism may have originated in Corinth or its colonies, possibly linking it to the school of Archimedes in Syracuse. [[antikythera-mechanism]] [[archimedes]]

## Source record

This section details special holy magic spells that can only be used by those who believe in a specific god among characters with the priest skill. The spells are categorized by deity and include their properties, effects, and limitations.

## Key supported claims

- Reincarnation allows a soul to be reborn into a new body, with memories regained based on a final score (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.105-107).
- Sense Evil detects evil thoughts in an opponent's mind, focusing on evil intent rather than individuals (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.105-107).
- Banish removes unnatural beings from the material world, with no effect on humans, fae, youma, titans, mythical beasts, magical beasts, animals, titan creatures, plants, etc. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.105-107).
- Jihad grants great fighting ability to believers of Pharis, with effects including temporary increase in fighter skill level and absolute obedience to the caster's commands (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.105-107).
- Luck allows one failed success roll to be turned into a success, limited to defensive actions such as evasion rolls, resistance rolls, death checks, and other defensive success rolls (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.105-107).

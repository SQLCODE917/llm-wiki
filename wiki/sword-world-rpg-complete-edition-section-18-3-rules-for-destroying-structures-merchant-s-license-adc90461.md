## Statements

- While merchants don't have a guild, to be recognized as a merchant and conduct business, they must register with the king. Those who are approved receive a permit. This is the Merchant's License. Without it, one cannot legally open a shop. If someone conducts business in public without a license, they will be punished. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11926))_

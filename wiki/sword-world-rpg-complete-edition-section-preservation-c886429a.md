# [ Preservation ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a spell that protects objects that would normally rot from decay. Objects that have this spell cast on them will no longer decay naturally. It can be used to preserve food, and it is also effective in protecting corpses that are planned to be brought back to life in the future with Resurrection . It is also a counter spell to the dark magic Rot , and if cast within the duration of Rot , it can restore things that have decayed back to their original state. However, even if you cast it on something that has completely decayed after the duration of Rot has expired, or something that has naturally decayed, it will not return to its original state. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04501))_
- If the effect of this spell is removed, such as with Perfect Cancellation , etc. the decay will begin to progress at normal rate from that moment on. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04502))_

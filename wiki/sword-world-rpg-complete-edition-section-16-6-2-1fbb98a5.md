### Two-Handed Attacks

- If a character has weapons in both hands, he or she has three options for attacking. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07243))_

### Attack with both hands at the same

- **time** = _-2_ penalty to attack with _dominant_ hand, _-4_ penalty to attack with _opposite_ hand. Also, the required strength for _one_ of the weapons is limited to _half your own strength (rounded up)_ . If you’re fighting with the thief skill, the required strength for _both_ weapons is only _half_ the character's strength. There’s _no_ need to reduce one to a quarter. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07246))_
- When a character is ambidextrous, there is _no_ opposite hand. If you attack with only _one_ hand, there is _no_ penalty, and if you attack with _both_ hands, the penalty is only _-2_ for each. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07247))_

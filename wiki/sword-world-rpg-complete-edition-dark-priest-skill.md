## **Dark Priest Skill**

The method of acquiring and increasing the dark priest skill is almost the same as the priest skill. However, many of the dark gods that dark priests believe in have no temples, and most dark priests will be those who have _awakened to faith_ by some occurrence. Many such awakenings could be things like a longtime believer of a god of light being betrayed by their god. There are also cases such as a friend who was a priest of Faris who, in order to maintain order, turned in his own brother, who was plotting a rebellion, and after grief about his actions, awoke to the teachings of _The Nameless God of Madness_ (though usually you'd just say _they went mad_ about things like this...) 

## Acquisition

The dark priest skill is acquired through awakening to faith, often as a result of a traumatic experience or betrayal that leads to a rejection of one's former beliefs. This is unlike the priest skill, which typically requires joining a temple or religious organization.

## Abilities

Dark priests have access to dark magic, which is distinguished from holy magic used by regular priests. The dark magic is considered anti-social and dangerous, and the followers of wicked gods are generally abhorred by society. Dark priests cannot worship multiple gods, and they must choose one wicked god to follow.

## Restrictions

- Dark priests cannot join adventurer parties due to their religious beliefs.
- The skill is incompatible with the priest skill and dragon priest skill.
- Game masters should not allow player characters to become priests of wicked gods, as it can lead to destruction of the character and party.
- Dark priests cannot worship multiple gods.

## Comparison with Other Skills

The dark priest skill is distinct from other rune master skills such as the sorcerer skill, shaman skill, and priest skill. While it shares some commonalities with the priest skill in terms of magical ability, it is more specialized in its focus on dark magic and wicked gods.

## Key Concepts

- **Awakening to Faith**: The process by which dark priests gain their skill, often through betrayal or trauma.
- **Wicked Gods**: The dark gods that dark priests worship, which are anti-social and dangerous.
- **Incompatibility**: The dark priest skill cannot be combined with the priest skill or dragon priest skill.

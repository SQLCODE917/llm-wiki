# **(Undine/Water Spirit)**

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about **(undine/water spirit)**:

## Statements

- A wall made of water can't be destroyed by physical means or even magic that deals damage, as it quickly compensates for any damaged areas. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02472))_
- Undine is waiting inside the water wall. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02472))_
- Water walls do not obstruct your gaze, but projectiles cannot pass through them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02472))_
- Area=One, or a boat-sized object Duration=1 hour Effect=Eliminates the buoyancy of water, causing floating people and objects to sink Type=Counters (Negates): _Water Walking_ Countered (Negated) By: _Water Walking_ Countered (Overpowered) By: _Create Ghost Ship_ _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02355))_
- A wall of fire cannot be destroyed by physical means or magic that deals damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02470))_
- Those who've had this spell cast on them lose their liquid buoyancy and sink completely to the bottom. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02357))_
- If you cast this spell on something under the influence of _Water Walking_ , their effects will negate each other, removing the effects of both spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02357))_
- Counters (Negates): _Spirit Wall: Salamander_ Countered (Negated) By: _Spirit Wall: Salamander_ _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02469))_
- Those inside the wall will automatically drown after a number of rounds equal to their life force (points) have elapsed, unless rescued from the outside. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02472))_
- You cannot escape on your own. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02472))_
- Anything that attempts to touch or pass through the wall must attempt to resist. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02472))_
- Moreover, any rescuers must also be able to get inside the wall in order to save those that have been swallowed up by it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02472))_

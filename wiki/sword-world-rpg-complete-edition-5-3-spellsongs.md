## Source record

This section describes spellsongs, magical songs usable by bards in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition. It outlines the mechanics, limitations, and specific effects of various spellsongs.

## Key supported claims

- A spellsong is a song with magical power created during the ancient kingdom period, usable by bards through their bard skill (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.109-111).
- Using spellsongs does not consume a bard's mental power, and the effect cannot be expanded (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.109-111).
- Spellsongs have specific effects, such as waking up sleeping creatures, arousing curiosity, summoning animals, and influencing actions (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.109-111).
- Bards cannot sing spellsongs for long periods; once interrupted, the effect disappears, and they cannot take other actions while singing (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.109-111).
- To use a spellsong, a bard must play a unique instrument and sing lyrics in High Ancient (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.109-111).

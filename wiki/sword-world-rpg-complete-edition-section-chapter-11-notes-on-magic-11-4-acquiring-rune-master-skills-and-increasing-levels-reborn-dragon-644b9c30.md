## Statements

- The ultimate dragon roar magic spell. It is no exaggeration to say that dragon priests undergo rigorous training for this spell. Upon using this spell, death will come to the dragon priest. At this time, the caster must make a success roll to determine a final score. If this final score does _not_ reach 20, the caster will _die_ as they are. They _cannot_ be revived by any means. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04117))_
- If a final score of 20 or greater _is_ attained, then after the caster's death, their corpse will be wrapped in a dragon egg, and a few years later, will be reborn as a lesser dragon. The egg _cannot_ be destroyed by any means. Even _Perfect Cancellation_ is ineffective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04118))_
- When you’re reborn, your memories of your previous life will be lost, but your potential as a dragon is high, and after several decades you will shed your skin and transform into an elder dragon. At this time, along with the memories from when you became a dragon, the memories and skills you had during your lifetime will be resurrected. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04119))_
- They _cannot_ be revived by any means. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04117))_

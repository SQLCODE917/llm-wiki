## Source record

Chapter 11.2 Dark Magic (part 2) from Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering special dark magic spells created by wicked gods, including Phalaris, The Nameless God of Madness, Kardis, and Mirleaf.

## Key supported claims

- Priests of wicked gods use basic holy magic, dark magic, and god-specific spells (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148); Each wicked god creates unique dark magic spells for their priests (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148).
- Spells affect the target's mind and behavior, including *Evil Impulse* and *Insanity* (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148); *Berserk* spell causes targets to destroy property and attack indiscriminately (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148).
- *Death* spell from Kardis instantly kills targets (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148).
- *Create Ghost Ship* spell turns ships into ghost ships (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148).
- Spell effects can be amplified by the caster's mental power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.142-148).

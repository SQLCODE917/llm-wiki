## Statements

- The four other ability scores of dexterity, agility, intelligence, and strength are rarely reduced, but if this does happen and these ability scores _do_ fall to 0 or less, the adventurer will still become disabled. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00168))_
- An ability score value is related to the success or failure of an action that an adventurer tries to perform, but in _Sword World RPG_ , success or failure is determined by a **bonus** derived from the ability score, rather than the ability score itself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00177))_
- The result of each ability score divided by 6 (rounded down) will be the bonus for that ability score (Table 1-1: Ability Bonuses). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00177))_
- Once sub-ability scores are determined, ability scores are calculated by addition. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00499))_
- Ability scores apply not only to humans, but also to the various non-human races that become adventurers. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00159))_
- There are six types of ability scores: **dexterity** , **agility** , **intelligence** , **strength** , **life force** , and **mental power** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00159))_
- In Sword World RPG, these basic characteristics of an adventurer are represented by numbers called **ability scores** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00159))_
- It also represents the ability to perceive things, such as finding hidden objects and sensing abnormalities spur of the moment. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00172))_
- However, temporary reductions do _not_ affect modifiers for only two scores: life force and mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00177))_
- Each of these numbers is referred to as a _dexterity bonus_ , _intelligence bonus_ , etc., using the name of the ability score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00177))_

- Agility represents an adventurer's physical balance and agility. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00170))_
- Intelligence represents the speed of an adventurer's mind and the amount of knowledge they hold, or in other words, their so-called smarts. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00172))_
- Above all, it is important when using magic, a major attraction in fantasy. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00172))_
- If life force and mental power are reduced by wounds or exhaustion, they will recover once the wounds are treated or the exhaustion is wiped away. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00173))_

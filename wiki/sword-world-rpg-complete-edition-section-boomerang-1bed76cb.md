# **Boomerang**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- When this weapon is thrown at an enemy and misses, it **returns to the user's hand** at the _end_ of the round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07961))_
- However, it will _not_ return if you roll _double ones_ on your hit check or if the boomerang hits the enemy. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07961))_

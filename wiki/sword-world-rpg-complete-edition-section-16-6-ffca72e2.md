### Holding Weapons in Both Hands

- In the basic rules, characters cannot hold separate weapons in each hand. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07237))_
- This section presents extended rules for **holding weapons in both hands** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07237))_
- However, there are many people who admire dual wielding. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07237))_

### 16.6.1 Character's Dominant Hand

- Characters are generally righthanded or left-handed, but in actuality there are also people who are _ambidextrous_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07239))_
- Left-handed or right-handedness is decided by the player's preference. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07240))_

### Two-Handed Attacks

- If a character has weapons in both hands, he or she has three options for attacking. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07243))_

### Attack with both hands at the same

- There’s _no_ need to reduce one to a quarter. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07246))_
- Also, the required strength for _one_ of the weapons is limited to _half your own strength (rounded up)_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07246))_
- Also, the required strength for _one_ of the weapons is limited to _half your own strength (rounded up)_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07246))_
- When a character is ambidextrous, there is _no_ opposite hand. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07247))_
- If you attack with only _one_ hand, there is _no_ penalty, and if you attack with _both_ hands, the penalty is only _-2_ for each. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07247))_
- If you attack with only _one_ hand, there is _no_ penalty, and if you attack with _both_ hands, the penalty is only _-2_ for each. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07247))_

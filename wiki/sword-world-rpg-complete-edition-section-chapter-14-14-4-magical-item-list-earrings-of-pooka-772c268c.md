## Statements

- When wearing these earrings and focusing your thoughts, you can transform into small animals such as mice or birds. After shapeshifting, you can return to your original form by either touching the earrings while concentrating, or by using Dispel Magic (target score 15). Additionally, after shapeshifting, you retain the same intelligence you had before the transformation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-10651))_

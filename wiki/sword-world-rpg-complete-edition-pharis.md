# Pharis

Pharis is the supreme god in the Sword World RPG, ruling the sun and one of the six major gods. Pharis is associated with the sun, and some say that the sun is the lost body of Pharis himself. Pharis is the god of the light side and is the ruler of the material world. He is also the god of justice and wisdom.

Pharis is one of the six major gods who were born from the death of the Titan, the Creator of All Things. His right hand gave birth to the war god Myrii, while his left hand gave birth to the fortune god Cha Za. Pharis is also associated with the holy magic used by priests in the game.

Pharis is the central figure in the "Holy Kingdom" of Anoth, where he is the state religion. The Pope, who serves as both king and the authority to appoint priests in Anoth, is the highest religious authority of Pharis. The Pharis temple in the royal capital of Farz is said to be the largest on the continent of Alecrast.

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The Sword World RPG World Guide , published by Fujimi Shobo, is a comprehensive introduction to the continent of Alecrast, the background setting of the game. It is very useful when creating your own scenarios, and for those who are mainly players, it is also effective to have a general outline of the world. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00134))_

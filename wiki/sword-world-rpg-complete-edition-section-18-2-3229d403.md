### Guidelines for Adventurer Age and Ability Score Changes

- In the case of non-human races, they are considered equal to humans until they reach adulthood. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11731))_

### Elves

- Aging is not considered. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11734))_

### Dwarves, Grassrunners, Half-Elves

- Aging begins at the age of 100. Divide the age by half and apply it to the human table. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11736))_

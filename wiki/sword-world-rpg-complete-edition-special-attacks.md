In the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, special attacks refer to unique combat abilities that monsters possess, which go beyond standard physical attacks. These attacks often have specific effects that require resistance checks from characters, typically involving mental power or life force resistance.

Special attacks are categorized in Chapter 13.6 of the rulebook, with different types requiring different resistance rolls:

1. **Attacks requiring mental power resistance rolls** - These include abilities like magic, hypnotism, gaze/ray attacks, possession, curses, screams, and roars. Characters must make a mental power resistance roll against a specified target score to avoid the effect.

2. **Attacks requiring life force resistance rolls** - These include poison and parasitism. Characters must make a life force resistance roll against a specified target score to avoid the effect.

3. **Other special attacks** - These include disease, strangling, capture, entangling, swallowing whole, bloodsucking, acid, corrosion, stealing mental power, and iron corrosion. These attacks often have unique mechanics and may require specific resistance checks or actions to escape.

Understanding these special attacks is crucial for both players and game masters to properly handle monster encounters and their various abilities.

## Statements

- This is the ability to follow an opponent without them noticing. The side that's following must use _thief skill level + agility bonus_ as their baseline score, while the side being followed must use _adventurer level + intelligence bonus_ , and success or failure is determined by comparing the final scores of their success rolls. A tie is a failure to follow. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03195))_
- If you fail, your opponent will realize that they are being followed. What action the person takes after that is up to them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03196))_

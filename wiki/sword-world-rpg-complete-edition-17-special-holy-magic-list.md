# 5.1.17 Special Holy Magic List

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about 5.1.17 special holy magic list:

## Statements

- This is a spell that erases unnatural beings belonging to spirits, undead, daemons, and magical creatures from the material world. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03081))_
- However, creatures summoned by _summon_ dragon roar magic can be targeted by this spell _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03084))_
- This spell has no effect on humans, fae, youma, titans, mythical beasts, magical beasts, animals, titan creatures, plants, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03084))_
- Priests of Myrii can receive Myrii's assistance by offering a song in holy prayer to Myrii. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03103))_
- This _weapon_ can also take effect against monsters that can only be wounded by silver or magic weapons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03112))_
- Note that the critical target can only be lowered for _weapon_ attacks, while the critical target of damage magic _cannot_ be lowered. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03125))_
- Resistance rolls against magic, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03142))_
- In particular, it can _never_ be used on checks for hitting others with attacks or checks for casting magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03145))_
- A _defensive_ success roll should be judged by whether it means you _luckily survived_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03145))_
- The spells listed here can only be used by those who believe in a specific god among characters with the priest skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03070))_
- When they remember their previous personality depends on the final score from when the spell was cast (see Table 5-8: Reincarnation Memory). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03064))_
- The soul of the target of this spell will be reborn as a baby in another location within one week. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03064))_
- At that stage they will be able to use all their previous memories and skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03064))_
- This spell is cast immediately after an individual's death. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03064))_

## Source record

A section from JavaScript Allongé discussing coffee preparation methods, particularly Café Allongé or Espresso Lungo.

## Key supported claims

- Café Allongé is a drink midway between Espresso and Americano in strength (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.6-6).
- Café Allongé can be made by adding hot water to Espresso Ristretto (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.6-6).
- Café Allongé can also be made by pulling an extra long double shot of Espresso (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.6-6).

# ⬤ **Earth Spirits**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Particularly strong spirit power is at work in the mountains. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02252))_
- Since the power of water spirits is strong on the ocean floor and the bottoms of lakes, the spirit power of earth does not work there. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02252))_
- This is a power that’s at work in places where the ground is exposed or underground. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02252))_
- Likewise, this spirit power does not work on the floors of buildings (whether they're stone or wood). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02252))_

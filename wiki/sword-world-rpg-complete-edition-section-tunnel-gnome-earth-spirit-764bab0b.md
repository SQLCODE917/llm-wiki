# [ Tunnel ] (Gnome/Earth Spirit)

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-5-1-14-55816c7f]] - broader source section: 5.1.14

## Statements

- This is a spell that creates a hole with a 1 meter radius in walls and ground made of stone or earth, as well as the sides and ceilings of caves. This spell allows you to create holes up to 10 meters in length. However, if the wall is less than 10 meters thick, the spell's effect ends there. In other words, you can't open holes in two walls with one spell. The effect of this spell can be expanded regarding the size of the hole being made (target volume). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03961))_
- Also, this spell has absolutely no effect on walls made of wood. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03962))_
- If this spell is used at the feet of a human, etc. those present can attempt to resist. If the resist is successful, the hole will open, but those on top will be able to immediately jump out of the way and prevent themselves from falling into the hole (assuming they have a place to escape, of course). If the resist fails, the hole will open slowly and they will not suffer damage from the fall. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03963))_
- Once the duration has expired, the hole will slowly return to its original state. At this time, those at the bottom of the hole will not be buried alive. This is because the bottom of the hole will rise back up. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03964))_
- This is because the bottom of the hole will rise back up. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-03964))_

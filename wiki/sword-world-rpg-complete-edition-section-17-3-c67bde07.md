# 17.3

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Basically, a mage's staff is used as a magic catalyst, but if the game master approves, you may use another object as a magic catalyst. Commonly used ones include **mage’s wands** , **mage’s rings** , **mage’s medals** , etc. In any case, when chanting a spell, you must hold the magic catalyst in at least one hand, and cannot hold anything else in that hand at the same time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07375))_
- Weapons other than staves (swords are preferred) can also be catalysts, and some sorcerers who call themselves _magic warriors_ use them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07376))_

A wraith is a being whose ethereal body was separated from its physical body by the ancient magic spell _Wraith Form_ and became undead because it did not return within the spell's duration. It resembles a specter, with a vaguely transparent image of when it was alive. The scores given here are for an average wraith, and can vary considerably depending on its previous knowledge, mental power, sorcerer skill level, etc.

## Characteristics

- **Level**: 8
- **Rarity**: 14
- **Agility**: 24
- **Movement Speed**: 24 (air)
- **Number**: One
- **Frequency**: Very Rare
- **Intellect**: High
- **Reaction**: Neutral
- **Attack Points**: None
- **Strike Points**: None
- **Evasion Points**: None
- **Defense Points**: None
- **Life Points/Resistance**: None
- **Mental Points/Resistance**: 16/16 (9)
- **Special Abilities**: 8th level ancient magic, Retains previous skills, Physical attack immunity, Sleepless, Instantly killed by sunlight
- **Habitat**: Ruins
- **Languages**: Those previous (always includes low ancient)
- **Perception**: Pseudo

## Behavior

Wraiths are ethereal beings without physical bodies. Attacks using weapons or damaging spells have no effect on them. Although they have lost all of their physical abilities, their knowledge-related abilities and rune master skills are still effective, and they can use magic. However, holy magic cannot be used (dark magic is allowed). Unlike specters, wraiths do not have the ability to _possess_.

## Weaknesses

If a wraith's mental points become 0 due to a _Mental Attack_, etc., the wraith will vanish. If it's directly exposed to sunlight, it will also disappear without even attempting to resist.

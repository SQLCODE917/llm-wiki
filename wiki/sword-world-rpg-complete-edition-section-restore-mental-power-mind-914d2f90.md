# **Restore Mental Power (Mind)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Every time you listen to this spell for one hour, you can recover 1 point of mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03119))_
- If you interrupt the spellsong midway through, there will be no effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03119))_
- Those who wish to take part in the effect of this spellsong must remain completely relaxed for the duration of the performance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03119))_

## 5.1.12 Ancient Magic List (part 4)

This section details 4th level ancient magic spells.

### **[ Petrify ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=36

Distance=Caster

Area=Caster

Duration=18 rounds

Effect=Turns target to stone

Type=Target score

Counters (Overpowers): _Curses_ cast on the caster him or herself, all magic except _non-removable_

Expansion=Final score, duration

Resist=None

This spell instantly turns the target to stone. Only creatures with natural life (humans, plants, animals, mythical beasts, magical beasts, fae, youma, and daemons) can be targets of this spell, with the exception of magical creatures such as spirits, undead, and golems. A petrified target will cease all life activity until they're released, so they will not age. If it's removed with _Dispel Magic_ , etc., they will be revived in the same state they were before being petrified. However, if a petrified statue is destroyed in the meantime, it will be revived while broken apart at the time the spell is removed, so it will naturally die instantly (or suffer wounds or irreversible injury). 

### **[ Rune Shield ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=36

Distance=Caster

Area=Caster

Duration=18 rounds

Effect=Create a barrier that completely protects against magic

Type=Target score

Counters (Overpowers): _Curses_ cast on the caster him or herself, all magic except _non-removable_

Expansion=Final score, duration

Resist=None

This is a spell that creates a magical barrier that glows white around the caster. This barrier has the effect of completely blocking magic from others. You can attempt to remove it with _Dispel Magic_ , but all other possible spells will be blocked by this barrier, and cannot have any effect. However, it cannot stop attacks other than magic. During the duration of this spell, the caster can still use other spells, but any spells that also affect the caster will be blocked by the _Rune Shield_ and nullified just the same. Any spell effects that were applied to the caster will also be lost.

### **10th Level Ancient Magic List**

### **[ Dimension Gate ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=50

Distance=10 meters

Area=A plane with a 25 meter radius

Duration=18 rounds

Effect=Create a dimension gate that connects distant places

Type=Target score, lost

Expansion=Final score

Resist=None

This spell creates a gate between two distant places, allowing you to instantly move to a destination. The gate is a silver disc 5 meters in diameter, which appears on the floor. A disc of the same shape will also appear above the destination. The disc can be penetrated like water, and those who jump into it will jump out of the other disc.

There is no restriction on the travel distance, but the destination must be a place the caster knows. If the caster knows about the underworld, it's also possible to open a gate to the underworld.

A normal _Dimension Gate_ lasts for only 3 minutes, but the sorcerers of the ancient kingdom knew the technique for fixing gates semi-permanently, and so used them as a way of moving between distant places or within labyrinths.

### **[ Death Cloud ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=50

Distance=30 meters

Area=A space with a 5 meter radius

Duration=Instant

Effect=Makes the air within the area of effect into a lethal substance, killing living things instantly

Type=-

Expansion=Final score, distance, area

Resist=Negated effect

_Death Cloud_ turns the air within its area of effect into a lethal poison gas. All creatures within range must attempt to resist this spell, and if they fail, they will die instantly. If successful, they will suffer no effect.

### **[ Perfect Cancellation ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=50

Distance=10 meters

Area=One, one object, or a space with a 5 meter radius

Duration=Instant

Effect=Removes the effects of all spells

Type=Lost, ritual

Counters (Negates): All magic other than _Reborn Dragon_

Expansion=Final score, distance, targets or area

Resist=None

This spell is a powerful version of _Dispel Magic_ , which removes the effects of _all_ spells. Even spells of the _nonremovable_ or _curse_ type, which cannot be removed by _Dispel Magic_ , will be nullified. However, to remove a spell with a target score, you'll need a final score accordingly. In addition, only the dragon roar magic _Reborn Dragon_ cannot be removed by this magic.

### **[ Meteor Strike ]**

Base Mental Power Cost=50

Distance=30 meters

Area=A space with a 10 meter radius

Duration=Instant

Effect=A meteorite falls from the sky, causing a huge explosion

Type=Damage (Explosion-type)

Expansion=Final score, distance, area, damage certainty

Resist=Reduced Effect

The fact that there are countless unseen masses of rock floating in the sky is considered a matter of course among sages. These rocks fall to the ground at a rate of about once every hundred years. These rocks floating in the sky are called meteorites. The spell _Meteor Strike_ is a spell that allows the caster to control a meteorite (a very small one) at will, and make it fall to the ground. The meteorite will fall the moment you finish the spell, causing a large explosion. Targets within the area of effect will be hit by shockwaves and fragments of the meteorite, suffering strike power 50 damage. Of course, its destructive power would be enough to destroy buildings and ramparts.

Please note that this spell cannot be used unless you can see the sky (it has no effect if used inside a cave or dungeon).

## 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill

Silent spirit are runes used to communicate with spirits and draw out the power controlled by those spirits. The skill for using spirit magic is called the _shaman skill_ , and those who have this skill are called _shamans_ . The shaman skill allows you to use all basic abilities and spirit magic up to the caster's skill level.

## Restrictions on Using 5.1.13.1 Spirit Magic

The basic rules for casting magic are the same, but the following conditions are required in order to use the shaman skill.

1. Must not wear metal armor

2. Both hands must not be full

3. The **spirit power** corresponding to the spell must be working

#1 is due to the spirits' characteristic disdain for metal. However, only silver and mithril silver are excluded from this subject. This is because silver is a metal that also exists in the spirit world and has inherently magical power. In particular, mithril silver is a metal that barely exists in the material world and has strong magical powers. This is why silver weapons can be used against monsters that are only harmed by magic weapons, but metals that are not silver or mithril silver will still inhibit spirit magic, even if they are endowed with magical power.

#2 is because the procedure for casting spells requires the use of one hand. Therefore, you must not wear clothing or armor that severely restricts movement of your arms, and your fingers must be completely free. You can use either your left or right arm to cast a spell. You may hold a two-handed weapon in only _one_ hand while using the _remaining_ hand to use spirit magic, but if you're armed with a weapon in _one_ hand and a shield in the _other_ , then unless you drop the weapon or shield on the ground or put one away, you _cannot_ use spirit magic.

#3 is ultimately decided by the game master, but below are some guidelines for where spirit power can be found at work.

## ⬤ **Wind Spirits**

It must be in a place where the wind blows in its natural state. The spirit power of wind does not work inside buildings, deep caves, or even underground passages.

## ⬤ **Earth Spirits**

This is a power that's at work in places where the ground is exposed or underground. Particularly strong spirit power is at work in the mountains. Since the power of water spirits is strong on the ocean floor and the bottoms of lakes, the spirit power of earth does not work there. Likewise, this spirit power does not work on the floors of buildings (whether they're stone or wood).

## ⬤ **Water Spirits**

The spirit power of water is at work wherever there is water, such as the ocean or a lake. Even if you carry water in a water bottle, etc., if you open the lid, the spirit power will work.

## ⬤ **Fire Spirits**

This spirit power is at work wherever there's fire burning. You can also use fire from a torch you're carrying with you.

## ⬤ **Ice Spirits**

This spirit power is at work wherever there is snow or ice.

## ⬤ **Mind Spirits**

This spirit power is at work wherever there are living things with functioning minds. Of course if the caster him or herself is like that, there's no problem. Some of these types of spirits include those that control the nature of the mind, and those that control fluctuation of emotions. It's said that if one emotion has too much of an outburst, it can cause phenomena such as alienation or complete loss of functions of other spirits, which can lead to mental disorders.

## ⬤ **Plant Spirits**

Very strong power is at work in forests, etc. but in places where plants and trees grow, they're only at work in small amounts.

## ⬤ **Other Spirits**

These are the spirits that control light, darkness, and life. Explanations of where these are at work can be found under each individual spell.

## Basic Abilities of the 5.1.13.2 Shaman Skill

Shamans have the following basic abilities, which they can use without any restrictions.

## 《 **Infravision** 》

Salamander and frau control fire and ice, as well as heat and cold. Infravision is the ability to see things dimly even in the dark and find places where the temperature is unusually different compared to the surrounding area, by sensing the workings of these spirits. However, you cannot see colors at all.

## 《 **Sense Aura** 》

This ability is used to know what kind of spirit power is at work in your surroundings. You cannot use spells that belong to a specific spirit in places where that spirit power is not at work. When a player declares that they will use this ability, the game master must tell the player what spirit power is at work in that location.

Since there are spirits of life and spirits of undeath (which control negative life force), the sense aura ability can be used to distinguish between living things and undead. Ordinary corpses and monsters created by magic (magical creatures) cannot be distinguished from living things by this ability.

- Characters (including NPCs and monsters) do not have to act based on their agility and can instead delay their actions. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01334))_
- In such a case, a character may lower their action order to 0 . In other words, they will act only at the very end of the round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01336))_
- In this case, an intended attack on an enemy that is about to withdraw can be changed to another target that you're engaged with. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01338))_
- If the enemy that's trying to withdraw has a higher agility than you, you must switch to a different target, because according to your action order, the enemy will have escaped.

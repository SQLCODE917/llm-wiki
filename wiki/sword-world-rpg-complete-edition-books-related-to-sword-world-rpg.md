## Source record

Summary of books related to Sword World RPG, including Replay Collections, Scenario Collections, World Guide, Novels, and other rulebooks.

## Key supported claims

- The _Sword World RPG Replay Collections_ help learn how to play the game via conversations between GM and players, but they're based on the old version of rules and may have contradictions (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.8-13).
- The _Duda RPG Replay Collection_ series uses Sword World RPG rules but with added humorous mystery elements like 'inept detective checks' (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.8-13).
- The _Sword World RPG Scenario Collections_ save GMs from creating scenarios and can be used nearly as-is, though some follow the old version of rules (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.8-13).

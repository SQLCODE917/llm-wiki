## Source record

Overview of books related to Sword World RPG, including Replay Collections, Scenario Collections, and other supplements.

## Key supported claims

- The Sword World RPG Replay Collections are a good way to learn how to play the game, but they're based on an old version of the rules (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.8-9).
- Some of the Sword World RPG Scenario Collections follow the old version of rules, but can be used nearly as-is (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.8-9).
- The Duda RPG Replay Collection series uses Sword World RPG rules but introduces modified rules not found in the main rulebook (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.8-9).

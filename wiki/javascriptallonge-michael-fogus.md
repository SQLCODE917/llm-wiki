## Source record

Review of JavaScript Allongé by Michael Fogus

## Key supported claims

- Fogus was skeptical about the pay-what-you-want ebook model for JavaScript Allongé, but was humbled by the book's quality (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.14-14).
- Fogus appreciates the Leanpub model's efficiency in the read-feedback-change process for book development (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.14-14).
- Fogus finds JavaScript Allongé an entertaining exploration of interesting aspects of Reg's art, with something to teach even experts (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.14-14).
- Fogus, a functional programming advocate, admires Reg's accomplishment and the book's approach to functional techniques without dogma (raw/javascriptallonge.pdf p.14-14).

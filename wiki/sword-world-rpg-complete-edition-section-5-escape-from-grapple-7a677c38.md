# 5. Escape from Grapple

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- To escape from a grapple, make a success roll check using adventurer level + agility bonus as the baseline score. An opponent who is trying to maintain the grapple may counter this by making one using adventurer level + agility bonus or adventure level + strength bonus as the baseline score, whichever is greater. If the one trying to escape's final score is greater, or if there's a tie, the grapple will be broken. Both sides will fall prone , with a short distance between them. Weapons you dropped when entering the grapple will probably be lying around nearby. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08974))_
- Those being strangled or pinned down cannot attempt to escape from a grapple unless they fi rst escape from those conditions. If the strangler or pinner attempts to escape, he or she will automatically succeed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08975))_
- It' s fine for the game master to have certain monsters attempt to grapple. Wolves, tigers, etc. perform such attacks. For adventurers who rely on weapons, this may pose a threat. Of course, a monster that does not have hands cannot strangle . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08976))_

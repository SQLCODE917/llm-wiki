# Chapter 14: / [ Seal Stone ]

## Statements

- This stone's magical power will appear when you aim it at an opponent and chant a password in low ancient. Those who fail to resist will have their soul sealed in the stone (resist target score is 18). A body that's lost its soul must faithfully obey the commands of the stone's owner. It has no effect on undead, etc. which do not have souls. Only one person's soul can be sealed in at a time. No memories will remain while the soul is sealed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08630))_
- If you hold the stone and chant the password of release, or if you destroy the stone, the sealed soul will be released and return to its body. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08631))_
- Only one person's soul can be sealed in at a time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08630))_

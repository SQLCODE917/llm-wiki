# 《 Usage 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The usage of the poison is written in parentheses after the name. These are divided into injected , inhaled , dose , and touch . Injected poisons are poisons that need to be delivered directly into the bloodstream, usually in liquid form. It's applied to small daggers and needles and used as traps on doors and treasure chests. It can be applied to knives and arrows to increase their killing power, but the poison usually wears off after about three uses and loses its effect, so it must be reapplied frequently. Many creatures, such as giant scorpions, poisonous snakes, etc. use injected poison as a weapon. This type of poison only takes effect if you lose 1 or more points of life force when you are hurt by a poisoned blade. Even if the blade hits, if it doesn't cause a wound, it has no effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06526))_
- Inhaled poisons are in gas or powder form and take effect when inhaled through the respiratory tract. This is also often used as a trap, but another method is to store it in a breakable bottle and use it by slamming it into your opponent. In rare cases, some creatures use poison gas as a weapon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06527))_
- If you're trapped in a room filled with poison gas, you must make a resistance roll every round. If you hold your breath, you will not be affected by the gas, but of course you will not be able to speak or cast spells while holding your breath. An adventurer can only hold their breath for a number of rounds equal to their life force. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06528))_
- Dose poison is used by mixing it into food or drink and comes in powder or liquid form. It is mainly used for assassinations, so adventurers will barely use it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06529))_
- Touch poison comes in liquid or powder form and can penetrate through the skin. There are not many types of them, and as expected they are often used as traps. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-06530))_

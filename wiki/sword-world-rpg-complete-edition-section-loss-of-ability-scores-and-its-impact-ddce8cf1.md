# **Loss of Ability Scores and Its Impact**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Dexterity represents the dexterity of an adventurer's hands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00172))_
- It relates to a variety of tasks that involve the use of one's hands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00172))_
- This is especially important when wielding weapons in combat. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00172))_
- If either life force or mental power fall to 0 or less, the adventurer will fall unconscious and be unable to act. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00173))_
- In _Sword World RPG_ , this is represented by a loss of life force and mental power (point reduction). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00173))_
- The four other ability scores of dexterity, agility, intelligence, and strength are rarely reduced, but if this does happen and these ability scores _do_ fall to 0 or less, the adventurer will still become disabled. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00173))_

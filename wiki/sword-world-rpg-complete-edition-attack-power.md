Attack power is a key measure in the Sword World RPG that determines a character's ability to hit enemies in combat. It is calculated based on the character's skill levels and attributes.

In combat, when a character attacks a monster, they must make a success roll hit check using attack power as the baseline score. The character's attack power is added to the result of a 2D roll to meet or exceed the monster's evasion points.

Attack power is determined by the character's fighter, thief, or ranger skill level, plus a dexterity bonus.

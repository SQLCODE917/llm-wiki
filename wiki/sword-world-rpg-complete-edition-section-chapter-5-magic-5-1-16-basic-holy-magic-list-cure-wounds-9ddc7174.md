## Statements

- This spell has the effect of instantly healing any wound. Use the key number 10 column on the Rating Table to determine the base life force (points) to be recovered, same way as for attack rolls and defense rolls. The caster can add their own magic power to this result. When using the _Rating Table_ , the _critical_ process is _not_ performed. Only _one_ check needs to be made. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02724))_
- This spell can also deal damage to undead. If you use it on undead, and the undead fail to resist, it deals strike power 10 damage. However, even in this case, _no_ critical will occur, and if the undead successfully resist, it'll have no effect at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02725))_
- Whether it's healing or dealing damage to undead, by expanding the effect, you can use the _Rating Table_ multiple times and use the maximum value. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02726))_
- This has no effect on magical creatures that do not have normal life, such as skeleton warriors, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02727))_

# 【 **Mental Power** 】

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Mental power represents the adventurer's willpower and perseverance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00169))_
- Using magic is hard work, and one cannot hope to cast much magic without a high mental capacity. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00169))_
- Mental power is also related to magic resistance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00169))_

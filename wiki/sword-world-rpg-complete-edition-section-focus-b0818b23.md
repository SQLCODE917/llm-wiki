# 《 Focus 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Magic has a fixed duration . Magic of the focus type is that in which the magic's effect lasts as long as the caster (the one who casts the magic) is focused . While focusing, the caster cannot take the following actions: _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02690))_
- Even while focusing, if another attacks you with a weapon, etc. you can still attempt to evade it. If magic is cast on you, it's also possible to resist it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02695))_
- Also, when the target is no longer within the magic's area of effect (out of reach, out of sight, etc.), the magic will lose its effect, even if you continue to focus. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02696))_
- If the caster suffers damage to their life force or mental power, their focus will be interrupted and the magic's effect will be lost . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02697))_
- If the caster wishes to voluntarily release their focus (in order to take another action), they must declare it at the beginning of the round, when no one else has made their action announcements yet. If your focus is interrupted due to damage, etc. your focus will be interrupted at the timing in which the damage is dealt. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02698))_

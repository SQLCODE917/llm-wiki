# 《 **Damage Expansion** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- For spells that deal damage, expansion can increase the certainty of that damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01324))_
- The damage you will deal is equal to the _greatest_ of all the strike rolls you made in this way ( _not_ the total). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01324))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, **damage certainty** is written in the _expansion_ section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01325))_

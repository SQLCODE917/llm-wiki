## Statements

- Lesser dragons have super strength, so this restriction is not a matter of weight, but of space for riding (they can carry many more people if you use a basket, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04113))_
- Up to _two_ adult humans can ride on the back of a lesser dragon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04113))_
- Although greater than a wyvern, a lesser dragon's intellect is also not very high, so it can only be used to fight enemies or to carry you on its back. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04113))_

# Ghost

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- A ghost is a moving being whose corpse still holds a lingering grudge. Depending on how long ago they died, they can look like zombies (rotting corpses) or skeletons (moving skeletons). Their strength varies considerably depending on which abilities they had in life. The scores listed here are averages. All ability scores will be the same as previous, except for mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06918))_
- Mental power is the only one that's previous + 6 . They have all the skills they had previously. Those who had rune master skills can use magic of that level and magic power. However, they cannot use holy magic (but they can use dark magic). Also, to use ancient magic, they will still need a magical catalyst (such as a magic wand). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06919))_
- If a ghost's corpse is destroyed, it will always attempt to possess someone nearby. If this fails, it'll become a phantom (see below) and be bound in place. Even after becoming a phantom, it'll attempt to possess if given the chance. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06920))_
- When its lingering regrets and grudges are cleared, or when its mental points are reduced to 0 or less due to a Shade , etc. the ghost will vanish, leaving only a crumbled corpse behind. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06921))_
- All ability scores will be the same as previous, except for mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06918))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill / ⬤ Earth Spirits

- This is a power that’s at work in places where the ground is exposed or underground. Particularly strong spirit power is at work in the mountains. Since the power of water spirits is strong on the ocean floor and the bottoms of lakes, the spirit power of earth does not work there. Likewise, this spirit power does not work on the floors of buildings (whether they're stone or wood). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02154))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill / ⬤ Water Spirits

- The spirit power of water is at work wherever there is water, such as the ocean or a lake. Even if you carry water in a water bottle, etc., if you open the lid, the spirit power will work. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02156))_
- The spirit power of water is at work wherever there is water, such as the ocean or a lake. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02156))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill / ⬤ Fire Spirits

- This spirit power is at work wherever there’s fire burning. You can also use fire from a torch you're carrying with you. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02158))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill / ⬤ Ice Spirits

- This spirit power is at work wherever there is snow or ice. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02160))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill / ⬤ Mind Spirits

- This spirit power is at work wherever there are living things with functioning minds. Of course if the caster him or herself is like that, there's no problem. Some of these types of spirits include those that control the nature of the mind, and those that control fluctuation of emotions. It's said that if one emotion has too much of an outburst, it can cause phenomena such as alienation or complete loss of functions of other spirits, which can lead to mental disorders. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02162))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill / ⬤ Plant Spirits

- Very strong power is at work in forests, etc. but in places where plants and trees grow, they're only at work in small amounts. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02164))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill / 《 Infravision 》

- Salamander and frau control fire and ice, as well as heat and cold. Infravision is the ability to see things dimly even in the dark and find places where the temperature is unusually different compared to the surrounding area, by sensing the workings of these spirits. However, you cannot see colors at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02170))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.13 Spirit Magic and the Shaman Skill / 《 Sense Aura 》

- This ability is used to know what kind of spirit power is at work in your surroundings. You cannot use spells that belong to a specific spirit in places where that spirit power is not at work. When a player declares that they will use this ability, the game master must tell the player what spirit power is at work in that location. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02172))_
- Since there are spirits of life and spirits of undeath (which control negative life force), the sense aura ability can be used to distinguish between living things and undead.

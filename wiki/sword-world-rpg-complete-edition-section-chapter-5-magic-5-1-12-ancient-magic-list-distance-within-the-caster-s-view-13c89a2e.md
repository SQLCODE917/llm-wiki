## Statements

- _Telekinesis_ is a spell that allows you to move things using only the power of your consciousness. The target can be an individual or object up to 1 cubic meter in volume. Targets who attempt to resist this spell and fail, or do not attempt to resist, will be moved at the caster's will. Its movement speed is 10 meters per round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01955))_
- This spell cannot move only one part of the target. One person, one rock, etc. all have to be moved in one piece, and it's impossible to tear them apart. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01956))_

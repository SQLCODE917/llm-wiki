## Statements

- Some magic allows for multiple expansion types. In these cases, you can perform multiple expansions at the same time. However, at this time, the amount of mental power consumed will become extremely large. By multiplying the rates determined for each expansion, you can determine the total multiplied rate of mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01334))_

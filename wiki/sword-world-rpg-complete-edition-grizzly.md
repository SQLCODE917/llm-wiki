Grizzlies are formidable and dangerous animals found in the forests of Alecrast. They are known for their immense strength and agility, despite their seemingly bulky appearance. These creatures are generally solitary, except when a mother bear is with her cubs. They are omnivores and will eat almost anything they can fit into their mouths.

Grizzlies usually avoid humans, but they will attack if they are hungry or if they feel threatened, particularly when protecting their cubs. It is said that once a grizzly bear has tasted human flesh, it will attack humans more frequently.

## Combat

Grizzlies are classified as one of the most terrifying animals due to their super strength and agility. Their special attack is a _bear hug_, which involves holding their opponent in their thick arms, squeezing them, and biting at the same time. This attack is treated as strangling. A bear that captures an opponent in a bear hug will both strangle and bite the opponent at the same time, gaining a +4 bonus to attack points. During this attack, the bear cannot attack other opponents.

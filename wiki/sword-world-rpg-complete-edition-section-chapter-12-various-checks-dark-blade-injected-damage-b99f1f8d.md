- A poison often used by assassins, it is a pitch-black viscous liquid. If you are wounded by a knife coated with this poison, you will suffer damage every 10 minutes thereafter. The victim continues to suffer damage until the poison wears off or they die. During this time, you will be in terrible pain and unable to take any action. If treatment is delayed, you may remain in a coma for several days after the poison wears off. If you succeed on a resistance roll, you will not suffer any damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-04829))_

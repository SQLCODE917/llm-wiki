## Statements

- Type=Mind, target score Expansion=Final score Resist=None As the god of war, Myrii is said to lend his power to those who fight justly. Priests of Myrii can receive Myrii's assistance by offering a song in holy prayer to Myrii. Basically, this spell takes effect on anything within the reach of the caster's singing voice, but the caster can also exclude any target from being targeted by the spell when casting the spell. Those under the influence of this spell gain a +2 bonus to attack power. Unlike _focus_ magic, this spell will _not_ be interrupted even if the caster suffers damage, but the spell _cannot_ be maintained if the caster takes any other voluntary action. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02960))_

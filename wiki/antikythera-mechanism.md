## Key supported claims

- The device is a Greek geared orrery and early analog computer. (raw/antikythera-mechanism.md)
- The wreck find dates to about 150-100 BC after recovery in 1901. (raw/antikythera-mechanism.md)
- The research project used tomographic imaging to recover inscription evidence. (raw/antikythera-mechanism.md)
- Its engineering exceeded known surviving ancient mechanisms until medieval clocks. (raw/antikythera-mechanism.md)

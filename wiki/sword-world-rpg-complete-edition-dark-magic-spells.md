Dark magic spells in Sword World RPG are used by dark priests and are derived from the power of wicked gods. These spells are distinct from holy magic, as they are not allowed by the gods of light, and can include both basic and special dark magic spells.

## Basic Dark Magic Spells

### 1st Level

- **Wounds**: Cuts up the opponent's body, dealing damage.
- **Mental Attack**: Reduces the target's mental power (points) by double the mental power consumed by the caster.

### 2nd Level

- **Deafness**: Takes away the target's hearing.
- **Blindness**: Takes away the target's vision.

### 3rd Level

- **Poison**: Creates a poison within the target's body, with two types:
  - **Paralysis Poison**: Paralyzes the opponent's nerves.
  - **Damage Poison**: Deals damage to the opponent's life force (points) over time.

### 4th Level

- **Control Imp**: Summons and controls an imp familiar.
- **Steal Life**: Absorbs life force (points) from a target to recover when the caster is wounded.

### 5th Level

- **Curse**: Imposes penalties that are not directly life-threatening.
- **Create Zombie**: Resurrects the deceased as a zombie slave.

### 6th Level

- **Mesmerize**: Hypnotizes the target and makes them move at will.
- **Rot**: Accelerates the decay of objects.

### 8th Level

- **Cripple**: Takes away freedom of a part of the target's body.
- **Summon Servant**: Summons animals that represent a god.

### 9th Level

- **Plague**: Infects the target with a highly contagious plague.
- **Recall Spirit**: Brings a soul back to life using a sacrifice.

### 10th Level

- **Call God**: Summons a dark god into the body of a sacrifice.

## Special Dark Magic Spells

Special dark magic spells are unique to the wicked gods that dark priests worship. These spells are not detailed in this overview but are part of the dark priest's repertoire.

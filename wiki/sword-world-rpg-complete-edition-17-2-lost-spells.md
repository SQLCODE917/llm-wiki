## Source record

This section covers lost spells in the Sword World RPG, detailing how they are learned and used.

## Key supported claims

- Lost spells are not taught in the guild, as they are known only to a limited number of sorcerers (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.273-276).
- Sorcerers must meet spell level requirements to use lost spells (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.273-276).
- Lost spells can be learned from sorcerers or spellbooks (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.273-276).
- The game master determines which spells are lost, and may designate spells not on the list as lost (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.273-276).
- Lost spells have specific rules for casting and resistance, including the possibility of failure if double ones are rolled (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.273-276).

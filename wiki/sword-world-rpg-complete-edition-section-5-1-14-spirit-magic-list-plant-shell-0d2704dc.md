## Statements

- When you chant this spell, plants will grow along the perimeter of a 5 meter radius circle centered on the place the caster touches, creating a 2 meter tall dome of natural plants on the next round. The dome serves as a tent that protects from the elements and as a temporary shelter when a suitable campsite cannot be found. The inside of the dome is covered with a carpet of soft fresh grass, making it moderately warm and moderately cool. The outer walls of the dome are tough and densely intertwined with living plants, protecting those inside from attacks by wild beasts and less powerful monsters. When entering or exiting the outer wall, the entrance can be partially opened or closed with one command from the caster. Additionally, the dome itself blends in well with the terrain, especially in forests and jungles, making it extremely difficult to locate the dome itself. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04164))_

### Table 1-3: Determining Sub-Ability Scores
| Sub-Ability Score | Human | Dwarf | Grassrunner | Elf | Half-Elf |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| A | 2D | 2D+6 | 1D+6 | 1D+6 | 1D+4 |
| B | 2D | 1/2D | 2D+4 | 1D+6 | 1D+6 |
| C | 2D | 1D+4 | 1D+6 | 1D+6 | 1D+4 |
| D | 2D | 1D | 1D | 1D+6 | 1D+6 |
| E | 2D | 1D+4 | 1/2D | 1D | 1D+2 |
| F | 2D | 1D+6 | 1/2D | 1/2D | 1D+2 |
| G | 2D | 2D+4 | 2D+6 | 1D+4 | 1D+4 |
| H | 2D | 2D+4 | 2D+4 | 1D+6 | 2D |

Note: "D" stands for dice. 1D stands for one die, 2D stands for two dice, and 1/2D stands for half the result of one die (rounded up). A notation such as 1D+6 means to roll one die and add 6 to the result.

## Spell Area and Size Expansion

### Table 5-1: Area and Size Expansion
| Expansion Rate | Area Expansion Length | Volume Expansion Length |
|---|---|---|
| x 2 | 1.4 | 1.3 |
| x 3 | 1.7 | 1.4 |
| x 4 | 2 | 1.6 |
| x 5 | 2.2 | 1.7 |
| x 6 | 2.4 | 1.8 |
| x 7 | 2.6 | 1.9 |
| x 8 | 2.8 | 2.0 |
| x 9 | 3.0 | 2.1 |
| x 10 | 3.2 | 2.2 |

Note: The expansion rate is the multiplier for mental power consumed. When you cast a spell by consuming that multiplied amount of mental power, multiply the number in the area expansion length or volume expansion length column by the number (radius, side) that represents the spell's area of effect, and the result will be the spell's area.

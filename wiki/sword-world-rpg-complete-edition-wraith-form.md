## **Wraith**

A **wraith** is an ethereal body created by the *Wraith* spell, which separates the caster's spiritual body from their physical body, allowing only their ethereal body to move around freely.

### **Spell Details**

- **Base Mental Power Cost**: 40 (or 45 in some cases)
- **Distance**: Touch
- **Area**: One
- **Duration**: 18 rounds
- **Effect**: Separates the caster's spiritual body from their physical body, allowing only their ethereal body to move around freely
- **Type**: Focus, non-removable
- **Expansion**: Duration, distance, targets
- **Resist**: None

### **Usage**

This spell allows the caster to separate their spiritual body from their physical body, allowing only their ethereal body to move around freely. This ethereal body is called a wraith. Wraiths are a kind of undead, but they can resume their life activities once they return to their own bodies. 

If you do not (or cannot) return to your original body after the spell's duration has expired, you will lose complete contact with your body, and must remain adrift for eternity as a wraith. The same applies if your body is destroyed (your life force or life points become 0 or less) while you are a wraith. If the wraith itself is destroyed, the caster dies. In this case, the body becomes nothing more than an empty husk without a soul, so it cannot be brought back to life by any means.

See p. 219 for more details on wraiths.

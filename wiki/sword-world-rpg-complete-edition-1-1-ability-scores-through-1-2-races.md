## Source record

Overview of ability scores and races in Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, covering pages 12-13.

## Key supported claims

- There are six types of ability scores in Sword World RPG: dexterity, agility, intelligence, strength, life force, and mental power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.12-12).
- Human ability scores range from 4-24, with an average of 14 (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.12-12).
- Ability scores apply to both humans and non-human races (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.12-12).
- Ability scores are related to success or failure of actions through derived bonuses (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.12-12).
- Strength represents muscular strength (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.13-13).

Spellsongs are magical songs from the ancient kingdom period in Sword World RPG, which produce spell-like effects when sung with musical instruments.

## Details

A **spellsong** is a magical song created during the ancient kingdom period, and when played with a melody on an instrument or by singing a poem, it produces an effect similar to a spell. 

Musical instruments are _always_ required to sing spellsongs. Being able to sing is also a necessary condition, but there is an exception if you choose a flute as your instrument. 

A bard can learn _one_ spellsong per skill level. 

If the game master wants to provide the players with information in the form of ancient poems or folklore, please decide on a _rarity_ and use it as the target score when making a success roll check.

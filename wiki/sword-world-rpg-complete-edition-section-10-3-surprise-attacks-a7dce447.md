# 10.3 Surprise Attacks

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The game master must manage the actions of NPCs and monsters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03843))_
- In combat, the planned actions of NPCs and monsters must be announced at the beginning of the round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03843))_
- At this time, the game master does not need to make a specific announcement. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03844))_
- All they have to do is tell you what you can understand from the character's perspective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03844))_
- However, that does _not_ mean that you can make loose announcements and change them depending on the situation. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03845))_
- However, the game master should decide in advance which actions they are going to take. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03846))_

## Statements

- This spellsong has the effect of arousing the curiosity of those who hear it. The resistance roll is made on the fourth round after the song begins (count the beginning of the song as the first round, and make the roll when the bard's action order occurs on the fourth round). Those who fail to resist will be tempted to catch a glimpse of the performer. The result is that they'll come out of their hiding place and reveal themselves to the performer. It has no effect on those who can already see the performer. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03412))_

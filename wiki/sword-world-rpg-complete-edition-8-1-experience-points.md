## Source record

Chapter 8.1 of Sword World RPG - Complete Edition covers the experience points system, including how characters gain experience, spend points to increase skill levels, and the specific requirements for different skills as shown in Table 8-1.

## Key supported claims

- Experience points indicate character growth and are gained after adventures (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-135).
- Characters can spend experience points to increase any number of skills by any number of levels at once, up to 10th level (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-135).
- The experience points required to increase each skill are shown in Table 8-1: Experience Points by Skill (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-135).
- Experience points cannot be negative and cannot be sold back for points (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-135).
- When a character has both sorcerer and sage skills, fewer experience points are required to acquire both (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-135).

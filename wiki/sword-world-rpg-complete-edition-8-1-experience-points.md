## Source record

Experience Points rules for allocating points to skill and ability advancement, including special handling for sorcerer and sage skills.

## Key supported claims

- **Experience points** are points given to characters as an indicator of how much experience they have gained as a result of their adventures. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-135)
- The experience points required to increase each skill are shown on Table 8‑1: Experience Points by Skill. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-135)
- In any case, a character's skill level can only reach 10th level. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-135)
- When a character who does not have either the sorcerer skill or the sage skill newly acquires a 1st level sorcerer skill, they can also acquire a 1st level sage skill at the same time without spending experience points. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-135)
- It may seem strange to carry over experience, but think of it as cutting off the adventurer's growth by the amount of experience points carried over for now. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.130-135)

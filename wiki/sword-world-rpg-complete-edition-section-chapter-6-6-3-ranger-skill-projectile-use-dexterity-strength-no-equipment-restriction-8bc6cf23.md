## Statements

- The ranger skill is treated the same as the fighter skill (attack power, bonus damage modifiers) only when attacking with projectiles. You can use any projectile as long as your strength allows it. The ranger skill is not relevant to the use of or defense from melee weapons. Adventurers attack with the ranger skill and defend with the fighter or thief skill. When using the thief skill for defense, you must be lightly armed (required strength of weapons and armor must be half your own strength or less). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-05036))_

# 5.1.8 Magic Contraction

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- For spells that can perform this expansion, **distance** is written in the _expansion_ section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01382))_
- The damage you will deal is equal to the _greatest_ of all the strike rolls you made in this way ( _not_ the total). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01384))_
- For spells that deal damage, expansion can increase the certainty of that damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01384))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, **damage certainty** is written in the _expansion_ section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01385))_
- There's some magic whose effects are negated or reduced if they're resisted by the target, but will also let you _increase_ the magic's final score (resist target score) in order to prevent this. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01387))_
- This increase in final score may also take effect if a nullifying spell is cast later. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01387))_
- For spells that can perform this expansion, **final score** is written in the _expansion_ section of the magic details. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01389))_
- Final score can only be increased up to a maximum of _caster's rune master skill level ÷ 2 (rounded up)_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01390))_
- This expansion requires a deep proficiency in magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01390))_
- Therefore, it cannot be done infinitely. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01390))_
- Final score can only be increased up to a maximum of _caster's rune master skill level ÷ 2 (rounded up)_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01390))_
- Therefore, it cannot be done infinitely. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01390))_
- For spells that can perform these special expansions, **effect** is written in the _expansion_ section of the magic details, and an explanation is given under each spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01392))_
- Some magic allows for multiple expansion types. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01394))_
- However, at this time, the amount of mental power consumed will become extremely large. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01394))_
- In these cases, you can perform multiple expansions at the same time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01394))_
- Suppose a sorcerer casts an _Energy Bolt_ on three enemies ( _x 3_ expansion) with a +1 to his final score ( _x 2_ expansion) and he makes 4 damage checks for each ( _x 4_ expansion). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01396))_
- However, this does _not_ reduce the amount of mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01399))_
- Magic can also be **contracted** as opposed to expanded. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01399))_
- The shape (usually a circle) _cannot_ be changed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01403))_
- You also cannot cast it in such a way that it hits the enemy directly in the eyes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01403))_
- Again, the shape cannot be changed, and you cannot directly aim it at the enemy's eyes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01405))_
- You may _only_ set it to wear off at the _end_ of a round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01407))_
- Magic that’s already been cast cannot be canceled at will. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01407))_
- Magic with a duration can be optionally shortened. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01407))_
- You must also decide how long you want the magic to last _before_ you cast it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01407))_
- However, you _cannot_ set a time that causes the effect to wear off in the middle of a round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01407))_
- Magic power can be optionally reduced. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01409))_
- Also, if the side casting the magic rolls double sixes, the resisting side will _also_ need double sixes, no matter _how much_ magic power was declared to be decreased (if it's a monster, it _cannot_ resist). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01409))_
- This will reduce the final score and, if it's damage magic, _also_ reduce bonus damage (it’s not possible to _only_ reduce the final score or _only_ reduce bonus damage). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01409))_
- If used on multiple targets, you must reduce it by the same amount for _all_ of them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01409))_
- All magic that can expand their effects can also contract their effects. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01411))_

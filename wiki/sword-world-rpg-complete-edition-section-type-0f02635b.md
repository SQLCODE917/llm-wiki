# 《 **Type** 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- **Counters (negates):** and **counters (overpowers):** describe magic that can be negated _by_ this magic, while **countered (negated) by:** and **countered (overpowered) by:** describe magic that can negate _this_ magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01467))_
- Magic of the **mind** , **physical ability** , and **weapon enhancement** types also omit _counters_ and _countered by_ , since they are sufficiently described. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01467))_

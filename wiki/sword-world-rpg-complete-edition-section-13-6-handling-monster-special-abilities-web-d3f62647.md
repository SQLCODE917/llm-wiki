## Statements

- Spiders, etc. can generate sticky webs. If a character inadvertently steps into it, he or she must make a resistance roll using adventurer level + agility bonus as the baseline score, and if he or she fails they'll be caught in the web. To escape from this state, you must make a resistance roll using adventurer level + strength bonus as the baseline score, similar to escaping from capture . Otherwise, you'll suffer a penalty based on the strength of the web. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09935))_

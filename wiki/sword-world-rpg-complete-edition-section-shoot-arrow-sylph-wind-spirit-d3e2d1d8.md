# **[ Shoot Arrow ] (Sylph/Wind Spirit)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- When you cast this spell, the caster's arrow will be carried by a sylph, ensuring that it hits the target. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02531))_
- The strike power of the arrow is a uniform 20, the magic power of the caster is used as bonus damage. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02531))_
- Also, if you roll 10 or greater on your damage check, a _critical_ will occur. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02531))_
- The target cannot resist this spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02531))_
- However, armor protection is effective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02531))_
- This magic is a counter spell to the spirit magic _Missile Protection_ , so when the target of the arrow is under the influence of _Missile Protection_ , the wind spirit powers of the same nature will interfere with each other, nullifying both. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02532))_
- However, you cannot launch multiple arrows at a single target at once. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02533))_
- It is possible to expand the effect of this spell to launch multiple arrows and attack multiple targets at once. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02533))_
- (unless the arrow itself is magical or silver). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02533))_
- (unless the arrow itself is magical or silver). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02533))_

### Technical frame 1: Chapter 15: Experience Points / 15.1 Four Types of Experience Points / Accomplishing Missions

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07041))_

> Most experience points are earned by accomplishing missions. When creating an adventure scenario, the game master sets one goal. If you're going into ruins to search for treasure, the goal is probably the treasure hidden there, and if you're rescuing a captured dignitary, it’s to bring that person back safely.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07042))_

> If the game master deems it to be an incomplete success, but not _exactly_ a failure, he or she may decide to award experience points somewhere in between, such as 700 or 800.

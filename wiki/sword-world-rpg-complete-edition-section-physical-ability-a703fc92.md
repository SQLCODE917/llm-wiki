# 《 Physical Ability 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is magic that affects the target's physical abilities (strength, etc.). Magic of this type can only take effect on humans , fae , and youma . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01320))_
- Also, with magic of the physical ability type, two or more cannot affect the same target at the same time. Please resolve them as counters (overpowers) magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01321))_

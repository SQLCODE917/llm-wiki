## Source record

This section explains how to interpret the data presented in the monster catalog, detailing formats for monster level, rarity, agility, movement speed, number, frequency, intellect, reaction, attack points, strike points, evasion points, defense points, life points/resistance, mental points/resistance, special abilities, habitat, perception, and languages (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.172-175).

## Key supported claims

- Monster level indicates general strength, raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.172.
- Rarity score indicates monster fame, raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.172.
- Intellect score describes intelligence, raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.173.

Citation: (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.172-175)

If _Attack Points=Horn:12 (5)/Hoof:12 (5)_ is written as _Strike Points=17/16_ , then if the horn attack hits, it'll deal 17 points of damage, and the hoof attack hits, it'll deal 16 points.

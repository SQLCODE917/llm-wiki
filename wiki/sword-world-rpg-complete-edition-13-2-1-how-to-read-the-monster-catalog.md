## Source record

This section explains how to read the monster catalog, covering data structure and key concepts such as type, movement, and attack.

## Key supported claims

- Monsters in Alecrast may belong to none of the twelve standard types. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.172-175)
- A monster’s location can be changed only if a player‑understandable reason is provided. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.172-175)
- When a monster moves faster than a character’s agility, escape is impossible unless the character devises a plan. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.172-175)
- A 'none' intellect means the monster lacks language; it cannot be spoken to. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.172-175)
- Multiple attack lines indicate a single attack per round, not multiple attacks. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.172-175)

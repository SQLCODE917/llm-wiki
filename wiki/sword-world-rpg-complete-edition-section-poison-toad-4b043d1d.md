# Poison Toad

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a toad about 50 cm long. Since they feed on fish and insects, they rarely attack humans, but if they're attacked or startled, they'll try to protect themselves by spewing venom from the secretory glands in their heads. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06332))_
- Anyone within 3 meters of a poison toad when it spews its venom must succeed on a life force resistance roll against target score 9. If you fail, the poison will get into your eyes and you will lose your vision. It'll recover naturally after 3 days, but it can also be cured with the holy magic Cure Poison . Even if you succeed on the resistance roll, your skin will be irritated and itchy for a while. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06333))_
- It'll recover naturally after 3 days, but it can also be cured with the holy magic Cure Poison . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06333))_

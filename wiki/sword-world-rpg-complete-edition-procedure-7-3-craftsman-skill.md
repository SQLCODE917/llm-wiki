# 7.3 > Craftsman Skill

## Craftsman Skill Process

The process of creating items as a craftsman involves two main steps: Craft and Value Check. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 126))_ Additionally, craftsmen must belong to a guild to legally produce goods. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 126))_ 

## Craftsman Requirements and Quality

In Sword World, all production is done by hand, making the quality of items depend entirely on the craftsman's skill. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 126))_

# 7.3 > Craftsman Skill

## Crafting Process

The first step in the craftsman skill procedure is to ① Craft an item. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 126))_ 

## Evaluation and Guild Requirements

Following the crafting step, a ② Value Check determines the item's market worth. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 126))_ 

All craftsmen are required to belong to a guild in order to legally produce goods. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 126))_ 

## Craftsmanship and Quality

In Sword World, all items are produced by hand. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 126))_ 

The skill of the craftsman directly determines the quality of the item. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (p. 126))_

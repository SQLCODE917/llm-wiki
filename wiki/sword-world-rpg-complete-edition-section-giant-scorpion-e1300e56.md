# [ Giant Scorpion ]

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The giant scorpion is a huge scorpion with a body length of over 3 meters. Their personality is ferocious, moreover, they're carnivorous, always hungry and when any moving thing comes into view, they'll attack it immediately. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07979))_
- When in combat, a giant scorpion can use the pincers on its arms and the barb on its tail to attack different targets (of course, it can also concentrate its attacks on a single target). The barb on the tip of its tail is a stinger that secretes a deadly poison (toxicity score 10), and those stung must make a life force resistance roll. If you fail, your whole body will become paralyzed after an hour, then you will draw your last breath. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07980))_
- If you fail, your whole body will become paralyzed after an hour, then you will draw your last breath. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-07980))_

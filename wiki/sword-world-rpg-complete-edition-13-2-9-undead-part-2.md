## Source record

This section covers various undead monsters including headless horses, undead knights, wraiths, mummies, vampires, and undead kings, detailing their attributes, abilities, and behaviors. Some details about their origins and specific mechanics are unknown.

## Key supported claims

- Headless horses appear with dullahans and can gallop through air, acting faithfully to their master's commands (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Undead knights are ghosts appearing as knights in armor, immune to normal weapons and capable of stealing mental power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Wraiths are ethereal beings separated from physical bodies by ancient magic, immune to physical attacks, and can use mental magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Mummies are magical servants buried in tombs, cursed with inability to recover ability reductions, and weak to fire (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Vampires are undead creatures that steal lifeblood and mental power, regenerate from negative life force, and are weak to sunlight (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).

## Source record

This section covers various undead monsters including headless horses, undead knights, wraiths, mummies, vampires, and undead kings, detailing their attributes, abilities, and behaviors. Some details about their origins and specific mechanics are unknown.

## Key supported claims

- Headless horses appear with dullahans, galloping through air faithfully (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Undead knights are ghosts in armor, immune to normal weapons, stealing mental power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Wraiths are ethereal beings, immune to physical attacks, using mental magic (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Mummies are magical servants, cursed with inability to recover ability reductions, weak to fire (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Vampires steal lifeblood and mental power, regenerate from negative life force, weak to sunlight (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).

## Source record

Summary of undead type rules from pages 214‑221 of Sword World RPG – Complete Edition.

## Key supported claims

- An undead knight is a ghost that appears in the form of a knight wearing armor and holding a large two‑handed sword (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- The Undead King is the ultimate undead, a powerful necromancer king from an ancient kingdom with negative power (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- An undead knight may be accompanied by multiple wights, though the exact number is unknown (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- Those whose mental power is reduced to 0 by an undead knight’s weapons or gaze die instantly and are resurrected as a wight 24 hours later (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).
- If an undead knight’s attack hits, the character suffers damage to mental power in addition to normal damage (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.214-221).

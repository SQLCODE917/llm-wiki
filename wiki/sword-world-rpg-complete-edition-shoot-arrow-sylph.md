# Shoot Arrow Sylph

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about shoot arrow sylph:

## Statements

### 5.1.14 Spirit Magic List / [ Shoot Arrow ] (Sylph/Wind Spirit)

- Type=Counters (Negates): Missile Protection Expansion=Distance, targets Resist=None When you cast this spell, the caster's arrow will be carried by a sylph, ensuring that it hits the target. The strike power of the arrow is a uniform 20, the magic power of the caster is used as bonus damage. Also, if you roll 10 or greater on your damage check, a critical will occur. The target cannot resist this spell. However, armor protection is effective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04011))_


## Technical atoms

### Technical frame 1: 5.1.14 Spirit Magic List / [ Shoot Arrow ] (Sylph/Wind Spirit)

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04012))_

> This magic is a counter spell to the spirit magic Missile Protection , so when the target of the arrow is under the influence of Missile Protection , the wind spirit powers of the same nature will interfere with each other, nullifying both.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04013))_

<a id="atom-technical-atom-05a489a35d62f7bd"></a>

> Note that this spell cannot deal damage to monsters that can only be wounded by silver or magical weapons, such as werewolves, etc.

A representative race of fae, often seen where the fairy world and material world meet, appearing as beautiful elf women with thin but strong wings.

## Characteristics

Fairies are a representative race of fae, and many of them still live in the fairy world, and rarely come to the material world, the world we live in. However, there are some places in the material world that have contact with the fairy world. Stories are often told of humans who inadvertently wandered into the fairy world from these places.

Fairies take the form of beautiful elf women, often mostly naked or clad in thin silk garments. They are about the same height as elves, but the most prominent difference between them and elves is the beautiful wings that grow from their backs. These wings are very thin but strong, allowing them to fly, albeit at a slow speed.

## Habitat and Behavior

Fairies are sometimes seen where these two worlds meet. Just as humans wander into the fairy world, there are also fairies who wander from the fairy world into the material world. They are friendly to neutral towards humans, but rarely interact directly with them.

## Abilities

Fairies have a deep connection with spirits and use spirit magic up to 3rd level. They rarely wield weapons for self-defense, preferring to use their power of spirit magic instead. They may carry items such as silver daggers, etc. for self-defense, but they rarely wield them.

## Key Concepts

- **Spirit Magic**: Fairies use spirit magic up to 3rd level. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Fairy World**: Fairies primarily live in the fairy world, but occasionally wander into the material world. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Wings**: Fairies have thin but strong wings that allow them to fly, albeit at a slow speed. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)
- **Shapeshifting**: While not explicitly stated, fairies' connection to the spirit world suggests they may have abilities related to transformation, similar to other fae. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-180)

This page summarizes the Humans section (13.2.12) from *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition*, covering normal humans, bandits, soldiers, and dark priests.

- Normal humans are common and known to everyone, with low combat ability, found in villages. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)
- Bandits have henchmen, leaders, and chiefs with increasing power levels. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)

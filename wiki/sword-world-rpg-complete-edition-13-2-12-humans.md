## Source record

This page summarizes the Humans section (13.2.12) from *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition*, covering normal humans, bandits, soldiers, dark priests, and lycanthropes.

## Key supported claims

- Normal humans are common and known to everyone, with low combat ability, found in villages. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234); Maligdorai are daemon monsters with illusion abilities that can manipulate the minds of those who fail to resist. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)
- Bandits have henchmen, leaders, and chiefs with increasing power levels. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)
- Soldiers range from normal to knight level with increasing combat abilities. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234); Doppelgangers are greater daemons that can shapeshift into other beings, including copying their abilities and memories. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)
- Dark priests serve wicked gods and can be trained as warriors. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)
- Lycanthropes can transform between human and beast forms, with abilities determined by the game master. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)

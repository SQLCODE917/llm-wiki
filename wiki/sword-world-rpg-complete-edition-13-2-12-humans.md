## Source record

Chapter 13.2.12 covers human characters, bandit hierarchy, and lycanthrope rules.

## Key supported claims

- A Lycanthrope is a monster that can shift from human to beast form. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)
- The game master may decide the lycanthrope’s abilities while it remains in human form. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)
- Some lycanthropes may conceal their identity among humans, though when the time comes they often act strangely. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)
- Human characters are common: rarity listed as “Everyone knows them”, agility 10, movement speed 10, and they possess no special abilities. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)
- Lycanthropes have life points 14, mental points 14, and a 3rd‑level dark‑magic ability. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.230-234)

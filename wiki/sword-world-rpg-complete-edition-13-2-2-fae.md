## Source record

Fae entities including pixies, fairies, pookas, featherfolk, mermaids, and mermen are described in this section, detailing their characteristics, habitats, and interactions with humans.

## Key supported claims

- Pixies are small, friendly fae with insect-like wings, often avoiding humans (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-177).
- Fairies are often seen in the material world, appearing as beautiful elf women with wings (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-177).
- Pookas can transform into forest animals and are friendly, sometimes playing tricks (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.176-177).

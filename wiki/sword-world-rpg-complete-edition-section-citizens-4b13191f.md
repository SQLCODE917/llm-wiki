## Statements

- Citizens work in various occupations including farmers, merchants, and craftsmen. Of course, since there are many other occupations as well, we collectively refer to all of them as citizens. In exchange for paying taxes, citizens receive protection from the king. During times of war, they may be conscripted into military service. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-11907))_

# When an Adventurer Casts Magic on Adventurers

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The following procedure is used to make a check when data created for a character casts magic on adventurers, and vice versa. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02540))_
- First, the side casting the magic rolls 2D. The final score (magic power + 2D) must then be determined this one time only. Each resisting side must then make a mental power resistance roll, using that determined final score as the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02541))_
- The evil sorcerer Gorman (magic power 5) unleashes Lightning on the three adventurers. First, Gorman rolls 2D and adds his own magic power. If we assume his 2D roll is 8 , each of the three adventurers must make a mental power resistance roll, with a target score of 5 (magic power) + 8 (roll) = 13 . Suppose that the baseline score of all three of their mental power resistance rolls is 4 . If their rolls are each 2 (double ones!), 6 , and 9 , then only the last one is able to resist the Lightning . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02543))_
- Handling double ones and double sixes is no different from when there's only one target. If the side casting the magic rolls double ones, the magic has no effect on any targets. If the side casting the magic rolls double sixes, no one can resist, except for those who also roll double sixes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02544))_
- The final score (magic power + 2D) must then be determined this one time only. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02541))_
- If their rolls are each 2 (double ones!), 6 , and 9 , then only the last one is able to resist the Lightning . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02543))_
- Handling double ones and double sixes is no different from when there's only one target. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02544))_

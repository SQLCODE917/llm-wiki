# **3. Pin Down**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- If the pinner’s is greater, the pin is successful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07821))_
- At this time, please make the same check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07822))_
- Once pinning, the pinner will automatically continue in that state and can perform normal attacks (of course, weapons are restricted). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07822))_
- However, the one being pinned can attempt to break free. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07822))_

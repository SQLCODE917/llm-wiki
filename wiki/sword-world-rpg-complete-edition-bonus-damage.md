# 【 Bonus Damage 】

## Source-Backed View

- Attack power strike power bonus damage speed defense power and damage reduction. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01436))_
- In addition characters with high strength can wield weapons more powerfully. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01448))_
- Characters with deep experience in combat can handle weapons effectively. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01448))_
- This is represented by bonus damage. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01448))_
- If fighter (or thief ranger) skill is 0 bonus damage is 0 regardless. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01451))_
- It is sufficient to write down each weapon. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-01475))_
- Damage calculation takes three steps ① Add bonus damage and ③ Subtract the. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02147))_
- This will be the total amount of damage. _(raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02172))_

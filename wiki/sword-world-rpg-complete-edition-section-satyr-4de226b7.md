# **[ Satyr ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Satyrs are a forest-dwelling race with the upper body of a hairy human, pointed ears, short horns sprouting from their heads, and the lower body of a goat. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05308))_
- Their staple food is nuts and berries, they have gentle personalities, they're fond of alcohol, music, and women, and they love festivities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05308))_
- They don't like fighting and will run away if danger approaches. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05308))_
- Female satyrs are called she-satyrs. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05308))_
- Humans who hear the satyr's flute may suddenly become frightened and run away, get excited and start dancing, or become lewd and start taking off their clothes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05309))_
- They always carry a flute and can manipulate human emotions with their melodies. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05309))_
- To resist this, you must succeed on a mental power resistance roll against target score 10. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05309))_

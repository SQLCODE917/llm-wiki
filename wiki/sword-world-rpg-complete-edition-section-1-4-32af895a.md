### 1.4.1 Choose a Race

- First, the player character's race must be chosen. There are five types to choose from: human, dwarf, grassrunner, elf, and half-elf. Each race has their own characteristics and limitations. Each also has strengths and weaknesses in the ability scores that form the basis of the character. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00267))_
- Each race has their own characteristics and limitations. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00267))_

### 【 Human 】

- Humans have the most average ability scores and can do almost anything without a hitch. They have no restrictions on skill selection. This is probably the most suitable race for a beginner to choose. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00269))_

### 【 Dwarf 】

- Dwarves excel in strength, life force, and mental power. On the other hand, they tend to be less agile and less intelligent. In terms of skills, sorcerer and shaman cannot be chosen. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00271))_

### 【 Grassrunner 】

- Grassrunners have excellent dexterity, agility, and mental power. Their strength, on the other hand, is decidedly inferior. Their intelligence and life force are average. Rune master skills cannot be acquired. At the same time, their low strength does not let them benefit much from acquiring the fighter skill. It's a good idea to advance the thief skill acquired from the beginning. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00273))_

### 【 Elf 】

- Elves excel in dexterity, agility, intelligence, and mental power. On the other hand, their strength and life force tend to be low. There is a restriction that they cannot acquire the priest skill. It would be better to advance the shaman skill acquired from the beginning, or to acquire the sorcerer skill. They are also a suitable race for thief. They're not very suitable for fighter. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00275))_

### 【 Half-Elf 】

- Half-elves are halfway between human and elf in terms of ability. Therefore, their dexterity, agility, intelligence, and mental power are slightly higher, while their strength and life force are slightly lower. However, they do not deviate very far from the average and are fully capable of handling all skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00277))_
- If they were raised by elves, there is a restriction that they cannot acquire the priest skill. On the other hand, if they were raised by humans, there is no limit on acquiring skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00278))_
- Therefore, their dexterity, agility, intelligence, and mental power are slightly higher, while their strength and life force are slightly lower. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00277))_
- On the other hand, if they were raised by humans, there is no limit on acquiring skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00278))_

# **Skill Basics**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- There are several types of skills, which are divided into several groups. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00230))_
- For each skill, a character's proficiency is represented by a **level** from 0~10. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00230))_
- 0 levels indicates that someone is completely unrelated to that skill, while 10 levels represents the highest proficiency that a normal human can attain. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00230))_
- It is also possible for one character to acquire (have levels in) more than one skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00231))_
- Skills are referred to by names such as _fighter_ , _thief_ , _merchant_ , etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00231))_
- As you can see, they have a strong connotation of professional job functions rather than skills. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00231))_
- Skills are referred to by names such as _fighter_ , _thief_ , _merchant_ , etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00231))_
- Those who have these skills are often referred to by their skill names. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00232))_
- So instead of _someone who has the fighter skill_ , they are simply called _fighters_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00232))_
- Half-elves have a longer life span than humans due to their elven characteristics, with a life span of around 200 years. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00233))_
- When raised by humans, they're not all that different from humans. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00234))_

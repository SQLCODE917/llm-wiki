## Statements

- Casting this spell allows the caster to fly freely. The speed of flying in the sky is what would be movement speed 50, and during full movement, 50 km per hour is the maximum speed. However, even if movement speed changes, the caster's agility itself does not change. If the spell's effect is removed while you're flying, you'll naturally fall. In this case, apply the rules for falling damage (see p. 122). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01851))_

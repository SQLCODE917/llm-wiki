In the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, mental power resistance is a mechanic used to determine whether a character can resist certain magical or supernatural effects that target the mind or mental faculties. When a monster or spell has an effect that requires a mental power resistance roll, the character must make a resistance check against a specified target score to avoid the effect.

This concept is central to handling various monster special abilities, such as hypnotism, gaze/ray attacks, possession, curses, screams, and roars. It also applies to resisting certain types of magic that affect the mind, such as fear spells or other mental attacks.

Mental power resistance is one of the three main resistance types in the game, alongside life force resistance and physical attack resistance. Characters can improve their resistance through various means, including magic, equipment, or innate abilities.

The mechanics for mental power resistance are detailed in Chapter 13.6 of the rulebook, which outlines how different monster abilities and magic effects work in relation to this resistance type.

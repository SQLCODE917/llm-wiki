## Statements

- This spell is cast immediately after an individual's death. The soul of the target of this spell will be reborn as a baby in another location within one week. Reborn babies have their ability scores preserved (they have the same ability scores after becoming adults). As the baby grows up, at a certain moment it’ll remember its former personality. At that stage they will be able to use all their previous memories and skills. When they remember their previous personality depends on the final score from when the spell was cast (see Table 5-8: Reincarnation Memory). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02925))_
- When you cast this spell, the hand of the gods will help the caster and their companions escape to a safe location (the caster's home, a temple, a familiar city, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02926))_
- By expanding the effect of this spell, you can simultaneously increase the area of effect as well as the number of people it targets. If you _double_ the mental power consumed, it will target _20_ people within a _14_ meter radius, and if you _triple_ it, it will target _30_ people within a _17_ meter radius. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02927))_
- The caster can remove anything from the target of this spell, even within the area of effect. Conversely, you _cannot_ force this spell to take effect on those who do _not_ agree to it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02928))_
- Even before a person regains their personality, if someone who knew them well before reincarnation can identify their body after reincarnation, they can try to restore the person's old memories. You can help them recover their memory through constant supervision, so you can roll 2D once every year, and if you roll a 10 or greater, they will be able to regain their personality. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02929))_
- This spell is cast immediately after an individual's death. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02925))_
- Reborn babies have their ability scores preserved (they have the same ability scores after becoming adults). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02925))_
- The soul of the target of this spell will be reborn as a baby in another location within one week. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02925))_
- If you _double_ the mental power consumed, it will target _20_ people within a _14_ meter radius, and if you _triple_ it, it will target _30_ people within a _17_ meter radius. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02927))_
- The caster can remove anything from the target of this spell, even within the area of effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02928))_

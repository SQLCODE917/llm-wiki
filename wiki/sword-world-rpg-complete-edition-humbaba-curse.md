The humbaba curse is a disease that can be contracted from the humbaba's fire attack. When a creature suffers even 1 point of damage from the humbaba's fire, there is a chance of contracting this dreadful illness. 

To contract the disease, a 2D roll must be made, and if the result is not a 7 or greater, the creature becomes infected. The disease causes severe symptoms, though specific details are not provided in this section. 

Please refer to page 166 for more details on this disease. 

## **[ Humbaba Curse ]**

Symptoms=Extreme fever, vomiting, diarrhea, shortness of breath
Rarity=16
Progression Speed/Progression=Slow
Treatment=Cure Disease, Restore Health, or magic that clears spirit power imbalance
Duration=Until treated or until death

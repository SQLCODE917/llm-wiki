## Statements

- If an adventurer's life force falls to 0 or less due to wounds, it will not recover naturally and there is a risk of death if left untreated. **First-aid** provided by a ranger will restore your life force to 1 point, allowing you to regain consciousness. It cannot fail, but it will take about 30 minutes of game time, and it cannot be done while in a state where you can't focus, such as in the middle of combat, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03219))_
- The ranger skill is also limited by equipment, just like the thief skill, if you want to make full use of its abilities. The armor you wear must have a required strength of up to _half_ your own strength, and must be ring mail or non-metal _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03220))_
- The ranger skill has some similar abilities to the thief skill. However, while the thief skill can be used both outdoors and indoors, the ranger skill is mostly useful only outdoors. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03221))_

# Cast

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about cast:

## Statements

### 3.1.1 Order of Action Announcements

- In an action announcement, you cannot declare something impossible. Casting a spell while running at full speed is prohibited by the rules. If there's an enemy in front of you that's attacking you, attacking another enemy several meters away is not possible, just by common sense. These impossible actions cannot be declared, as a matter of course. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-00627))_

### full effect

- When a monster casts magic, the procedure is different from when an adventurer casts magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01140))_

### Procedure When an Adventurer Casts Resistible Magic on an Adventurer

- In other words, you compare the magic power + 2D score of the one casting the magic with the mental power resistance + 2D score of the one on whom the magic is being cast. If the score of the one casting the magic is greater, the magic will take full effect, but if there is a tie or if the score of the one on whom the magic is being cast is greater, the magic is resisted and will only take an incomplete effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01158))_

### When an Adventurer Casts Magic on Adventurers

- First, the side casting the magic rolls 2D. The final score (magic power + 2D) must then be determined this one time only. Each resisting side must then make a mental power resistance roll, using that determined final score as the target score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01185))_

### Example:

- Handling double ones and double sixes is no different from when there's only one target. If the side casting the magic rolls double ones, the magic has no effect on any targets. If the side casting the magic rolls double sixes, no one can resist, except for those who also roll double sixes. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01188))_

### When an Adventurer Casts Magic on a Mix of Adventurers and Monsters

- Sometimes enemies written in the same data format as a character may also be accompanied by monsters. For example, a sorcerer who leads undead monsters to attack villages. Casting magic on such a group is handled the same way as when an adventurer casts magic on (a group of) adventurers, as described above. At this time, the monsters must also roll the dice to make resist checks. Please use the optional rules under 17.1: Changes to Magic Procedure (see p. 273). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01190))_

### Table 5-1: Area and Size Expansion

- When you cast a spell by consuming that multiplied amount of mental power, multiply the number in area expansion length or volume expansion length by the number (radius, side) that represents the spell's area of effect , and the result will be the spell's area . For spells cast on a flat surface, use the number in the area expansion length column, and for spells cast into space, use the number in the volume expansion length column. For example, when you cast the ancient magic Light , whose area is A space with a 10 meter radius , by consuming double the mental power, the resulting area will be a spherical space with a radius of 10 x 1.3 = 13 meters . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01239))_

### Example:

- Suppose a sorcerer casts an Energy Bolt on three enemies ( x 3 expansion) with a +1 to his final score ( x 2 expansion) and he makes 4 damage checks for each ( x 4 expansion). In this case, the multiplied rate of mental power consumed is 3 x 2 x 4 = 24! _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01259))_

### 《 Magic Power Decrease 》

- Magic power can be optionally reduced. This will reduce the final score and, if it's damage magic, also reduce bonus damage (it's not possible to only reduce the final score or only reduce bonus damage). You must declare how many points you'll reduce before you roll the dice. If used on multiple targets, you must reduce it by the same amount for all of them. Also, if the side casting the magic rolls double sixes, the resisting side will also need double sixes, no matter how much magic power was declared to be decreased (if it's a monster, it cannot resist). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01274))_

### 《 Damage 》

- In either case, the caster's magic power can be added as bonus damage to the result of the strike roll. Also, the target's adventurer level (or the monster's monster level) is subtracted from the damage. However, there's an exception if you roll double ones during your strike roll. In this case, no matter how much magic power your character (monster) has, they cannot deal damage to the target at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01286))_

### 《 Counters (Overpowers) 》

- The other, is when the prior magic has a target score . In this case, according to the definition of magic with a target score , the magic cast after will not take effect unless its final score is greater than or equal to the determined target score . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01307))_

- If the magic cast prior has a target score and the magic cast after is resistible , please follow the procedure below to make a check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01308))_

- ② Next, the one casting the magic must roll 2D to find their final score (by adding their magic power). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01310))_

- Also, all magic counters (overpowers) itself. Therefore, if certain magic takes effect, and you cast that same magic on the same target multiple times, the effect will not be cumulative. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01315))_

### 《 Focus 》

- If the caster suffers damage to their life force or mental power, their focus will be interrupted and the magic's effect will be lost . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01342))_

### 《 Ritual 》

- With these spells, you can add +1 to your final score for each day you perform the ritual. If additional casters who can use the same spell also participate in the ritual, you will gain +1 to your final score for each one. Even humans who cannot use spells can participate in the ritual. In this case, you can gain +1 to your final score for every 10 people (rounded down). However, the ritual procedures are very complicated, so those who have not been taught the procedure in advance will not be able to participate in the ritual. If it's ancient magic, the caster must have been taught at a school such as an academy of sages , etc. and if it's holy (dark) magic, they must be a believer. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01355))_

### 《 Distance 》

- This represents the distance the magic can reach. Basically, magic works on things that the caster can see and are within this distance. Any exceptions will always be noted in the explanation that follows. If the distance is touch , the caster must touch the target with their hand or (for ancient magic) with their magical catalyst (see below). To cast these spells in combat, you must be engaged with the target. At this time, there is no need to roll dice to determine whether you've touched your opponent, like with an attack hit check (you can touch them automatically). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01376))_

- This spell is powerless against spells that belong to detection . Therefore, objects with this spell cast on them will respond to Sense Magic . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01562))_

### [ Hard Lock ]

- Hard Lock is a spell that's more powerful than Lock . You can place a magical lock on an object that opens and closes, such as doors, gates, treasure chest lids, etc. to prevent them from opening. Once cast, this spell's effect is permanent until it's removed. Also, no matter what material it's made of, this gives it the same strength as iron. In other words, opening a door or lid with Hard Lock requires the same effort as destroying an iron wall or an iron box. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01652))_

### [ Familiar ]

- This spell must always be performed as a ritual. The caster requires 3 full days of preparation, followed by 12 hours of spell chanting. Please refer to Table 5-2: Familiars to see what kind of animal will appear as a familiar in response to a summon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01657))_

- The caster can also borrow mental points from their familiar to cast spells. In this case, the mental power that would've been consumed by the caster will instead be reduced from the familiar's mental points. Of course, you cannot make a substitution that would cause mental points to become negative, and a familiar whose mental points reach 0 will lose consciousness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01659))_

- When a familiar is involved in magic, etc. the caster can use their adventurer level as the baseline score for the resistance roll. When a familiar takes damage, the caster can also use their adventurer level to reduce the damage. If a caster and their familiar are separated by more than 1 kilometer, contact between them will be temporarily interrupted. Until they're both within 1 kilometer of each other again, they cannot share senses, nor can the caster borrow mental points from their familiar. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01662))_

### [ Analyze Enchantment ]

- This is a spell that identifies the type of magic being cast on a target and specifies its effect. If it's a regular magical item, you can almost certainly identify the magic. However, some items are safe from the effect of this spell due to the lost spell , Seal Enchantment . When you use this spell on an object that had Seal Enchantment cast on it, its effect cannot be inferred if your final score does not exceed the target score determined by Seal Enchantment . ### Create Undead ]

- Create Undead is a spell that creates undead monsters such as skeletons and zombies. To use this spell, of course, you'll need a corpse (usually a human corpse is used). Depending on the state of decomposition of the corpse, it will determine whether it'll become a skeleton or a zombie. If you use this spell on a mummified or severely decomposed corpse, the corpse will be completely skeletonized and a skeleton will be born. If it's still a corpse before that happens, then a zombie will be born. The caster can give commands to the skeletons and zombies created in this way. The commands must be extremely simple, at best they can be made to protect a specific location. Even walking alongside you as guards is too difficult for them. For details on human skeletons and zombies, see 13.2.9: Undead (see p. 214). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01911))_

- If you wish, you can turn non-human animals, fae, youma, titans, mythical beasts, magical beasts, etc. into undead, but to cast this on something larger than human size, you must consume additional mental power (points) to expand the effect. The required mental power (points) increases in proportion with the size of the target ( double for an ogre, seven times for a chimera, and over 1000 times for a 20-meter long dragon!). On the other hand, turning small animals into undead does not mean that they consume less mental power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-01912))_

If you do not (or cannot) return to your original body after the spell's duration has expired, you will lose complete contact with your body, and must remain adrift for eternity as a wraith. The same applies if your body is destroyed (your life force or life points become 0 or less) while you are a wraith. If the wraith itself is destroyed, the caster dies. In this case, the body becomes nothing more than an empty husk without a soul, so it cannot be brought back to life by any means. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02144))_

### ⬤ Mind Spirits

- This spirit power is at work wherever there are living things with functioning minds. Of course if the caster him or herself is like that, there's no problem. Some of these types of spirits include those that control the nature of the mind, and those that control fluctuation of emotions. It's said that if one emotion has too much of an outburst, it can cause phenomena such as alienation or complete loss of functions of other spirits, which can lead to mental disorders. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02276))_

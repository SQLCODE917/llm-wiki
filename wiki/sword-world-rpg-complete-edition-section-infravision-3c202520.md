## Statements

- Salamander and frau control fire and ice, as well as heat and cold. Infravision is the ability to see things dimly even in the dark and find places where the temperature is unusually different compared to the surrounding area, by sensing the workings of these spirits. However, you cannot see colors at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02285))_

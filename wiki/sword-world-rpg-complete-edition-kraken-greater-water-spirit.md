# **(Kraken/Greater Water Spirit)**

What [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]] covers about **(kraken/greater water spirit)**:

## Statements

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.12 Ancient Magic List / [ Suppress Elemental ]

- This spell has the effect of negating any one of the four major spirit powers of earth, water, fire, or wind. However, only one kind of spirit power can be erased at a time. This spell cannot be expanded to block two or more kinds of spirit power from working at the same time. As a result of this, spirit magic that depends on that spirit power will no longer be usable. Also, if a spell effect is present due to the negated spirit power (either gnome, undine, salamander, or sylph) within the spell's area of effect, it will be negated. If that spirit is present, it will also disappear from existence and return to the spirit world. However, this has no effect on greater spirits (behemoth, kraken, efreet, djinn). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01820))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.14 Spirit Magic List / (Kraken/Greater Water Spirit)

- This spell can be cast on rivers, canals, lakes, oceans, etc. to adjust the flow of water there. Casting this spell while on a ship makes it easier to go against the natural flow. Movement speed is limited to 50 meters per round, and can be adjusted by the caster, but the flow cannot be changed in a very complicated way. Also, this spell cannot be used to dam the flow or divide the surface of the water to open a path. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02585))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.15 Holy Magic and the Priest Skill / (Kraken/Greater Water Spirit)

- This is a spell that creates a large whirlpool on the surface of the water, dragging everything floating within its range and sinking it. To cast this spell, a sea or lake with a depth of at least 100 meters is essential. The spell's effect can work on anything that can fit inside the whirlpool. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02640))_

- If a ship is caught in the whirlpool, the person at the helm must make a success roll using the _ship steering_ ability. The baseline score is _sailor skill level/adventurer level + dexterity bonus_ for a small ship, and _sailor skill level + intelligence bonus_ for a large ship. The target score is the final score from when the spell was cast. For more information, please refer to the section Sailor Skill (see p. 128). If successful, the ship will be able to safely survive the whirlpool, but if you fail, it will be swallowed up by the whirlpool and sink into the water. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02641))_

- If the ship is swallowed up by the whirlpool, everyone on board will be thrown into the whirlpool. In that case, make a success roll using the _swim_ ability to determine whether you drown. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02642))_

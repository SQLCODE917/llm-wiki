## Statements

- _High ancient_ are the great runes that were developed and studied during the time of the ancient kingdom. The power of these runes releases magic forces and brings about various effects. Many of the spells and runes of this system were lost with the fall of the ancient kingdom, but a small amount of them have been passed down to modern Forcelia, and are now referred to as **ancient magic** . The skill for using ancient magic is called the _sorcerer skill_ , and those who have this skill are called _sorcerers_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01478))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.11 Ancient Magic and the Sorcerer Skill / 《 Base Mental Power Cost 》

- The base mental power cost represents how much mental power is consumed when using that magic. As previously explained, this base mental power cost divided by the corresponding rune master skill level equals the actual mental power consumed by the caster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01454))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.11 Ancient Magic and the Sorcerer Skill / 《 Distance 》

- This represents the distance the magic can reach. Basically, magic works on things that the caster can see and are within this distance. Any exceptions will always be noted in the explanation that follows. If the distance is **touch** , the caster must touch the target with their hand or (for ancient magic) with their magical catalyst (see below). To cast these spells in combat, you must be _engaged_ with the target. At this time, there is no need to roll dice to determine whether you've touched your opponent, like with an attack hit check (you can touch them automatically). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01456))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.11 Ancient Magic and the Sorcerer Skill / 《 Duration 》

- This represents how long the magic will remain in effect. This includes **instant** , **18 rounds** , **1 day** , **permanent** , **until focus ends** or **until certain** **conditions are met** . For magic whose duration is _instant_ , the magic itself disappears in an instant, but its effects remain. Wounds caused by a _Fireball_ explosion will remain, and if you receive healing by _Cure Wounds_ , those wounds will be recovered instantly. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01463))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.11 Ancient Magic and the Sorcerer Skill / 《 Type 》

- Represents the type of magic. Please see 5.1.9: Magic Types. **Counters (negates):** and **counters (overpowers):** describe magic that can be negated _by_ this magic, while **countered (negated) by:** and **countered (overpowered) by:** describe magic that can negate _this_ magic. However, _Dispel Magic_ , _Counter Sense_ , _Suppress Elemental_ , _Dispel Order_ , _Rune Shield_ , _Perfect Cancellation_ , _Neutralize Elemental_ , and _Remove Curse_ omit this. Magic of the **mind** , **physical ability** , and **weapon enhancement** types also omit _counters_ and _countered by_ , since they are sufficiently described. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01467))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.11 Ancient Magic and the Sorcerer Skill / 《 Low Ancient 》

- This was an everyday language of the ancient kingdom, called **low ancient** , and was the language used by citizens and slaves. Sorcerers can speak, read and write in this language. ### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.11 Ancient Magic and the Sorcerer Skill / 《 Spellbook 》

- A sorcerer must somehow preserve the ancient magic spells they've learned. Otherwise, even those with the greatest minds cannot possibly memorize all the words, actions, etc. needed to cast spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01494))_
- For this reason, it is customary for sorcerers to write these spells down in a book called a **spellbook** . A spellbook is, so to speak, just a handwritten notebook, and the spellbook itself has no magical power of its own. However, because it's a very important thing, it would be a disaster if it were lost or stolen, so some sorcerers may keep several copies in reserve. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01495))_
- When you go on an adventure, or you're in a situation where you'd need to use magic, you'd usually bring your spellbook with you. However, no sorcerer looks at their spellbook every time they try to cast a spell. This is because the words and actions of a spell are usually sharply memorized in your head. A spellbook is used to review them, always confirming and ensuring your memory. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01496))_
- There are no special restrictions on spellbooks according to the rules. Nevertheless, keep in mind that sorcerers in Sword World always keep their spellbooks with them, and remember to take very good care of them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01497))_

### Chapter 5: Magic / 5.1.11 Ancient Magic and the Sorcerer Skill / 《 Magical Catalyst 》

- Sorcerers require a special medium to cast spells. This is a **magical catalyst** , and it is through this that a sorcerer exercises the power of a spell. Usually, a specially-made mage's staff is used as a magical catalyst, made from a wooden staff with a _Create Device_ spell cast on it. This is why you must have a mage's staff as a restriction when using ancient magic. However, some sorcerers substitute other things besides staves. For example, Laverna, the witch of Orphan, uses an amethyst ring she wears on her right middle finger as her magical catalyst, and Cyanos, leader of the magic knights of Ramliearth, carries a small twig wand in his left hand as a magical catalyst. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01499))_

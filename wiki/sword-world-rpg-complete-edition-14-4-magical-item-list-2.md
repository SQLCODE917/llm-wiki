## Key supported claims

- Magical items vary by rarity and enchantment level. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.247-251)
- Item prices depend on strength requirements and base trade prices. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.247-251)
- Some items have hidden drawbacks or special effects. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.247-251)
- Enchanted items fall into categories: armor, shields, robes, cloaks, accessories, amulets, staves, wands, rings. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.247-251)
- Magic crystals store power and may be used as currency. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.247-251)

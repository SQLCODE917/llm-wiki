# 7. Old Version of the Rules

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Three volumes have been published by Fujimi Dragon Books: Sword World RPG , and Sword World RPG Advanced Rules Part 1 and 2 . These are the rulebooks for Sword World RPG , that is, they serve exactly the same purpose as this book. This book is a re-edited and partially revised version of the contents of these three books, so they're unnecessary if you're holding this in your hands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00149))_

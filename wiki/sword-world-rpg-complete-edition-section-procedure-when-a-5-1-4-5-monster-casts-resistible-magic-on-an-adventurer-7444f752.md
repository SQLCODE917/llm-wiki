# **Procedure When a 5.1.4.5 Monster Casts Resistible Magic on an Adventurer**

## Statements

- When a monster casts magic, the procedure is different from when an adventurer casts magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01270))_
- The target score is a score called the monster’s **magic intensity** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01271))_
- In the case of _resistible_ magic, the issue is resolved from the perspective of whether the adventurer is able to resist the magic cast by the monster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01271))_
- First, in the case of _non-resistible_ magic, a monster can _never_ fail. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01271))_
- There is no need to roll the dice, as the magic will _definitely_ take its effect. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01271))_
- This is determined by a success roll using the character's _mental power resistance_ , that is, a _mental power resistance roll_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01271))_

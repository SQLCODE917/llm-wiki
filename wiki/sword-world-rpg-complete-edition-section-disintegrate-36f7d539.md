## Statements

- The spirits, will-o-wisps and shade, will disintegrate upon the slightest shock (for instance, by bumping into a character, etc.). At this time, the character will suffer damage to both their life force and mental power. This is treated as damage magic. In other words, a strike roll damage check is made, and a successful mental power resistance roll will reduce strike power and prevent a critical from occurring. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09834))_

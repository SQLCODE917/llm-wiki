## Statements

- This is a power that's at work in places where the ground is exposed or underground. Particularly strong spirit power is at work in the mountains. Since the power of water spirits is strong on the ocean floor and the bottoms of lakes, the spirit power of earth does not work there. Likewise, this spirit power does not work on the floors of buildings (whether they're stone or wood). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-02268))_

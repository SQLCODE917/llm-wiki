- Characters with 0 or negative life force remain unconscious, even if they successfully survived a death check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01047))_
- Any adventurer who falls to _0 or negative_ life force is now at risk of _dying_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01048))_
- If the character's life force is _negative_ , the negative amount will be used as a _penalty_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01049))_

- At this time, their life force becomes _1_ point. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01052))_
- If the result is _double sixes_ (automatic success), the character will regain consciousness on their own. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01052))_
- life force resistance + 2D - (damage applied beyond life force) < 7 → **death** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01053))_

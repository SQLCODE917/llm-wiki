# **[ Thermal Ring ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This ring has the effect of keeping the wearer's body temperature constant. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07404))_
- However, it’s not effective against _too_ high or low temperatures or _sudden_ changes in temperature, so it has absolutely _no_ effect against fire or cold produced by _magic_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07404))_

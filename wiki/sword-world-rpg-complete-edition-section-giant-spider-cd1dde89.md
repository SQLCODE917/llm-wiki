# Giant Spider

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Giant spiders are large spiders with a body length of about 50 cm. They usually feed on small animals such as rabbits, but if they can't stand their hunger they may attack larger creatures than themselves such as humans, etc. They do not build webs, nor are they poisonous. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06628))_
- Giant spiders attack their prey by shooting sticky string at it. If hit by the string attack, an adventurer will become entangled and lose their freedom of movement. Escaping from this should be resolved the same way as strangling (see p. 236). The giant spider will bite the opponent with its fangs and attempt to suck out their fluids. An entangled opponent suffers a -4 penalty to evasion speed (points). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-06629))_

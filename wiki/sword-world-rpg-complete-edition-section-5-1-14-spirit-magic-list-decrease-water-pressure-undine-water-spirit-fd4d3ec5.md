# 5.1.14 Spirit Magic List / [ Decrease Water Pressure ] (Undine/Water Spirit)

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-5-1-14-spirit-magic-list-55816c7f]] - broader source section: 5.1.14 Spirit Magic List

## Statements

- Targets under this spell are saved from the effects of water pressure when diving deep underwater. All water pressure exerted on the body by the spell will be alleviated regardless of its strength, and reduced to a point where normal actions are no longer a problem. If you cast this spell, you can fight underwater without any penalties at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04119))_
- In addition, to actually dive, it is essential to use Water Breathing in order to be able to breathe underwater. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04120))_

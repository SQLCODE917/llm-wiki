# 【 Dark Priest 】

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- The gods are divided into two main groups. The gods of light and the gods of darkness . This is the same classification as the two factions that waged war in ancient times. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00294))_
- Gods who belonged to the gods of darkness are now considered wicked gods . Each of the gods teaches and guides people, but the teachings and guidance of the gods of darkness are extremely individualistic and deny human reason, because they are far removed from human morality , so to speak. Another hindrance to belief in the wicked gods, is the fact that the majority of people in Forcelia today believe in the teachings of the gods of light . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00295))_
- However, even the wicked gods have their believers. As agents of the wicked gods, there are dark priests who use daemon scream and cast dark magic , which is essentially equivalent to Divine Magic. Players may not choose the dark priest skill as one of their character's skills. This is because it would be significantly more difficult to have a character practice the teachings of the gods of darkness , when examining a roleplaying game from the role-playing perspective. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00296))_

## Statements

- This is a skill related to trade. Merchants are a class with a lot of freedom in Sword World , and often travel for trade purposes. Among them are peddlers who are always traveling and doing business. Merchants are usually issued a merchant's pass by the king as proof of their status. A person holding this is given the right to open a shop in public. Therefore, if some of the adventurers have this skill, they can also sell the items they obtain from their adventures in shops or on the street. A person without this certificate cannot conduct transactions in public.

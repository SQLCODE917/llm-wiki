## Source record

This section covers the rules for merchant and craftsman skills in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition, detailing the abilities and mechanics of trading and crafting.

## Key supported claims

- Merchant skill is related to trade and allows adventurers to sell items in shops or on the street (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.125-125).
- Merchants are issued a merchant's pass by the king, which allows them to open a shop in public (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.125-125).
- Holders of the merchant skill have abilities including Value Check and Negotiation (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.125-125).
- The merchant skill allows for a check to determine the value of items within their specialty (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.126-126).
- The craftsman skill involves creating items by hand, with quality depending on the craftsman's skill level (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.126-126).

# Unconscious and Death Checks

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Related pages

- [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-section-4-9-8bd45d4a]] - broader source section: 4.9

## Statements

- If, as a result of damage (accumulated damage), a character's (monster's) life force (points) becomes 0 or negative , that character (monster) will fall unconscious . It is no longer possible for them to act. Also, that character (monster) is now in danger of dying . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02316))_
- Ducard II, with 2 points of life force left, was hit by Bucky's ax again in the next round, suffering 6 points of damage. As a result, Ducard II now has a life force of -4 . Ducard II faints and collapses . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02318))_

# [ Sense Evil ] (Spell Level=1)

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Sense Evil is a spell that allows you to see through those who harbor (so-called evil) feelings that would destroy the justice and order taught by Pharis. Think of this spell not as constantly detecting people called villains , but only the moment when someone is thinking evil thoughts . In other words, this spell works on the evil intent of those that have minds, but is not a spell that detects people who commonly have evil intent (these kinds of people are usually called villains). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-03246))_

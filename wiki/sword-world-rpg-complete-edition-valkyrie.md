A valkyrie is a mind spirit in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition that governs the courage of men who fight. Her appearance is that of a human woman clad in shining white armor, and her noble appearance is said to fascinate all men. However, a mad valkyrie is extremely cruel and terrifying. They attack from above, casting the spell _Valkyrie Javelin_ where sword attacks can't reach. They will also cast a _Fanaticism_ spell to provoke you. Casting these spells does not consume spirit points. To harm a valkyrie, you need magical or silver weapons.

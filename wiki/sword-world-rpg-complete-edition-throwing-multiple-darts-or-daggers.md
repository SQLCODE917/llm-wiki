## Throwing Multiple Darts or Daggers

This concept refers to an optional rule in the *Sword World RPG - Complete Edition* that allows characters to throw multiple darts or daggers at once. It introduces specific restrictions and penalties to balance gameplay.

### Key Aspects

-   **Same Weight Requirement**: All objects thrown at the same time must have the _same weight_ (required strength).
-   **Strength Limitation**: The _total required strength_ must be up to the _character's strength_ (or _half_ that if it's the thief skill).
-   **Penalty for Number Thrown**: The _number_ thrown at the same time becomes a _penalty_ to attack power. For example, if you throw _three_ at the same time, that’ll incur a -3 penalty to your attack power for each hit check.
-   **Single Target**: You _cannot_ choose _multiple_ targets. There must _always_ be _one_ target.
-   **Engagement Restriction**: Projectiles _cannot_ be used while in an _engagement_.

This rule increases the chance that a high-level character will defeat a low-level character or monster in one blow.

## Key supported claims

- The Familiar spell allows a sorcerer to summon an animal familiar, with specific rules on mental contact, point borrowing, and limitations (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.72-75).
- Physical Enchant has six variations (Sharpness, Dullness, Quickness, Slowness, Strength, Weakness) affecting dexterity, agility, or strength (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.72-75).
- The Bone Servant spell creates a bone golem from bones, with specific requirements and forms based on the bones used (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.72-75).
- The Lightning spell releases a powerful electric shock that can reach up to 20 meters, with options to double or quadruple the mental power for increased length (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.72-75).
- The Counter Sense spell negates detection spells and items, revealing the type, caster, and whereabouts of the spell or item used (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.72-75).

## Statements

- This jar is a magical jar that can seal things from another world, such as the spirit world, within it. It's said to be able to seal not only lesser spirits such as salamander and sylph, but also greater spirits such as djinn and efreet. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08621))_
- If you point this jar at a spirit and chant a password, any kind of spirit can be entrapped. Once sealed, a spirit remains locked up until the lid is opened. If you open the lid, they immediately return to the spirit world. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08622))_

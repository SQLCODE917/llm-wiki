# 13.6 Handling Monster Special Abilities / 《 Entangling 》

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- In the case of the giant webspinner, which is a typical monster that performs this attack, it’s almost impossible to cut the thread. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06239))_
- Each time you fail, you’ll normally become more and more entangled, and the target score will increase each time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06239))_
- When a thread is cut, damage will also be applied to the entangled character at the same time. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06239))_
- If the difference between the baseline score and the target score reaches 13 or greater, the character will be unable to escape on his or her own, and will eventually suffocate to death. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06239))_
- Whether or not another can rescue them, by attacking or burning the thread, depends on the monster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06239))_

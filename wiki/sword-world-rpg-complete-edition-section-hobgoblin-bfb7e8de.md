# **[ Hobgoblin ]**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- They are a feared monster with a physique comparable to that of a human, strong and powerful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04736))_
- When fighting, they use either a crude club or the fangs that protrude from their jaws. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04736))_
- They have reddish-brown skin and very little hair on their heads, but they do have thick hair all over their bodies. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04736))_
- Although they have a belligerent personality, they are also surprisingly cowardly, and will run away as soon as they see that the situation is not going well. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04736))_

Monster Level in the Sword World RPG - Complete Edition is a score that corresponds to a character's adventurer level, and represents the general strength of that monster. If it's 1, it's an extremely weak monster. If it's 10, even a seasoned adventurer will have a hard time crossing swords with it. Monster level, similar to a character's adventurer level, indicates how difficult it is for a monster to die. When a monster suffers damage from magic, etc., the damage can be reduced by this monster level score.

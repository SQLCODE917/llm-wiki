- If it's removed with _Dispel Magic_ , etc., they will be revived in the same state they were before being petrified. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02093))_
- A petrified target will cease all life activity until they're released, so they will not age. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02093))_
- However, if a petrified statue is destroyed in the meantime, it will be revived while broken apart at the time the spell is removed, so it will naturally die instantly (or suffer wounds or irreversible injury). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02093))_
- Those who fail to resist this spell, no matter what they're made of, will instantly be turned to stone. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-02093))_

## Statements

- Attack power modifier indicates the modifier added to an adventurer's attack power when using that weapon. Weapons with higher numbers are more likely to hit an opponent, while weapons with smaller numbers are less likely to hit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08866))_

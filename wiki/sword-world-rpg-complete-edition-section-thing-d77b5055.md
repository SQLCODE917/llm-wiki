# Thing

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Like an imitator, a thing is an amorphous creature, and according to one theory it's said to be an imitator that's turned feral. The difference is that they have intellect and can transform into any creature so long as it's close to human size. Although it is nearly impossible to see through its clever transformation, in contrast to an imitator, it's not very good at transforming into inanimate objects. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07318))_
- Things often eat humans. They approach their prey in the form of a human or elf, catch their opponent off guard, then aim to attack when they're alone. They target humans because they're easier to deceive than animals. Animals have a keen sense of smell, so no matter how closely a thing resembles the real deal, they'll be able to tell by the difference in smell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07319))_
- During combat, a thing can attack up to 6 times by transforming its body so as to produce numerous arms and tentacles. The game master must roll the dice to randomly determine how many attacks are made in a round. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-07320))_

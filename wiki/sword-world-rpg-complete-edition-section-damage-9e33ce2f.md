## Statements

- A spell that deals damage to the target's life force or mental power. Spells of the damage type always have a clearly defined strike power . If you manage to break through the target's resist, use the spell's strike power to determine damage by making a strike roll using the Rating Table, same as if you had hit with a weapon in weapon combat. The key number is, of course, the indicated strike power . If you roll 10 or greater during the strike roll, a critical will occur and deal even more damage, same as in weapon combat. Conversely, If you roll double ones, it does not deal any damage at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02639))_
- Most magic of the damage type can still deal damage even if it's resisted by the target, only with a reduced effect. In this case, you must make the strike roll after subtracting 10 from strike power. Also, at this time, no matter how high you roll during your strike roll, a critical will not occur. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02640))_
- In either case, the caster's magic power can be added as bonus damage to the result of the strike roll. Also, the target's adventurer level (or the monster's monster level) is subtracted from the damage. However, there's an exception if you roll double ones during your strike roll. In this case, no matter how much magic power your character (monster) has, they cannot deal damage to the target at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02641))_
- Also, even magic of the damage type may in rare cases take no effect if resisted. At this time, if the target successfully resists, you cannot make a strike roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02642))_
- In this case, you must make the strike roll after subtracting 10 from strike power. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-02640))_

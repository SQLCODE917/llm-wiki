### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01806))_

> This is a spell that allows the caster to teleport their possessions into the palm of their hand. However, even though it's their possession, the caster must have a complete memory of it, and moreover must be able to identify specifically where it's kept. Also, the size of the target is limited to something you can hold in your hand (for example, you can summon a sword, but not armor). If the duration expires or if the spell is canceled, the target immediately returns to its original location.

### Technical atom 7

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01810))_

> Duration=Permanent, until focus ends Effect=Can create illusions using images and sounds

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01813))_

> This spell creates illusions that affect the senses of hearing and vision. The illusion can be anything within its area of effect, but it cannot cause any realistic effects. For example, you can create a wall of fire via illusion, but you won't feel any heat even if you approach it, and you won't burn to death even if you jump into it. However, the illusions created by this spell are so realistic that they cannot be discovered just by looking at them normally. The spell's effect continues even if you touch it. If an illusion of a stone wall is in a passage, you can't see beyond it, but you're completely free to pass through it (if you try). However, this result will not destroy the illusion of the stone wall. As long as the caster maintains focus, they can freely change the illusion, but if they lose focus, only static or simple repeating illusions can remain. The caster can freely cancel the spell's effect, but a _Dispel Magic_ spell is required if someone else attempts to remove it.

A surprise attack in the Sword World RPG occurs when one combatant is unaware of the other's presence, allowing the surprised party to take no assertive action in the first round. The side that was surprised is unable to take any assertive action. It’s still possible to take actions such as trying to avoid enemy attacks or resisting magic cast on you. However, evading an attack will incur a -4 penalty to evasion speed (points).

This concept is detailed in the [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition-chapter-10-3-surprise-attacks]] chapter.

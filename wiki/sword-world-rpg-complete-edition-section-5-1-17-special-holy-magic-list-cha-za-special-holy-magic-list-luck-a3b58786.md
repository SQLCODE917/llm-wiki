- Duration=Until the caster falls asleep Effect=You can turn a failed success roll into a success only once Type=Non-removable Expansion=- Resist=None Cha Za's power as the god of luck is said to bestow good fortune on the caster through the hand of an invisible god. If you cast this spell, you can turn a failure into a success only once on a success roll. However, the effect of this spell is limited to the following: _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04756))_
- Resistance rolls against magic, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04758))_
- Other defensive success rolls approved by the game master. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04760))_
- A defensive success roll should be judged by whether it means you luckily survived . If the game master is unsure, treat it as something where it can't be used. In particular, it can never be used on checks for hitting others with attacks or checks for casting magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04761))_
- This spell cannot be recast until the caster falls asleep. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04762))_

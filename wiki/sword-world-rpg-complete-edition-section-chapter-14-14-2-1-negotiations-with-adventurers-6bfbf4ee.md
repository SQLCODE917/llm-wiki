## Statements

- Please consider the estimated reward for asking an adventurer to resolve a case to be around 50~200 times the (total of) each adventurer level. For a party consisting of three 2nd level characters and two 3rd level characters, the estimated reward is around (2 x 2 x 3 + 3 x 3 x 2) x 50~200 = 1500~6000 gamels total. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08080))_
- Even if there's variation in level between the party, please make sure the rewards presented are equal per person. In the above example, the reward presented would be 300~1200 gamels per person. A 3rd level character would not be presented 450~1800 gamels nor would a 2nd level character be presented 200~800 gamels. Rune masters tend to be lower level, but that doesn't mean they're any less useful than other characters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08081))_
- This reward is basically for a case that's expected to be resolved in one day, or 3~4 days at most. If it's shorter, there'll be less of a reward, and if it's longer, there'll be more. Of course, this does not mean that the reward will increase if the adventurer intentionally delays the resolution. Such behavior would result in lost trust and likely void the agreement. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08082))_
- In these contracts, 10~30% is given upfront as an advance payment. If you fail to resolve the case, you will not be paid the remaining money. If an adventurer abandons a job at his or her own convenience, it' s customary to return double the advance payment to the client. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-08083))_

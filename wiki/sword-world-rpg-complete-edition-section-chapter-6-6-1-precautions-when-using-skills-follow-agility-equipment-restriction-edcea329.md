## Statements

- This is the ability to follow an opponent without them noticing. The side that's following must use thief skill level + agility bonus as their baseline score, while the side being followed must use adventurer level + intelligence bonus , and success or failure is determined by comparing the final scores of their success rolls. A tie is a failure to follow. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04971))_
- If you fail, your opponent will realize that they are being followed. What action the person takes after that is up to them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04972))_
- What action the person takes after that is up to them. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-04972))_

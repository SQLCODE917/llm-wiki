## Statements

- This item was developed so that even those without the sorcerer skill can use ancient magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06843))_
- It does _not_ consume mental power when used. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06843))_
- Magic intensity is 15, and the effect _cannot_ be expanded at all. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06844))_
- 252 manual written in low ancient is included in its box, and if you chant a spell name in low ancient while tearing up the card, the magical power previously bestowed on the card will be released. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06844))_
- You can tell the effect of the card by the picture drawn on it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-06846))_

# **Lullaby (Mind)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a spellsong that puts the listener to sleep. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03110))_
- If the performance is interrupted _before_ it is completed, the effect of the spellsong will disappear at that point, but once the spellsong _is_ completed, you will enter a natural state of sleep, so there is no need to play it any further. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03110))_
- Once it starts, the effect begins on the fourth round (resistance rolls are made at this point), and the spell is complete after 12 rounds. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03110))_

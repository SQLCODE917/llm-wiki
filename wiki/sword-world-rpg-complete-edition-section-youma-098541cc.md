## Statements

- Youma are a race of fae that have evil personalities. They may also use spirit magic or dark magic. They have a very strong hostile relationship with the fae, and of course they are not friendly with humans either. If you encounter them, there's a high probability that you'll get into a fight. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-05070))_
- Typical examples of youma include goblins and gremlins. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-de1806a5-05071))_

## Source record

This section describes various types of treasure and magical items found in the world of Alecrast, including artwork, gems, precious metals, and magical items. It outlines how these items can be appraised and valued, as well as provides a list of specific magical items with their properties, rarities, and base trade prices. Some values are unknown.

## Key supported claims

- Magical items include weapons, armor, handicrafts with magical power, etc. (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-246)
- Treasure types include artwork, gems, precious metals, and magical items (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-246)
- The value of magical items ranges from 10 silver coins to 10,000 silver coins, with the most expensive ones being of immeasurable value (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.242-246)

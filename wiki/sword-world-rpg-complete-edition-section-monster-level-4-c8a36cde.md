- Sylph is a spirit of wind. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05160))_

- Sylphs also use spirit magic that is sourced from the power of the wind. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05160))_
- Its magic intensity is 13 (magic power 6). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05160))_
- Its pale upper body is translucent, but from the waist down it is completely invisible and nothing can be seen. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05160))_
- To harm a sylph, you need a magical or silver weapon. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05160))_
- _Wind cutter_ is treated as strike power 10 damage magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05160))_
- However, spirit points are not consumed even if they use wind cutter attacks or spirit magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-05160))_

## Statements

- Magic basically only works up to a certain distance. However, there are spells that can extend their reach. If reach distance is _doubled_ or _tripled_ ...... the mental power consumed will also be _doubled_ or _tripled_ ....... You can also increase it by a fraction, such as x1.5, in which case the fraction is _rounded up_ when determining the multiplier for mental power consumed. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01319))_

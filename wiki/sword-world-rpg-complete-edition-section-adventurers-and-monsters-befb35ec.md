# **Adventurers and Monsters**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- Sometimes enemies written in the same data format as a character may also be accompanied by monsters. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01325))_
- Casting magic on such a group is handled the same way as when an adventurer casts magic on (a group of) adventurers, as described above. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01325))_
- At this time, the monsters must _also_ roll the dice to make resist checks. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01325))_
- Please use the optional rules under _17.1: Changes to Magic Procedure_ (see p. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01325))_
- For example, a sorcerer who leads undead monsters to attack villages. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01325))_

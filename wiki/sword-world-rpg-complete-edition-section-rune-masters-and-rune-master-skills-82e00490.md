# **Rune Masters and Rune Master Skills**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- In _Sword World_ , all those who use magic utilizing runes are called **rune masters** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01227))_
- Rune masters are divided into five types according to the type of magic (runes) they use. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01227))_
- Sorcerer uses **ancient magic** Shaman uses **spirit magic** Priest uses **holy magic** Dark priest uses **dark magic** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01228))_
- and dragon priest uses **dragon roar magic** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01229))_
- Each corresponds to the skill for which it is named. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01230))_
- Those with the _sorcerer skill_ can use _ancient magic_ and are called _sorcerers_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01230))_
- It is only possible to achieve a defined and limited set of effects by weaving certain words (in runes), called **spells** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01231))_
- 1st level spells are very rudimentary, while 10th level spells are very powerful. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01231))_
- Magic is not omnipotent. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01231))_
- Depending on the strength of their effects, spells are divided into _levels_ from _1-10_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01231))_
- There are several types of spells, each with different effects. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01231))_
- It is only possible to achieve a defined and limited set of effects by weaving certain words (in runes), called **spells** . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01231))_
- However, working with higher level (i.e., more advanced) spells requires a high rune master skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01232))_
- A rune master can use _all_ magic up to _their_ skill level, but _cannot_ use magic of a _higher_ level than their current skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01232))_
- For those who have acquired multiple rune master skills, consider the level of spells you can use for each type of magic. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01233))_
- If you’ve acquired _multiple_ magic skills, consider the magic power of each _separately_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01235))_

## Statements

- You want a high-level sage to appraise the treasure you found during your adventure. You found a beautiful gemstone and would like to have it forged into a ring. As you progress through your adventures, such things will happen. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04517))_
- In these cases, adventurers can request NPCs to do this kind of work. If it’s a very simple task like those above, you can have it done for _NPC skill level x skill level x 30 gamels_ . For example, if you request an appraisal from a 5th level sage, you will have to pay 5 x 5 x 30 = **750** gamels. If the task becomes a little more complex, use numbers such as x _40_ or x _50_ instead of x 30. Also, if there’s no one available to do this for you, there is of course no point in paying any amount of money. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04518))_
- If the task you're requesting from an NPC is something that will completely restrain that NPC, you will need to **hire an NPC** . In this case, higher compensation is required. The average price per day is about _NPC's skill level multiplied by 50 to 200 gamels_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04519))_
- Please follow the standards on Table 11-2: NPC Presence by Level (see p. 154) to find out which skill level NPCs are in which locations. Of course, this depends on the type of skill. Even in villages, there are likely to be people with a fairly high farmer skill level (although it’s unlikely that you’ll ask such people for anything). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04520))_
- For requests for such work, you'd usually give 10% to 30% as an advance payment. If the work _fails_ , you do _not_ have to pay the remainder. If the contractor willfully abandons the job, it is customary to return double the advance payment to the client. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04521))_
- This is the price an _adventurer_ must pay when making a request to an _NPC_ . Adventurers _themselves_ cannot go around charging these same rates. If you don't have clients, you won't get paid (so just patiently wait for work at the adventurer shop, okay?). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04522))_
- For example, if you request an appraisal from a 5th level sage, you will have to pay 5 x 5 x 30 = **750** gamels. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04518))_
- If the task becomes a little more complex, use numbers such as x _40_ or x _50_ instead of x 30. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04518))_

- If you crush, brew, and drink this stone, you will receive a +4 bonus on progression/healing checks for illnesses that affect the mind (such as dance macabre). One lignia stone harvested from one Lynx can provide three days worth of medicine for one adult, and the stone as well as the powder crushed from it can be stored semi-permanently. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04514))_

In the Sword World RPG, daemons are underworld monsters that live in a material world different from ours. They are known as terrifying slaughterers who followed the dark god Phalaris in the age of the gods, and were summoned to this material world by sorcerers skilled in summoning magic in the age of the ancient kingdom.

## Characteristics

Daemons have a number of special abilities and wicked personalities, making them extremely dangerous. They are said to have a deep connection with the dark gods, and many are also well-versed in dark magic. Although they rarely make their presence felt in this world, those summoned during the days of the ancient kingdom still dwell in underground labyrinths and remote ruins.

Many of them seem to have a humanoid appearance, but there are many different types, such as those with horns, tails, wings, etc. Some sages claim that just as there are different races in our world, there are also different subspecies of daemon. Furthermore, in the original world in which daemons live - the underworld - it's said that they form society just like humans.

## Hierarchy

There are different ranks of daemons. The highest rank ones, called daemon lords and archdaemons, are said to have powers comparable to that of ancient titans or dragons. Below them are types called greater daemons and lesser daemons, and even the lowest rank Lesser Daemons have powers that far exceed those of humans.

With the discovery of two similar daemons (lesser daemons and greater daemons) called doubleburg and doppelgänger, it's become an established theory among sages that daemons are born as lower rank daemons and grow to become higher rank.

## Origins

In the underworld where daemons live, there are also animal-like creatures with low intellect. Sages say that they are comparable to the animals in our world, and claim that this is proof that there are multiple material worlds. These underworld creatures are far more dangerous than the animals of the material world, just as daemons are far more dangerous than humans.

## Key Entities

- **Dark God Phalaris**: The dark god associated with daemons in the age of the gods.
- **Sorcerers**: Characters who can summon daemons using summoning magic.
- **Daemon Lords**: The highest rank daemons with powers comparable to ancient titans or dragons.
- **Archdaemons**: The highest rank daemons, similar to daemon lords.
- **Greater Daemons**: Daemons of a higher rank than lesser daemons.
- **Lesser Daemons**: The lowest rank daemons, but still more powerful than humans.
- **Doubleburg**: A type of daemon that is similar to lesser daemons and greater daemons.
- **Doppelgänger**: A type of daemon that is similar to lesser daemons and greater daemons.

## Key Concepts

- **Underworld**: The material world where daemons live, distinct from the material world of humans.
- **Summoning Magic**: Magic used by sorcerers to summon daemons to the material world.
- **Dark Magic**: Magic associated with daemons and the dark gods.
- **Daemon Hierarchy**: The ranking system of daemons, from lesser to greater to daemon lords/Archdaemons.
- **Multiple Material Worlds**: The idea that there are multiple material worlds, as suggested by the existence of underworld creatures.

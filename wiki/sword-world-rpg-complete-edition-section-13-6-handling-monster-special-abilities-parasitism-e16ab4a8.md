## Statements

- Among monsters, some can affect a character's central nervous system and take over their body. If you're attacked by a monster with this ability, you must make a life force resistance roll against the indicated target score. If you fail, your body will be taken over. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-09842))_

- A role-playing game, like most fiction, takes place in a fictional world. As mentioned earlier, Forcelia, the world you’ll deal with in _Sword World RPG_ , is different from our real world. There is magic, and creatures that can only be found in fiction will come to life. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00060))_

Each has their own characteristics and limitations. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-00061))_

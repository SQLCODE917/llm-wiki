## Statements

- Sorcerers require a special medium to cast spells. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01499))_
- This is a **magical catalyst** , and it is through this that a sorcerer exercises the power of a spell. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01499))_
- Usually, a specially-made mage's staff is used as a magical catalyst, made from a wooden staff with a _Create Device_ spell cast on it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01499))_
- For example, Laverna, the witch of Orphan, uses an amethyst ring she wears on her right middle finger as her magical catalyst, and Cyanos, leader of the magic knights of Ramliearth, carries a small twig wand in his left hand as a magical catalyst. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01499))_

## Technical atoms

### Technical atom 1

**Context:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01496))_

> When you go on an adventure, or you're in a situation where you'd need to use magic, you'd usually bring your spellbook with you. However, no sorcerer looks at their spellbook every time they try to cast a spell. This is because the words and actions of a spell are usually sharply memorized in your head. A spellbook is used to review them, always confirming and ensuring your memory.

**Atom:** _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-01499))_

> This is why you must have a mage's staff as a restriction when using ancient magic.

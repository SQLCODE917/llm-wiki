## Statements

- A score equal to a monster’s **evasion points - 7** is written next to it in parenthesis. This is used here. The game master should roll 2D and add it to the number in parenthesis. This is the target score for a character to hit the monster with an attack. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07071))_
- As in the previous section, this is the process for a success roll with _monster's evasion points -7_ as the _difficulty_ . Therefore, a tie would result in _character's attack success = monster's evasion failure_ . Please remember that the game master should be considered _first_ to roll the dice. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07072))_
- Once the attack hits, it's the same. A monster's _defense points_ are fixed, and the difference is based on the character's strike roll. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07073))_
- Therefore, a tie would result in _character's attack success = monster's evasion failure_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-07072))_

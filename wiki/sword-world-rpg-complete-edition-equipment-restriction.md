- If you use weapons or armor that exceed the thief skill's restrictions, you will automatically have to fight with the fighter skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03191))_
- The ranger skill is also limited by equipment, just like the thief skill, if you want to make full use of its abilities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03220))_
- Also, it is of course illegal to use this skill to commit theft, and if caught, punishment awaits. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03149))_
- The thief skill includes the following abilities. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03150))_
- These can be attempted again if the baseline score for a success roll increases (that is, if the skill level increases or the ability bonus of the relevant ability score increases). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03150))_
- Thieves are skilled at activities that require physical balance and agility, such as tightrope walking and jumping from branch to branch. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03157))_

- This is the ability to move without making a sound. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03168))_

- If the _thief's_ final score is greater, the sound will _never_ be heard. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03169))_
- If there is a _tie_ or the _listener's_ final score is greater, the thief _will_ be overheard. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03169))_
- At this time, the thief makes a success roll using _thief skill + dexterity bonus_ as the baseline score, and the person trying to hear the sound makes one using _adventurer level + intelligence bonus_ as the baseline score. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03169))_
- In particular, if they're not paying _attention_ to the sound, impose a -4 penalty on the listener and make the same check. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03170))_
- At this time, if you can hear the sound, you will be able to discern not only the presence of something sneaking around, but _also_ its general appearance (walking speed, stride length, number of people if there is more than one, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03171))_
- If you fail, it will definitely be exposed to the opponent. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03174))_

- It’s a success if the final score on the thief's side is greater, but it is a failure if it's a tie or if the side being pickpocketed's is greater. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03174))_
- This is the ability to hide in the shadows. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03178))_
- Please make the same check as for _stealthy steps_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03178))_
- This ability is used to climb walls and cliffs. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03185))_
- Please refer to that section for details on determining target scores, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03185))_
- This critical target modifier is _always_ -1, regardless of your thief skill level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03190))_
- For this reason, a thief's attacks have a greater chance of dealing intense damage in one hit. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03190))_
- For more information, please refer to _Chapter 4: Weapon Combat_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03190))_
- However, the way a thief fights is quite different from that of a fighter. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03190))_
- Even thieves have combat abilities and can fight using their thief skill level. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03190))_
- You may change the skill you use in combat each round, but you may not use _different_ skills for attack and defense on the _same_ round (such as defending with the fighter skill, then attacking with the thief skill, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03191))_
- If an adventurer's life force falls to 0 or less due to wounds, it will not recover naturally and there is a risk of death if left untreated. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03219))_
- **First-aid** provided by a ranger will restore your life force to 1 point, allowing you to regain consciousness. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03219))_
- It cannot fail, but it will take about 30 minutes of game time, and it cannot be done while in a state where you can't focus, such as in the middle of combat, etc. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03219))_
- The armor you wear must have a required strength of up to _half_ your own strength, and must be ring mail or non-metal _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03220))_
- The ranger skill has some similar abilities to the thief skill. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03221))_
- However, while the thief skill can be used both outdoors and indoors, the ranger skill is mostly useful only outdoors. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03221))_
- Rangers acquire a good intuition, which can be called a sixth sense. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03235))_
- The target score is determined by the game master depending on the situation, but in general it should be set high. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03235))_
- This ability is the ability used to sense when danger is approaching. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03235))_
- The ranger skill is treated the same as the fighter skill (attack power, bonus damage modifiers) _only_ when attacking with projectiles. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03257))_
- When using the thief skill for defense, you must be lightly armed (required strength of weapons and armor must be _half_ your own strength or less). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03257))_
- The ranger skill is _not_ relevant to the use of or defense from melee weapons. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03257))_

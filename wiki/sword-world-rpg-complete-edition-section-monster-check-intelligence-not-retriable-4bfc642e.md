# **[** ① **Monster Check [Intelligence]] (Not retriable)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This includes knowledge of the various monsters that live in Alecrast. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03283))_
- **[** ③ **Astrology [Intelligence] ] (Not retriable)** Sages have a wide range of knowledge. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03283))_
- From the movement of celestial bodies, you can know the signs of events that will occur in the near future. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03284))_
- Signs should be given as rough information about the incident. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03284))_
- It's enough to let you know that a king passes away or that a major war is about to happen (you can think of it as just a headline in a newspaper, etc.). _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03284))_
- However, this ability _only_ allows you to know about events related to the country or the world, and when _no_ events occur, _no_ signs appear. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03284))_
- It is entirely up to the game master to decide what kind of sign there is. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03284))_
- Each monster has a score called _rarity_ . _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03285))_
- Using _sage skill level + intelligence bonus_ as the baseline score, make a success roll with this rarity as the target score, and if you succeed, you know the monster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03285))_
- If the check fails, the character has no knowledge of the monster. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-03286))_

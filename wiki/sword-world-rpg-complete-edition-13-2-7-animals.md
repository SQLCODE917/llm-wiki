## Source record

This section covers various animals and monsters in the Sword World RPG setting, including wolves, giant rats, hanger legs, eagles, and others.

## Key supported claims

- Wolves are carnivorous and usually act in packs, attacking unsuspecting animals (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-202)
- Rarely, wolf cubs are captured and domesticated as hunting dogs (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-202)
- Such wolves have greater morals than normal ones (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.199-202)

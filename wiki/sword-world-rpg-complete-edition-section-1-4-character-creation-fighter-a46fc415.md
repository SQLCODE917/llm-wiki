## Statements

- There are no restrictions. The basic idea is to choose weapons and armor whose required strength is the same as your own strength. Weapons and armor that have a required strength less than your own strength will make your character's attack and defense incomplete, and weapons and armor with a required strength greater than your own strength cannot be handled. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-0b9dec90-00990))_

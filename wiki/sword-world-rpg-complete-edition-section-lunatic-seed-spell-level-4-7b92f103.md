# **[ Lunatic Seed ] (Spell Level=4)**

From [[sword-world-rpg-complete-edition]].

## Statements

- This is a spell that plants the seeds of madness deep within the target's mind. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04159))_
- They have to be irrational, based on insanity. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04159))_
- However, the caster's commands must be something _erratic_ , so to speak. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04159))_
- This spell's effect lasts until it is negated (as it is a curse, it cannot be removed by other magic belonging to _mind_ ) or until the caster renounces it. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04159))_
- Rational commands will be ignored. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04159))_
- Those under the spell's influence will be unable to act intelligently, even without the caster's commands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04159))_
- Those who've had this spell cast on them will be unable to disobey the caster's commands. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04159))_
- This is a spell that plants the seeds of madness deep within the target's mind. _(Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf (source-range-870a16bc-04159))_

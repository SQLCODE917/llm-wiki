## Source record

This page summarizes the structure of the Sword World RPG rulebook and essential items needed to play, including character sheets, dice, and handling fractions.

## Key supported claims

- Rulebook has five major parts, including player and game master sections (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.7-7).
- Players need character sheets, pencils, erasers, and notepads (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.7-7).
- Game uses six-sided dice, often rolling two dice at once (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.7-7).
- Fractions are rounded up if not specified otherwise (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.7-7).
- Players do not need to memorize game master rules (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.7-7).

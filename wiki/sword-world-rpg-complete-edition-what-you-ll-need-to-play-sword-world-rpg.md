## Source record

This section introduces the structure of the Sword World RPG rulebook and what players need to know to start playing.

## Key supported claims

- The rulebook consists of five major parts, including a welcome section, player section, game master section, optional rules, and a guide to Forcelia and Alecrast (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.7-7).
- Players need character sheets and basic supplies like pencils, erasers, and notepads to play (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.7-7).
- The game uses ordinary six-sided dice, with rolling 1D or 2D referring to rolling one or two dice respectively (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.7-7).
- When division results in a fraction, it should be rounded up if not otherwise specified (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.7-7).
- The game is set on the continent of Alecrast, part of the world called Forcelia, which players should understand for common background setting (raw/Sword World RPG - Complete Edition.pdf p.7-7).

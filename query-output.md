A build order in Age of Empires II is a sequence of actions that guides players to efficiently reach specific ages in the game. It is designed to minimize villager movement between resources and help players get to feudal age efficiently. Build orders are written sequences that simplify and tune gameplay into formats where villagers move between resources as little as possible.
